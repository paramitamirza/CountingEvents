Covington man dead in accidental shooting
COVINGTON –
A Covington man is dead after an apparent accidental shooting in the village .

Covington police and medics were summoned to an address on Regency Court between 1:30 and 2 p.m. on Tuesday after a man was reported to have been shot .

Chief Lee Harmon of the Covington Police Department said that the shooting appears to be accidental .
“
Two brothers were going to go target shooting at their parent ’s house , ” said Harmon , “ when the gun that one of the brothers was carrying discharged .
”

Harmon said that the weapon was a shotgun .

The victim was transported to Upper Valley Medical Center but was pronounced dead on arrival .

No names have been released pending notification of family .
