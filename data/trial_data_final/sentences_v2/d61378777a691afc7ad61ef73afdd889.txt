2017-02-20
12-year - old boy fatally shot , police investigating
Texarkana , Ark. Police are investigating a shooting that killed a 12-year - old boy Saturday in Texarkana .

According to police they responded to E. 50th Street around 2 p.m. Saturday to a report of an accidental shooting .
Upon arrival officers discovered a 12-year - boy had been shot .

The boy was pronounced dead at a local hospital .

A 13-year - old juvenile was also on scene .
According to police the two juveniles were playing with the gun when it went off .

According to TAPD , “ the criminal investigation division is continuing an investigation into the cause of the shooting .
”
 Counselors will be available to students at College Hill Middle School , the school the victim attended .
