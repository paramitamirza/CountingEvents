2100 block of Indiana Avenue closed after shooting - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports
Savannah Chatham Metropolitan Police responded to a shooting on the 2100 block of Indiana Avenue after a man was shot around 1 p.m. on Saturday .

The shooter is believed to have shot the victim after a possible argument .

Police say the shooter took off in a getaway car .
At this time there is no description of the car , the shooter , or where he or she might be .

We will update as we get more information .
