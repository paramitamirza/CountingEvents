4-year - old accidentally shot , mother arrested
Milwaukee police are investigating an accidental shooting involving a four - year - old boy .

It happened around 1 p.m. Sunday near the the 4900 block of N. 60th Street .

Milwaukee police say the mother left her handgun in a location that was accessible to the child and he accidentally shot himself .

The boy was taken to a local hospital for a non - life - threatening injury .

The boy 's mother was arrested and Milwaukee police say the case will be reviewed by the Milwaukee County District Attorney 's Office later this week .
