Man accused of killing 14-year - old indicted on murder charge - Toledo News Now , News , Weather , Sports , Toledo , OH
A man accused of shooting and killing a 14-year - old boy is back in jail after a Lucas County grand jury indicted him on a murder charge .

Leonard Temple was arrested and charged with reckless homicide in January for the shooting death of 14-year - old Dominique Alexander .

Temple was out on bond when the grand jury indicted him for murder in the Alexander case .

Temple is back in the Lucas County Jail .

His court appearance has not yet been announced .
