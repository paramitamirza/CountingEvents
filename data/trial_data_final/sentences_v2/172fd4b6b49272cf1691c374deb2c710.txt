 A juvenile male was taken to HSHS St. Anthony 's Memorial Hospital in Effingham Monday due to an apparently self - inflicted gunshot wound .

Effingham City Police Chief Jeff Fuesting said police were called at 11:47am about the incident , which occurred at 811 East Jefferson .
Chief Fuesting said he 's not certain who notified police of the incident .
The name of the juvenile is not yet available .

Fuesting said additional information surrounding the incident is still being gathered .
