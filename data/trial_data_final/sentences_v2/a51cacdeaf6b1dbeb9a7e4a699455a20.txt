 SAN DIEGO -- A 16-year - old boy accidentally shot himself Monday in Encanto , police said .

The teen was loitering in the area of 6000 Brooklyn Ave .
around 7:45 p.m. when a resident asked him to leave , said San Diego police Officer John Buttle .

Shortly after , the teen attempted to remove a handgun from his waistband and shot himself in the left thigh , Buttle said .

He was taken to a hospital with a non - life - threatening injury , Buttle said .
