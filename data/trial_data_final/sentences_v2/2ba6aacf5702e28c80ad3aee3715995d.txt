Newton County deputies investigate accidental shooting
NEOSHO , Mo. - It happened at the conservation area gun range east of Neosho around 10 Friday morning .

The Newton County Sheriff is not releasing the name of the victim and his exact condition is unknown right now .

The Sheriff 's Office says the victim was about 15 yards in front of another shooter whose gun discharged by accident .
The Sheriff says the gun was being put back together when it fired , with the bullet striking the victim in the back .
