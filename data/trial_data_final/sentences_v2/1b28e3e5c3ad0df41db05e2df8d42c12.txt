Man Shot in Family Feud
Swoyersville , Luzerne County ( WBRE/WYOU ) -- A family feud sends a man is to the hospital Friday , after he got shot by his brother .

Police are investigating what they call an accidental shooting that happened on Denison street in Swoyersville around midnight .
Police tell Eyewitness News two brothers got into an argument - and one man picked up a shotgun , to " Intimidate " his brother .

They say he did n't think it was loaded and pulled the trigger .
His brother , was recovering in the hospital Friday morning .

The suspect is in custody .
