Police : Man tries to shoot alleged burglar , hits girlfriend instead
Police say a homeowner attempting to shoot an alleged home invasion suspect accidentally shot his girlfriend instead .
( Photo : KHOU )
 HOUSTON - Police say a homeowner attempting to shoot an alleged home invasion suspect accidentally shot his girlfriend instead .

The incident happened Friday night at a home off of I-10 near Silber Road in west Houston .

Police say the homeowner heard someone breaking into his home , so he grabbed his gun and opened fire , striking his girlfriend .

The woman was transported to the hospital and is expected to recover from her injuries .

It is unknown at this time whether the homeowner will face charges .
