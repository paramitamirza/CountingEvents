Police Investigate West Erie Shooting - Erie News Now : News , Weather & Sports | WICU 12 & WSEE
Police are investigating a shooting on Erie 's west side .

It happened just after 9:30 a.m. in the 300 block of West 3rd Street .

An 18-year - old man was accidentally shot in the face , according to reports from the scene .

He was reportedly conscious as emergency crews took him to UPMC Hamot for treatment .
