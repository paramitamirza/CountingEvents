1 shot in Miami 's Brownsville neighborhood
MIAMI - A shooting left one person wounded on Friday night in Miami 's Brownsville neighborhood , according Miami - Dade Fire Rescue .

Paramedics responded to the corner of Northwest 24 Avenue and Northwest 51 Street about 9 p.m.
 MDFR rushed the victim to Jackson Memorial Hospital 's Ryder Trauma Center .
