Teen shot in Jennings - KMOV.com
A 15-year - old boy was shot in the chest area in Jennings Monday afternoon , according to St. Louis County Police .

Police responded to the 3000 block of Glade Avenue where it 's believed the victim ran to , according to a neighbor .

Another neighbor told News 4 a car sped down Ellison Drive and shots came from the car towards a group of people walking along Solway Avenue .

The witness said the car then backed up and traveled down Ellison Drive .

Police do n't have any suspect description at this time .
