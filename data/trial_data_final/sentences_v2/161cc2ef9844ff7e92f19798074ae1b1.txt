2 people injured in shooting in Chelsea
Teen killed in Chelsea shooting ID'ed , new images of car released
 A teenager is dead after a shooting in Chelsea Friday .

 The shooting happened at Shurtleff and Bellingham streets around 5:30 p.m.

The 15-year - old victim , identified as Jimmy Vasquez , was found in the hallway of a building in the area , shot at least once in the abdomen .
Police said the victim and his friends were hanging out nearby when the shots rang out .

A second teen , also 15 years old , was shot in the foot and was treated at an area hospital .

Chelsea Public Schools said the Vasquez attended Chelsea High School and has three younger siblings .

Police said they are looking for a car that the shooter fled in described as a gray 2010 Toyota Corolla with a resident parking sticker on the back .

The vehicle is seen on surveillance video parking at the corner of Shurtleff St. at Grove St. in heavy traffic , backing and turning down the street against one way traffic onto Broadway .

Authorities said the investigation is still active and they are seeking the public 's help in locating the vehicle .

Anyone with information is asked to call Chelsea police at 617 - 466 - 4880 .
