1 man dead in shooting , another in custody
GREEN BAY , Wis. - Police are investigating a fatal shooting in Green Bay .

Officers responded to the shooting scene on the city 's east side about 3:20 a.m. Monday .
They found one man dead and took a second man in custody .

Police said they are in the early stages of the investigation , but are confident the public is not in danger .
