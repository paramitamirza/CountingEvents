Police : Teen shot while trying to commit robbery
A Milwaukee police squad car .
( Photo : Mike De Sisti / Milwaukee Journal Sentinel )

A 17-year - old boy committing a robbery was shot by his would - be victim Monday night , suffering a non - life - threatening injury , Milwaukee police said .

The teen told police he tried to rob someone about 11:30 p.m. near S. 25th and W. Mitchell streets when he was shot , investigators said .
The boy went to a local hospital for treatment .

The names of the teen and shooter were not released .
An investigation is ongoing .
