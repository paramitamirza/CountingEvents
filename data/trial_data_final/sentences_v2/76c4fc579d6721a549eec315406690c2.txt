Three teens shot in Miami
 Three teens were shot in northwest Miami - Dade County on Thursday , police said .

The shooting took place in the 2400 block of Northwest 50th Street .

The victims include a 14-year - old boy , a 15-year - old girl and an 18-year - old man .

All three victims were taken to Ryder Trauma Center in stable condition .

Miami - Dade Public Schools Superintendent Alberto Carvalho took to Twitter to comment on the shooting .
