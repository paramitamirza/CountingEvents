Sinai Man Shoots Himself While Unloading Gun
( Photo by George Frey/Getty Images )

The Brookings County Sheriff ’s Department has confirmed an accidental shooting in a small town southwest of Brookings .

On Tuesday ( January 3 ) in the 300 block of 4th Street , Sinai a 37-year - old male was unloading a 9mm handgun that accidentally fired and the round struck him .
He was taken by ambulance to the Brookings Hospital and later airlifted to Avera McKennan Hospital in Sioux Falls .

The Sheriff ’s Office is investigating .
The Sinai Fire Department and Brookings Ambulance also responded .
