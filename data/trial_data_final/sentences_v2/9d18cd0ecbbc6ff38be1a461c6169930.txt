Teen shot , calls for help behind home
 Omaha police were called around midnight Saturday to an area near 48th and Saratoga streets for a report of shots fired .

Advertisement
 Four minutes later , a man called 911 , telling them he was behind a house near 48th and Browne streets and that he had been shot .

According to police , a vehicle drove by shooting multiple rounds toward the teenager on 48th Street and Grand Avenue , hitting him at least once .

The victim ran northbound a couple blocks before calling police and was taken to Nebraska Medicine and is expected to be OK.

Police closed off parts of 48th and Camden Streets and 48th and Grand Avenue while investigating .

Police have not released any additional information .
