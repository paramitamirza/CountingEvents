Teen accidentally shoots , kills self - KAIT Jonesboro , AR - Region 8 News , weather , sports
An Arkansas teen is dead following an accidental shooting Monday night , according to the Crittenden County Sheriff 's office .

Crittenden County Sheriff 's Office said a 16-year - old accidentally shot himself while putting a gun in his waist band .

The shooting happened around 10:30 p.m. Monday on Lincoln Avenue in Earle , Arkansas .

Deputies said they are still investigating the shooting .
