One person dead in officer - involved shooting in NE Portland - KPTV - FOX 12
One person was killed in an officer - involved shooting in northeast Portland Thursday , according to the Portland Police Bureau .

Police responded to the scene in the 8300 block of Northeast Hancock Street around 7:30 a.m.

According to officers , the shooting occurred during the search for the suspect of a robbery that occurred earlier at the Portland Value Inn on Northeast 82nd Avenue .

Police said the suspect was killed and no officers were injured .
