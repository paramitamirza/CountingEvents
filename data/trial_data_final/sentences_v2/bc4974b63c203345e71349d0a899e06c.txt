2 killed in western Montana shooting , suspect in custody - KTVQ.com | Q2 | Continuous News Coverage | Billings , MT
Two people are dead after a shooting west of Lolo in western Montana .

MCSO Spokeswoman Brenda Bassett confirmed one male and one female were found dead at the scene .

Bassett said the suspect surrendered to law enforcement and is now in custody .

A handgun has also been recovered from the scene .
