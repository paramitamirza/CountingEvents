2017-02-07
Frazee accidental shooting under investigation
by Don Haney
 FRAZEE , MINN , ( KFGO ) - The Becker County Sheriff 's Office says a Frazee man accidentally shot himself in the hip .

Deputies responded to an emergecy call around 7 last night on East Main Avenue in Frazee .

Investigators says 27-year old Jared Hanson was handling a revolver when it discharged .

Hanson was taken to the Detroit Lakes hospital and was later transferred to Fargo .

A condition is not available .

The incident is under investigation .
