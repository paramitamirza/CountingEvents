Police seek suspect in hotel shooting - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports
Savannah - Chatham Metropolitan Police Department is investigating after a juvenile was shot in the hand at the Suburban Extended Stay Hotel on Abercorn Street , near the Truman Parkway .

The victim 's wounds are non - life threatening .

Police are seeking Anthony Taylor as a possible suspect in this case .
He is described as an African - American male standing at 6'2 and weighing 350 pounds .
He was last seen driving westbound on Abercorn Street in a silver 4 door Dodge with a paper tag .
If you see Taylor , please call 911 .
