Police : Man wounded in arm by accidental shooting

EVERETT —
Police believe a shooting near Lake Stickney on Monday was accidental .

A man in his 40s was shot in his hand , said Shari Ireton with the Snohomish County Sheriff ’s Office .
It happened about 4:30 p.m. on 12th Place W. , between Lynnwood and Everett .

The man was alone in the home at the time , Ireton said .
He told detectives that he was placing the gun on a table when it fired .
