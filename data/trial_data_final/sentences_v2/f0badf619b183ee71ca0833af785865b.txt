Possible charges coming after K - State student injured by self - inflicted gunshot
A nineteen - year - old Kansas State University student who was injured when he accidentally shot himself Sunday could face charges for the incident .

Campus officials say campus police are investigating criminal charges against the student , whose name has not been released .
They note that the University 's policy bars possession of firearms in residence halls .

The University says campus police were called shortly after 8:00 p.m. to a student 's room in Goodnow Hall .
When they arrived , they determined the student was injured by a gunshot .

The student is reportedly in stable condition .

The University says there is no threat to the university .

Kansas State University 's Office of Student Life and Counseling Serves are offering counseling and assistance to students and employees .
