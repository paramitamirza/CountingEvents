A teenager was taken to the hospital Sunday afternoon after being shot on Tidewater Drive in Norfolk .

Police say the shooting , which happened near Princess Anne Road , was reported around 2:45 p.m. Responding officers found a 16-year - old boy suffering from a gunshot wound .

The boy told officers he was walking on Tidewater Drive when he heard gunfire , and realized he had been shot .
Police say he was taken to the hospital and is expected to recover .

Police say there is not suspect information .
