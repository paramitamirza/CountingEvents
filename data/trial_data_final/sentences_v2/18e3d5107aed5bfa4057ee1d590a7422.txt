Wichita , Kansas News , Weather , Sports - 1 dead after southeast Wichita shooting Saturday
One person has died early Saturday morning after a shooting in southeast Wichita

The shooting occurred overnight in the 1700th block of S. Yale – near Harry and Hillside , said Wichita Police Sgt .
Doug Gerdes .

“
My understanding is that there was a disturbance and one individual was shot and was later taken to the hospital where he later succumbed to his injuries , ” Gerdes said .

Two persons of interest are in custody .
Investigators are interviewing people in the case .
