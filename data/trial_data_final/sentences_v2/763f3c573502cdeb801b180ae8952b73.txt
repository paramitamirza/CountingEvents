4 year old accidentally shoots self playing with gun , life threatening injuries
BIRMINGHAM , Ala ( WIAT ) :
A 4 year old child accidentally shot himself while playing with a gun , says Lt .
Sean Edwards of Birmingham Police .

Edwards says the child is facing life threatening injuries .

No one is in custody at this point , says Sgt .
Bryan Shelton of Birmingham Police .

WIAT has a crew on the scene and will update as we get more information .
