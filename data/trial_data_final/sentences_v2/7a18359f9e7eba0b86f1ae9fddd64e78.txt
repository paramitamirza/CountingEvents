Suspect in South Bexar County murder arrested
James Cardona , a suspect in the fatal shooting of a woman in South Bexar County , has been taken into custody , The Bexar County Sheriff 's Office said Saturday .

Cardona was arrested without incident just after 8 p.m. Friday at his aunt 's home in Fentress , Texas by the Lone Star Fugitive Task Force .

Cardona is accused of shooting Tamara Martinez , 31 , and her 13-year - old daughter in the 21300 block of South Highway 281 on March 28 .

The Bexar County Sheriff ’s Office has said Martinez was Cardona ’s common - law wife .

Cardona has now been taken to Caldwell County jail where he has been charged murder and aggravated assault with a deadly weapon .

No bond has been set yet .

Cardona will be extradited back to Bexar County in the near future , the Bexar County Sheriff 's Office said .
