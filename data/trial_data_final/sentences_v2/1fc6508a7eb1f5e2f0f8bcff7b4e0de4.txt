Two Accidental Shootings Reported in Bartow County Wednesday
Bartow County Emergency Responders were dispatched to two accidental shootings on Wednesday .

Reports stated that the first occurred at Heritage Dr off East Valley to where an 18 year - old male was shot in the leg .
The wound was quickly assessed and treated but due to the severity of the injury he had to be flown to Grady Hospital .

The second occurred on Benefield Circle off Grassdale Rd .
This shooting involved a 31 year - old male victim with a gunshot wound to his leg .
Reports added that the man said he was cleaning his gun when it went off .

The victim was transported to Cartersville Medical Center for further treatment .
