NOPD : Two women shot in N.O. East
NEW ORLEANS –
Police are investigating a double shooting Sunday that left two women injured in New Orleans East . NEWLINE
The incident happened shortly after 8:30 p.m. in the 8900 block of Curran Boulevard . NEWLINE
Police said initial reports showed one woman was shot in the leg .
The other woman received a graze wound to the head , said authorities .
There is no word on their conditions . NEWLINE
No suspect or motive has been released . NEWLINE
Anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers at ( 504 ) 822 - 1111 . NEWLINE
