Woman dies after being shot inside a vehicle near Twain and Maryland
A woman was shot inside a vehicle about 4:30 a.m. today in the central valley in what Metro says appears to be an accidental shooting NEWLINE The shooting occurred in the 3700 block of Cambridge , near East Twain Avenue and just west of South Maryland Parkway , according to Metro . NEWLINE
The victim was transported to Sunrise Hospital but died after arrival . NEWLINE
The investigation initiated by Metro 's Homicide Section indicated the victim and her female friend were in a parked vehicle with two males they had met while attending a New Year ’s Eve event , according to a Metro news release .
While inside the vehicle , investigators believe a firearm was handled and accidentally discharged , striking the female victim . NEWLINE
At this time , no arrests have been made and this remains and ongoing investigation .
Once completed , this case will be submitted to the Clark County district attorney ’s office for review and consideration of criminal charges NEWLINE This is the first homicide investigated by Metro in 2017 .
The identity of the victim , as well as the cause and manner of death , will be released by the Clark County coroner ’s office . NEWLINE
