The fatal shooting happened about 9:30 p.m. , when a black Dodge Ram pulled up next to 27-year - old Geovani Mexicano and a 29-year - old woman in the 4700 block of South Leamington Avenue . NEWLINE
They started arguing with the people in the truck , who then opened fire .
Mexicano was shot in the chest and pronounced dead on the scene , sources said .
The woman was shot in the shoulder and went to Mount Sinai Hospital .
