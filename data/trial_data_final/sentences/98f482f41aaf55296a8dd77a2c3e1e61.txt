2 teenage boys wounded in East Garfield Park shooting
2 teenage boys wounded in East Garfield Park shooting NEWLINE Two teenagers were wounded in an East Garfield Park neighborhood shooting early Sunday on the West Side . NEWLINE
About 4:20 a.m. , the boys , ages 15 and 17 , were standing on the sidewalk in the 700 block of North Hamlin when someone in a black SUV fired shots , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
The older boy suffered multiple gunshot wounds to the abdomen and was taken in serious condition to Stroger Hospital , police said .
The younger boy was shot in the left shoulder and was also taken to Stroger , where his condition was stabilized .
