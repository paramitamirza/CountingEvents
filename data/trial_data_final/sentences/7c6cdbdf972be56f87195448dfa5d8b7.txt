UPDATE : Student brings BB gun to Dunbar Middle School
A student brought a BB gun to Dunbar Middle School on Monday morning , according to a Lynchburg police spokesman . NEWLINE
No one was hurt , and classes were not interrupted . NEWLINE
The spokesman would not say at this time whether the student is facing any charges , or what the motive might have been . NEWLINE
Lynchburg police are investigating the incident . NEWLINE
Here is the complete announcement from Lynchburg police : NEWLINE On January 23 , 2017 at approximately 9:32 a.m. , School Resource Officers investigated an incident at Dunbar Middle School in which a student was allegedly in possession of a handgun on school property . NEWLINE
An investigation has revealed that the handgun was actually a BB pistol which was located in a backpack .
The backpack had been brought to school by a 13-year - old student .
This student did not display , brandish , or otherwise use the BB pistol in a manner that was threatening to others and it was not brought openly to school .
At this time , it appears this is an isolated incident and it is not believed there is any danger to faculty or students as related to this incident . NEWLINE
At this time , no charges are being filed and the Lynchburg City School NEWLINE administration will be working with the School Resource Officers to determine an effective resolution to this investigation . NEWLINE
