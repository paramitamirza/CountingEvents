ROCHESTER , N.Y. ( WROC - TV ) -
The Rochester Police Department says a man has died in what they believe to be an accidental shooting that took place on Sunday . NEWLINE
Police say they were called to Orange Street for reports of a person shot .
They say the man had died by the time they arrived at the scene . NEWLINE
Police are still working to determine what may have caused the accident and are continuing their investigation at this time .
