14-year - old shot outside Northside club
JACKSONVILLE , Fla. - A 14-year - old was shot in the leg early Sunday morning in the parking lot of a nightclub on New Kings Road , just north of Moncrief Road West , according to police . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 2:30 a.m. outside the LaVilla Sportsman Club .
The teenager went to a home near the club , where police were called . NEWLINE
The boy , whose name was not released , was taken to the hospital with a non - life - threatening gunshot wound . NEWLINE
The Jacksonville Sheriff 's Office is searching for the gunman and have not released suspect information .
It 's unknown why the teen was at the club . NEWLINE
The shooting remains under investigation .
Anyone who witnessed the shooting or has any information is asked to call police at 904 - 630 - 0500 or CrimeStoppers at 866 - 845-TIPS. NEWLINE
