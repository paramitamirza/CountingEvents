2-year - old Texas boy shoots himself in head , father arrested - KCBD NewsChannel 11 Lubbock
CORPUS CHRISTI , Texas ( AP ) -
Police in South Texas have arrested a father hours after his 2-year - old son found a loaded gun on the kitchen table and fatally shot himself in the head . NEWLINE
Corpus Christi police officer Gena Pena says 22-year - old Eric Javier Morales is charged with criminally negligent homicide and is being held at the Nueces ( nyoo - AY'-sis ) County jail . NEWLINE
Pena says the child shot himself Sunday morning .
Morales was not home at the time , but the child 's mother was there , as was a 1-year - old child . NEWLINE
Morales went to the hospital where the 2-year - old boy was taken and later met with investigators before being charged . NEWLINE
Jail records do n't indicate whether Morales has an attorney who can respond to the allegations against him . NEWLINE
