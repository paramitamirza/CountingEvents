Investigation into Shooting at 8535 Martin Luther King Boulevard
Incident Update NEWLINE Houston Police Department NEWLINE FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE NEWLINE DATE : January 17 , 2017 2:20:54 PM CST NEWLINE Investigation into Shooting at 8535 Martin Luther King Boulevard NEWLINE Houston police are investigating the shooting of at least three people at 8535 Martin Luther King Boulevard about 6:20 p.m. on Monday ( Jan. 16 ) . NEWLINE
The victims , a male , 17 , and two females , 13 and 17 years of age , were transported to area hospitals and are expected to survive their injuries . NEWLINE
HPD Homicide Division Sergeant H. Garcia and Officer K. McDonald reported : NEWLINE At least three persons were shot by an unknown suspect as a large group of people were gathered at the intersection of MLK Boulevard and Reed Road .
At this time , there is no known motive or suspect(s ) in the shooting . NEWLINE
Investigators are attempting to determine if there are any other confirmed shooting victims . NEWLINE
