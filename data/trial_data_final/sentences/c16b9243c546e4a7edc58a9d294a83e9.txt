Juvenile shooting victim jailed following release from hospital - WRCBtv.com | Chattanooga News , Weather & Sports
Chattanooga police are investigating a shooting Friday evening that sent a juvenile to the hospital and ultimately to jail . NEWLINE
The shooting was reported shortly before 4:45 p.m. NEWLINE
Police say the shooting victim showed up at a local hospital with a gunshot wound that was not life - threatening . NEWLINE
The victim would not cooperate with police when they tried to get information about the incident . NEWLINE
Police say they were able to connect the victim to a shots fired call at 2:54 p.m. Friday from the 3900 block of Manor Drive . NEWLINE
Police say the victim had multiple outstanding warrants and was taken to the juvenile detention center after being released from the hospital . NEWLINE
Anyone with any information about this case is asked to call Chattanooga police at 423 - 698 - 2525 . NEWLINE
