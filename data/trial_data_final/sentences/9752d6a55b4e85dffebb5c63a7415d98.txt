Duluth Police Investigate Shooting , Enter East Hillside Apartment
Officers responded to a hospital at about 1:30 p.m. on a report of a shooting victim , and then established a crime scene in the area of 7th Avenue East and 4th Street in the East Hillside neighborhood .
The intersection was closed and police tape was put up around an apartment building . NEWLINE
Just before 5 p.m. , what appeared to be a tactical team approached an apartment 's door , said they were executing a search warrant , and demanded the occupants come to the door .
Authorities entered the apartment about 15 minutes later .
