Man accidentally shoots himself in Colorado Springs car dealership parking lot
A 19-year - old man was arrested Tuesday after he accidentally shot himself in the leg while on a break from his job at a Colorado Springs car dealership , police said . NEWLINE
At about 1:49 p.m. , police were called to a dealership on the 700 block of Automotive Drive .
The employee , Daniel Starkman , was allegedly handling his personal handgun alone in his car when he inadvertently discharged the weapon , said Lt .
Robert Weber of Colorado Springs police . NEWLINE
Starkman was taken to an area hospital .
He was arrested on suspicion of prohibited use of a weapon , served court papers and released , Weber said .
