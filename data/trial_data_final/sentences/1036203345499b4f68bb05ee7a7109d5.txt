Man accidentally shoots friend in leg
Posted Wednesday , February 22 , 2017 11:18 am NEWLINE FORSYTH COUNTY , Ga. —
While looking at his friend ’s new guns , a man accidentally shot the friend in the leg . NEWLINE
The friend said he was showing the man his new guns and told him the gun was unloaded .
The man looked at the first gun and pulled the trigger and nothing happened .
He did it again with the second , but didn’t realize it was loaded and accidentally shot his friend in the leg . NEWLINE
The friend was helped by EMS and the shooter wasn’t arrested because all evidence indicated this was an accident .
