Hope , AR man dies in reported accidental shooting - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports
Hope , Ark. , police are investigating a report of a deadly accidental shooting . NEWLINE
It happened about 2:30 p.m. Tuesday at a business in the 200 block of East Third Street , Assistant Police Chief Kim Tomlin said . NEWLINE
Officers found the business owner Norman Bobo lying on the floor with a gunshot wound to the chest at B&B Wrecker Services & Auto Repair . NEWLINE
Bobo was in critical condition when he was taken to Wadley Regional Medical Center at Hope by Pafford EMS , Tomlin said .
He was pronounced dead just before 3 p.m. NEWLINE Police said their preliminary investigation based on statements by witnesses indicates two men were handling a weapon when it fired . NEWLINE
