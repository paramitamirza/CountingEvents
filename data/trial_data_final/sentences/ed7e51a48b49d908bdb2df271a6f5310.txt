Boulder police : Man accidentally shot by friend playing with handgun
A Boulder man was accidentally shot in the chest after the gun his friend was playing with went off after the two had returned from a night of partying this weekend . NEWLINE
Boulder police were called to Boulder Community Health 's Foothills Hospital on New Year 's Day after a 23-year - old male came in with a gunshot wound to his chest .
Police said that the man and a friend had been out clubbing overnight and came back to a house in Boulder and started playing with a gun . NEWLINE
The gun then accidentally went off , hitting one of the men .
Boulder police spokeswoman Shannon Cordingly said he is expected to survive the gunshot . NEWLINE
Cordingly said the incident is still under investigation and police have not yet determined if any charges are warranted in the case . NEWLINE
