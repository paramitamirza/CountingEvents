Shotgun blast kills 11-year - old in Tennessee hunting trip - WRCBtv.com | Chattanooga News , Weather & Sports
SPRINGFIELD , Tenn. ( AP ) -
An 11-year - old boy has been fatally shot during a hunting trip in northern Tennessee . NEWLINE
Adams Fire Chief Ray Brown says the boy was shot in the side by a 20-gauge shotgun . NEWLINE
Robertson County Emergency Management Services Assistant Director Russell Gupton says the boy was accidentally shot Sunday afternoon at the Three Feathers Hunting Preserve in Cedar Hill . NEWLINE
It is unclear how the shooting occurred or who was with the boy at the time .
He was not immediately identified , and no charges were immediately filed . NEWLINE
Gupton says initial reports indicated he was 12 , but records from Robertson EMS say the child was 11 and lived in Clarksville . NEWLINE
Three Feathers ' website says the preserve specializes in private hunts for game birds . NEWLINE
