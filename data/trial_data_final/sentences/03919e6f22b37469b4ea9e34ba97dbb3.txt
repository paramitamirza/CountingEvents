4 shot outside West Englewood convenience store
4 shot outside West Englewood convenience store NEWLINE Four people were shot outside a convenience store Sunday afternoon in the West Englewood neighborhood on the South Side . NEWLINE
About 12:15 p.m. , all four victims were inside a convenience store in the 1800 block of West 63rd Street when they heard several gunshots , according to Chicago Police .
They ran outside , where they were met by four armed males who opened fire . NEWLINE
A 26-year - old man was shot in the right eye and driven by a family member to St. Bernard Hospital , then was transferred to Stroger Hospital in critical condition , police said . NEWLINE
Another 26-year - old man was shot in the buttocks and left foot and taken to Christ Medical Center in Oak Lawn , where his condition stabilized , police said . NEWLINE
A 56-year - old woman was shot multiple times in the left leg and also taken to Christ , where her condition stabilized , police said . NEWLINE
A 48-year - old woman was shot in the right hip and also taken to Christ , where her condition stabilized . NEWLINE
After the shooting , the four suspects got into a white car and fled the scene , police said .
