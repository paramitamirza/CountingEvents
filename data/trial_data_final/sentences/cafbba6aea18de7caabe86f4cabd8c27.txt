Police : One man dead , child injured in North Charleston shooting - Live5News.com | Charleston , SC | News , Weather , Sports
One man is dead and an infant child is injured after a shooting off East Montague Avenue Friday night , said a North Charleston Police spokesman . NEWLINE
Spokesman Spencer Pryor said officers responded shortly before 10 p.m. to the area of E. Montague near Wright Avenue in reference to someone shooting another person . NEWLINE
When officers arrived on scene , they located a man on the ground near Independent Avenue and E. Montague suffering from gunshot wounds , Pryor said . NEWLINE
Medical personnel arrived on scene and later pronounced the man dead . NEWLINE
Pryor said a man wearing all black was described by witnesses as a suspect in the shooting . NEWLINE
The child who was with the victim at the time of the shooting , suffered a wound to the leg and was transported to MUSC with non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
E. Montague Avenue at Hassell Avenue to Ezekiel Avenue was blocked off for several hours Friday night . NEWLINE
Bystanders gathered in the area as officers controlled the crime scene , and the forensic unit collected evidence from the area . NEWLINE
The incident remains under investigation . NEWLINE
