Police investigate after man found dying on Diplomat Drive - ABC21 : Your Weather Authority
Police are investigating after a man was found dying on Diplomat Drive . NEWLINE
The Fort Wayne Police Department said officers responded to the 3200 block of Diplomat Drive around 6:20 Thursday morning on a report of a man down .
When police arrived at the scene , they found a man down along the curb line .
The man was transported from the scene in critical condition , but the department said he was later pronounced dead at a local hospital . NEWLINE
The man was found by a passerby who called the police department .
Detectives do not know who the man is at the time of this report and are canvassing the immediate neighborhood attempting to get more information .
The Allen County Coroner will conduct an autopsy to establish the cause and manner of death .
