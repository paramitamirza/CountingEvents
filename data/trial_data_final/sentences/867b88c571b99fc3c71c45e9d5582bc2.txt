3 adults , 5 minors shot at MLK Day celebration in Miami
Aerial shots showed police entering a home near 31st Avenue in Miami , following a shooting that injured 3 at an MLK Day celebration .

Eight people were shot , including five minors , during a Martin Luther King Day celebration in Miami , Miami - Dade Police told CNN affiliate WPLG. NEWLINE Shots fired just after 3:40 p.m. sent crowds into chaos near the Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial Park at NW 60th Street and NW 30th Ave . NEWLINE
Miami - Dade Police says they have detained two people , who are being questioned , and recovered two weapons . NEWLINE
Miami Beach Police and Miami - Dade Fire Rescue responded and attended to the injured .
They were transported to Miami area hospitals . NEWLINE
According to Miami - Dade Fire , one woman was shot in the MLK Park , while two other people were shot near NW 32nd Avenue .
Police spokesman Det .
Daniel Ferrin says the victims range in age from 11 to 30 . NEWLINE
Ferrin also says five of the victims are stable , while one is in critical condition .
