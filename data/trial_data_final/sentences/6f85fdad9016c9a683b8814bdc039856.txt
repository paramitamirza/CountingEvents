1 hospitalized after shooting in northeast Las Vegas Valley
Las Vegas police are investigating a shooting in the far northeast Las Vegas Valley . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 8 p.m. on the 6900 block of Issac Drive , near Lake Mead and Hollywood boulevards , Metropolitan Police Department Lt .
Carlos Hank said . NEWLINE
An officer on scene said a person , possibly a juvenile , was taken to a nearby hospital with injuries that were not life - threatening . NEWLINE
As of 10 p.m. , police were still trying to find a suspect .
No other information was available . NEWLINE
