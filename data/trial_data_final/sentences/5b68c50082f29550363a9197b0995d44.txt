SPRING , Texas ( AP ) - Authorities are tracing the family of a 15-year - old boy who died after a Russian roulette shooting . NEWLINE
Harris County sheriff 's detectives are withholding the name of the boy , who shot himself Friday at the home of a friend near Spring , a northern suburb of Houston .
He died Saturday . NEWLINE
The father of the friend said he picked up the boy from a trailer park Thursday evening for a sleepover .
The man said he went to the gym to work out Friday morning when his son called frantically . NEWLINE
The father said he does n't own a gun and does n't know where the death weapon came from , but his son abstained from the deadly game . NEWLINE
The father told investigators that the wounded boy had occasionally been a group home resident and under foster care . NEWLINE
