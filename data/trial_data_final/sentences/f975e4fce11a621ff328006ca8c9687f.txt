Boulder police investigate accidental shooting
Police investigate a possible shooting on 5th Street in Boulder on Friday evening .
( Jeremy Papasso Staff Photographer ) NEWLINE
Boulder police are investigating an accidental shooting which occurred Friday evening and left two men wounded . NEWLINE
The incident occurred about 6:30 p.m. Friday at a home in the 3100 block of 5th Street . NEWLINE
Upon arrival officers discovered two male victims , one with an arm and abdominal wound , the other with a hand wound . NEWLINE
After the dust cleared it was determined that the two males were in an un - attached garage and the homeowner wanted to show his friend a .45 pistol that is kept in a safe in the garage . NEWLINE
The pistol was accidently discharged , according to police , with the bullet going through the owner 's hand , through his friend 's arm and into his friend 's abdomen .
Both were transported to the hospital .
Their conditions were not known on Saturday evening . NEWLINE
