UPDATE : Kansas teen hospitalized after apparent accidental shooting
RENO COUNTY –
Law enforcement authorities in Reno County are investigating an apparent accidental shooting . NEWLINE
On Tuesday evening the Reno County Sheriff ’s Department reported Detectives had talked to the parents of the shooting victim at a Wichita hospital . NEWLINE
The parents say that the teen is in fair condition however is still in the Intensive Care Unit .
They stated that he is looking at another surgery in the next few days and is expected to make a full recovery . NEWLINE
Investigators are still looking into all aspects of the case to get a clear idea of what happened causing the accidental shooting .
Once the investigation is complete , all reports will be forwarded to the Reno County District Attorney ’s Office for review and determination on whether criminal charges are warranted . NEWLINE
Just after 8 pm , Monday deputies from the Reno County Sheriff ’s Office and EMS were dispatched to 2203 Nickerson Blvd on the west side of Hutchinson for a 15-year - old that had been shot in the chest with a shotgun , according to a social media report . NEWLINE
It appears that two teens , including one who lived there and a friend that was visiting , had been out shooting shotguns earlier in the day . NEWLINE
They were on the backside of a barn and there was an accidental discharge , striking the friend in the left upper chest and left arm . NEWLINE
The young man was talking to officers and EMS at the scene and was been transported to Wichita by ground ambulance in serious but stable condition , according to the sheriff ’s department . NEWLINE
Names are not being released at this time .
