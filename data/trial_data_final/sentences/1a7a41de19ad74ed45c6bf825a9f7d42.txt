Four people dead in murder - suicide ; victims may be minors
EL CENTRO , Calif. - Police officers found three people shot dead inside a home two hours after responding to what appeared to be a suicide , both incidents may be related , according to police . NEWLINE
-- NEWLINE 5:54 p.m. NEWLINE El Centro Police Department ( ECPD ) officers say the incident looks like a murder - suicide and it may involve minors . NEWLINE

At 1:45 p.m. , just shy of two hours later , someone requested a welfare check on family members in the 2000 block of Elm Avenue . NEWLINE
