Woman arrested for beating , shooting at The Saint Showbar
SAN ANTONIO - San Antonio police said witnesses helped put a young woman behind bars after she allegedly beat one person and shot two others . NEWLINE
The incident occurred Saturday around 7 a.m. at The Saint Showbar , an establishment located in the 800 block of Lexington Road that had been closed and is in the process of being reopened under a new name . NEWLINE
According to the arrest affidavit , witnesses told police 22-year - old Selena Jade Martinez got into a heated argument with 33-year - old Crystal Galaviz . NEWLINE
The affidavit said the pair started fighting when Martinez hit Galaviz in the head with a gun .
That 's when , the affidavit said , the gun apparently went off , hitting two people across the room . NEWLINE
Both women shot in the incident are both expected to recover . NEWLINE
Martinez has been charged with aggravated assault with a deadly weapon . NEWLINE
