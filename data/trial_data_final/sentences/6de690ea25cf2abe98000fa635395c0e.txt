Penn Hills Shooting : Teenager killed at Linton Middle School
Updated : Mar 28 , 2017 - 11:20 PM NEWLINE PENN HILLS , Pa. - A teenager was shot and killed outside the middle school in Penn Hills . NEWLINE
The shooting happened around 4:30 p.m. Tuesday at Linton Middle School on Aster Street . NEWLINE
The body of Deven Holloway , 16 , was found near a car in the parking lot of the school .
According to a statement from the school district , the victim is a Penn Hills student but did not attend Linton . NEWLINE
Police said Holloway was with a friend , but it 's not clear why they were at the school . NEWLINE
" It 's our understanding that they go up and play basketball at the basketball courts , " said Chief Howard Burton of the Penn Hills Police Department . NEWLINE

Defense attorney Phil DiLucente said his client , who is 22 , was being questioned by police Tuesday night .
DiLucente said his client was there to play a game of basketball .
The man is licensed to carry a firearm and was defending himself , DiLucente said . NEWLINE
Police are trying to determine where a gun that was found next to the victim 's body came from . NEWLINE
A prayer vigil will be held at 6 p.m. Wednesday at Linton Middle School . NEWLINE
