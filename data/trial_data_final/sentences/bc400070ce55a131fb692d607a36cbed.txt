Christian Dawson NEWLINE Dallas police are asking for the public ’s help after a 19-year - old man was fatally shot in northeast Dallas early Sunday morning . NEWLINE
Police said Christian Dawson was shot at 9220 Skillman Street .

Multiple others were also hurt in the incident , but their injuries were non - life - threatening .
