A 9-year - old boy was shot in the head in an accidental shooting near Benedict , in southern Ward County last night . NEWLINE
The Ward County Sheriff 's Department reports the boy was hit when he and his 11-year - old cousin were playing with a gun they thought was empty . NEWLINE
The boy was hit by a round from the 22 caliber pistol and taken to the Garrison Hospital by a private vehicle . NEWLINE
There is no word on the boy 's condition .
