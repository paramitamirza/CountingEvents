Police : Boy finds gun , accidentally shoots himself in leg
COLUMBUS , Ohio ( AP ) — Columbus police say a 12-year - old boy accidentally shot himself in the leg while handling a gun that he found in his grandfather ’s bedroom . NEWLINE
Police say the boy was taken to a hospital and was in stable condition after the shooting Monday evening at a home on the city ’s southwest side . NEWLINE
