WILKES COUNTY , N.C. -- A two - year - old boy was airlifted to the hospital after accidentally shooting himself in the chest with his parents ' handgun on Sunday evening . NEWLINE
Wilkes County Sheriff 's Office said that the boy 's parents brought him into the emergency room following the accident . NEWLINE
The parents told police that their son got the .380 semi - automatic gun from the driver 's side door of the car that he was in . NEWLINE
The incident occurred at the 400 block of Chuckwood Road in Thurmond . NEWLINE
The child was later airlifted to Brenners Children Hospital in Winston - Salem for further treatment . NEWLINE
The condition of the child is not available at this time . NEWLINE
Police are currently investigating this case to find out more information . NEWLINE
