2017-03-31
2 people shot , 1 fatally in Newark 
Two people have been shot , one of them fatally , in Newark on Friday afternoon . It happened on the 400 block of Hawthorne Avenue . There was a very large police presence there . Just across the street from the shooting scene there seems to be a community garden and further down the street there is actually an elementary school . The second shooting victim is in stable condition . It 's not yet clear what led to the shooting . So far there are no arrests .
