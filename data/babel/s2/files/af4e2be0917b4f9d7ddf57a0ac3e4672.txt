Accidental shooting sends juvenile to hospital 
Daily Journal NEWLINE IUKA – A Tishomingo County boy is recovering from what appears to be an accidental gunshot wound . NEWLINE The Tishomingo County Sheriff ’s Department was called to the North Mississippi Medical Center–Iuka on Saturday about a person who had been shot . After talking with family members , deputies discovered that the juvenile male and a brother had been target shooting with a 9 mm pistol . When the victim attempted to disassemble the weapon , he accidentally shot himself in the abdomen . NEWLINE The victim was transported Le Bonheur Children ’s Hospital where he underwent surgery and was listed in critical condition . NEWLINE The incident is being treated as an accidental shooting , and no charges are being filed at this time .
