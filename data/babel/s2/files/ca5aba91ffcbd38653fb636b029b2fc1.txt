Teenage boy critical after being shot multiple times in Juniata 
Police say a teenage boy is fighting for life after being shot up to ten times in the Juniata section of Philadelphia . It happened at 12:30 a.m. Monday in the 4000 block of Maywood Street . Officers arrived to find the 17-year - old boy suffering multiple gunshot wounds . They rushed him to Temple University Hospital in critical condition . Investigators say the boy was shot with a .22 caliber weapon . Police say the suspect fled an older model Pontiac Grand Prix . They are working to determine who shot him and why .
