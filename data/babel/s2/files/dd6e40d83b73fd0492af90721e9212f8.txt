Two teens seriously injured in Kent shooting 
( Photo : KING ) NEWLINE KENT , Wash. -- Two teenagers are at Harborview Medical Center with life - threatening injuries after a shooting Saturday night on the border between Kent and Federal Way . NEWLINE Police say it happened around 10:40 p.m. in the area of 2200 block of South 272nd St. NEWLINE Two teens , ages 14 and 17 , were found lying in the roadway with gunshot wounds . NEWLINE Both were transported to the hospital with life - threatening injuries . NEWLINE Police believe they were involved in a fight prior to the shooting . Two weapons were found at the scene , believed to belong to both victims . NEWLINE Police say that the two victims were associated with prior criminal activity in the area .
