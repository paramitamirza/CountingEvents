Covington man dead in accidental shooting 
COVINGTON – A Covington man is dead after an apparent accidental shooting in the village . NEWLINE Covington police and medics were summoned to an address on Regency Court between 1:30 and 2 p.m. on Tuesday after a man was reported to have been shot . NEWLINE Chief Lee Harmon of the Covington Police Department said that the shooting appears to be accidental . “ Two brothers were going to go target shooting at their parent ’s house , ” said Harmon , “ when the gun that one of the brothers was carrying discharged . ” NEWLINE Harmon said that the weapon was a shotgun . NEWLINE The victim was transported to Upper Valley Medical Center but was pronounced dead on arrival . NEWLINE No names have been released pending notification of family .
