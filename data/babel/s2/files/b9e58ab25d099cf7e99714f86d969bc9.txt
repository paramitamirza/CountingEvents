Report : 2 people shot in car in West Toledo 
Two people with gunshot wounds have been found in a car at a West Toledo intersection , authorities said . NEWLINE Toledo police were working to find out where the shooting occurred and why . NEWLINE The victims were taken to ProMedica Toledo Hospital . Their names and conditions were not immediately released . NEWLINE Police and paramedics were dispatched at 8:15 p.m. today to West Sylvania and Hazelhurst avenues . Initial indications were that the car was driven there after the shooting . Investigators checked the area around Willys Park , about two blocks south , for evidence of shots fired . NEWLINE Police also investigated a report that someone was shot about 9:35 p.m. at Spencer Street and South Avenue .
