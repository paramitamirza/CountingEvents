16-year - old boy dies 2 days after Englewood shooting 
16-year - old boy dies 2 days after Englewood shooting NEWLINE A 16-year - old boy died on Friday , two days after he was shot in the Englewood neighborhood on the South Side . NEWLINE Officers found Diabolique Anderson about 4:45 p.m. Wednesday on a sidewalk in the 6800 block of South Green with gunshot wounds to the head and thigh , according to Chicago Police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office . Witnesses saw four males take off in a black SUV after the shooting . NEWLINE Anderson , of the 100 block of East 68th Street , was pronounced dead at 2:28 p.m. Friday at Christ Medical Center in Oak Lawn , the medical examiner ’s office said . An autopsy showed he died of gunshot wounds and his death was ruled a homicide . NEWLINE Area South detectives were investigating .
