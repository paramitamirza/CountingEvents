NEWLINE When emergency responders arrived on scene , they found Christopher Chatman , 26 , suffering from a gunshot wound to the leg . NEWLINE The probable cause report states that Chatman initially told police that he did n't know who shot him . However , the officer saw a box of ammo near his foot and that 's when he allegedly changed his story . NEWLINE Police say Chatman admitted to having a gun and that it accidentally went off in his pocket . NEWLINE Chatman was transported to the hospital and treated for his injury . Due to his status as a convicted felon , police charged him with felon in possession of a firearm , illegal use of a weapon , firearm altered , illegal carrying of a weapon , and criminal mischief .
