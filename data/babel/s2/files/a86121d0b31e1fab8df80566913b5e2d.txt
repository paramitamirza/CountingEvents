Crossville man charged in home shooting death - WSMV Channel 4 
A Crossville man has been charged in the shooting death of a woman in his home early Saturday morning . NEWLINE Michael Dale , 26 , got in an altercation with another man inside his home on the 1000 block of Old Mail Road in Crossville . During the fight , Dale fired a shotgun inside the house . NEWLINE Projectiles from the gunshot went through an interior wall , hitting and killing 21-year - old Kelsey Nicole Brady in the next room . NEWLINE Dale is charged with aggravated assault resulting in death , reckless homicide and aggravated assault . NEWLINE As of yesterday , Dale was being held in the Cumberland County Jail on $ 200,000 bond .
