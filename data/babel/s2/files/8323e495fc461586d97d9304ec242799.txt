Arrest made in shooting death of Lexington teenager 
An arrest has been made in the murder of a Lexington teenager . NEWLINE A 16-year - old male has been charged in the shooting death of 15-year - old Robert Durrum . The 16-year - old has not been identified because he 's a juvenile . He 's charged with murder , robbery , and tampering with physical evidence . NEWLINE Lexington police say Durrum was found in the road on Belmont Drive near Preakness Drive around 11:30 p.m. Wednesday . According to the coroner , Durrum died from his injuries at University of Kentucky Medical Center shortly after midnight . NEWLINE Police say Durrum was shot at least once in the abdomen . NEWLINE Police say Durrum knew the suspect . According to police , Durrum and the 16-year - old had an altercation after meeting , leading to the shooting .
