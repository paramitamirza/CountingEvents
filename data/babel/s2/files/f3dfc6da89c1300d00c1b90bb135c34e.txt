Man , teenager injured in South Philadelphia shooting 
Police are investigating a double shooting in South Philadelphia that injured a man and a teenager . Police say bullets grazed a 16-year - old boy and a 24-year - old man in the 900 block of South 4th Street around 12:30 a.m. Sunday . Both were taken to Jefferson University Hospital in stable condition . Investigators are investigating what led up to the shooting .
