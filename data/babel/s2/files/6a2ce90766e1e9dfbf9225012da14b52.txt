Police : Woman seriously injured in Williamsburg shooting 
A woman suffered serious injuries after a shooting Thursday morning in Williamsburg . NEWLINE Police responded to a reported shooting around 9 a.m. on Mt. Morgan Road and found a woman on the ground , according to a news release . She was shot in the shoulder . NEWLINE The woman was flown to the University of Tennessee Medical Center for serious injuries . NEWLINE Nearby Williamsburg Independent School went under lockdown for a short time after the shooting . NEWLINE Police have not arrested anyone .
