15-year - old identified in fatal Chelsea shooting 
CHELSEA ( WHDH ) - A 15-year - old has died following a shooting in Chelsea on Friday . NEWLINE The shooting occurred on Shurtleff Street just after 5:30 p.m. Friday . NEWLINE The 15-year - old victim has been identified as Jimmy Vasquez , a freshman at Chelsea High School . NEWLINE The teen was rushed to Whidden Memorial Hospital , where he was pronounced dead . NEWLINE Another teen , also 15 , was shot in the foot . He was treated and released from an area hospital . NEWLINE Police say they don’t have a motive yet , but they say it ’s possible the teens were targeted . NEWLINE Officials say the teen and his friends were standing out in front of a building when a car came up and started firing . NEWLINE At least nine shots were fired , according to officials . NEWLINE Police are looking for a silver or gray car . NEWLINE The car was seen on surveillance video at the corner of Shurtleff Street at Grove Street around the time of the shooting . It was then seen leaving and turning down Grove Street against one way traffic after the shooting . NEWLINE If you have any information you can contact Chelsea Police at 617 - 466 - 4880 or the Suffolk County State Police Detective Unit at 617 - 727 - 8817 NEWLINE This shooting is still under investigation .
