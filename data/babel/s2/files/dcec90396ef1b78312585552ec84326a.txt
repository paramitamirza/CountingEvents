Victim critically injured in northeast side shooting 
× Victim critically injured in northeast side shooting NEWLINE INDIANAPOLIS , Ind.– Police say a person is in critical condition after a shooting on the northeast side . NEWLINE Officers were called to the scene in an alley west of the 3900 block of North Butler Avenue around 1:30 p.m. An older teenage male was found suffering from several gunshot wounds . NEWLINE Crews transported the victim to Sidney and Lois Eskenazi Hospital , where he remains in critical but stable condition . NEWLINE Detectives say the victim and a friend were walking town the street in the 3900 block of Grand Avenue when a black vehicle stopped . The driver got out and started shooting at the victim and his friend . NEWLINE The suspect then fled the area . No additional suspect information has been released .
