Boy , 16 , shot in Bridgeport NEWLINE A 16-year - old boy was shot Sunday night in the Bridgeport neighborhood on the South Side . NEWLINE The boy was standing on the sidewalk at 9:24 p.m. in the 2800 block of South Hillock when he heard several gunshots and felt pain , Chicago Police said . NEWLINE He was struck in his right calf , police said . A friend took him to Mercy Hospital , where his condition had stabilized .
