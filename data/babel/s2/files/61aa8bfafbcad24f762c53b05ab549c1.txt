2017-01-03
2 16-year - old boys killed , woman injured in West Side shooting 
Two 16-year - old boys were fatally shot Tuesday afternoon on the city 's West Side , police said . The shooting also injured Tillie Turner , 65 , a ComEd security guard who was getting ready for work when a bullet came through the door of her home , her daughters said . Turner was tying her shoes when she was struck in the leg , shattering a bone . She was hospitalized at Mt. Sinai Hospital . The incident happened at about noon in the 3400-block of West Fulton Boulevard in the East Garfield Park neighborhood , police said . Daughter Franchester Wilkerson , who was at home during the shooting , said a hail of gunfire was preceded by screeching tires . The teen boys were shot while on the street or sidewalk , police said . One boy was pronounced dead at the scene . The other died a short time after the shooting at a hospital , police said . The identity of the boys was not immediately available .
