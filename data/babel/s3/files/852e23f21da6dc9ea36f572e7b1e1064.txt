1 Dead , 2 Injured In Shooting Near Fronton 
FRONTON - The Starr County Sheriff ’s Office confirmed one person died after a shooting near the river banks in the Fronton area . NEWLINE Deputies along with Border Patrol agents and DPS troopers responded to shots fired in the area around 4:30 p.m. on Wednesday . NEWLINE The sheriff ’s office said two male individuals suffered non - life threatening injuries and a third person died . NEWLINE According to the victims , they were shot from the Mexican side while they were fishing on a boat . NEWLINE No additional information was released . NEWLINE The investigation is ongoing . NEWLINE The deadly shooting comes three months after a man was shot and killed at Falcon Lake . NEWLINE Oscar Eduardo Garza , of Rio Grande City , was on a boat with his friend at the time of the incident . An exchange with other people on another boat ensued . Authorities said Garza sped away after seeing what looked like a gun . NEWLINE Shots were fired and Garza was fatally hit . NEWLINE No arrests in the case have been made .
