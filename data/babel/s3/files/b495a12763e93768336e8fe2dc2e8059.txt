NEWLINE Eberle is accused of pointing his loaded rifle at a 22-year - old man and shooting him in the side of the face , officials said . NEWLINE the victim suffered serious , but non - life - threatening injuries . He remains hospitalized in stable condition . NEWLINE Authorities have not released the name of the victim .
