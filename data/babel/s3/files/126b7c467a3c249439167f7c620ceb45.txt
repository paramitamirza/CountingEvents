Woman dies in shooting , man faces involuntary manslaughter charge 
A woman who was shot in the head in a Twin Falls apartment on Thursday night died from the wound . NEWLINE The Twin Falls Police Department identified the woman as 20-year - old Katrina Budden . She was taken to St. Luke 's Magic Valley where she died from the injury . NEWLINE The Twin Falls Police Department arrested Brandon Smithson in relation to the incident . He is being charged with involuntary manslaughter . Smithson will be arraigned in court on Friday . NEWLINE The Twin Falls County Coroner scheduled an autopsy for Monday morning .
