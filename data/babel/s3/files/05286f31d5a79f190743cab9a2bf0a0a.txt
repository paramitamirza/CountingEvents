Two teens injured in northwest side shooting 
× Two teens injured in northwest side shooting NEWLINE INDIANAPOLIS , Ind.– Police are investigating a double shooting on the city ’s northwest side . NEWLINE Officers were called to the scene at Scarborough Lake Apartments , near North High School Rd and West 46th Street , just after 9 p.m. on a report of a shots fired . NEWLINE Police confirmed two boys , ages 15 and 16 , were shot as they were walking through the parking lot . Another juvenile was with them , and said an unknown man shot at them from behind a car . NEWLINE The witness said he didn’t know why the suspect shot at them . NEWLINE Both teens have minor , non life - threatening injuries . They walked to the ambulance on their own . NEWLINE A bullet did go into an apartment wall , but did not injure the person who was inside . NEWLINE This story will be updated as more information becomes available .
