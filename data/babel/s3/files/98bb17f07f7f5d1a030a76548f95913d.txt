2 teens shot on northwest side 
INDIANAPOLIS ( WISH ) – Two people have been shot on the city ’s northwest side Friday evening . NEWLINE The shooting happened in the 6400 block of Whitehaven Road just after 9 p.m. NEWLINE Two teenagers , a 15-year - old and a 16-year - old , were walking through an apartment complex parking lot when they were struck . NEWLINE Police said the teens were shot by an unknown male hiding behind a vehicle . NEWLINE Several cars were also hit by gunfire . One bullet did go into one of the apartments , an occupant was home at the time , but was not injured . NEWLINE According to IMPD , both teens were taken to IU Health Methodist Hospital in stable condition . NEWLINE The shooting remains under investigation .
