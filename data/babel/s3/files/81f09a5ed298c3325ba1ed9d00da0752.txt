Pinellas teen charged after accidentally shooting friend 
Detectives in Pinellas County have arrested a 17-year - old boy after he accidentally shot his friend in the back with a handgun at a residence in Seminole . NEWLINE Teenage victim accidentally shot by friend NEWLINE Deputies : suspect was already on probation , now faces multiple charges NEWLINE Victim was hospitalized with serious but non - life threatening injuries NEWLINE The incident occurred at about 11:15 p.m. on Saturday , Jan. 14 at 11439 60th Terrace North . NEWLINE When deputies arrived , the victim and three other male juveniles were found at the residence . According to reports , the victim informed deputies that he had been accidentally shot . NEWLINE He was transported to Bayfront Medical Center for serious , but non - life threatening , injuries . NEWLINE Detectives interviewed the other minors at the scene . According to reports , they believe the teens had been smoking marijuana when the suspect started playing with a handgun . NEWLINE The weapon was accidentally discharged , and the bullet struck the victim in the back . NEWLINE Detectives say the handgun , a Beretta .45 caliber , was reported stolen during a residential burglary that occurred in Seminole on December 9 , 2016 . NEWLINE The suspect – who we’re not naming due to our crime guidelines – was already on probation for a possession of a controlled substance charge NEWLINE He was charged with one count of delinquent in possession of a firearm , one count of delinquent in possession of ammunition , one count of culpable negligence , one count of resisting an officer without violence , and one count of felony violation of probation .
