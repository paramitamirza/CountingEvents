2 men , woman arrested in fatal shooting of 17-year - old boy 
Michael Edwards , Xavier Stroman , both 20 , and Marie Derival , 19 , face murder charges in the death of Antonio Neeley . NEWLINE LAUDERHILL , Fla. - Two men and a woman were arrested Monday in connection with the fatal shooting of a 17-year - old boy , authorities said . NEWLINE Michael Edwards and Xavier Stroman , both 20 , and Marie Derival , 19 , face murder charges in the death of Antonio Neeley . NEWLINE Police said the shooting was reported about 4 p.m. Saturday at an apartment complex at 5401 NW 27th St. in Lauderhill . NEWLINE Police said first responders found Neeley lying in front of the building , suffering from a gunshot wound . NEWLINE He was pronounced dead a short time later .
