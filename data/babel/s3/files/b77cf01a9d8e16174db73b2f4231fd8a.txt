Fight breaks out at Riverside party , 5 people shot 
Five people were shot after a fight at a party in Riverside 's Arlanza neighborhood on Friday evening , March 3 , a police official confirmed . NEWLINE Officer Ryan Railsback said all five people were at hospitals with non - life - threatening injuries . NEWLINE He said the shooting was reported shortly before 10:30 p.m. at a home in the 8300 block of Trey Avenue and that several people were reported fleeing the scene . NEWLINE As officers arrived and began securing the area , they received a call that four people had been brought to a hospital for gunshot wounds . A fifth person was at another hospital , he said . NEWLINE He said a suspect has not yet been identified . NEWLINE Railsback encouraged anyone with information about the shooting to contact the Riverside Police Department at 951 - 826 - 5700 .
