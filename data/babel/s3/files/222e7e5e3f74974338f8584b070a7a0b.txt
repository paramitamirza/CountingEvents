13-year - old dies in accidental shooting in Perry County 
PERRY COUNTY , Tenn. ( WKRN ) – A 13-year - old died in an accidental shooting in Perry County Sunday afternoon . NEWLINE It happened on Culps Bend Road in the White Oak Community . NEWLINE According to the Perry County Sheriff Department , the child , along with a 12-year - old , was playing with a handgun they found in a family member ’s backpack . NEWLINE The 13-year - old was transported to Perry County Hospital where he died . NEWLINE The identity of the child has not been released . NEWLINE No other details were immediately available .
