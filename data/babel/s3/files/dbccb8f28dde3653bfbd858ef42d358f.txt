2 people shot near Jean ’s dry lake bed 
Two people were shot near Jean on Saturday afternoon , Las Vegas police said . NEWLINE About 1:10 p.m. Las Vegas police received reports of two people shot , one in the leg and one in the ribs , near the dry lake bed area near Jean , Metropolitan Police Department Lt . Grant Rogers said . NEWLINE “ We’re not sure if the shooting was an accident , ” Rogers said , as many people target practice in that area , he said . NEWLINE The shooting ’s exact location is unknown . Both people self - transported to a local hospital , Rogers said . NEWLINE “ This will probably be more incidental than anything , and we don’t have a crime scene out there , ” Rogers said .
