13-year - old shot , killed in Perry County - WSMV Channel 4 
Kaden Hammond was shot and killed by a relative on Sunday . ( Source : gofundme.com/12yr - old - nephews - memorial ) NEWLINE A 13-year - old boy was accidentally shot and killed by a 12-year - old relative in Perry County on Sunday afternoon . NEWLINE According to the Perry County Sheriff ’s Department , the two children were in a bedroom playing with a handgun taken from a family member ’s backpack when Kaden Hammond was shot . NEWLINE Kaden was taken to Perry County Hospital where he died from his injuries . NEWLINE The incident occurred Kaden ’s aunt and uncle ’s house on Culps Bend Road in the White Oak Community in Perry County . NEWLINE “ At some point they entered the parents’ bedroom and discovered the handgun , the loaded 9mm Glock , ” said Perry County Sheriff Nick Weems . NEWLINE Weems said securing or locking up guns is something he stresses to everyone . NEWLINE “ I think that everyone should really educate your children . I know that I educate mine that you don’t touch firearms . But you never know what a child is going to do when you’re not looking , ” the sheriff said . NEWLINE According to Safe Tennessee Project , there have been four incidents where a child got a hold of a gun and shot someone so far this year . Last year , it happened a total of 22 times . NEWLINE Weems said he feels more needs to be done . NEWLINE “ Something needs to be in place to have some type of responsibility , but I just don’t know the answer to it , ” he said . NEWLINE No charges have been filed so far . NEWLINE A GoFundMe page has been set up to help Kaden ’s family with funeral expenses . Click here for more information .
