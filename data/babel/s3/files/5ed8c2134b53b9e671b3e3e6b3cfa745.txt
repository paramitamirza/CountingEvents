Girl , man killed in Charles Co. shooting ; 2 others in hospital 
WASHINGTON — A young girl and a man are dead after a shooting Thursday night in Waldorf , Maryland . Two other people have been airlifted to an area hospital . NEWLINE The shooting happened around 8:17 p.m. in the 3300 block of Westdale Court , the Charles County Sheriff ’s Office said . NEWLINE When deputies entered the townhouse , they found the man and the little girl dead on the scene , said Diane Richardson , a spokesperson with the sheriff ’s office . A gun was found near the man ’s body . NEWLINE A woman and a teenage girl are in the hospital in critical condition , Richardson said . NEWLINE The sheriff ’s office initially says the shooting is domestic related . NEWLINE Preliminary info : At least 4 shot . One adult male and one toddler deceased . Two females flown out , one juvenile and one adult . — Charles Co Sheriff ( @CCSOMD ) March 24 , 2017 NEWLINE This is a developing story . Stay with WTOP as we get more details .
