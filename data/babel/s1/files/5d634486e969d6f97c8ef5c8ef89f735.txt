2nd Man Wanted in Connection to Southeast Missouri Night Club Shooting 
KFVS Courtesy of KFVS NEWLINE KFVS Courtesy of KFVS NEWLINE YolandaYan testing : 0 NEWLINE CARUTHERSVILLE , Mo. ( KFVS ) -- Authorities are looking for a second man in connection to a nightclub shooting in Caruthersville , Missouri on Sunday , February 12 . NEWLINE A warrant for first degree murder was issued for Anton " DunnDunn " Jones . He is considered armed and dangerous . NEWLINE According to authorities , Jones was involved in the shooting at Club Envy . They say after the shooting , he left the scene with two unknown women in an unknown vehicle going in an unknown direction . NEWLINE They say attempts to try to find Jones have been unsuccessful . NEWLINE A Caruthersville man is behind bars on a murder charge in connection to the deadly shooting . NEWLINE It happened around 3:30 a.m. on Sunday , Feb. 12 . NEWLINE One person was killed and five others were hurt in the shooting at Club Envy . NEWLINE Travis Ware II , 25 , is charged with 1st degree murder and armed criminal action . NEWLINE The victim , Freddie Rodgers , Jr. died at a local hospital . NEWLINE Missouri Highway Patrol Sgt . Clark Parrott said a party started in Kennett , and then people went to Caruthersville to resume the party at Club Envy . NEWLINE There were around 300 people in the club at the time of the shooting , according to Parrott . NEWLINE One person was injured as a result of a fight during the time of the shooting , and the conditions of the five others who were shot are not known yet . NEWLINE Victims were taken to various hospitals around the area , including two flown to Memphis . NEWLINE Ware is being held in the Pemiscot County Jail without bond . NEWLINE Authorities are asking if anyone has any information about this to call Caruthersville Police , Pemiscot County Sheriff 's Department or Missouri Highway Patrol .
