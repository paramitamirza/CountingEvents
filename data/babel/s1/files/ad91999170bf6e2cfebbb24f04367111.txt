Woman accidentally shoots self inside Kroger - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee 
A woman accidentally shot herself inside a Memphis Kroger , according to Memphis Police Department . NEWLINE Police said the shooting happened just before 7 p.m. on Tuesday in the bathroom at Kroger on South Third Street . The woman had her 18-month - old with her . NEWLINE The victim was taken to the hospital in non - critical condition . The child is OK and was not harmed . NEWLINE Kroger issued the following statement : NEWLINE Tonight , an unfortunate , isolated , accident occurred inside our Southgate Kroger Store .
