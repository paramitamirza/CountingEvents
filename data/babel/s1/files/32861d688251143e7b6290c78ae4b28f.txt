Teen shot at South Philadelphia rec center 

A teenager is hospitalized after a shooting at a South Philadelphia recreation center . It happened just after 6 p.m. Wednesday at Vare Recreation Center in the 1700 block of South 26th Street . Police say a 17-year - old male was shot in the chest . He was taken to Children 's Hospital of Philadelphia in stable condition . No arrests have been made .
