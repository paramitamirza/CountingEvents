Teenager Killed In McKees Rocks Shooting 
McKEES ROCKS ( KDKA ) — Police are investigating after a teenager was shot in the head and died Tuesday night in McKees Rocks . NEWLINE Jihad Cromer , 17 , was shot once inside a unit at Uansa Village . He was rushed to a local hospital , where he later died . NEWLINE Allegheny County Homicide Detectives , as well as Allegheny County Housing Authority Police are investigating . NEWLINE Housing Authority Police Chief Mike Vogel says officers were called around 6:30 p.m. NEWLINE “ At approximately 6:30 this evening , the officers received a call of a man shot in the head , ” said Chief Vogel . “ They responded to a particular unit here at Uansa Village . Upon their arrival , they discovered a person that was shot… and at that point in time , the paramedics came . They provided EMS services to that individual and then transported him out at that time . ” NEWLINE Heather Abraham ’s Report : NEWLINE Chief Vogel says a group of people were playing video games in another room when they heard a gunshot and then ran . He says the victim was found in a bedroom . NEWLINE Police used flashlights to scour the area for a possible weapon . NEWLINE Two people were taken in for questioning , but police say they are not suspects . NEWLINE There ’s no word yet on what prompted the shooting . NEWLINE Meanwhile , Cromer graduated from Sto - Rox High School in 2016 , where he was a member of the basketball team and loved by many . NEWLINE The superintendent at Sto - Rox said guidance counselors are on hand at the high school , but they have really been tending to some of Cromer ’s younger family members at the elementary school . NEWLINE Coincidently , the superintendent said they are holding a previously planned youth summit today at the high school , where the topic is gun violence .
