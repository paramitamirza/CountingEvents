Teen shot and wounded in Chula Vista - CBS News 8 - San Diego , CA News Station - KFMB Channel 8 
CHULA VISTA ( CNS ) - A man in his late teens was shot and wounded Wednesday night in Chula Vista . NEWLINE The shooting occurred about 8 p.m. in the area of Max Avenue and Prospect Street , according to Chula Vista police . NEWLINE The victim , who was shot during a robbery or robbery attempt , went to a nearby home to seek help and was later taken to a hospital for treatment of a gunshot wound , police said . NEWLINE His condition was not immediately available , nor was any suspect information .
