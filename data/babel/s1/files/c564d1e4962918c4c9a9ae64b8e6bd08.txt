Police : 14-year - old boy shot to death in Austin 
A 14-year - old boy was shot to death Saturday afternoon in the Austin neighborhood on the West Side , according to Chicago police . The boy was shot in the buttocks just after 3 p.m. in the 4900-block of West Hubbard , police said . He was on the sidewalk when a vehicle drove by and someone in the vehicle shot at him . He was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital , where he was pronounced dead , police said . The boy was identified as Laquan Allen , who lived about 2 miles away in the West Garfield Park neighborhood .
