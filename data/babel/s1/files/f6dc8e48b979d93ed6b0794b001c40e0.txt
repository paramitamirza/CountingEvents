Man , 45 , held in fatal shooting of Arkansas high school student 
Police arrested an Arkansas man Thursday in the shooting death of a 17-year - old Sheridan boy . Austin Moody , who was a member of the Sheridan High School football team and competed in track and field for the school , was shot and killed in front of his home on Arkansas 35 north of Sheridan on Jan. 19 .
