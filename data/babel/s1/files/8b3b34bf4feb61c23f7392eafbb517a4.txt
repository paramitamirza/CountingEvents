2 dead , 4 wounded in shooting at Houston apartment complex 

HOUSTON ( AP ) - Authorities say two people are dead and four others wounded following a shooting outside an apartment complex in Houston . 
Harris County Sheriff 's Office spokesman Thomas Gilliland says the incident occurred Sunday evening at the Haverstock Hills apartments on the city 's northeast side . 
Two men were killed in the shooting , and two other men and two women were wounded . Gilliland says the four wounded are hospitalized in critical but stable condition . 
None of the people shot have been identified . 
Authorities say the shooting followed an argument between two groups of people . The spokesman says three suspects were inside a vehicle when one man stepped out of the car and opened fire . 
No suspects are in custody and the sheriff 's office is investigating .
