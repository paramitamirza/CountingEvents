Opelousas Teen Hospitalized After Shooting Pronounced Dead 

An Opelousas teen hospitalized after a weekend shooting has died . 
Opelousas Police Sgt . Crystal Leblanc says the 15-year - old male was shot in the 600 block of Pulford Street Sunday afternoon . 
On Thursday , the same home was sprayed with more than two dozen bullets . However , police are not saying whether the shootings are related . 
Police are not releasing details of a suspect . 
Anyone with information is urged to call Opelousas Police .
