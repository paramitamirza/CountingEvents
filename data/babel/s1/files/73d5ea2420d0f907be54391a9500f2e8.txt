Lake Charles shooting ; one dead and several injured - KATC.com | Continuous News Coverage | Acadiana - Lafayette 
Police are investigating an early morning shooting on Tulip Street in Lake Charles . NEWLINE According to KPLC , Lake Charles Police have confirmed that one person is dead and a number of other victims are injured . Police say the incident that occurred at 3:47 a.m. Thursday morning is believed to be a drive by shooting . Four people were confirmed to have been shot . NEWLINE The passenger in a vehicle , KPLC reports , was killed in this incident . Two victims have been taken to hospitals with moderate injuries , another victim has sustained critical injuries . The victims have been transported to Lake Charles and Lafayette hospitals . NEWLINE Police are actively searching for a suspect at this time . NEWLINE This is an ongoing story , we will provide updates as they become available .
