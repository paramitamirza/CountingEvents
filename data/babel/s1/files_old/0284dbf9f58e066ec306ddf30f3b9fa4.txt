2017-04-03

Boy , 2 , shoots himself in head ; father arrested 

CORPUS CHRISTI , Texas - A Corpus Christi man was arrested after his 2-year - old son found a loaded gun on the kitchen table and fatally shot himself in the head . 
Corpus Christi police Officer Gena Pena said Eric Javier Morales , 22 , is charged with criminally negligent homicide . He is being held in the Nueces County Jail . 
Pena said the child , Ryan Morales , shot himself Sunday morning . 
The child 's father was not home at the time , but the child 's mother and a 1-year - old sibling were . 
Eric Morales went to the hospital where his son was taken , and later met with investigators before he was charged , Pena said .
