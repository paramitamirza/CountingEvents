Accidental shooting in Cabela ’s parking lot sends one man to the hospital 

KANSAS CITY , Kan. — An accidental shooting sent one person to the hospital with minor injuries on Sunday . 
Police said shortly before 11:30 a.m. , an accidental shooting occurred in the parking lot of Cabela ’s in KCK. 
Police did not say exactly how that shooting happened , or precisely how he was injured , but said the man is expected to be okay .
