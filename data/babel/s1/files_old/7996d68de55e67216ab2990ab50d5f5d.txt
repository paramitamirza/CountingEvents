2017-04-11

Person shot , killed at Holladay Park in Northeast Portland ; suspect at large 

Police on scene of shooting at Holladay Park ( Photo : KGW ) 
PORTLAND , Ore. – One person was shot and killed at Holladay Park in Northeast Portland on Tuesday afternoon , police said . 
The suspect is at large , according to Portland police . 
Witnesses told KGW 's Mike Benner that the victim was a teenager . Police have not released the person 's name or age . 
TriMet tweeted MAX blue , green and red lines were delayed . 
The park will be closed for several hours as police investigate .
