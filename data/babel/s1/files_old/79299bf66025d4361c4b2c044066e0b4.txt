Child Dies After Accidental Shooting In Robertson County - LEX18.com | Continuous News and StormTracker Weather 

ROBERTSON COUNTY , Ky ( LEX 18 ) A child is dead after KSP says he was accidentally shot . 
KSP said that Bracken County EMS transported the child to Meadowview Regional Medical Center . 
Dispatchers say that the victim is a 4-year - old boy . KSP says he died at the hospital . 
The shooting happened in the 4200 block of Pinhook Road near Mt. Olivet on Tuesday morning around 11:20 a.m. 
The investigation continues .
