Man dies in accidental , self - inflicted shooting at Pueblo motel , police say 

A 21-year - old man died early Monday after accidentally shooting himself at a Pueblo motel , police say . 
The shooting happened at about 2 a.m. at the Motel 6 on the 4100 block of North Elizabeth Street , on the city ’s north side . 
Pueblo police say their preliminary investigation shows there were several people at the hotel drinking in a room with guns when the man shot himself . 
The man ’s name has not been released . 
An investigation into the shooting continues , Pueblo police said .
