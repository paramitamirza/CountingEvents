2017-02-11

2 teenage boys shot in Roseland 

Two teenage boys were shot Friday evening in the Roseland neighborhood on the Far South Side . The boys - both 17 - were walking about 6:50 p.m. in the 10300 block of South Corliss when someone approached them , pulled out a gun and opened fire , according to Chicago Police . The first boy was shot in the back and taken to Christ Medical Center in Oak Lawn , and the second was shot in a leg and taken to Roseland Community Hospital , police said . Both were in good condition .
