2017-01-21

Juvenile shot on the Westside 

JACKSONVILLE , Fla - The Jacksonville Sheriff 's Office is investigating a shooting on the Westside . 
Police say around 10:45 a.m. Saturday . Officers were dispatched to a shooting of a juvenile on Firestone Road at Cheshire Court . 
Police would not release many details , only that the victim was a male who was transported to Orange Park Medical Center with non - life - threatening injuries . 
JSO did not have any suspect information and did not disclose the age of the shooting victim .
