Kenosha police arrest 15-year - old in fatal shooting 

Getty Images ( Photo : Getty Images ) 
Kenosha police said Sunday that they have arrested a 15-year - old boy in connection with a fatal weekend shooting . 
Police were called to a residence in the 3600 block of 48th St. around 7:30 p.m. Saturday . 
There they found a 23-year - old Kenosha man with a single gunshot wound to the head . A weapon was recovered at the scene . 
Anyone with information is asked to call Kenosha Crime Stoppers at ( 262 ) 656 - 7333 .
