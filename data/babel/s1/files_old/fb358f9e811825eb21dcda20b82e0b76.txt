Police : Stamford gunshot victim ‘ doing well’ 
BRIDGEPORT—A woman who was shot Friday night around 8 p.m. and treated at Stamford Hospital last night is “ doing well , ” according to police . 
Initial police dispatch reports indicated that the woman ’s attacker was from Bridgeport , and local police there were looking for the suspect ’s vehicle , a red Honda , late into the evening . 
The woman suffered one non - life threatening gun shot . 
Police had not made an arrest in the case as of Saturday afternoon . 
Police have not released a full accounting of the incident , but dispatch reports indicated that the shooting may have been an accident that occurred when the victims and the shooter were high on the synthetic hallucinogenic drug PCP , known as Angel Dust .
