2017-03-28

Teen shot , barricade situation in North Philadelphia 

Police are trying to flush out a shooting suspect who has barricaded himself in a house in North Philadelphia . Officers responded around 4 p.m. Tuesday to 17th and West Susquehanna Avenue . Police say the man shot a 17-year - old boy in the right hip . The victim was rushed to Temple University Hospital in critical condition . SWAT teams have also responded to the scene . Officials say avoid the area as the investigation continues . Stay with Action News and 6abc.com as more information becomes available.------
