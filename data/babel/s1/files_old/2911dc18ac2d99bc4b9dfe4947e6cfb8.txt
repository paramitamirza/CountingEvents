11-Year - Old Grazed By Bullet After Shots Fired Outside Her Home 

Copyright 2017 Nexstar Broadcasting , Inc. All rights reserved . This material may not be published , broadcast , rewritten , or redistributed . 
MEMPHIS , Tenn. ( localmemphis.com ) - Memphis police are investigating after an 11-year - old girl was grazed by a bullet Wednesday night in Fox Meadows . 
It happened just before 9:00 p.m. in the 3200 block of Hickory View Place . Police say the girl was inside her home when she was grazed by a stray bullet shot from outside . 
The girl was treated on the scene and is expected to be okay . 
Police say the suspects are believed to have been driving a black Porsche SUV. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at ( 901 ) 528-CASH.
