Policerespond to shooting , attempted robbery near McClellan High School 

LITTLEROCK , Ark. ( KTHV ) - The Little Rock Police Department responded to a shooting on Monday evening where a juvenile was shot in the leg near McClellan High School . 
Police said the 16-year - old victim was shot in the upper leg after an attempted robbery at the 10000 block of Republic Lane . They believe the victim was a student at McClellan High School and school security was on scene of the shooting . 
According to police , the suspect is driving a red vehicle and police are actively searching for the suspect .
