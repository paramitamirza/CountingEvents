5-year- old shot in hand , taken to hospital in Lancaster 

A5-year - old boy was taken to a hospital in Lancaster after being shot in the hand . 
On Friday , The Kern County Sheriff 's Department responded to a call regarding a child suffering from a gunshot wound . 
Once deputies arrived on scene they discovered the 5-year - old boy had a wound to his left middle finger , the boy was rushed to the hospital for non - life threatening injuries . 
Officials believe the injury could be related to a fight earlier that day in the neighborhood .
