1killed , 4 injured in Panola County shootings - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee 

Fivepeople were shot in two separate houses on Smart Road in Panola County on Friday night . 
The shootings happened around 10:45 p.m. 
One person was killed ; four were injured . 
Investigators said they do not believe it was a drive - by shooting , but they have not specified what sparked the shooting or who may be behind it . 
Identities of the victims in the shooting have not been released .
