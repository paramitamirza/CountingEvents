13-year- old shot in Northwest Jacksonville 

JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - A 13-year - old boy has been hospitalized after being shot in the lower body in Northwest Jacksonville , according to detectives . 
Jacksonville police said around 9 P.M. Sunday they were called to West 45th Street and Avenue B near Paris Avenue where they found the teen shot . He had been walking along the road when the shooting happened . 
Police have not identified the teen who was taken to UF Health and expected to be okay .
