BreakingNews : Joplin Teen Killed By Accidental Gun Fire 

Breakingnew this Friday morning . Joplin Police confirm that an 18-year - old man was killed by accidental gun fire Thursday night . Around 9:30p.m . emergency services responded to 2400 Block of South Pennsylvania in regard to a shooting . Upon arrival it was learned that Ronnie Daniels had been shot and was transported to the hospital by ambulance . Daniels died as a result of his injuries . Next of kin has been notified . Joplin Police Detectives are currently investigating the incident . No other information is available . News Talk KZRG will provide more details as information is released .
