Shooting victim walks into emergency room at Richmond hospital - NBC12 - WWBT - Richmond , VA News On Your Side 

Richmond police were at Chippenham Hospital after a man walked into the emergency room saying he had been shot . 
Officers say the victim is going to survive , and they believe he was shot at a nearby apartment complex on Jahnke Road in the city 's Southside .
