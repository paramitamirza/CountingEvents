Fourfound fatally shot in Preston home 

PRESTON, ID - Police are investigating after four people were found shot to death in a Preston home . 
A nearby neighbor discovered the bodies Sunday evening . 
According to a news release , Preston Police Chief Ken Geddes says officers were called to the home around 5 p.m. Sunday . 
The deceased have been identified as an adult male , Tate Spillet , his 15-year - old stepson Corbin Hillaker , his 7-year - old son , McCoy , and his 6-year - old daughter Jessica . 
Police say there is no threat to the public and are now investigating the incident as a murder - suicide . 
A family friend has set up a crowd funding campaign to help cover funeral costs .
