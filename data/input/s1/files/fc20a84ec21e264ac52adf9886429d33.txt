2 teens wounded in Stockton shooting 

Police : Victims are in critical condition 
Two teenage boys were shot and wounded Monday evening in a Stockton neighborhood , police said . 
Advertisement 
The victims , 13 and 15 years old , were taken to the hospital in critical condition , Stockton police said . 
The shooting happened around 5:50 p.m. in the 7700 block of Santa Ines Court . The boys were outside of a home when the two shooters rode by on bicycles . 
Police are looking for the suspects . 
No other details were released .
