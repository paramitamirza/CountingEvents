16-year - old boy shot , critically wounded in South Chicago 
Chicago Police investigate in the 8000 block of South Marquette , where a 16-year - old boy was shot Monday night . | NVP News 
A 16-year - old boy was shot and critically wounded Monday night in the South Chicago neighborhood . 
He was standing on a front porch at 11:24 p.m. in the 8000 block of South Marquette when another male walked up and opened fire , according to Chicago Police . 
The teenager suffered multiple gunshot wounds to the body and was taken in critical condition to Northwestern Memorial Hospital , police said .
