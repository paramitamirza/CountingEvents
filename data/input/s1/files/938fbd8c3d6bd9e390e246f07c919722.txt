Teenaccused of shooting teen in Vicksburg released on bond - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports 

VicksburgPolice Chief Walter Armstrong said a teen suspect , wanted for shooting another teenager , has been arrested . 
Armstrong said the unnamed male teen was arrested Saturday night . He was released on a $ 25,000 bond , according to Warren County Jail records . 
Police tell us the unnamed teen victim was shot by the suspect around 2:00 Saturday afternoon . 
The shooting happened at New Main Apartments on Alcorn Drive . 
The teen was shot in the abdomen and is currently being treated an Merit Health River Region . He is alert and his injuries do not appear to be life threatening .
