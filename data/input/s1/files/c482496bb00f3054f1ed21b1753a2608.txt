Youngwoman dies after NC park shooting , suspect on the run 

MOUNTHOLLY , N.C. ( WBTV ) — A female who was shot in a Gaston County park Monday afternoon died at the hospital . 
The shooting happened around 3 p.m. in River Street Park in Mt. Holly . This is on N. River Street off of E. Charlotte Avenue . 
“ I saw police and the news vans . I was like , what is going on here , ” one neighbor asked . 
The victim ’s name has not been released because police say they’re having a hard time identifying her . 
Chief Don Roper described her as a light skinned , black female , who was around 5’1 and between the ages of 12 and 18 . 
“ There ’s no identification at this time , but just from looking at the victim , it ’s obvious , she ’s young , ” Roper said . 
Neighbor Troy West says the park is always filled with children . 
“ My kids play here , so I’m kinda shocked . I really don’t know what I think about it , ” he said . 
Officers could be seen checking the bathrooms at the park with guns drawn . They’re still searching for a suspect , but have some leads . 
“ I really hope they catch whoever done it , ” West said . 
There is no word on what may have led to the shooting .
