Accidentalshooting kills man in LaMoure County 

Edgeley, N.D. ( KFGO ) - A 57 year - old man from outside of North Dakota has died in an apparent accidental shooting northeast of Edgeley . 
LaMoure County Sheriff Bob Fernandes says the man was among 3 people hunting coyotes . The 911 call came in at around 11:20 Tuesday morning . 
He says the victim died at the scene . Fernandes says the shooting does not appear suspicious . 
Fernandes says the body has been sent to the State Medical Examiner in Bismarck .
