Policeinvestigate 2 shootings Wednesday night 

Louisvillepolice are investigating after two separate shootings Wednesday .

The first shooting was reported around 8:30 p.m. in the 300 block of South Shawnee Terrace . 
Police said a 16-year - old was shot in the hand . They were taken to the hospital . 
There is no word on suspects . 
The second shooting was reported around 10:37 p.m. in the 4100 block of Vermont Ave . 
Police said a person was shot in the ankle . There is no word on their condition . 
Police have not given any information on suspects in the shooting .
