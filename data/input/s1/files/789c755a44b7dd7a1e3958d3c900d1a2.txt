Police: Boy shoots himself in thighs , lies about it - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports 

AShreveport juvenile is left answering questions to police after he reportedly shot himself Tuesday night . 
The incident happened around 10:15 p.m. along Gilbert Drive . 
Police say the boy initially reported that he heard gunshots while walking along the street and then started to run home . Officers say they later learned the juvenile shot himself in both of his thighs . 
The boy expected to be okay , but police say it is unclear how he ended up shooting himself . 
No charges have been filed and police are investigating .
