Police : Teen shoots himself in leg during attempted robbery 
Three teenagers are in custody after police say they attempted to rob a home in North College Hill . 
Advertisement 
Police said the trio broke into a home around 11:30 p.m. Saturday in the 6800 block of Parish Road . 
Authorities said during the attempted robbery , one of the teens accidentally shot himself in the leg . 
He was taken to UC Medical Center from the scene . His condition was not immediately available . 
The two others were arrested .
