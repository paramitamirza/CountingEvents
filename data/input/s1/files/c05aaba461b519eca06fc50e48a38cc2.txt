NorthIdaho child killed in accidental shooting 

NEZPERCE COUNTY , Idhao - Thursday evening Nez Perce County Sheriff 's Office responded to a call of a boy with a gunshot wound at a residence off south Peck . 
Three boys were playing in a bedroom when a gun was located . One of the boys attempted to move the gun to a safe location and the gun accidentally went off , the bullet struck one of the boys . 
The victim was taken to Clearwater Valley Hospital , where he later passed away .
