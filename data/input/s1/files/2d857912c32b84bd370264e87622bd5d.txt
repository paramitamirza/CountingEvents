Metro investigating fatal shooting on 900 block of Pennsylvania - WTOC - TV : Savannah , Beaufort , SC , News , Weather & Sports 

Savannah - Chatham Metro Police are investigating a fatal shooting that took place in the 900 block of Pennsylvania Avenue , around lunchtime Thursday . 
Police say the 17-year - old black male victim was taken to the hospital , where he was pronounced dead . 
Officials say the shooting does not appear to be random , and they do not believe the suspect is still in the area .
