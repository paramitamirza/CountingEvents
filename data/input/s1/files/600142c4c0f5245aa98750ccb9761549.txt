Massillonboy accidentally shot by older brother as they played with gun ; no robbery occurred 

MASSILLON, Ohio - The 10-year - old boy who was shot Monday was injured by his brother as they played with a gun . 
Massillon police and city officials said Chandler Woods and his 16-year - old brother were playing with a gun when the younger brother was accidentally shot in the shoulder , according to a news release . 
The case has been forwarded to the Stark County Juvenile Prosecutor 's Office for review to determine if charges will be filed .
