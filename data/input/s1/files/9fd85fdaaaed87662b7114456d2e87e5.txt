Womanfatally shoots herself after target practice 

NIMISHILLENTOWNSHIP , Ohio - Deputies of the Stark County Sheriff 's Office responded to a shooting on Friday night in Nimishillen Township . 
Upon arrival , deputies discovered Robin Lily , 49 , with a single gunshot wound . Officers reported Lily accidentally shot herself while putting her firearm away after target practice . 
She was transported by the Nimishillen Township Fire Department to Mercy Medical Center in Canton where she died from her injuries at 12:48 a.m. on Saturday .
