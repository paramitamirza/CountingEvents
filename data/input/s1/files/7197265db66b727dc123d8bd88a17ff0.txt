4-year- old child injured in apparent accidental shooting in Zephyrhills , police say 

ZEPHYRHILLS, Fla. - The Pasco Sheriff 's Office , along with Fire Rescue , responded to an accidental shooting of a four - year - old . 
Deputies received the call of an accidental shooting at a residence on the 6700 block of Boulder Run Loop in Zephyrhills at 9:17 p.m. on Wednesday . 
According to officials , the child was injured and was flown to an area hospital and is currently in stable condition .
