The Richland County Sheriff 's Department has identified a man wanted for the murder in a deadly shooting Monday . 
Deputies are looking for 18-year - old Laquantae Jordan . He is wanted in connection to a shooting Monday on Joe Frazier Court that led to the death of Westley Bell , 17 . 
Deputies were called to the scene at about 5 p.m. Monday and found Bell on the ground with a gunshot wound to his torso . He was taken to an area hospital where he later died .
