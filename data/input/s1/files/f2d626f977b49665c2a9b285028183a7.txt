TwoJuveniles Shot in Accidental Shooting 

MOUNTAINBURG, Ark.-- - Two juvenile boys were sent to the hospital after an accidental shooting in Mountainburg . 
According to police , the two juveniles had a .22 caliber pistol with them while they were walking in the woods near one of the juveniles residence . One of the juveniles told police he was trying to pull the gun to the front of his waist band on his pants when the gun went off , hitting both juveniles in the leg . 
Both juveniles were taken to Mercy Hospital in Fort Smith and are expected to recover .
