2017-02-14

2 teens shot in Phoenix , 1 killed - CBS 5 - KPHO 

A 17-year - old male was shot and killed on Monday night , and a 15-year - old male who was with him was shot and critically wounded , according to Sgt . Vince Lewis with the Phoenix Police Department . 
It happened at approximately 7:45 p.m. in the area of 3300 West Harmont Drive , near 35th and Northern avenues . 
Lewis said the victims were outside of a residence when they were approached by two people . 
One of those people fired a gun at the victims striking both of them before fleeing with the other person . 
Both victims were transported to hospitals and the 17-year - old was later pronounced dead . He has been identified as Carlos Villareal . 
The 15-year - old suffered a critical injury but is expected to survive . 
Police have not arrested any suspects . 
The investigation is ongoing . 
Anyone with information about this crime should call the Phoenix Police Department or Silent Witness at 480-WITNESS ( 480 - 948 - 6377 ) or for Spanish ( 480 ) TESTIGO. Callers may remain anonymous and could earn a reward of up to $ 1,000 .
