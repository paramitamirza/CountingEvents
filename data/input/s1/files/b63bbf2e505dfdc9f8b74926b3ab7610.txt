Teenshot , injured in hunting accident in Fellsmere 

Ateen has been airlifted to a hospital after being shot in what the Indian River County Sheriff 's Office is calling a hunting accident . 
The incident happened around 3 p.m. near 134th Court and 99th Street in Fellsmere . 
The 15-year - old was hunting with another minor when he was accidentally shot , the sheriff 's office said . 
The youngster was flown to the Arnold Palmer Hospital for Children , the sheriff 's office said . 
NewsChannel 5 's Andrew Lofholm said The Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission told him the injuries do not appear to be life threatening . 
FWC is investigating the case .
