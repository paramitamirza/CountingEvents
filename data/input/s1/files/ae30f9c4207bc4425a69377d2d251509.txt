Teenshot in leg in Lackawanna neighborhood 

JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - Police are searching for the person who shot a 17-year - old in the city 's Lackawanna neighborhood early Sunday morning . 
The teenager was walking near Burke Street , off McDuff Avenue , about 2:30 a.m. when someone opened fire , the Jacksonville Sheriff 's Office said . Police said they found the teen on Willow Branch Avenue , about a block away . 
The teen was taken to the hospital with a non - life - threatening injury to his leg . 
Police said they do not know if the gunman opened fire from a vehicle or was on foot , and said they do not have any suspect information . 
The shooting remains under investigation 
Anyone with information that can help police is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 866 - 845-TIPS.
