14-year- old shooting victim identified ; teen killed in restaurant parking lot on Indy 's west side 

INDIANAPOLIS-- The identity of a 14-year - old who was shot and killed in the parking lot of a restaurant on Indianapolis ' west side Monday night has been released . 
Police were called to the 3200 block of West 16th Street just before 7:30 p.m. 
Witnesses told officers that a car pulled up in the back parking lot of Popeye 's , someone got out of the vehicle and shot the teen . Anthony Lee Hughes Jr. was taken to Eskenazi hospital where he later died . 
Police do not have any suspects at this time . 
The shooting remains under investigation by the Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department .
