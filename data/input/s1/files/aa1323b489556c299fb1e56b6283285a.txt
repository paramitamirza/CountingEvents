16-year - old injured in south Columbus shooting 

COLUMBUS ( WMCH ) — Columbus Police say a teenager was taken to Children ’s Hospital after a reported shooting in south Columbus Saturday night . 
It happened around 10:44pm on the 1500 block of Parsons Avenue . According to police , a 16-year - old male was involved in an altercation and an unknown male drew a handgun and shot at the teenager twice . The victim was struck by a bullet in the lower left leg . He was transported to Nationwide Children ’s Hospital in stable condition . 
Police say there is no suspect information at this time . Anyone with information is asked to call the Columbus Police Assault Unit at 614 - 645 - 4141 or Central Ohio Crime Stoppers at 614 - 461-TIPS(8477 ) .
