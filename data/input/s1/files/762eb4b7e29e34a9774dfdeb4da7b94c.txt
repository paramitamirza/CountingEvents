ST. LOUIS , Mo. ( KMOV.com ) – A shooting occurred in the 3100 block of Mount Pleasant Saturday afternoon . 
A female described as being in her early 20 ’s was shot in the leg and a 5-year - old female was shot in the shoulder . 
Both victims are conscious and breathing when they were taken to a local hospital . 
There are no suspects at this time .
