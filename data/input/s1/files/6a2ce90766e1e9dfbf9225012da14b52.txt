2017-01-12

Police: Woman seriously injured in Williamsburg shooting 

Awoman suffered serious injuries after a shooting Thursday morning in Williamsburg . 
Police responded to a reported shooting around 9 a.m. on Mt. Morgan Road and found a woman on the ground , according to a news release . She was shot in the shoulder . 
The woman was flown to the University of Tennessee Medical Center for serious injuries . 
Nearby Williamsburg Independent School went under lockdown for a short time after the shooting . 
Police have not arrested anyone .
