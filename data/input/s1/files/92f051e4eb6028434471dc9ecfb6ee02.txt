PoliceInvestigating Accidental Shooting That Sent One To Hospit - LEX18.com | Continuous News and StormTracker Weather 

LAWRENCEBURG, Ky. ( LEX 18 ) - A Lawrenceburg man is in critical condition after what police are calling an accidental shooting . 
Police responded to Alton Station Road on Friday night around 11:00 . 
Deputies say when they arrived on scene , they found 24 year - old Zachary Cotton with a gunshot wound to the head . 
He was flown to UK hospital where the Anderson County News reports he remains in critical condition at this time . 
Deputies say no foul play is suspected as of now , but the investigation is ongoing .
