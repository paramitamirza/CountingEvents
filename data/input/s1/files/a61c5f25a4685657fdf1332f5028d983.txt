Youngman found shot on Frontenac street - KMOV.com 

Aphotographer takes evidence photos at the scene of a shooting on Carole Lane in Frontenac Monday . Credit : KMOV 
Police are investigating a shooting that occurred in Frontenac Monday night . 
A young man was found shot on Carole Lane around 7:00 p.m. 
Police said they are searching for the suspect . Authorities are not providing information on the victim 's condition . 
Other information was not immediately known . 
News 4 has a crew on the scene and will have more on News 4 at 10:00 .
