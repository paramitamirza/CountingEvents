Witnesses: Teen accidentally shot in chest in Gwinnett County - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports 

TheDuluth Police Department is investigating after a teen was found with a gunshot wound to the chest . 
The incident occurred on Hailston Drive . The 19-year - old victim was transported to Gwinnett Medical Center - Lawrenceville . His last known condition was listed as critical . 
During the initial investigation , witnesses told police the shooting was accidental . 
CBS46 is still gathering details . Stay tuned to CBS46 and CBS46.com for the latest developments .
