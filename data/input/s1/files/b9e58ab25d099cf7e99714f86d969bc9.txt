2017-02-22

Report: 2 people shot in car in West Toledo 

Twopeople with gunshot wounds have been found in a car at a West Toledo intersection , authorities said . 
Toledo police were working to find out where the shooting occurred and why . 
The victims were taken to ProMedica Toledo Hospital . Their names and conditions were not immediately released . 
Police and paramedics were dispatched at 8:15 p.m. today to West Sylvania and Hazelhurst avenues . Initial indications were that the car was driven there after the shooting . Investigators checked the area around Willys Park , about two blocks south , for evidence of shots fired . 
Police also investigated a report that someone was shot about 9:35 p.m. at Spencer Street and South Avenue .
