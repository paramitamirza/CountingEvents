1person injured , 16-year - old dead in early morning shooting - WRCBtv.com | Chattanooga News , Weather & Sports 

TheChattanooga Police Department responded to a person shot in the 3500 block of Clio Avenue early Thursday morning . 
The call came in just before 1 a.m. 
Two victims were found at the scene . 
A 16-year old female victim was found dead inside a car from an apparent gunshot wound . 
A 20-year old male victim was transported to the hospital with non - life threatening injuries . 
No arrest have been made so far . 
Members of the Violent Crimes Bureau are following all actionable leads .
