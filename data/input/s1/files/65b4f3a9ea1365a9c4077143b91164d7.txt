2017-02-12

11-year - old girl shot in the head on South Side 

An 11-year - old girl was shot in the head while sitting in a van Saturday night on the city 's South Side , according to a police tweet . The girl was taken in critical condition to Comers Children 's Hospital . The shooting occurred at about 7:40 p.m. in the 6500-block of South King Drive in the Woodlawn neighborhood , police said . An offender was not immediately in custody , police said .
