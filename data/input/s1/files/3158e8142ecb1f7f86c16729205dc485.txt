Manand woman shot inside Franklin hotel room - WSMV Channel 4 

Aman and a woman were injured in a shooting at a Franklin hotel Saturday afternoon . 
According to Franklin Police , officers were called to the Hyatt Place hotel at 650 Bakers Bridge Ave . just before 4 p.m. Saturday . 
They say a man and a woman were shot inside a hotel room . One round was fired , hitting both people . 
Both were transported to Vanderbilt University Medical Center with non - life threatening injuries . 
Police say they are not searching for any suspects , however . They believe the man and the woman to be the only people involved in the incident . 
Police are still investigating to figure out exactly what happened .
