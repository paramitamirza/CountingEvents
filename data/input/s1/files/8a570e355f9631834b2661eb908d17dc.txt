UPDATE : Police determine Madison shooting to be accidental 

× UPDATE : Police determine Madison shooting to be accidental 
MADISON , Ala. – Madison Police have learned who shot a man overnight on Shelton Road . And they say it was all an accident . 
Police were called to 194 Shelton Road just after 11:00 p.m. Sunday night after a man went to Madison Hospital with a gunshot wound . The man reported the shooting happened at the Shelton Park Apartments . 
Officers say the incident happened inside the victim ’s apartment . They believe a roommate was handling a loaded pistol when the shot went off . 
The victim was struck in the neck , but didn’t have life - threatening injuries .
