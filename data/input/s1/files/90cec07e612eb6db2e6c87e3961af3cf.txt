2017-01-01

Womandies after being shot inside a vehicle near Twain and Maryland 

Awoman was shot inside a vehicle about 4:30 a.m. today in the central valley in what Metro says appears to be an accidental shooting 
The shooting occurred in the 3700 block of Cambridge , near East Twain Avenue and just west of South Maryland Parkway , according to Metro . 
The victim was transported to Sunrise Hospital but died after arrival . 
The investigation initiated by Metro 's Homicide Section indicated the victim and her female friend were in a parked vehicle with two males they had met while attending a New Year ’s Eve event , according to a Metro news release . While inside the vehicle , investigators believe a firearm was handled and accidentally discharged , striking the female victim . 
At this time , no arrests have been made and this remains and ongoing investigation . Once completed , this case will be submitted to the Clark County district attorney ’s office for review and consideration of criminal charges 
This is the first homicide investigated by Metro in 2017 . The identity of the victim , as well as the cause and manner of death , will be released by the Clark County coroner ’s office .
