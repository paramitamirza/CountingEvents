15-year- old boy shot in East Oakland 

OAKLANDâ€” A 15-year - old boy was in critical condition after being shot Tuesday morning in East Oakland , police said . 
Some people were detained for questioning and some guns recovered , police said . 
The boy , an Oakland resident , was shot about 1:25 a.m. Tuesday in the 1600 block of 36th Avenue in the Fruitvale district . 
What led to the shooting has not been determined . Police said the boy lives in another part of Oakland and they are trying to determine why he was in the neighborhood where he was shot . 
Police are also investigating a Sunday night shooting in East Oakland that left a man paralyzed . 
The man was shot about 9:45 p.m. Sunday in the 2000 block of Foothill Boulevard . 
Police believe he got into a confrontation with someone on the street that ended with him being shot . What prompted the confrontation is under investigation . 
The man was taken to a hospital where he is in critical condition . 
Police and Crime Stoppers of Oakland are offering up to $ 5,000 in reward money for information leading to the arrest of the shooters in each incident . Anyone with information may call police at 510 - 238 - 3326 or Crime Stoppers at 510 - 777 - 8572 .
