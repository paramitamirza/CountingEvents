JeffersonCounty Sheriff 's Deputy ' OK ' after accidentally shooting himself 

AJefferson County Sheriff 's Deputy is expected to be OK after accidentally shooting himself in the arm .

Sheriff Carl Yates said the 15-year veteran was heading to his off - duty job when the shooting happened . 
He said the deputy was standing outside of his car trying to holster his off - duty weapon , when somehow the gun slipped and discharged . We 're told the bullet went into his right bicep . 
Yates said the deputy had surgery at University Hospital and is now recovering . He is expected to survive .
