Mandead after shooting in Harrison Township identified 

HARRISONTOWNSHIP , Ohio ( WDTN ) – Police are investigating a reported accidental shooting that left a man dead in Harrison Township . 
Officers were called to the 2100 block of Claggett Drive around 6:30 p.m. on Tuesday . 
A woman called 911 and said she accidentally shot her boyfriend . Police say she claimed the man was threatening to commit suicide . She said she was trying to get the gun away from him when it went off , killing him . 
The Montgomery County Coroner ’s Office identified the victim as 32-year - old Peter Underwood . 
Police took the woman in for questioning . The shooting is under investigation .
