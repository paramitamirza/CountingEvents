A man in West Texas was arrested on outstanding warrants last weekend after he shot himself in a strip club bathroom then fabricated a story , according to the Ector County Sheriff ’s Office . 
Juan Trevino , 23 , initially told authorities that he had been shot in the thigh while in the parking lot of the Jaguar ’s Golden Club in Odessa on Saturday . He initially gave investigators a fake name , according to a news release .
