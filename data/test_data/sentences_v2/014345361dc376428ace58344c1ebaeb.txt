2016-02-22
4 shot near Bessemer , one dead
Jefferson County deputies are investigating a shooting which killed one man and injured three others near Bessemer .

It happened around 5:25 p.m. Saturday in the 2000 block of Elm Street Southwest near Bessemer .

Investigators say when deputies arrived on the scene , they found one person suffering life - threatening injuries .

The 37-year - old victim later died .

The other victims , ages 28 - 45 , are recovering from non - life threatening wounds .

So far , the investigation has revealed that several people were sitting outside in a vacant lot drinking alcohol before an argument erupted and two men opened fire .

Investigators say two shooters fled the scene and are still on the loose .

WVTM13 News has a crew at the scene working to get more information .
