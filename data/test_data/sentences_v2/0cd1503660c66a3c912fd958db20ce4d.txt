2005-11-25
Vacant House Fire in Little Egg Harbor
A call for a fire in a vacant house summoned the response of the West Tuckerton Fire Company in Little Egg Harbor , NJ on Monday Nov. 21 .
7100 arrived to find heavy fire in the 1.5 sty wood frame at apporximately 2:30 pm on Madeline Drive .
It was determined that the structure was unafe to enter and a defensive attack was initiated .
A call for mutual aid brought an engine from Tuckerton and a tanker from the Mystic Island Fire Company along with an ambulance from Great Bay Regional EMS. One firefighter was transported to Atlanticare Regional Medical Center for chest pains .
The Little Egg Harbor Police Department and the Ocean County Fire Marshal 's Office was investigating the cause of the fire .
