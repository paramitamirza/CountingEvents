Six - Year - Old Shot In Finger
In Chester
PHILADELPHIA ( CBS ) —
A six - year - old boy was shot in Chester , Delaware County on Monday afternoon .

Chester police were dispatched to the 1200 block of W. 9th Street around 4 p.m. Monday to find numerous shell casings in different locations .

The victim ’s mother told police her son and other children were playing in front of a house when a man began shooting in their direction .

The boy was taken to the hospital and is being treated for an injury to his left index finger .

Police are still investigating the shooting , anyone with information is asked to contact Chester Detective Lawrence Weigand at ( 610)-447 - 8426 , or Detective Victor Heness at ( 610)-447 - 8429
