4 People Shot Outside Feltonville Club
PHILADELPHIA ( CBS ) —
A man and 3 women were shot outside the Alounge Social Club in Feltonville , after an argument inside the bar .

It happened around 5:15 a.m. , on Macalester Street , near East Hunting Park .

Officials say there was a fight between a man and a woman inside the club , and when the others got involved , they all were kicked out .
That ’s when authorities say , shots were fired .

All four were taken to local hospitals , and listed in stable condition .
