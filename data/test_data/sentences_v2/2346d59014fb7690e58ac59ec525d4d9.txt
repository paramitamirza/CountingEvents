4-year - old Iowa boy dies after accidental shooting - KWWL - Eastern Iowa Breaking News , Weather , Closings
UPDATE : Fayette County Sheriff Marty Fisher confirms that a 4-year - old boy died after an accidental shooting in Elgin Friday afternoon .

Sheriff Fisher says he believes the boy accidentally shot himself in the head .

The Fayette County Sheriff says this is a very active investigation .

According to initial reports , the boy 's mom and two children were visiting a friend at the Elgin home .

The sheriff says the 4-year - old , as well as a younger child , were left inside while the mom and a friend stepped outside .
That is when the gun was fired .

The child was taken to the West Union hospital where he died .

The Fayette County Sheriff confirms the gun is legally owned by the homeowner .

KWWL spoke to a very shaken witness who said the mother ran to a near by business screaming for help .

The witness also told us , the homeowner was not at home at the time of the incident .
