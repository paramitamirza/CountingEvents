Deputies investigate shooting after Labadieville teen ends up on - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports
Assumption Parish deputies are investigating a shooting that happened Saturday , leaving a 17-year - old Labadieville boy on life support .

Deputies were called to a home on Violet Street around 11:30 p.m. Saturday .
When they arrived , they found Dronald Washington shot in the head with a .38 caliber revolver .
The person who called in the incident said that Washington was playing with the gun when it went off .

Washington was air lifted to the hospital where he was pronounced clinically dead .

The investigation into the incident is ongoing .

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Criminal Investigative Division of the Assumption Parish Sheriff 's Office at ( 985 ) 526 - 1627 .
