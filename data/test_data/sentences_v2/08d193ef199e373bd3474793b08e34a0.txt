Man , child injured in shooting in Lake Highlands
A man and child were injured Friday during an exchange of gunfire in Lake Highlands .

The incident occurred about 3:30 p.m. in the 9700 block of West Ferris Branch Boulevard , not far from Interstate 635 .
Witnesses told police that a man shot into a crowd during a physical altercation , injuring the child .

Another man in the crowd then fired back , hitting the initial shooter , police said .

Both were hospitalized with non - life - threatening injuries .

Police are investigating the incident .
