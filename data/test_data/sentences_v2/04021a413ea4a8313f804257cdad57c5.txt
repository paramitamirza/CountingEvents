2-year - old accidentally shoots father in Southwest Baltimore
A 2-year - old accidentally shot his father over the weekend in Southwest Baltimore , police confirmed .

Police said the father was shot in his side , in their home in the 400 block of North Bend Road , in the Westgate neighborhood on Sunday around 11 a.m..

The boy was not injured , and the father was treated and released from a hospital .
It was not clear how the child acquired the weapon .

No charges have been filed and the investigation is ongoing , police said .
