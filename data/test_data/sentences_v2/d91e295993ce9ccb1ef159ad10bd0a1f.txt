Police looking for leads in southwest Atlanta fight , shooting
Police looking for leads in southwest Atlanta fight , shooting News Police looking for leads in southwest Atlanta fight , shooting Police are trying to piece together what led to the shooting of three people and the beating of another person in southwest Atlanta on Saturday afternoon .

- Police are trying to piece together what led to the shooting of three people and the beating of another person in southwest Atlanta on Saturday afternoon .

It happened in the 1100 block of Cordova Street near White Oak Avenue just after 2:40 p.m. Atlanta police said a fight broke out in a crowd of about 30 to 40 young adults gathered in the area .
The crowd was dispersing as police arrived , leaving behind shell casings and evidence of a shooting .
Police said two handguns were found at the scene .

The victims fled the scene , according to Major Paul Guerrucci , ending up a several different locations , widening the crime scene to several city blocks .
All were transported to Grady Memorial and listed in stable as of Monday afternoon .

Police interviewed the victims , but Major Guerrucci said some of them are not cooperating .
