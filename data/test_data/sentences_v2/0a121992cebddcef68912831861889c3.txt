Police Investigate 2 North Nashville Shootings
NASHVILLE , Tenn. - An investigation got underway after at least two people , one of whom was a teenager , were shot in north Nashville .

The first incident was reported around 9:30 p.m. Tuesday in the 1400 block of 16th Avenue North .

Officials with Metro Nashville Police said a 16-year - old was fighting with three friends , in what they called an attempted robbery .

At that time , detectives said the victim pulled out a handgun and accidentally shot himself in the leg .
He then fired at the alleged robbery suspects .

The investigation continues ; however , evidence supports the victim 's wound is a self - inflicted gunshot .
He 's expected to recover .

Police have yet to identify the three alleged robbery suspects .

Not long after , a 28-year - old showed up at an area hospital , saying he was shot during an argument half a mile away outside a market .

He 's expected to be okay but is not cooperating with police .
No suspects have been taken into custody .

Detectives said it 's not clear if these two incidents are related .
