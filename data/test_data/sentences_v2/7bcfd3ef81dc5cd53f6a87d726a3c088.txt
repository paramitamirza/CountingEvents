Funeral set for 10-year - old killed in accidental shooting - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports
A 10-year - old who was killed in an accident shooting will be laid to rest .

Micheal Wooten 's funeral will be at 5 p.m. on Thursday , May 26 at McRae Chapel in Boaz .

Visitation will be held on Wednesday , May 25 from 5 p.m to 8 p.m. at the funeral home .
