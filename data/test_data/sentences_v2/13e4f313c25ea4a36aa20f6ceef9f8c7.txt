 22-month - old shot at Felton home
 Felton , MN ( WDAY TV ) - The Clay County Sheriff 's Department and the Minnesota BCA are investigating a shooting that injured a 22-month - old girl in Felton .

 Sheriff Bill Bergquist says she is in stable condition .
