2 year old dies after accidentally shooting himself
LUNENBURG COUNTY , Va. ( WRIC ) —
The Lunenburg County Sheriff ’s Office has confirmed that the two year old who accidentally shot himself Monday morning has died .

The toddler who was visiting with his family from Rustburg had been transported by helicopter to MCV after suffering an accidental self inflicted gunshot wound .

Sheriff Arthur Townsend of Lunenburg County told us that shortly before 9:30 a.m. Monday morning , the child got their hands on a handgun that was sitting on a dresser .
The gun discharged , injuring the toddler .

The incident took place on Doswell Town Road , in the Meherrin area of the county .

No charges have been place .
The investigation is ongoing .
