Teen Girl Found Shot Dead in Bronx Apartment Identified
The teenage girl found shot dead inside a Bronx apartment has been identified .

Police have identified the girl as Zion Willis of Canarsie , Brooklyn .

She was found with a gunshot wound to the head in a fourth - floor apartment at 363 East 171st St. in Claremont at about 6 p.m. Thursday , according to police .
She was pronounced dead at the scene .

Police are investigating whether she accidentally shot herself while visiting a friend and are looking to speak with a 42-year - old man who lives in the building and is believed to be the owner of the gun .
