 Teen accidently shot during target shooting in Lebanon
 LEBANON , Ohio ( WKRC ) -- A teenager has been accidentally shot in Lebanon on Saturday .

Two teenagers were reportedly target shooting on Wilmington Road and one was accidentally hit .

The juvenile shot is still in the hospital , and there has been no word on his condition yet .

Detectives are investigating and plan on releasing a statement on Monday .
