4 spectators injured in shooting at illegal drag race on Detroit 's west side
DETROIT - Four spectators at an illegal drag race on Detroit 's west side were injured Sunday after a fight broke out , Detroit police said .

The fight happened overnight near Grand River and McGraw avenues .

Police said someone fired shots and four people were hit .
They are recovering from minor injuries .

Authorities have no information on a possible suspect .
