Six people were wounded in a shooting outside an Uptown grocery store Friday evening .

Someone in a four - door sedan fired shots at a group of people gathered outside the JJ Peppers Food Store in the 4800 block of North Sheridan Road at about 7:15 p.m. , said Officer Michael Sullivan , a police spokesman .

A 23-year - old woman was shot in both thighs , a 24-year - old man was shot in the buttocks and another 24-year - old man was shot in the ankle .
All three of them were taken to Illinois Masonic Hospital .
