2016-03-23
UPDATE : Mother of 2-year - old who shot himself arrested , charged
- UPDATE :

DeKalb County PD says Valencia Burks , the mother of the two - year - old shot at the Knights Inn on Panola Road , has been arrested and charged with Reckless Conduct in the shooting .

Police say the two - year - old shot himself in the stomach and is continuing to improve and expected to recover .

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

A two - year - old boy underwent surgery Monday morning after shooting himself in the stomach .

The child was in a room at the Knights Inn on Panola Road in DeKalb County , according to investigators .
Police think he got his hands on a gun located in his mother 's purse .

The single shot was fired around 2:15 a.m. Monday .
Police said paramedics rushed the toddler to Children 's Healthcare of Atlanta Egleston , where at last check he was listed in critical but stable condition .

The boy , his mother , an older brother and an adult female friend were in the room .
They checked into the hotel the night before , coming from North Carolina .

No one else was hurt .

For now , police said this appears to be an accidental shooting .
The mother is with the boy at the hospital .
Detectives are talking to her and the adult friend to determine what , if any , charges will be filed .

Police said doctor believe the child will survive .
