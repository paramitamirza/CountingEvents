Police : Child shot in Millville apartment complex
Police are investigating after a child was shot inside an apartment complex in Millville , New Jersey .
It happened around 7:30 p.m. Sunday at Oakview Apartments , located on the 1700 block of East Broad Street .
Police say the boy was struck shot while outside an apartment building in the complex when the gunfire took place .
Witnesses stated they heard approximately 5 to 7 shots , which they believed to be fired from a vehicle that was driving through the complex .
The child was taken to Inspira Medical Center Vineland in stable condition .
