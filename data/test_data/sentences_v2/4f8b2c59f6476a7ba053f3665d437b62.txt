Four Injured in NE Miami - Dade Drive - By Shooting : Police
A drive - by shooting in northeast Miami - Dade over the weekend has left four people injured .

Miami - Dade Police say the shooting occurred Sunday evening just before 6 p.m. at Northeast Seventh Avenue and Northeast 166th Street .
Police say four victims were shot in their lower extremities by a drive - by shooter .

Two of the victims were taken to Aventura Hospital in stable condition , while the other two were treated on the scene and released .

Police have not identified a motive in the shooting .

Authorities are searching for an unknown black vehicle in connection with the incident .

Anyone with information is asked to contact Miami - Dade Crime Stoppers at ( 305 )
471-TIPS.
