Two male teenagers were hospitalized Thursday after being accidentally shot by another youth in Eastpointe , police reported .

Lt .
Jason Gibson said a group of teenage males were playing with the gun at a house on the 22000 blocks of Hayes Road near Nine Mile Road about 3:30 p.m. when the shooting took place .

“
One of the teens had his father ’s firearm and didn’t know
it was loaded , ” Gibson said .
“
And the gun discharged , striking two of the teens .
”
 Eastpointe firefighters rendered medical care at the scene for the injured teens , ages 14 and 16 .

The teens were then transported to St. John Hospital & Medical Center in Detroit , where they are reported to be in serious condition .
Police described the teens’ injuries as being non life - threatening .
