Police : 7-year - old girl dies after shooting in Salisbury
A 7-year - old girl was killed early Sunday morning after a Salisbury home was sprayed with bullets , WSOC reports .
Police said the shooting happened around 4:20 a.m. on Harrel Street .
Officers found a 56-year - old who had been shot in the leg and a child who had died at the scene .
The 56-year - old was taken to the hospital .
Police did not identify the victims .
