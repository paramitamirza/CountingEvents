Boy , 14 , fatally shot on Skokomish Reservation
June 24 , 2014 at 10:19 PM
 A 14-year - old boy was fatally shot on the Skokomish Indian Reservation this evening , according to the Mason County Sheriff ’s Office .

The boy was shot in the chest , the sheriff ’s office said .
It ’s unknown whether the shooting was intentional or accidental .

The shooting is under investigation .
