Child Accidentally Shot In Woodruff County
WOODRUFF COUNTY , Ark.-- An 11-year - old boy is in the hospital Sunday after he was shot and seriously injured in Woodruff County .

It happened in the Mayberry Community , west of Cotton Plant .

State police say the boy had walked from a local home to another where a family member resided .

The family member may have thought the boy was a possible intruder into the home .

The child was taken to children 's hospital where he was initially reported to be in critical condition .

The shooting is still under investigation .
