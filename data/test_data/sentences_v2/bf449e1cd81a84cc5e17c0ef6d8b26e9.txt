2016-10-22
Girl , 9 , shot in Bedford Dwellings
A 9-year - old girl was shot in Bedford Dwellings Friday afternoon , and police are searching the area for a suspect .

VIDEO : Watch the Pittsburgh 's Action News 4 report
 Eight shots were fired near courtyard where the girl was outside playing .
One of the bullets hit the girl in the ankle as she was running away .
Police do not believe that she was the intended target .

Police tell Pittsburgh 's Action News 4 that they have a vague description of the suspect , who they say ran from the scene on Bedford Avenue .
Police are reviewing surveillance video .

The shooter is described a wearing a black tassel hat , a black hooded sweatshirt and dark pants .

The victim is in stable condition at the hospital .

The Group Violence intervention Unit is continuing the investigation .
