Spring Branch teen killed in accidental shooting
 A teenager accidentally shot himself inside an apartment late Friday night while playing with a gun .

Around 10:30 p.m. , the 19-year - old was a playing with a gun with several other friends , when the gun accidentally went off inside the apartment located near Long Point Road and Oak Tree Drive .
The teen died of a gun shot wound to the head .

Police interviewed the witnesses inside the apartment , but no charges are expected .
No other information was available about the teen .
