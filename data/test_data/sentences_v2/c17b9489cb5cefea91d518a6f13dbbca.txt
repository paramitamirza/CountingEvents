2015-09-20
1 killed , 11 injured after shooting , rollover crash near Dan Ryan
At least one person is dead and 11 others injured after a SUV got caught in gang - related crossfire in the city 's South Side , police said .
The incident happened at the intersection of 57th Street and La Salle around 2 a.m. Sunday , police said .
That 's just east of the Dan Ryan Expressway .
Police say the vehicle , which was carrying six people , got caught in the middle of gang - related gunfire exchanged between two groups of people at the intersection .
The driver then lost control of the vehicle , causing it to roll over .
The six car passengers injured in the incident are all expected to recover , officials said .
The deceased victim - one of six others standing near the intersection - died as a result of the shooting .
No one is in custody .
