Cops : Florida grandmother Nilda Sheffield kills daughter , grandchildren , self
GREENACRES , Fla. --
Officials say a 53-year - old Palm Beach County , Fla. woman killed her 30-year - old daughter and two young grandchildren before turning the gun on herself .

Their bodies were found Saturday by a family friend who was checking on their well - being .

Greenacres Police Lt .
Brady Myers says Nilda Sheffield killed Elizabeth Flores and 7-year - old Xavier Neff and 2-year - old Sofia Chiddo .
They are still searching for a motive for the apparent murder - suicide .

A makeshift shrine had been set up in the yard of the duplex where the family lived .
