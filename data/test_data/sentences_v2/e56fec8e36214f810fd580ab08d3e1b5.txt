Fatal shooting at Burbank hotel ruled an accident
Authorities have determined the shooting death earlier this month of a 34-year - old man outside the Los Angeles Burbank Airport Marriott Hotel was an accident .

The Los Angeles County coroner ’s office said Justin Malone was killed Nov. 10 when a gun he had in his wheelchair fell to the ground and discharged a bullet that struck him in the head .

Officers found him on the ground in the hotel ’s valet - parking area .
He was pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics .
