2016-10-16
2 dead , 2 injured during violent 24 hours in St. Louis - KMOV.com
Two people are dead , and two other victims are suffering from their gunshot wounds in area hospitals following several separate shootings overnight and this morning in St. Louis .

The first of two homicide cases occurred around 10:26 p.m. in the 6000 block of Cates Avenue , in the West End neighborhood .

When police arrived on the scene , they found a male victim , later identified as Lemmuel Williams , 27 , inside the apartment with multiple gunshot wounds .
He was pronounced dead on the scene .

Almost three hours later , police were called to the 4400 block of Farlin Avenue , in the Penrose neighborhood .
Police found 17-year - old Dontay Jordan shot in the head .
He was pronounced dead on the scene .

The homicide unit is handling all of the investigations .
Currently , there is no information available on the suspects in all three cases .

The homicide unit is also handling another shooting .

A man was transported to the hospital after he was shot in the 3600 block of Louisiana Avenue , near Gravois Park .
The shooting occurred just after 12:30 a.m..

The victim is currently in critical/unstable condition .

The final case during this 24-hour span was in the 4400 block of Lexington , where a 29 year - old man sustained a gunshot wound to his side .
Police found the victim conscious and breathing .
At this time , his condition is unknown .

Police initially investigated a shooting on Dr. Samuel Shepard Drive as a homicide , but later determined the man died from a self - inflicted gunshot wound .

All the cases are ongoing and suspect information is not known .

News 4 will update this story as more information becomes available .
