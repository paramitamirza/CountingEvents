16-year - old boy killed , another teen wounded in Austin shooting
 Chicago Police investigate in the 5500 block of West Quincy , where two teenage boys were wounded in a shooting Monday night .
| Network Video Productions

A 16-year - old boy was killed and a 15-year - old boy wounded in a shooting Monday night in the West Side Austin neighborhood .

The boys were standing outside at 10:11 p.m. in the 5500 block of West Quincy when they heard gunfire and realized they’d been shot , according to Chicago Police .
Both were taken to Stroger Hospital .

The 16-year - old , identified as Elijah Sims , was shot in the head , and was pronounced dead at Stroger at 7:30 a.m. Tuesday , according to police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office .
He lived in the 1900 block of West Harrison .

The younger boy was shot in the back , and his condition was stabilized , police said .
