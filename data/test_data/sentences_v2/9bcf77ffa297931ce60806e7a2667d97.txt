A boy was shot in the head Friday morning in Portage Park , police said .

The shooting , which occurred at 7:45 a.m. in the 4700 block of West Addison Street , appears to be accidental , said Officer Stacey Cooper , a Chicago Police Department spokeswoman .

The boy was taken to Advocate Illinois Masonic Medical Center , but police did not know his condition , Cooper said .
