Teenager shot and killed in Waterbury
WATERBURY , Conn. ( WTNH ) —
A 17-year - old was killed in an afternoon shooting in Waterbury Saturday .

The shooting happened in the area of 30 Porter Street just before 4:30 p.m.
The victim was shot three times including once in the head , according to Waterbury Deputy Police Chief Fernando Spagnolo .

The male victim was taken to Waterbury Hospital where he died at 6:40 p.m.
The victim ’s name has not yet been released .

Police are continuing to investigate the circumstances surrounding the shooting .

Anyone with information is asked to contact Waterbury Police at ( 203 ) 574 - 6920 .
