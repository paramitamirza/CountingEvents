Cleveland police seeking information about east side drive - by shooting
CLEVELAND - Cleveland police are searching for information about an overnight drive - by shooting on the city 's east side .

Police said that a group of five black men wearing hooded sweatshirts and driving a silver sedan fired shots at four people near East 163rd Street and Sorrento Avenue at about 2:30 a.m. on Tuesday .

A 20-year - old man , Veron Black of Maple Heights , was shot several times and died at a hospital .

Another 20-year - old man was shot in the back and treated and released from the hospital .

A 17-year - old girl and a 20-year - old man were not injured .

Anyone with information about the shooting is asked to call Cleveland Police Homicide Unit at ( 216 ) 623 - 5464 .
