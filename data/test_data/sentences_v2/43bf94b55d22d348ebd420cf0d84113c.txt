UPDATE : Two Killed in North St. Louis Home
story updated at 4:04pm on August 10 , 2013
 ST. LOUIS ( KMOX ) –St .
Louis city police are releasing more details on the north side shootings Friday night that left two dead .

Officers say 27-year - old Brian Jermon answered the door of his home in the 4200 block of East Kossuth and was shot in the neck .

Twenty - six - year - old Franchelle Harris of Jennings was then shot in the back .

Both Jermon and Harris died a short time later at hospitals .

Police say two others in the home , identified only as a 22-year - old woman and 29-year - old man , were also shot , but are expected to survive .

Officers do not have a good description yet of the gunman .
