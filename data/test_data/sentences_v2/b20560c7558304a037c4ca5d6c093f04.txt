Shooting outside Miami Gardens night club leaves 1 dead - WSVN - TV - 7NEWS Miami Ft .
Lauderdale News , Weather , Deco
MIAMI GARDENS , Fla. ( WSVN ) --
Authorities are investigating after a security guard was fatally shot at a Miami Gardens night club .

The shooting happened outside of Mint Lounge , just after 4 a.m. , Tuesday , along Northwest 25th Avenue and Miami Gardens Drive .

According to police , the guard was among four people shot .
The three other victims were transported to nearby hospitals .

Police are now looking for the shooter .

If you have any information on this shooting , call Miami - Dade Crime Stoppers at 305 - 471-TIPS.
Remember , you can always remain anonymous , and you may be eligible for a $ 3,000 reward .
