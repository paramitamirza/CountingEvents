Four - year - old in stable condition after suffering gunshot wound , suspect arrested
30-year - old Shaletres Townsend was arrested after placing a firearm in an area where children were playing , resulting in a 4-year - old accidentally being shot 10/16/16 ( LVMPD )
 MORE MEDIA Child shot , Live in Prime
 Metro detectives tell News 3 a four - year - old girl was at home with her parents , another adult , and three siblings when a gun went off and at least one bullet hit her .

In a release by LVMPD , detectives determined that the suspect , identified as 30-year - old Shaletres Townsend , had placed her firearm on a chair in a bedroom where children were playing .
The firearm was accidentally discharged , hitting the child .

Police say they are still investigating
but she may have accidentally shot herself according to the initial reports .
Officers are looking to see if there are other possibilities in what they believe is an accidental shooting .

The shooting was first reported around 2:30 p.m. in the 4200 block of Studio Street , near Craig and Lamb .

According to officers , the father called 911 after the shooting .
The child was taken to UMC in critical condition .

Police say at least one bullet entered near the neck or chin .
Her condition has improved since reaching the trauma center at UMC where authorities say the child is in stable condition .

Both Child Protective Services and LVMPD Abuse/Neglect detectives were on scene .

One neighbor said she has seen an increase in crime in the last few years , but still , legal gun owners should still keep their weapons locked for safety .

" You keep that away from your kids .
They supposed to be behind locks , " the neighbor said .

Metro has yet to confirm if the gun was legally owned by a person in the home , but said the parents are cooperating with police .

In a release , LVMPD wants to remind parents to responsibly store their firearms and to secure them in a locked container or to use a gun lock device to prevent them from being used unintentionally .

LVMPD Lt .
Grant Rogers says all gun owners should take precautions with their weapons .

" If you have a safe or a gun locker or just some ways of storing a weapon is really key for any gun owners out there , " Lt .
Rogers said .

As a result of the investigation , Townsend was arrested and booked into the Clark County Detention Center on one count of child neglect with substantial bodily harm .
