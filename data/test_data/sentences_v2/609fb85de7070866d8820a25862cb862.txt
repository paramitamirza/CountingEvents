14-year - old shot in west Charlotte , police believe accidental - | WBTV Charlotte
A teenager is suffering from a gunshot wound following a shooting in west Charlotte Sunday afternoon .

The shooting happened just after 2 p.m. on the 4900 block of Arborwood Drive .

The victim , a 14-year - old male , was taken to Carolina Medical Center and treated for minor injuries .

Police believe the shooting was an accident .

We have no more details at this time .
