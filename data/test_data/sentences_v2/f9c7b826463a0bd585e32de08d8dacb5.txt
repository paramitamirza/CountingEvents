2016-08-17
Boy identified in deadly shooting range accident
Authorities are investigating the death of a 10-year - old boy at a gun range in northern Iowa .

Police say in a news release that Cade Hartwig , of Clear Lake , suffered a head wound Sunday afternoon from a pellet gun at the Garner shooting range .

Garner City Administrator Randy Lansing said Wednesday that the boy and a younger brother went to the range with their grandfather , Tom Hartwig Sr . , who also lives in Garner .

Lansing says exactly what occurred at the range Sunday remains unclear , but no charges are expected to be filed .

Police have characterized the incident as an accidental shooting death .
