3-year - old accidentally shot at south Nashville home
NASHVILLE , Tenn. ( WKRN ) –
A 3-year - old child accidentally shot himself in the arm in south Nashville Monday night .

Metro police said the shooting happened just before 7 p.m. on Whitsett Road not far from Thompson Lane and Nolensville Pike .

Investigators told News 2 the boy was alone in his parents’ bedroom when he grabbed a revolver from a bookshelf .

The family then heard a single shot and found the 3-year - old boy hurt in the bedroom .

The child was transported to a nearby hospital with non life - threatening injuries .

The Metro Police Youth Services division is investigating .
Further details weren’t immediately released .
