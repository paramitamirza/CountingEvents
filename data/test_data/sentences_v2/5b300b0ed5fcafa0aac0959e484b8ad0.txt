Police in Memphis have charged a man in the shooting of a 4-year - old child .

Media outlets report 26-year - old Johnathan Miller was charged with aggravated assault , reckless endangerment with a deadly weapon and unlawful possession of a weapon .

Police say the 4-year - old was taken to a hospital with a gunshot wound on Aug. 7 .

A police affidavit says witnesses identified Miller through a photo lineup as a suspect .
He was booked into jail on Friday on $ 300,000 bond and is due in court for a hearing Monday .
