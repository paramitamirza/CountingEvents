2016-12-11
Two men killed in shooting
Two men were shot and killed Saturday night in unincorporated Palm Beach County .

The shooting happened just after 9 p.m. at the Serrano Apartments located in the 6000 block of Sherwood Glen Way , just off of Forest Hill Boulevard and three miles east of the Florida Turnpike .

Upon arrival to the scene , Palm Beach County Sheriff 's Deputies found two men dead with gunshot wounds .

The incident is under investigation .
There is no motive or suspect information at this time .
