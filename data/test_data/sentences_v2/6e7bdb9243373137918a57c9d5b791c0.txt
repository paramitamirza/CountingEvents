WPD : 15-year - old gang member charged after accidentally shooting -

 Wilmington police have charged a 15-year - old validated gang member after he accidentally shot himself Tuesday afternoon .

According to officials , the incident happened around 1:30 p.m. at the intersection of 15th and Castle streets .

When emergency crews arrived at the scene , they found the juvenile suffering from a gunshot wound to his upper thigh area .
The teen was taken to New Hanover Regional Medical Center with non - life threatening injuries .

After investigating , officers determined that the teen shot himself while sitting in a vehicle .
He then called 911 and reported he was struck by a stray bullet .

The juvenile was charged with discharging a firearm within city limits , possession of a handgun by a minor , and filing a false police report .
