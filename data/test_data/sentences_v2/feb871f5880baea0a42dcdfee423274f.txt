Shooter Fires Into Crowd , Injuring Child In Dallas
Follow CBSDFW.COM : Facebook | Twitter
 DALLAS ( CBSDFW.COM ) –
A child was shot this afternoon in Dallas during a fight that ended with a man shooting into a crowd .

It happened in the 9700 block of West Ferris Branch Boulevard .

Witnesses said another man in the crowd shot back at the suspect , injuring him .

Both the suspect and the minor child were transported to a local hospital with non life threatening injuries .
