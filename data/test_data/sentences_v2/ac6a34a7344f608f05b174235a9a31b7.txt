7-year - old accidentally shot in Lodi
Lodi police said a 7-year - old girl was struck by a bullet Sunday after a gun was accidentally discharged .

The shooting happened around 4 p.m. at an apartment on North Church Street .

Police said the bullet went through two walls and then struck the girl , who was outside in a courtyard .

She was taken to the hospital with non - life - threatening wounds .
