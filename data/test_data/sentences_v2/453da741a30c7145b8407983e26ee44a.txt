Caretaker turns herself in after 4-year - old accidentally shoots himself , police say
The caretaker of a 4-year - old who accidentally shot himself has turned herself in , according to Greenville Police Department .

Lydia Riley has been taken into custody and charged with unlawful neglect of a child , deputies say .

A 4-year - old accidentally shot himself on Aug. 1 , according to Greenville police .

The shooting happened around 12:45 p.m. at a home on Hillside Circle , Officer Gilberto Franco said .

Police at the scene said the child was shot in the hand .

Franco said the boy was alert when he was taken to the hospital .
