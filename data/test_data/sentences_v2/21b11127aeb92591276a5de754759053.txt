Police name victim of hotel shooting - KATV - Breaking News , Weather and Razorback Sports
New details are emerging from Tuesday night 's shooting that left one man dead and three others injured .
Police have identified 25 year old Michael Strickland as the victim who was shot to death .

Little Rock Police Department responded to America 's Best Value Inn Tuesday in response to a shooting in Room # 140 .

Police believe the shooting stemmed from an argument that escalated outside of the hotel room .

Three other men were also wounded .
Calvin Williams , 24 was shot in the chest , Kalone Mcgee , 26 was shot in the left leg and Jamie clayton , 22 was shot in both legs .

Clayton transported to the Pulaski county detention facility where he was charged with possession of cocain with the intent to deliver , possession of marijuana , simultaneous possession of firearm and drugs and possession of a firearm by certain persons .

Channel seven caught up with Cameron Coleman who witnessed the incident and heard the gun shots .

”Lil mike , he was one of my close friends that was really sad , ” Coleman said .

Tuesday 's shooting was not the first time LRPD has responded to the hotel .
According to LRPD they have responded to disturbances at the Scott Hamilton location 63 times in the last six months .

Even with its track record , the incident still came as a surprise to Coleman .
 “
So many things that could have been said , so many things that could have prevented I guess it did n't , “
Coleman said .

Police still have not released a motive or a lead on who might be responsible for the fatal shooting .

Police are still investigating .
