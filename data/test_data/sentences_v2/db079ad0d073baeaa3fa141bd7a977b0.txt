New Orleans teen shot while playing with gun with friends
.
( Photo : KING 5 News )

NEW ORLEANS --
A 16-year - old is being treated for a gunshot wound after being shot early Thursday morning while playing with a gun with friends , police say .

According to New Orleans Police , it is unclear who fired the weapon .
The teen was transported to the hospital for treatment .
The condition of the teen is unknown .

Police reported the incident shortly before 1 a.m. Thursday in the 6000 block of Downman Road .
