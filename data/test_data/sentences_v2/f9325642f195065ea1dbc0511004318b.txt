Deputies : 7-year - old accidentally shot after gun found in NC woods
A 7-year - old girl was taken to the hospital after being accidentally shot by another juvenile in some North Carolina woods Wednesday , deputies said .

The incident was reported around 3:45 p.m. near Rutherfordton about 50 miles southeast of Asheville .
The girl was shot in the buttocks and taken to a hospital in Spartanburg , S.C.
 Rutherford County deputies said the victim and three other juveniles found a gun inside a backpack in a wooded area near the victim 's home .
The other juveniles included two family members and a neighbor friend .

When one of the juveniles pulled out the gun , the victim was accidentally shot , deputies said .

Rutherford County deputies and county Department of Social Services officials are handling the investigation .
