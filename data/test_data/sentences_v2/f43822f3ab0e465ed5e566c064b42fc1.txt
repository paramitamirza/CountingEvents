2 Killed , 2 Hurt In West Side Detroit Shooting
DETROIT ( WWJ )
– Detroit police investigating an early morning shooting that left two people dead and two others injured .

It happened on Grand Street , near the Lodge Freeway and the Davison Freeway on the city ’s west side .

According to police , the two killed were a 28 year - old man and a 37-year - old man .
Those injured were 28 and 31 years old .
One of those injured was shot in the leg , but there were no further details available about the victims .

There have been no arrests and there was no immediate word about a suspect or motive in the shooting as an investigation continues .
