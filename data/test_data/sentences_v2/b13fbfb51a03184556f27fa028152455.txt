8-year - old shot in head , dies while playing with gun , police say
HOUSTON - An 8-year - old was shot and killed while playing with a gun inside a house in southeast Houston on Thursday afternoon , according to police .

According to officers , the incident was called in about 4:45 p.m. at 8442 Tavenor Lane near Hobby Airport .

Police said an 8-year - old and 11-year - old found two guns inside a home they were visiting and began playing with one of the guns .
According to officers , they were throwing the gun back and forth when it went off and the 8-year - old was struck in the head .

The child died at the scene .
The case will be handed over to the Harris County District Attorney 's Office .

" We believe that the magazine was not in the firearm but one round was chambered in the barrel , " said Detective Robert Klementich with the Houston Police Department homicide division .

Forensic testing will be done on the weapon to be sure .
Police said it was .380 semi - automatic handgun .

Investigators said the guns were unsecured in the home .

" We do n't own guns , I know they do n't own guns , so I do n't know , " said Jordan Hightower , whose 11-year - old brother was involved in the shooting .

Hightower told KPRC 2 News he knew the 8-year - old victim very well , and their families were close friends .
He did n't know there were guns in the home .

The boys were visiting the home where the shooting happened .

Police said the boys were home alone with two 12-year - old girls when the accident happened .
The adults had just run out to pick up dinner .

" He had a bright future , " Hightower said .
" He was smart in school and played football .
Now , I 'll never see him again . "

The victim has not been identified .

No charges have been filed at this time .
