One killed , multiple shot in Dayton
Dayton police now say the man killed in Sunday 's shooting was thrown out of the establishment for his behavior .
( WDTN Photo )
 DAYTON , Ohio ( WDTN ) —
Dayton Police are investigating a shooting that left one man dead and multiple people injured .

Gunfire erupted near the intersection of Germantown Street and McArthur Avenue around 4 a.m. Sunday .

Officers said about 100 people were at a house party when the shooting happened .

One person was pronounced dead at the scene .
Medics rushed a woman to the hospital after she had been shot .
Police said five other victims showed up at the hospital with gunshot wounds .

The Montgomery County Coroner ’s Office has identified the victim as Dwayne Curtis , 27 , of Trotwood .

Officers said they found another person a block away who had been shot in the leg .
Officials are looking into whether the victim ’s injuries are related to the shooting at the party .

No word on what exactly led up to the shooting .
