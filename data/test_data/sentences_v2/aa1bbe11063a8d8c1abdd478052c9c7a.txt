Hunter Shot On Grand Mesa
One person is dead after a reported hunting accident occurred Sunday morning on the Grand Mesa .

The Mesa County Sheriff 's office said they received a call through dispatch around 8 AM about a hunter being shot near the Big Creek Campground on the Grand Mesa .

Deputies and the Collbran Marshall responded to the scene .

The Mesa County coroner is working with investigators to determine the cause and manner of death .

The identity of the deceased person has not been released .

The investigation is currently on - going .
