Police : Boy Fatally Shot In Apparent Accident
December 30 , 2014 11:50 AM
 DETROIT ( WWJ/AP ) –
A 9-year - old boy who was home alone with his teenage cousins has died after what Detroit police say appears to be an accidental shooting .

Officer Adam Madera said the incident happened just before 9 p.m. Monday at a home in the 11200 block of Roxbury , in an east side neighborhood near I-94 and Cadieux Road .

Officers responded to the home after receiving a 911 call from a man who said his stepson had been shot in the face with a shotgun .
Police rushed the boy to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead .

Police said the boy and his three cousins , ages 17 , 14 and 13 , were alone in the house at the time of the shooting .
Madera said the boy was apparently playing with the 14-year - old in the basement when the gun was fired .
The older teen rushed downstairs after hearing the gunfire , Madera said , and found the boy wounded .

Investigators have not yet revealed how the gun was fired , but said it “ appears accidental .
”

It wasn’t immediately clear what the stepfather was doing at the time of the shooting .
Police said he was not present at the home .

The owner of the gun used in the shooting , described as the boy ’s mother ’s significant other , is being held by police .

Names of those involved were not immediately released .

An investigation is ongoing .
