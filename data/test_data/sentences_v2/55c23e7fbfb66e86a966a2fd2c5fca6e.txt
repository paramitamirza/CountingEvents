Child wounded in shooting at Spring home
 Montgomery County deputies are investigating what they are calling an accidental shooting that hospitalized a child .

Deputies responded to reports of the shooting at around 5 p.m. Sunday at the 30700 block of Eastridge Drive in Spring , said Montgomery County Sheriff 's Lt .
Brady Fitzgerald .

The wounded child was taken to a hospital with non life - threatening injuries , he said .

He said no arrests have been made and declined to describe how the child was shot .

Fitzgerald said Monday that deputies are still investigating the incident .
