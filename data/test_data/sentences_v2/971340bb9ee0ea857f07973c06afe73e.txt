Man charged with killing of two men in May - WALB.com , South Georgia News , Weather , Sports
A man has been arrested in DeKalb County for the killing of two men in May.
 Tony Thomas , 19 , was taken into custody by members of the DeKalb County Sheriff 's Office on Thursday and charged with two counts of murder and one count of aggravated assault .

Thomas allegedly shot and killed Veondus Dennis and Antwan Wheeler on May 10 after a dispute that began at South DeKalb Mall and ended with a shootout on Shamrock Drive .

Thomas is also charged with shooting and injuring another person during the incident .

According to the arrest warrant , police believe the shooting may have been gang related .
