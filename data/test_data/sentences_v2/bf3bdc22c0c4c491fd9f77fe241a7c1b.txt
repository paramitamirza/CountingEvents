Titusville woman wounded after gift gun goes off
Titusville police are investigating an accidental shooting .
Photo illustration is of a Colt .45 ( Photo : FILE )

A woman was hospitalized with serious injuries Tuesday morning after a loaded .22 caliber handgun given as a gift accidentally fired off a round into the thigh , Titusville police reported .

The unidentified woman apparently did not know the gun was loaded and was in the bedroom when she was struck in her right leg .
The incident happened about 6 a.m. Tuesday at a house in the 2000 block of Karanda Street .
