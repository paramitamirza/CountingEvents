2016-10-05
Accidental shooting victim identified
NEWLINE The family of an Iron River teenager has released new information on an accidental fatal shooting this week .

TV6 has confirmed the victim was Frank Olson , 18 .

Olson was a student at West Iron County High school .

According to his father , Frank was accidentally shot by his friend who was cleaning a gun Sunday night .

The father says the friend did not know the gun was loaded .

Police are still investigating the matter and have not yet released any further information .
