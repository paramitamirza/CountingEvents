Police : Woman fatally shot as child plays with shotgun
Kalamazoo —
Police say a 12-year - old child was playing with shotgun at a Kalamazoo home when the weapon accidentally discharged , killing a 28-year - old family friend .

Kalamazoo Public Safety officers responded about 5:50 a.m. Friday following a report of the shooting , which happened while the child ’s family slept .

In a statement , police described the child as “ mentally impaired ” but didn’t release details .

Shotgun pellets went through a wall at the home , striking the woman as she slept .
Officers found her with a shotgun wound to the head and she died at the scene .
Her name wasn’t immediately released , but police say she had been temporarily living at the home .

Police say the shooting is under investigation .
