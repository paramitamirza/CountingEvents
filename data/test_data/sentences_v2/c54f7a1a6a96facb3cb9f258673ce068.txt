 PEORIA , Ariz. — Peoria police say a 15-year - old boy has been arrested in an Oct. 12 drug - related shooting in which an 18-year - old man was wounded .

Police say the 15-year - old was booked into a Maricopa County juvenile detention facility on suspicion of aggravated assault , armed robbery and other charges .
The Associated Press generally does not identify juveniles accused of crimes .

According to police , the shooting occurred during a struggle after the 15-year - old pulled out a handgun and robbed the man during an agreed - upon meeting to conduct a drug transaction .

Police say the 18-year - old was shot in the chest and leg but is expected to make a full recovery , while the 15-year - old also shot himself in the wrist .

It ’s not known if the 15-year - old has an attorney who could comment on the allegations .
