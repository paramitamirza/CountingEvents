 NORFOLK , Va. -
A man was killed in an Ocean View shooting Wednesday night .

Police say the incident happened in the 9500 block of 7th Bay Street .

Police Dispatch got a call about the incident at 9:57 p.m.

The victim has been identified by police as 17-year - old Keyshawn Rodriguez .
