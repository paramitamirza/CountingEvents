 KENTWOOD , Mich. —
Police say a 3-year - old boy accidentally shot his mother in the head on Saturday .

Officers responded to a report of a shooting at a home in Bailey ’s Grove around 2:40 p.m.

According to dispatch , upon arrival the mother had a gunshot wound to the head .
She was taken to a hospital with non - life threatening injuries .

The mother ’s name has not been released .

The case is still under investigation .
