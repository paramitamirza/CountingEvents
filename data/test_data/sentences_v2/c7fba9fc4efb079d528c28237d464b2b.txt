JPSO : Second victim in shooting that killed baby is 16-year - old - FOX 8 WVUE New Orleans News , Weather , Sports , Social
Jefferson Parish sheriff 's investigators said they have not yet been able to talk to a 16-year - old who was injured in a Marrero double shooting that claimed the life of a baby boy .

A Sheriff 's Office spokesman said the teen is recovering from his injuries but is heavily sedated and unable to help detectives .

The incident occurred Saturday around 4:30 p.m. in the 2100 block of Caddy Drive .

According to police , responding deputies found the 10-month - old boy and the teen with multiple gunshot wounds .
The 10-month - old , identified as Xyahir Davis , was taken to West Jefferson Medical Center where he was pronounced dead .
The teen underwent surgery at University Medical Center and is listed in serious condition .

Investigators learned that victims were in the home at the time of the incident with Davis ' father and three other men .
Witnesses told investigators that someone opened the front door and began firing in the home .

Multiple shell casings were recovered near the front door and inside the home .

Detectives are working to identify the person(s ) responsible as well as a motive .

If you have any information that could help police with this case , you are asked to call Crimestoppers at 504 - 822 - 1111 .
