1 dead , 4 injured in shooting in Forest
UPDATE : The Scott County Coroner said Freda Lockett was the victim who was killed in Sunday ’s deadly shoot .

Lockett was 39 years of age .

FOREST , Miss. ( WJTV ) –
Forest Police are investigating a deadly shooting .

The shooting happened Sunday night around 9:30 p.m. in a subdivision on Poplar Avenue .

The police chief tell us that a fight broke out and then shots were fired .

Four people were injured and one person was killed , police said .

Police are still investigating .
