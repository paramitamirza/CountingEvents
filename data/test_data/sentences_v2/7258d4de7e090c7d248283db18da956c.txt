Four people were wounded in a West Side shooting on Friday , officials said .

At 12:55 p.m. , four people were shot in the 4500 block of West West End Avenue , said Officer Janel Sedevic , a Chicago Police spokeswoman .

A Chicago Fire Department spokesman said multiple ambulances responded to the shooting and took four people to the hospital .
Two of the victims were in serious - to - critical condition , and two were in good condition , the spokesman said .

They were taken to Mount Sinai and Stroger hospitals .
