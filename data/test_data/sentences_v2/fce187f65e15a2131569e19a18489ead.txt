 Died July 31 , 2016
 200 East Sixth Street
 Teqnika Moultrie , a school bus driver from San Carlos , Calif.,was in Austin visiting her wife 's family when she was shot and killed in Sixth Street as the bars of the city 's entertainment district closed .

Austin police have said Endicott Ishmael McCray fired several shots in the early morning in an attempt to shoot his brother - in - law .
One of the shots hit Moultrie in the head , killing her .
Four other people were injured .

According to relatives , Moultrie had spent the day before rafting and went out at night to see the city .
She was with her wife when she was shot .

Moultrie was a tomboy growing up and an avid athlete , her brother Ricky Moultrie said .
She worked as a school bus driver because she loved kids .

“
She loved life , period , ” Ricky Moultrie said .
“ After our mother passed away , she filled those footsteps .
She was the glue to our family .
”

McCray fled Austin and was arrested in Atlanta on Aug. 3 .
He has been charged with murder .
