Mobile police investigating gunshot wound to head as accidental - WTVM.com - Columbus , GA News Weather & Sports
An 18-year - old male received a life - threatening gunshot wound to his head Saturday afternoon at South Haven Apartments , at 3577 Dauphin Island Parkway in Mobile , according to authorities .

A Mobile Police Department spokeswoman told FOX10 News the incident is being investigated as an accidental shooting .
It was not clear right away whether the gunshot wound was self - inflicted or someone else is responsible .

FOX10 News will continue to look into the matter .
