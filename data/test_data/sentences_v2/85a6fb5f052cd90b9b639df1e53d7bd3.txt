13 year old killed in accidental shooting in St. Mary Parish
The St. Mary Sheriff ’s Office says a 13-year - old boy died after an accidental shooting Friday night .

Authorities say a deputy responding to an unrelated complaint was flagged down by several people who notified him of gunshots heard at a nearby apartment building near Grandwood Drive in Patterson .

The deputy went to the source of the gunfire , and found an unresponsive 13-year - old male with a gunshot to the head .

Authorities say evidence points to an accidental shooting , and that no foul play is suspected .

The St. Mary Parish Sheriff ’s Office as well as the St. Mary Parish Coroner is investigating the death .

An autopsy is pending .
