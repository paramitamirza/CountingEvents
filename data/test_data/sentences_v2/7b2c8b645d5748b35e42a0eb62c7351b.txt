17-year - old boy shot himself during attempted robbery in Chatham
A suspected attempted robber shot himself during a robbery Sunday morning in the South Side Chatham neighborhood .

The 17-year - old boy shot himself in the right leg while he was attempting to rob someone in the 8300 block of South Cottage Grove about 10:30 a.m. , according to Chicago Police .

He was taken in good condition to Jackson Park Hospital , police said .
A second suspect ran away with the gun .

The attempted robbery victim was not injured .

Charges are pending against the suspect as of Sunday afternoon , police said .
