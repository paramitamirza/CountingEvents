1 killed 3 injured in weekend shooting in Charlotte - | WBTV Charlotte
CHARLOTTE , S.C. ( AP ) -
Police in Charlotte are investigating after one man was killed and three injured in a shooting at an apartment .

Police tell local media outlets that 20-year - old Adrian Potts was killed in the shooting that happened about 3 a.m. Saturday .
Authorities say a crowd had gathered in the apartment and fight broke out when the shooting occurred .

Three other people were treated and released from Carolinas Medical Center University .
No arrests have been made .

The apartment where the shooting occurred is located near the UNC - Charlotte campus .
