Baton Rouge police : drive - by shooters kill 2 , injure 2 Posted : Sunday , July 19 , 2015 6:17 PM EDT Updated : Sunday , July 19 , 2015 6:17 PM EDT
 BATON ROUGE , La. ( AP ) - Baton Rouge police say a drive - by shooting has killed two men and injured a third man and a 16-year - old boy .

Cpl .
Don Coppola Jr. says investigators do n't know of either suspects or motive in the shooting that killed 31-year - old Brandon Wallace and 28-year - old Terrell Heard .

He says in a news release that the boy and a 22-year - old man were taken to a hospital with wounds that doctors do n't consider life - threatening .

The shooting occurred about 7:40 p.m. Saturday around the 300 block of Brice Street .

Police are asking anyone with information about the shooting to call the Violent Crimes Unit or Crime Stoppers .
