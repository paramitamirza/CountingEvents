9-year - old accidentally shot in North County , police say - KMOV.com
A child is expected to be okay after being accidentally shot in North County .

Police said the 9-year - old victim was accidentally shot in the foot with a rifle just before 11:00 p.m.
The shooting occurred inside an apartment in the 4200 block of Cloverfield Trail .

It is unknown at this who fired the rifle .
At this time , police do not have anyone in custody .

The investigation is ongoing .
