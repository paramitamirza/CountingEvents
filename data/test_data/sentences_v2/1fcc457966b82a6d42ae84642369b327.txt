Elliston 13-year - old in stable condition after being accidentally shot
MONTGOMERY COUNTY ( WSLS 10 ) –
A 13-year - old is in stable condition Friday after being shot in Elliston Thanksgiving Day .

It happened in the 5100 block of Duke lane .
The Montgomery County Sheriff ’s Office said the shooting incident was accidental .

A call for help came to 911 dispatchers in around 3 p.m. for a 13-year - old boy who was accidentally shot .
He was airlifted to Roanoke Memorial Hospital .
Information about who shot him was not being released .

The Montgomery County Sheriffs Office was investigating this incident .
