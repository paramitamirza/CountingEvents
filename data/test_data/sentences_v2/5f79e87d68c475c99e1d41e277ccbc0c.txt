Slaying victims in barbershop shooting identified
< p>Authorities have released the names of three men slain at a barbershop.</p >
 Authorities have released the names of three men slain when a gunman opened fire into the rear of a Detroit barbershop where illegal gambling was taking place .

The Wayne County medical examiner 's office says 61-year - old Joezell Williams , 29-year - old Bryan Williams and 40-year - old Kevin Perryman died of multiple gunshot wounds .

Six other men were wounded early Wednesday evening at Al 's Barber Shop on the city 's east side .
Authorities have n't released the survivors ' conditions .

Police Chief James Craig said Thursday that at least 20 men were in a back room when shots were fired through an open outside door .

Craig says the shooting was the result of " an ongoing feud . "

Police say they 've arrested a person of interest on an unrelated charge and will question him in the shooting .
