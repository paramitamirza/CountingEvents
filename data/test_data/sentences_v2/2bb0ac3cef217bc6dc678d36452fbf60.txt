A man wanted in connection to a quadruple shooting during a party at a Miami Gardens apartment complex turned himself in to police Wednesday , officials said .

Ivan Clark , 21 , turned himself in and will face four counts of attempted second - degree murder , Miami Gardens Police said .
It was unknown if he has an attorney .

The shooting was reported in the 600 block of Northwest 177th Street Tuesday afternoon .

Investigators say it happened in broad daylight as people were gathering for a birthday party .
Right after the shots were fired a woman pulled into the complex to visit a friend .
When she arrived she said she found out her friend 's son was one of those hit by a bullet .
