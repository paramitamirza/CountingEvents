No Charges Filed in October Shooting in Downtown Reno - KTVN Channel 2 - Reno Tahoe Sparks News , Weather , Video
The Reno Police Department has announced that no charges are expected to be filed in a triple shooting that occurred in downtown Reno last month .

According to a press release , the incident happened at around 1 a.m. on October 23 in Douglas Alley near Siri 's Casino .
Three people were injured , including a 17-year - old female who succumbed to her injuries .

The investigation revealed that the 17-year - old female confronted a group of people , produced a firearm and fired several times .
One subject from the victim group returned fire in defense , striking the female and resulting in her death .
