LOUISVILLE , KY ( WAVE ) –
A 16-year - old died after he was shot in the head and now his 17-year - old friend is charged with his murder .

The victim 's mother , Tamarah Cunningham , said her son and the suspect have been friends for 10 years .
They even lived next to each other .

Louisville Metro police are remaining tip lipped about the evidence that led them to this murder charge .
