3-year - old dies after shooting self with grandfather 's gun

A 3-year - old boy who apparently shot himself with his grandfather 's gun in Spring has died , authorities said .

The boy , whose name Harris County Sheriff 's Office spokeswoman Tebben Lewis said was not being released , had been taken to Memorial Hermann Hospital Sunday in unknown condition .
He died on Monday .

It was unclear Sunday where the gun had been stored or whether it had a lock .

The boy 's death follows a series of child shootings this year in Harris County , three of which happened over the course of four days in February and March .
