16-year - old charged as adult in shooting of 4 Evansville teens - Tri - State News , Weather & Sports
An Evansville teen is being charged as an adult in connection with a shooting on Monday that sent four other teens to the hospital .

Court records show 16-year - old Jarnell Carter is being held on a $ 1-million cash bond .

[ PREVIOUS : Four teens taken to hospital after Evansville shooting ]

The shooting happened around 10:30 p.m. Monday on South Bedford Avenue , near East Cherry Street .

According to an arrest affidavit , the shooting stemmed from an ongoing gang feud .
Witnesses on the scene of the shooting say the feud was over a murder case from last year .

When the two groups met up Monday night , several witnesses identified Carter in lineups as the person who pulled out a gun and started shooting at the crowd .

The four people that were shot were either hit in their back , legs , or hand .
No one was seriously hurt , according to police .
