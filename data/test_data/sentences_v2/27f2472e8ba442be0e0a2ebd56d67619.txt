Teen recovering after accidentally shooting herself in the leg
A teenage girl was taken to a local hospital after she accidentally shot herself while playing with a gun she found .

Waco Police Sgt .
Patrick Swanton said a teenage girl shot herself in the leg Saturday .

He said the 15-year - old girl told police she found a revolver near the intersection of North 9th Street and Colcord Avenue .

She was playing with the gun when it accidentally discharged striking her in the thigh , he said .

Swanton said the teen was taken to Providence Health Center .

Her parents turned the weapon over to police .

No further details were immediately available .
