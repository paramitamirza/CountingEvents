Ã— Gang - Related Shooting in Long Beach Leaves 4 Wounded , 1 Critically , Police Say
 Four people were wounded , one critically , in a shooting Sunday night in Long Beach , authorities said .

The incident was reported at 9:40 p.m. in the 200 block of West 14th Street ( map ) , a spokesperson for the Long Beach Police Department said .

Investigators believe the shooting was gang - related , according to the spokesperson .

The four wounded were taken to a hospital .
One person was in critical condition ; the three others suffered injuries that were not life - threatening , police said .

A possible fifth victim may have transported him or herself to a hospital , authorities said .

No other information was immediately available .
