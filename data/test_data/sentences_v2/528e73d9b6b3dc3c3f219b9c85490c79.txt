Caretaker booked after 8-year - old shoots , injures brother Posted : Wednesday , May 27 , 2015 7:13 AM EDT Updated : Wednesday , May 27 , 2015 7:13 AM EDT
 BATON ROUGE , La. ( AP ) - Deputies say an 8-year - old Baton Rouge boy shot his 17-year - old brother as the teen was playing a video game earlier this month .

On Tuesday , East Baton Rouge Parish sheriff 's deputies arrested the adult who was supervising the child at the time of the shooting .

The Advocate reports 23-year - old Robert Payne was booked with child desertion and negligent injuring .

Sheriff 's deputies say the May 15 shooting shattered the teen 's spine and he 's paralyzed from the waist down .

Online jail records did not list an attorney for Payne .
