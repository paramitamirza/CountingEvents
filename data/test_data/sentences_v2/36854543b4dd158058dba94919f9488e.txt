Buffalo man shot while hunting in Chautauqua County
ARKWRIGHT –
A Buffalo man was shot in the left leg by a fellow deer hunter Saturday morning off Meadows Road in the Chautauqua County Town of Arkwright .

Joseph McAndrews , 33 , was airlifted to Erie County Medical Center , where he was released after treatment .

Trooper James O’Callaghan , State Police public information officer , said the incident occurred about 8:20 a.m. , when a 17-year - old hunter fired a 12-gauge shotgun at a doe and hit McAndrews instead .
The investigation remains open , but the shooting was deemed accidental , O’Callaghan said .
