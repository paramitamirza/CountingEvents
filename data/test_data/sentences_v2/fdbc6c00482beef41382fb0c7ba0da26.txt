Atlanta biker club shooting leaves 1 dead , 4 injured
Across Georgia graphic
 ATLANTA ( AP ) —
Authorities say one person has been killed and four others injured in a shooting at a southeast Atlanta biker club .

Atlanta police said in a news release that the shooting occurred early Sunday at Strikers Motorcycle Club .

The Fulton County Medical Examiner ’s Office says 44-year - old Fernando Wingfield was killed in the incident .

Police say another man underwent surgery after being shot multiple times .
Two women were listed in stable condition .
No information was released about the fifth person .

Authorities have not released further information about the shooting .
