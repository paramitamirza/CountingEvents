Police : Juvenile in trunk of SUV attempting to hide gun shoots self
A juvenile attempting to hide a gun while sitting in the trunk space of an SUV during a traffic stop shot himself Friday night , police said .

A DUI officer stopped an SUV on the 8300 block of Baymeadows Road with apparently just five people inside , including a baby , according to the Jacksonville Sheriff 's Office .

The officer smelled marijuana coming from the vehicle and he asked the driver to step out of the car .

During that conversation , the officer heard a single gunshot come from the SUV. Everyone left the vehicle at once and the officer drew his firearm .
All the passengers shouted that the passenger in the trunk shot himself , police said .

An investigation ensued and showed that a juvenile in the back of the SUV had indeed shot himself while trying to hide his gun , police said .
The juvenile was taken to UF Health in Jacksonville with serious but not life - threatening injuries .
