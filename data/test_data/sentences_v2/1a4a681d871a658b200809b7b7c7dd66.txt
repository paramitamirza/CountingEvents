Police identify suspects in Helena - West Helena drive - by shooting
Police have identified 19-year - old Dontaris Thomas and 19-year - old Hakeem Williams as suspects in Sunday 's drive - by shooting .
( Photo : Helena - West Helena Police Department )

Police have identified two suspects involved in a drive - by shooting in Helena - West Helena on Sunday .

19-year - old Dontaris Thomas and 19-year - old Hakeem Williams are accused of shooting a 7-year - old and her 33-year old father as they were walking to a relative 's home .

Both victims were taken to a hospital and treated for their injuries .

Investigators are still trying to determine why the father and daughter were targeted .

Thomas and Williams are wanted on charges of 1st - degree battery , terroristic act , and aggravated robbery .

Both are considered armed and dangerous .

If anyone has any information about the location of these suspects they are asked to call the Helena - West Helena Police Departement at ( 870)-572 - 3441 .
