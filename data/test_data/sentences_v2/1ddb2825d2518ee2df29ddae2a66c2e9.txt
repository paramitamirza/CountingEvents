17-Year - Old Man Charged With Negligent Homicide
After Accidentally Shooting Friend
 Posted on
 Baton Rouge Police have charged 17-year - old Ahmad Antoine of Baton Rouge with Negligent Homicide in the city ’s latest shooting death .

Investigators say Antoine and his friend , 17-year - old Devin Anderson , were playing with a shotgun on the front porch of Antoine ’s residence last night around 5:00pm .
Antoine said the gun accidentally went off , striking Anderson , who died at the scene .

Antoine was booked into the EBR Parish Prison .
