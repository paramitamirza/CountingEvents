An 11-year - old boy shot Monday initially thought he was hit by an errant firework while celebrating the Fourth of July in Heart of Chicago , according to police .

The boy was standing on a porch in the 2000 block of South Western Avenue at 9 p.m. Monday when he suffered a wound to his right arm that he and his family believed was from a firework set off nearby , police said .

It was n't until the boy was brought to St. Anthony Hospital that it was determined to be a gunshot wound , police said .
The boy is listed in good condition at St. Anthony .

No arrests have been made , police said .
