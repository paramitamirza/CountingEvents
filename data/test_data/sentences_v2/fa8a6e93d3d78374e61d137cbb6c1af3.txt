2 dead after shooting at Sarasota lounge - FOX 13 News
At least two people are dead after a shooting Sunday morning in Sarasota .

It happened around 1 a.m. at Kay 's Lounge on South Washington Boulevard .

Deputies say three men were found with gunshot wounds inside the bar , and they were rushed to the hospital .

Two of those men , Todd Thompson and Vincent Dipina , later died .

The third man survived .

A woman was also shot and drove herself to the hospital .

It 's still not clear who pulled the trigger .
