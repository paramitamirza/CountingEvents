2013-10-06
Four shot outside Zelmer Street party
Four men attending a birthday party at a Zelmer Street home were injured when gunmen fired at them from across the street .

Steven Wilson , Brandon Fryer , John Price and Wade Prise were on the porch of the house at 102 Zelmer when multiple gunshots were fired shortly before midnight Saturday .

The men were struck in the ankle , leg , arm and foot , and treated at Erie County Medical Center .

Police recovered 10 spent shells at the scene .
