10-year - old boy in surgery after being shot by 8-year - old relative
A 10-year - old boy is undergoing surgery after he was accidentally shot by an 8-year - old relative Thursday morning in the South Side Washington Heights neighborhood .

The 10-year - old was brought to Little Company of Mary with a gunshot wound to the side , according to Chicago Police .
He is in guarded condition and undergoing surgery , according to a source .

He was shot by an 8-year - old boy in the 9000 block of South Bishop about 10 a.m. , police said .
The boys are related , and police said the shooting appears to be accidental .
