4 Men Wounded in Shooting Outside Brooklyn House Party
Four men were shot outside a home in Brooklyn after leaving a party early Sunday morning , police said .

According to authorities , the men were leaving the party at around 1:30 a.m. when shots rang out near the corner of East 48th Street and Church Avenue in East Flatbush .
All four men were shot in the torso .

The victims , who range in age from 19 to 34 , were rushed to nearby hospitals where they are expected to recover from their injuries , officials said .

Police believe it is possible two gunmen were involved in the shooting .
No arrests have been made and there is no word yet on sparked the violence .
