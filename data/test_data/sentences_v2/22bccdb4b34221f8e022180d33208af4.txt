2016-08-05
2 men sought in shooting of 10-year - old girl in North Philadelphia
Police have identified two men being sought in connection with a shooting that left a 10-year - old girl injured in North Philadelphia .
Arrest warrants have been issued for 23-year - old Richard Holmes and 29-year - old Stephen Kearney .
The incident happened on July 28 on the 3100 block of North Rosewood Street .
Police say the girl was shot once in the arm after walking between Holmes and Kearney , who were engaged in an argument that turned violent when one of the suspects fired shots at the other .
After being struck , the young girl screamed to her grandmother , who called police .
The victim was taken to Temple University Hospital by police , and then transferred to St. Christopher 's Hospital for Children where she was treated and released .
Police said a third male was observed firing a gun from a second - floor window of a residence on the block .
Both shooters were apparently firing at the unarmed man involved in the argument .
Anyone with information on the whereabouts of Richard Holmes and Stephen Kearney is asked to please contact Northwest Detective Division at 215 - 686 - 3353 .
