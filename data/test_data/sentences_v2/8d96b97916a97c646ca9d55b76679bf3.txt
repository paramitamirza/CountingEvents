17-year - old shot in hand in central Toledo
Published : Wednesday , 10/7/2015
 17-year - old shot in hand in central Toledo
 A 17-year - old boy was shot in the hand in central Toledo late Tuesday by someone who fled , authorities said .

The name of the victim was not released .

The shooting occurred about 10:45 p.m. in the 1200 block of Buckingham Street , according to Toledo police .
The motive was not released .

There was no word of arrests .
The incident is being investigated as a felonious assault .
