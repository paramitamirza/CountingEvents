Authorities : 2 dead , 3 injured in Newark shooting
Two more people have been fatally shot in Newark .

City police and the Essex County prosecutor ’s office say three people were also wounded in the shooting , which occurred around 11:30 a.m. Tuesday on Springfield Avenue in the city ’s West Ward .

No details were immediately available on a motive behind the shootings , and further details on the injured people were not disclosed .
