2016-07-11
A 13-month old child injured in shooting
A 13-month - old boy and 20-year - old man were both shot overnight in Wichita .

Sergeant Brian Goward with the Wichita Police Department say both injured have non life - threatening injuries .

The victims were inside a vehicle when someone from outside started shooting according to police .
Goward says the shots came from outside the vehicle , but most likely not from someone driving by .

The shooting happened near the intersection of Harry and Volutsia .

The child 's mother took him to a nearby Quik Trip to get help .
The man was able to get himself to the hospital .

No arrests have been made .
Goward says they are still trying to talk to the victims to figure out what happened .
