2015-05-03
Panama City Police Investigate Shooting
Around 10:30 Sunday morning , the Panama City Police Department received a 911 call reporting a child had been accidentally shot .

Patrol officers responded to Andrews Place Apartments where they found that a 7-year - old female had been struck in the head by a bullet , fired by her father from a handgun .

The child was taken to Bay Medical Center where she later died .
It appears the shooting was accidental .

Panama City Police officials continue to investigate the incident .

Anyone having information in this case is urged to call the Panama City Police Department , 850 - 872 - 3100 , or they can report their tips anonymously to CrimeStoppers at 850 - 785-TIPS.
