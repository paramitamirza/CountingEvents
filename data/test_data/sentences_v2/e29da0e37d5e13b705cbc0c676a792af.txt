The 5-year - old girl who was shot by her brother Thursday night is expected to make a full recovery .

According to Rankin County Sheriff Bryan Bailey , the child was in critical condition last night , but is doing much better .

Authorities said her 9-year - old brother accidentally shot his sister with a .22 caliber firearm .
The shooting happened on Perez Cove , which is near the Simpson County line .

After the shooting , the girl was airlifted to the University of Mississippi Medical Center for treatment .
