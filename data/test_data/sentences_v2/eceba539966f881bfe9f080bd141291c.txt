Boy wounded in accidental shooting
A 16-year - old Rocky Mount boy was wounded Sunday when the handgun he was reportedly handling accidentally discharged .

Terrence Adrian Battle Jr. received non - life threatening injuries and was transported to Nash UNC Health Care after the incident that occurred at about 6:20 p.m. Sunday on the 400 block of Ambler Avenue .

Rocky Mount police Cpl .
Mike Lewis said no charges have been filed in connection with the incident so far but the matter is still under investigation .
Lewis also said the event serves as a reminder of the importance of gun safety .

“
Citizens need to careful to obey laws about the use and storage of guns , ”
Lewis said .
“
They should also always be conscious of safety when handling guns . ”
