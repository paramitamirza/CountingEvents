11-year - old boy shot in east Houston
HOUSTON - A young boy was shot and wounded at a home in east Houston , police said .

It happened in the 300 block of De Haven around 9:25 p.m. Sunday .

Police at the scene said the boy , 11 , appeared to be the victim of an accidental shooting .
He was rushed to the hospital where he was in critical condition at last check .

Police spoke to several witnesses at the home .
No charges have been filed at this time as the investigation continues .

The child 's identity has not been released .
