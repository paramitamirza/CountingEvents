A 16-year - old boy who was critically wounded in a shooting in North Lawndale on Friday died on Saturday , officials said .

At 6:13 p.m. , the boy was inside a home with two people in the 1500 block of South Kedzie Avenue when a gunshot was heard and the teen was hit , police said .

The teen was shot in his face and was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital in critical condition , police said .

The boy was identified by the Cook County Medical Examiner 's Officer as Christopher Brandon - Lucket .
Brandon - Lucket , of the 1800 block of North Fairfield Avenue , was pronounced dead at 2:19 a.m. on Saturday .

No one was in custody .
