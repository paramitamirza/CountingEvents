2016-12-03
Gunmen open fire in Bloomfield , New Jersey bar ; 5 injured
Authorities say five people were shot after gunmen opened fire on a bar in New Jersey .
Bloomfield police Director Sam DeMaio told NJ.com that three guns were used in the shooting outside of the Sports in the City Food & Lounge bar late Friday night .
DeMaio says the five people were being treated at University Hospital in Newark for injuries that were not life threatening .
The gun fire shattered the glass front door and side window of the bar .
About 30 people were inside for a party when the shooting occurred .
One gun was found at the scene and police were interviewing witnesses .
No arrests had been made as of Saturday morning .
