Juvenile arrested in Auburn shooting death of teen
AUBURN , Wash. -- Auburn police have made an arrest in the shooting death Friday of a 17-year - old .

Police describe the suspect only as a male juvenile .
Police say the pistol that was likely used in the shooting has also been found .

The 17-year - old was killed in what police say may have been a gang - related shooting Friday morning .
A shooter approached the victim as he sat in his car on 25th Street Southeast and fired several times .

The suspect is being booked into juvenile detention .

Police are still seeking more information and ask you to call 253 - 288 - 7403 if can help the investigation .
