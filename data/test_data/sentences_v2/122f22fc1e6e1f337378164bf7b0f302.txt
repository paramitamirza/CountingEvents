A 2-year - old boy was accidentally shot in his leg early Tuesday morning , police said .

Around 1:45 a.m. , the boy was at his home in the 7000 block of South Harper Avenue when an " accidental discharge " grazed his leg , according to Officer Jose Estrada , a Chicago Police spokesman .

The boy was taken to Comer Children 's Hospital , where he was listed in good condition , Estrada said .

Police said they 're questioning a member of the boy 's family , but no charges have been filed yet .
