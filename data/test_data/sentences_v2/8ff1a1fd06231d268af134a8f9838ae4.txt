State Police investigate accidental shooting involving teen in Windham
× State Police investigate accidental shooting involving teen in Windham
 WINDHAM– State Police are investigating what they say appears to be an accidental shooting that happened Tuesday night .

Detectives responded around 10:15 p.m. to 64 Windham Center Rd .
in Windham .
State police said the investigation determined that the victim was a 17-year - old boy and the shooting was accidental and self inflicted .
However , the shooting is still being investigated and the official cause hasn’t yet been determined .

The gunshot wound was to the head , and the victim , identified as Wesley Rodriguez , remains in critical condition .
