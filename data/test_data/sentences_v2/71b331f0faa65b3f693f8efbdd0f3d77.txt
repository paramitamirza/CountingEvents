NEWLINE Teenager Shot
 In Lake Stevens
 August 13 , 2016
 ( LAKE STEVENS , WA. ) -- The Snohomish County Sheriff 's office is investigating the shooting of a teenager in Lake Stevens Friday night .

A report by KIRO - TV in Seattle said the shooting was accidental , according to deputies and took place at a family event in the 13600 block of 12th Street Northeast .

The teen reportedly shot himself by accident in the head although details of the accident were not immediately available .
The station 's report said no crime was involved , according to deputies .
Condition of the teen was not known
