Several Wounded in Shooting Near South Los Angeles
Four people were wounded during a shooting near South Los Angeles on Tuesday , July 5 , 2016 .

At least four people were injured in a shooting near South Los Angeles early Tuesday morning , police said .

The shooting was reported just before 1 a.m. near 56th Street and Naomi Avenue , according to the Los Angeles Police Department .
