Shooting in Brooklyn park leaves five teens injured
A Brooklyn park turned bloody Monday afternoon when bullets started flying , striking five teenage boys , police sources said .

The victims were inside Harry Maze Park on East 56th Street in East Flatbush when someone took a bag from a teen there and he called his friend , who came over and started firing just before 5 p.m. , sources said .

The victims — who are all expected to survive – are between 15 and 17 years old .
They were taken to Kings County Hospital .

The shooter , who is believed to be between 17 to 20 years old , fled on foot .

He was last seen wearing a red shirt , black jeans and carrying a bookbag with the letters “ MCM ” on it , cops said .
