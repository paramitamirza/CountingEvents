4 shot outside Daytona Beach bar , cops say
DAYTONA BEACH -
 Four people were shot outside a bar in Daytona Beach after a man got into a fight then left and came back with a gun .

Daytona Beach police said that the incident occurred around 2:30 a.m. Saturday at the Baritz Club in the 300 block of Pearl Street .

The suspect was described as a black man with light skin in his 20s who is 4 feet 5 inches tall , has short spiked hair and wore a white tan top and shorts , police said .

The suspect was involved in a fight about 30 minutes prior to his return with a gun where he shot at the people still in the parking lot .
The victims were innocent bystanders and were not involved in the fight earlier .

Police said all the victims were taken to Halifax Medical Center .
One victim was a 36-year - old man who was shot in the left leg and stomach .
A 28-year - old woman was shot in the left leg .
A 43-year - old man was hit by a bullet on his left wrist area and a 35-year - old man suffered a shot to the abdomen ; his injuries appeared to be life - threatening .

The shooter remains at large .
