Police : Man shoots child , 3 , riding in car in Memphis
 by Associated Press
 MEMPHIS , Tenn. ( AP ) –
Police say a 3-year - old girl was shot multiple times while riding in a car in Memphis .

Police said in a statement that a woman reported being flagged down Sunday night by a man while driving through an apartment complex .
She told officers that the man began to flirt with her , and when she rejected his advances , he pulled out a handgun and began firing into her vehicle .

A child in the car was hit , and police spokesman Louis Brownlee said that the girl remained in extremely critical condition Monday morning .

Police say there were multiple bullet holes in the car windows on the passenger ’s side .

The investigation is continuing .
No arrests have been made .
