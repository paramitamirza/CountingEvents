Teen shot in bedroom of East Knoxville home
KNOXVILLE ( WATE ) –
A teen is recovering at University of Tennessee Medical Center after he was shot in the upper leg .

Knoxville Police Department said the shooting happened at 12:48 a.m. Sunday morning at a home in the 400 block of South Hembree Street , nar Austin East Magnet High School .
Officers said a 15-year - old boy was in his bedroom , with some friends , when he heard a loud bang and felt a sharp pain .

The teen ’s injuries appear to be non - life threatening , according to police .
During an investigation , officers could not find a weapon and could not determine whether the shooting was intentional .
