Teen accidentally shoots and kills friend in LaSalle , sheriff ’s office says
Weld County Sheriff ’s officers responded early Saturday morning after a 17-year - old teen reported he accidentally shot a friend .

A 23-year - old man was declared dead at a residence in the 18000 block of County Road 43 in LaSalle .
The sheriff ’s office will release his name once his family has been informed and they’ve completed their investigation .

The teen has been taken into custody and faces a charge of criminally negligent homicide .
The sheriff ’s office said it would not release the name of the teenager because of his age .
