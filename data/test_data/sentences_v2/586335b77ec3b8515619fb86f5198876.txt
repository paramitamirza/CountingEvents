Father charged after 2-year - old Toledo girl dies from gunshot wound
Police have charged a Toledo man with child endangering after his 2-year - old stepdaughter died from a gunshot wound .

Journi Johnson was pronounced dead at St. Charles Hospital this morning after being shot at least once , Toledo police said .

Her stepfather , Turhan Johnson , 27 , was arrested after he admitted to police that he left his 38-caliber handgun unsecured in his home in the 2400 block of Seaman Street , according to court documents .

An autopsy for the child is scheduled for Monday , Lucas County Coroner James Patrick said .

Mr. Johnson also is scheduled to appear in court Monday morning .
