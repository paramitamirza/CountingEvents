Body found on Cricket Drive in Dorchester County
Officials confirm a body has been found on Cricket Drive in Dorchester County .

Few details are available at this time , however officials have said foul play is not suspected .

DNR was called to the scene to investigate the incident they describe as a " hunting accident . "

Officials have confirmed the body found is that of a 36-year - old hunter reported missing early Monday morning .

We 're still awaiting confirmation from the Charleston County Coroner 's Office .
