Life Flight called after children shot in Humble
Four juveniles -- including two children -- were injured by gunfire in Humble on Monday evening after a neighborhood fight broke out between more than a dozen teens .
Eyewitness News has confirmed that 11-year - old Oscar Cerda and his six - year - old brother Daniel were outside playing soccer with friends when a fight broke out .
They were hit by stray bullets and transported to Texas Children 's Hospital , where Daniel underwent surgery surgery .
Investigators say the incident unfolded just after 6:10pm .
They believe all the kids involved in the fight live around the neighborhood .
A 15-year - old was also shot in the foot during the altercation , one of several who left the scene and came back .
A 16-year - old was also grazed in the back .
Neighbors say tension had been brewing between local kids over the past few weeks , and gunshots had rung out before , although nobody had been hit .
Nine people have been detained for questioning .
