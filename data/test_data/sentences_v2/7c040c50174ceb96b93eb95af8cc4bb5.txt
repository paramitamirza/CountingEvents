 Boy , 12 , shot himself in West Price Hill home
 WEST PRICE HILL , Ohio ( WKRC ) -- Police were called a house on Virgil Road in West Price Hill late Sunday night .

That 's where they found a 12-year - old boy who shot himself while playing with a gun .

He was shot in the finger and his side .

The boy was taken to Children 's Hospital and no charges have been filed at this time .
