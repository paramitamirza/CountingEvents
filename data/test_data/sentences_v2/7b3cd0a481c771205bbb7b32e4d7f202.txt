San Francisco : 5-year - old girl hit by stray bullet
A 5-year - old girl was struck in the right hand by an apparent stray bullet Wednesday evening in San Francisco 's Bayview District , police said Thursday .

The shooting was reported at 5:13 p.m. in the area of Whitney Young Circle and Hudson Avenue , according to police .

The girl was taken to a hospital to be treated for her injuries , which were not life - threatening .

Police are looking for three men in their 20s in connection with the shooting , but no arrests had been reported as of Thursday morning .
