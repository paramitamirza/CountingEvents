Deputies : Boy , 8 , accidentally shot 10-month - old with BB gun
A 10-month - old was shot by his brother in DeBary on Thursday night , according to the Volusia County Sheriff 's Office .

Deputies said the 8-year - old boy got a hold of the BB gun and accidentally shot his younger brother at a house on Plumosa Road .

The baby was taken to Florida Hospital then to Arnold Palmer Hospital for Children as a precaution , investigators said .

Deputies said the injuries are not believed to be serious .
