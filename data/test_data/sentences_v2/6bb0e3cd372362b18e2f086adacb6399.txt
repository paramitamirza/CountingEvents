Teen fatally shoots himself in the head by accident
HOUSTON-
A young man died after accidentally shooting himself in the head in northwest Houston on Friday night .

According to police , the incident occurred around 10:30 p.m. at an apartment complex at Long Point Road and Oak Tree Drive .

Police said the victim was playing with a gun inside the apartment with several other friends .
The gun went off and the victim accidental shot himself in the head .

He died inside the apartment .
