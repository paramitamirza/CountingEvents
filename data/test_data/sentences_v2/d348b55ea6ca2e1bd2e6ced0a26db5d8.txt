5-Year - Old Boy Accidentally Shot In Face
November 29 , 2014 10:10 PM
 Follow CBSDFW.COM : Facebook | Twitter
 NAVARRO COUNTY , Texas –
Police are on the scene of a shooting that sent a 5-year - old boy to the hospital .

It happened in rice earlier this evening .
Authorities said a sibling , also a child , shot the boy in the face with a 22-caliber rifle .

He was careflighted to a Dallas - area hospital his condition is unknown .
Authorities do not expect to file any charges .

Investigators are in the early stages of the investigation and are working to determine all of the unknowns ; where the gun was stored , was it accessible to the children , were their parents at home .
