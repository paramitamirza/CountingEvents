Police : 5-Year - Old Apparently Shot by 4-Year - Old Sibling
ST. LOUIS ( KMOX ) –
It appears the 5-year - old boy shot in St. Louis on Saturday morning was wounded by a 4-year - old sibling .

Investigators say when they first got to the 2800 block of Stoddard in the Jeff - Vander - Lou neighborhood shortly after 7 a.m. , the children ’s mother and her boyfriend told them the child had been hit by a stray bullet while playing outside .

However , detectives say , further investigation indicates that was not the case .

The 5-year - old is in stable condition with a chest wound .
