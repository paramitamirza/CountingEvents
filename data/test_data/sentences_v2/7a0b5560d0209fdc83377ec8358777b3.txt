4 Struck in Port Norris Shooting
A woman and three men are recovering after a shooting in Port Norris , NJ.
 Police say the victims were with a large crowd on the 1500 block of North Avenue shortly before 1 a.m. when a gunman opened fire .

One of the victims was flown to a hospital in Atlantic City while the other three victims drove themselves to hospitals in Vineland .
All four victims suffered non - life threatening injuries .

No arrests have been made .
