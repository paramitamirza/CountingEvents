× 17-year - old charged with manslaughter in fatal shooting of 13-year - old boy on JBLM property
 TACOMA —
A 17-year - old has been formally charged with second - degree manslaughter and unlawful possession of a firearm in the shooting death of a 13-year - old boy in a wooded area of Joint Base Lewis - McChord in October .

Police have said they believe the shooting was accidental .

The 17-year - old , Adonis Brown , was formally charged in Pierce County Superior Court on Monday .
He is currently scheduled for a jury trial on Jan. 27 .

The 13-year - old was shot in the face on Oct. 20 after he , his 14-year - old brother and the 17-year - old had sneaked through a fence into a wooded area of JBLM with a gun .
The 17-year - old was handling the gun when it discharged , police said .
