4 People Injured In Zanesville Bar Shooting
Police say four people were injured in a shooting in a Zanesville bar Sunday morning .

The shooting occurred at the Hoppy 's bar on Putnam Avenue .

Three of the victims were transported to Grant Medical Center via medical helicopter .
Another person was treated at Genesis Hospital .

The victims all appear to be in their 20s , according to police .

Genesis Hospital reported several fights broke out at the medical center following the arrival of the shooting victim .

Several people were arrested at the hospital .

The condition and names of the victims have not been released .
