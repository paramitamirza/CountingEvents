Cowley County teen hurt in accidental shooting
A Cowley County teen was hurt in an accidental shooting over the weekend .

The sheriff 's office says around 2 p.m. on Sunday , deputies were dispatched to the 22000 block of 12th Road .

There they found a 15-year - old with a gunshot wound to the thigh .
They say the teen was cleaning his dad 's revolver when it discharged and stuck him in the top of his right thigh .

The teen was flown to St. Francis Hospital in Wichita for treatment .

NewsCow ( http://bit.ly/2ec7Gy9 ) says he is currently listed in good condition .
