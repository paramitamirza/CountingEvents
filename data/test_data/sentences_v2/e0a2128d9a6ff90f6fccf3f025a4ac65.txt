Arrest made in birthday party shooting
 DYERSBURG , Tenn. —
The Dyersburg Police have a suspect in custody in connection to the fatal Thursday night shooting at a 2-year - old ’s birthday party in Dyersburg .

According to law enforcement officials , 30-year - old Torius Russell was arrested Friday morning in connection to the shooting that happened a little before 6:3o p.m. Thursday night .

Seven people were injured and at least one person has died after the shooting .
A mother of two , 21-year - old Shanice Amerson , died at the scene .

The 6-year - old who was injured and flown to a Memphis hospital has since gone into surgery , but further details on the child ’s condition has not been made available .

The Dyersburg Police Department is holding a press conference at 11:00 a.m. concerning developments in this case .
