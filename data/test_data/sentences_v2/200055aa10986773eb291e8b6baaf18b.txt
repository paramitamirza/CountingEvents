Boy , 7 , shot while playing with friends in Aurora home
AURORA , Colo.
-
 A 7-year - old boy is in critical condition after being shot in the head as he played with friends in an Aurora home .

The boy was shot Thursday afternoon when six children ages 7 to 12 were alone in the home .
Aurora Police spokeswoman Judy Lutkin says investigators do n't know how the children got a gun or who fired it .

Lutkin says that an adult neighbor called 911 .
She says it 's too soon to say whether any criminal charges would be filed in the case .

The wounded boy did not live in the home .
He is in serious condition at Children 's Hospital Colorado .
