 OAKLAND ( CBS SF ) —
Four people including a 12-year - old and an 8-year - old were injured in a shooting Sunday afternoon on the 1300 block of 104th Avenue , Police spokeswoman Officer Johnna Watson said .

Officers responding to the 2:21 p.m. shooting found one male adult , one female adult and an 8-year - old and a 12-year - old at the scene , all suffering non - fatal gunshot wounds , Watson said .

All four have been taken to local hospitals for treatment .

No suspects are in custody at this time , Watson said .
