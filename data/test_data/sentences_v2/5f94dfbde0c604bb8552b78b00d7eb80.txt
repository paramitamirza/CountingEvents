A two - year - old boy was shot in the head Tuesday afternoon at a Madison apartment complex .

It happened around 3:05 p.m. in the 300 block of Rio Vista Drive at the Charter Village Apartments .

The child , identified as Kendall Pinkerton , was taken to Vanderbilt University Medical Center in critical condition .

Metro police are questioning the people who were inside the apartment to find out more about the circumstances .

No charges have been filed at this time .
