Youngstown police identify victims of suspected murder - suicide
YOUNGSTOWN , Ohio ( WKBN ) –
Police have identified the victims of what appears to be a murder - suicide in Youngstown over the weekend .

Police said 43-year - old Bianca Arter and her son , 17-year - old Javeer Arter were found dead in their home in the 2100 block of Volney Road on Saturday .

Police are investigating and haven’t said who was responsible for the deaths .

This is a developing story .
