Affidavit : Constable 's wife shot him after alleged affair - KLTV.com - Tyler , Longview , Jacksonville |ETX News
Officials have released an arrest warrant for a woman accused of shooting a Smith County constable .

Meraland Taylor Jackson , 66 , is charged with two counts of aggravated assault with a deadly weapon , family violence after police say she shot her husband , Pct . 1 Constable Henry Jackson .

According to the affidavit , obtained Monday , Meraland Jackson told police she shot her husband because she believed he was having an affair with a coworker .
 "
Jackson continued to make ... statements that she intentionally shot her husband , Henry Jackson , and that he should be dead , and that she accidentally shot her grand - daughter , " the documents read .

The affidavit states that during an interview with police , Jackson continued to state that she shot her husband intentionally .
She also told deputies that her husband of more than 40 years had recently been out of town for a work - related conference and she had learned that his alleged mistress had attended the conference as well .

The shooting occurred about 7:30 p.m. Saturday in the 3200 block of Chandler Highway .

Law enforcement officials say Henry Jackson was shot one time .
The Jacksons ' 5-year - old granddaughter was also shot in the ankle .
They were both transported to ETMC by ambulance .
Hospital officials listed the constable in fair condition at this time .
His granddaughter was treated at the hospital Saturday and released on Sunday .

Meraland Jackson was taken to the Smith County Jail .
Her bond was set at $ 500,000 and was released Sunday evening after posting bond .
