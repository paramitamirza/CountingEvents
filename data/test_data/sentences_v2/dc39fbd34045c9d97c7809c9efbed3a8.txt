The Homicide Report
Kenneth Mills , a 22-year - old black man , was shot and killed May 23 in the 9500 block of Flower Street in Bellflower , according to Los Angeles County coroner ’s records .

About 7:45 p.m. , three black men entered a barber shop with hoodies over their heads and opened fire without saying anything , said Det .
David Gunner of the Los Angeles County Sheriff ’s Department .

Four people were injured in the shooting .
Mills , the manager of the shop , was taken to a hospital where he was pronounced dead at 8:32 p.m. , according to coroner ’s records .

German Booker , a 25-year - old customer at the shop , was also killed .

An 18-year - old man and a 30-year - old man who were wounded were expected to survive .

The barber shop was open for business at the time , but witnesses fled the scene .
Gunner declined to state the motive for the shooting , but said that it was not a robbery .

Anyone with information can call the Sheriff ’s Department Homicide Bureau at ( 323 ) 890 - 5500 .
Those who wish to remain anonymous can call Crime Stoppers at ( 800 ) 222 - 8477 .
