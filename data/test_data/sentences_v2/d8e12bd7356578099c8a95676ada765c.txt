2016-08-29
Child wounded in shooting in northwest Las Vegas
A child was hospitalized after a shooting Sunday night in the northwest valley , Las Vegas police said .

Just before 11:30 p.m. , officers responded to the accidental discharge of a firearm inside a home in the 4800 block of White Aspen Avenue , near West Ann Road and North Decatur Boulevard , Metropolitan Police Department spokeswoman Laura Meltzer said .

The preteen child was taken to University Medical Center with nonlife - threatening injuries .
Abuse and neglect detectives are investigating the incident , Meltzer said .
