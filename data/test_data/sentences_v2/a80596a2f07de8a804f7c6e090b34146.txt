Man charged in fatal shooting of Rock Hill teen
Robreece Omar Pickett CREDIT : Rock Hill Police
 ROCK HILL , S.C. – Police in Rock Hill have charged a 20-year - old man in connection with the fatal shooting of a teenager Saturday night .

Just before 10 o’clock , officers were dispatched to a shooting in the 800 block of Horseman Drive in Rock Hill .
When officers arrived on the scene , they found a 17-year - old male suffering from an apparent gunshot wound .
The victim , later identified as Jaterreon Thorne was pronounced dead on the scene .

Investigators determined that the shooting was not a homicide and was accidental .
According to Rock Hill Police , 20-year - old Robreece Pickett was handling a handgun when it went off , shooting the victim .

Pickett has been charged with involuntary manslaughter and unlawful carry of a weapon .
He is currently being held under a $ 115,000 bond .
