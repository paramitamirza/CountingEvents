Naples police : Teen shot in head at Coastland Mall
NAPLES , Fla. - NAPLES , Fla. -- One person is in the hospital after being shot in the head at a Naples mall .

It happened Monday inside the parking garage at the Coastland Mall in Naples .
Police say the victim is under the age of 18 .
but have not released how old he is .

He was with 4 other adults and teenagers at the time of the shooting , but police are figuring out if the shot was self - inflicted or accidental .

Police also emphasize this is an isolated incident and no one in the community is at risk .

UPDATE ( 8/16 ) :
Police say the teen has died from his wounds
