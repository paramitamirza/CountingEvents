Body found in Noble Twp , investigation underway
by Ken Delaney
 NOBLE TOWNSHIP , BRANCH CO , MI ( WTVB ) -
The discovery of a body Saturday night in a field in southwestern Branch County near the Michigan - Indiana line is under investigation .

Medical first responders and the Bronson Fire Department were dispatched at about 6:30 p.m. to a field off of Gunthorp Road in Noble Township .

Authorities are releasing no further information .
A spokesman for the Michigan State Police would only confirm they are investigating the incident .

The Michigan Department of Natural Resources and Life Care Ambulance were also at the scene Saturday .
The person was deceased by the time rescue crews arrived .
