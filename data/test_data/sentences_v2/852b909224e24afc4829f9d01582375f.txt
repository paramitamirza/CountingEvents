Police said a relative shot a one - year - old child Friday night in Northeast Dallas .

According to DPD spokesperson Chinh Weltman , officers responded to a shooting call in the 11500 block of Ferguson Road at 7:15 p.m.

The victim , a one - year - old girl , suffered a gunshot wound and was rushed to a hospital for surgery .
She was reported in critical but stable condition on Saturday morning .

Elliott Stricklin , 34 , was arrested and jailed for reckless injury to a child .
Bond was set at $ 25,000 .

Weltman said police were interviewing everyone who was at the location at the time of the gunfire .
