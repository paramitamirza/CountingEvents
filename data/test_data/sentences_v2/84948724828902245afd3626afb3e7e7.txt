Teen Injured in Avery St. Shooting
SAVANNAH , Ga. ( WJCL )
–
Savannah - Chatham Metropolitan Police are investigating a Friday night shooting that injured a 16-year - old male .

It happened at Fred Wessels Homes on the 200 block of Avery Street , just after 6 p.m.

The teen was reportedly shot on Randolph Street by an unidentified vehicle , but police say they have no evidence supporting this claim .
Preliminary investigation reveals the victim was with a group of teenagers when one of the victims accidentally discharged his gun .

Detectives are following leads to confirm the shooter ’s identity .

Anyone with information should call CrimeStoppers at ( 912 ) 234 - 2020 .
