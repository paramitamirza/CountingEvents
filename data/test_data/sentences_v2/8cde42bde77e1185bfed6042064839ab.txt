21-year - old Amish man accidentally shot by cousin in Indiana County
A coroner says a 21-year - old man was accidentally shot by his 12-year - old cousin in an Amish community in western Pennsylvania .

Indiana County Coroner Jerry Overman Jr. says the victim is Allen Troyer , of Smicksburg .

The coroner says the 12-year - old - who is n't being identified - was holding a black powder revolver when Troyer grabbed the barrel of the gun .
That caused it to fire , mortally wounding Troyer in the chest .

State police in Punxsutawney are investigating but , so far , authorities say they 're not sign of an argument or any foul play .

Overman has ruled the shooting an accident .

The shooting happened just after 9:30 p.m. Thursday .

Smicksburg is about 60 miles northeast of Pittsburgh .
