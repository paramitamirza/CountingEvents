3 men charged in NC Music Factory shootings
L to R :
Chris Ardrey , Cario Davis , and Jason Farmer have been charged following a shooting at the NC Music Factory .
( Photo : Charlotte - Mecklenburg Police )

CHARLOTTE , N.C. --
Charlotte - Mecklenburg Police have charged three men with their involvement in a shooting at the NC Music Factory on Friday night .

Chris Ardrey , 19 , and Cario Davis , 18 , were taken to police headquarters Friday after their vehicle was spotted leaving the scene .

Davis has been charged with assault with a deadly weapon with intent to kill inflicting serious injury , armed robbery , attempted armed robbery , conspiracy to commit armed robbery , possession of a firearm by a convicted felon , possession of a stolen firearm , and altering or removing the serial number from a firearm .

Ardrey has been charged with armed robbery , attempted armed robbery , conspiracy to commit armed robbery , possession of a firearm by a convicted felon , possession of a stolen firearm , and altering or removing the serial number from a firearm .

Ardrey and Davis have both been transferred into the custody of the Mecklenburg County Sheriff 's Office .

CMPD detectives have also identified a third suspect in the case .
Jason Farmer , 19 , sustained a gunshot wound and was transported to Carolinas Medical Center with serious injuries .
Authorities have obtained warrants for his arrest and will serve them once Farmer is released from the hospital .

Farmer has also been charged with armed robbery , attempted armed robbery , conspiracy to commit armed robbery and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon .

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 704 - 334 - 1600 .
