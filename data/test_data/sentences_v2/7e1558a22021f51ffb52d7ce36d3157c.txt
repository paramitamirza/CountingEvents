2015-08-16
Teen Charged With Accidental Shooting That Killed 14-Year - Old Girl On West Side
( STMW ) — A teenage boy has been charged as a juvenile after a 14-year - old girl was shot to death while they were playing with a handgun Wednesday afternoon on the West Side , police said .

The 17-year - old boy faces a count of involuntary manslaughter for the shooting that happened about 2:15 p.m. inside a home in the 700 block of North Homan , according to Chicago Police .
His name was withheld because he is a minor .

He and the girl , Tracy Davis , had been playing with the gun when it went off and struck her in the head , according to police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office .

Davis , of the 4400 block of West Adams , was dead at the scene .
Detectives recovered the weapon , police said .

The boy ran way after the shooting but later made arrangements to turn himself in , police said .

He is scheduled to appear in bond court Monday .
