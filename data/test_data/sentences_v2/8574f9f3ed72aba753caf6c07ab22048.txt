2016-04-10
4 shot , taken to hospital in South Los Angeles
Four men were shot in South Los Angeles on Sunday , according to the Los Angeles Police Department .
Police said the four victims were approached by four men near 84th Street and Towne Avenue at about 9:45 a.m. when multiple shots were fired .
The four victims , all in their 20s , we taken to the hospital .
One victim was listed in critical condition and the three others were listed in stable condition .
Police said the suspects remain at large and that their investigation into the shooting is ongoing .
