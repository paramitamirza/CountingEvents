6th Street shooting suspect booked into Travis County Jail
Endicott McCray was booked into the Travis County Jail on Aug. 18 , 2016 ( Travis County Jail Photo )

AUSTIN ( KXAN ) —
At 7:49 p.m. Thursday , Endicott McCray was booked into the Travis County Jail .

McCray , 24 , is accused of fatally shooting a woman in the head on East Sixth Street in the early morning hours of July 31 .
Thirty - year - old Teqnika Marie Moultrie was walking down the crowded sidewalk with her wife and friends , after leaving Voodoo Doughnuts at 212 E. Sixth , when McCray opened fire .

He was captured outside of Atlanta , Georgia on Aug. 3 after being spotted at a bus station by a U.S. Marshals Task Force member .
The marshal first noticed McCray because he was wearing a raincoat when it wasn’t raining .

Police say the incident began when McCray got into a fight with his brother - in - law and took out his gun , aiming it at the man .
McCray fired one shot into the ground and then raised his gun , pointing it sideways , and firing up to four more shots .
Moultrie , considered an innocent bystander by police , died at the scene .
Four others were injured in the shooting .

The four were taken to University Medical Center Brackenridge with life - threatening and non - life threatening injuries to the chest , thigh , ankle and buttock .
One of the injured victims was part of a bachelorette party on Sixth Street that night .

McCray ’s bond is set at $ 2 million .
