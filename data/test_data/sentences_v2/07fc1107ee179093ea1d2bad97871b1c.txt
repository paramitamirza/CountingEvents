Teen shot in Miami dies , two others wounded
A 16-year - old boy , one of three people hit by gunfire in Miami Tuesday night has died , Miami police announced Wednesday .

Police say Damien Ramirez was mortally wounded about 8:30 p.m. Tuesday when he and a friend were walking near Northwest 17th Avenue and 31st Street .
The two were confronted by two other males , who may be juveniles , who opened fire , police said .

A third person , a bystander , was struck in the arm .

The friend with Ramirez remains hospitalized , police said .

The reason for the shooting is not known and the investigation is continuing .
