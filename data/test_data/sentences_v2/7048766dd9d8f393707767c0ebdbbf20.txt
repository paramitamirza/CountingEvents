Father accidentally shoots son in northeast Columbus
COLUMBUS ( WCMH ) –
Police say a teen was accidentally shot by his own father Tuesday morning .

It happened just after 6 a.m. on Skywae Drive in northeast Columbus .

According to Columbus police , a teen was taken to an area hospital in stable condition after he was unintentionally shot in the leg .

Police said Tuesday that the teen ’s father , Biyaga Ceesay , originally told police that a stranger shot his son , but later said he thought the teen was an intruder and shot him .

Ceesay was transported to Franklin County jail and charged with misdemeanor charges of domestic violence and assault .
He will be arraigned Wednesday morning .

Assault squad detectives are handling the investigation .
