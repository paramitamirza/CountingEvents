2016-01-30
17-year - old killed in accidental shooting
- The mother of 17 year old teenager Roderick Holder told FOX 5 that her son was shot and killed early Saturday morning .
The mother told FOX 5 that she does not think that this was an accidental shooting .

DeKalb County police believe it was accidental .

Lt .
Shane Sanfield says four teens were handling a gun at an apartment complex on Meadowglen Village Lane , when the gun went off .
The bullet struck one teen .
He was rushed to Grady Memorial Hospital where he was pronounced dead .

Police questioned the other teens who are between the ages of 13 and 17 years old .
Police charged one of them with involuntary manslaughter .
