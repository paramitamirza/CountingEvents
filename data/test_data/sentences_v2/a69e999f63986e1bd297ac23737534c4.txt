Teen charged with involuntary manslaughter in shooting death of - WPSD Local 6 : Your news , weather , and sports authority
The Scott County , Missouri , Sheriff 's Department says an 18 year old has been charged with involuntary manslaughter in the death of another teen .

The sheriff 's department responded to a call reporting an accidental shooting at 5 p.m. on Thursday at a home on Bugg Street in Vanduser , Missouri .
Scott County Sheriff Rick Walter tells us Cole Kirkpatrick died after he was shot in the head with a 20 gauge shotgun .

Walter says Sean Thomas of Vanduser was arrested and charged with armed criminal action and involuntary manslaughter .
He is in jail , and bond has been set at $ 5,000 cash .
The sheriff says they do n't yet know what led to the shooting .

Walter tells us the teens were friends for years , and the investigation into the shooting is ongoing .
Kirkpatrick was a senior at Scott County Central High School .

The district closed all schools Friday at noon after learning about Kirkpatrick 's death .
The superintendent says he 's not yet sure whether schools will remain closed on Monday .
He says that will depend on funeral arrangements for Kirkpatrick .
