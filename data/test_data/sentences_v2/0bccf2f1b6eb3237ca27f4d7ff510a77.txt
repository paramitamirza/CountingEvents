Police identify man fatally shot while on North City porch Tuesd - KMOV.com
Man fatally shot , four others injured during shooting in the 2500 block of Semple Avenue Tuesday night
 ST. LOUIS , Mo. ( KMOV.com ) –
A late - night shooting Tuesday resulted in multiple victims , one suffering fatal injuries , in north St. Louis .

Around 11:15 p.m. , an unknown suspect opened fire , while sitting inside a four - door sedan , on a group of people who were on the front porch of a home in the 2500 block of Semple Avenue , hitting three women and two men .

Dominque Young , 21 , of the 2500 block of Semple , sustained multiple gunshot wounds and was pronounced dead at the scene .
The other four victims were taken to the hospital with various gunshot wounds and were listed in stable condition .

The shooting was one of five reported overnight across the St. Louis area .

Tuesday night ’s shooting occurred in the same block where a man in his 20 ’s sustained multiple fatal gunshot wounds Sunday .
Authorities have not said if they believe the two shootings are related .

The identity of the deceased victim has not been released .

Authorities have not released any information regarding suspects .

The investigation in ongoing .

Anyone with information regarding the shooting is asked to contact CrimeStoppers .
