Woman killed in accidental shooting inside vehicle , 3 people arr - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports
The Baton Rouge Police Department is investigating an accidental shooting that left a woman dead .

Police say they were called to the scene of a one vehicle accident in the 3500 block of Wayne Avenue around 3:40 p.m. Saturday .

When officers arrived at the scene , they discovered that a woman , 20-year - old Ajeanae Frazer , had been shot .
Frazer was pronounced dead at the scene .

During the investigation , officers learned that Frazer 's boyfriend , 25-year - old Michael Hasten , had been involved in an altercation earlier in the day and returned home to get help so that he could retaliate .

According to investigators , Hasten gave an assault rifle to a 16-year - old who got into the vehicle with Hasten and Frazer .

While riding in the vehicle , the rifle accidentally discharged striking Frazer in the upper torso .

Police say Hasten 's father , 50-year - old Mark Warner , allegedly removed the rifle from the scene and hid it before police arrived .

The 16-year - old was arrested and taken to the East Baton Rouge Juvenile Detention Center where he has been booked with negligent homicide and illegal possession of a firearm .

Hasten was booked into the East Baton Rouge Parish Prison where he has been charged with contributing to the delinquency of a minor and felon in possession of a firearm .

Warner was booked into the East Baton Rouge Parish Prison where he has been charged with obstruction of justice and contributing to the delinquency of a minor .
