Several people injured in shooting at Douglass Park in Lexington
Police in Lexington are investigating a shooting at Douglass Park .

Police say around 9:15 p.m. on Sunday , a basketball game turned violent when shots were fired .

Police say three people at the scene were taken to the hospital with injuries .

Police tell WKYT that two other people showed up at the hospital with gunshot wounds , shortly after the shooting .
Police believe they are connected to the incident at Douglass Park .

Officers say that one of the victims is in critical condition .
The other four people have non - life threatening injuries .

Right now , police do not have any description of the shooter and are asking for anyone with information to come forward .

" When the shots were fired , there was a lot of chaos , so we 're having difficulty identifying witnesses .
So we 're making a big plea for people if they were here and they saw anything or they know anything .
They do n't have to meet us in person , they can call 859 - 258 - 3700 or they can reach us through Blue Grass Crime Stoppers , " Lt .
Jonathan Bastian told WKYT.
 Police say they have recovered evidence in Douglass Park and the forensic investigators are searching the park .

Since there are conflicting witness statements , police say they do n't know how many shooters they are looking for .
