Five People Shot at House Party
ROCKFORD ( WIFR ) -- Five people are shot at a house party on Rockford 's west side .

Rockford police say the victims showed up at the hospital around 1:10am Monday .

They told police they were outside a home in the 900 block of Kent Street , when someone with a shotgun began firing .

Everyone was treated at the hospital and released .
