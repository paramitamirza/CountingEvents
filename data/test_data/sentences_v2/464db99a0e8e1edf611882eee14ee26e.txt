IN : 13-year - old injured by accidental gun shot
AP
 FLOYD CO. , Ind. –
The Floyd County Sheriff ’s Department responded to a report of a juvenile who was shot in the chest .

It happened in the 6200 block of State Road 62 .

Officials said that a 13-year - old boy had suffered a shotgun wound to the right armpit .

The child was transported to Kosair Hospital and is said to be in stable condition .

The investigation revealed that the child had been hunting and while loading the equipment in the truck , the firearm accidentally discharged striking the child under the arm .

The Sheriff ’s Department wants to remind the public about basic gun safety :

· Always keep the gun pointed in a safe direction .

·
Always keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot .

· Always keep the gun unloaded until ready to use .
