Police : 4-year - old girl shot herself with stolen gun - wave3.com - Louisville News , Weather & Sports
LOUISVILLE , KY ( WAVE )
- A 4-year - old who suffered a gunshot wound Thursday night in the Park Hill neighborhood accidentally shot herself with a stolen gun , according to Louisville Metro police .

LMPD spokeswoman Alicia Smiley said neither the child 's mother nor the woman 's boyfriend claims ownership of the weapon .
Charges are pending against one or both of them while the investigation continues .

 The incident happened about 8:45 p.m. Thursday inside a unit at Park Hill Housing Complex in the 1200 block of Brashear Drive .
The nature of the girl 's injuries has not been released , but Smiley said the victim 's wounds are not life - threatening .
