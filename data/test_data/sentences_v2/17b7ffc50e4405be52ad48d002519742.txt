2016-02-15
Gun used in homicide was stolen
BELOIT — Beloit police officials have confirmed that Marcus Seichter , 17 , of Beloit , was killed with a stolen gun .

Seichter was shot and killed on Dec. 22 , 2015 .
Nikolai Wasilewski , 17 , has been charged with homicide by negligent use of a dangerous weapon in Seichter ’s death .

In a separate case filed in Rock County Circuit Court , a 16-year - old was charged with the theft of 6 of 17 firearms from a Town of Newark home .
The criminal complaint notes the theft occurred Dec. 15 and one of the stolen firearms was used in a homicide .
