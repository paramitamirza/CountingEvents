Two boys shot in separate accidental shootings in Portsmouth
Portsmouth , Va. –
Two boys were injured Sunday in separate accidental shootings , according to police .

The first shooting happened around 10:40 a.m. at a home on Lancer Drive .

A woman called 911 saying she was driving her 7-year - old son to Maryview Medical Center with a gunshot wound to the hand .

He was transported to Children ’s Hospital of the King ’s Daughters a short time later .
The child ’s injuries are not believed to be life - threatening .

After investigating , police say they determined the 7-year - old had been playing with an unsecured firearm in his home .

Around 11:16 a.m. , medics and officers were called to another shooting involving a child .

According to police , a 9-year - old boy was shot in the leg on Moregate Lane .

He was transported to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital with non life - threatening injuries .

Detectives say that shooting was also accidental .
The 9-year - old was shot by another boy playing with a gun found outside .

Both investigations are still ongoing and charges are pending in both cases .

Portsmouth Police say these incidents are an important reminder to talk to your kids about gun safety , even if you don’t own a gun .

You can get more information and find resources at Project Child Safe and Everytown .
