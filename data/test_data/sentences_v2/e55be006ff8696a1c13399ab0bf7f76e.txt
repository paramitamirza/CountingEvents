2-year - old shoots 12-year - old in Nashville
NASHVILLE , Tenn. ( WZTV ) -
A child was accidentally shot by a toddler in Donelson on Sunday morning .

Metro Nashville Police responded to the shooting on Blue Hills Drive at 9:30 a.m. They say a 2-year - old boy who does n't live at the residence grabbed a gun from a door pocket of his aunt 's vehicle that had just driven to the location and fired the gun , hitting his 12-year - old relative in the hip .

 The victim , identified by police as Tyson Lucas , went to Vanderbilt Medical Center by ambulance with non - life threatening injuries .
He is expected to be released from the hospital today .

The driver of the vehicle , according to police , can legally carry the firearm in her vehicle , but does not have a permit to carry a gun .

Safe Tennessee Project says this is the 69th unintentional shooting so far this year , the 26th unintentional shooting involving a child under 18 , and the 18th incident related to an irresponsibly stored , loaded gun .

No charges are filed at this time .
The incident is under investigation by the Youth Services Division of the police department .
