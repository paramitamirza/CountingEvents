Three shot , including 6-year - old boy , in St. Roch
.
( Photo : KING 5 News )

A man , woman and 6-year - old boy were shot by a masked man in the St. Roch neighborhood Saturday morning .

According to a preliminary report from NOPD , the three victims were getting out of a car in the 2100 block of Arts Street around 1 a.m. when a man wearing a white mask and blue shirt opened fire on them .

All three victim were taken to the hospital by private conveyance and are reported to have non - life threatening injuries .

Police described the shooter as a short , black male with a dark complexion wearing a white make and a blue shirt .

If you have any information on this shooting , call CRIMESTOPPERS at 504 - 822 - 1111 .
