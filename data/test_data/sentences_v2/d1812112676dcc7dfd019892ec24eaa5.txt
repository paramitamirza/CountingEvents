2 arrested after child shot in Sanford

 Aaron Davis , 26 , and Devolia Sims , 23 , both of Sanford , are charged with child neglect with great bodily harm , according to the Sanford Police Department .

Investigators said the pair drove the child to Central Florida Regional Hospital for treatment of a gunshot wound to the hand Tuesday , and hospital staff contacted authorities .

Police interviewed Sims , who they said repeatedly changed her story .
Davis refused to give a statement , officers said .
