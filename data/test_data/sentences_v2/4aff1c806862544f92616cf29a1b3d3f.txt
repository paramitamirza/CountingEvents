2 killed , 2 injured in shooting at cock fighting event
MIAMI - The Miami - Dade Police Department identified two men killed and another two injured in a shooting among a group of people watching cock fighting .

The shooting happened at 19651 SW 168th Street about 7:42 p.m. Sunday .

The group was watching cock fighting when a fight began , investigators say .
Shots were fired , hitting four men .

Agustin Figueredo , 67 , and another man in his 80s were killed .
Rafael Garcia , 40 , and Angel Rivera , 49 , were injured .
Their conditions were n't immediately released .

If you have any information about the shooting , call Miami - Dade Crimestoppers at 305 - 471-TIPS.
