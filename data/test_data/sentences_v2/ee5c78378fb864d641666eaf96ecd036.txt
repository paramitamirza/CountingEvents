Accidental shooting hurts Wichita child
An accidental shooting sent an 8-year - old Wichita boy to the hospital .

A shot was fired just before 9:30 p.m. Tuesday in the 700 block of S Fountain .
Police say the gun involved belonged to a 36-year - old man , identified as a friend of the child 's dad .
The man stayed on scene to talk with police , and was arrested for aggravated battery .

The child was hit in the leg and is expected to make a full recovery .
