Man dies from close - range gunshot wound in Dickinson
A 21-year - old man died from a close - range gunshot wound in Dickinson Saturday .

Dickinson police responded to CHI St. Alexius about 1:40 a.m. for a report of a gunshot victim .

Tanner Carrico was shot in the abdomen and was pronounced dead at the hospital .

After police obtained a search warrant , they investigated the crime scene on Abraham Parkway and found the handgun they believe was used in the shooting .

The victim 's acquaintance , 20-year - old Boyd Stroh , was taken into custody Saturday morning and charged with Class B felony manslaughter .

Stroh is being held at the South West Multi County Correctional Center .
