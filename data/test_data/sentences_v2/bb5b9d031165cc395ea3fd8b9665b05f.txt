Teen accidentally shoots self while running from gunfire in Orange County
APOPKA , Fla. - An Orange County teen is recovering after accidentally shooting himself while trying to escape gunfire .

The accident happened near Warren Sapp Drive and Monk Avenue in Apopka .

Deputies say the 15-year - old told them he was in the area when he heard gunshots and took out a gun to defend himself .

Investigators say when he tried to run away from the gunfire , he accidentally shot himself .

The teen suffered non - life - threatening injuries .

It 's not known what sparked the initial gunfire , and an investigation is ongoing .
