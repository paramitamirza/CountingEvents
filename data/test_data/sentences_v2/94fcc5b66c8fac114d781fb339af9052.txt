Husband fatally shoots wife in the head
HOUSTON-
A young woman was fatally shot in the head by her husband on Saturday night in a Greater Eastwood home .

According to the Houston Police Department , they received a call about a woman being shot around 9:30 p.m. near the intersection of Pease Street and Ernestine Street .

Upon arrival at the scene , police found a woman in her mid-20 's with a single gunshot wound .
She was immediately transported to the hospital where she died .

Her husband told investigators that he " accidentally " shot her .
Police took her husband downtown for questioning .

The investigation is ongoing , and they have not stated where or not it was an accident .
