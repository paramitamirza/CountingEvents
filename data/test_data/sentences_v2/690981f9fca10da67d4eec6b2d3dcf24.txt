Grandma detained after boy killed with her gun
A Detroit grandmother was arrested Monday afternoon after a 13-year - old boy was fatally shot with a gun she left on her nightstand , police said .
( Photo : Detroit News )

A Detroit grandmother is being questioned by police Monday evening after a 13-year - old boy was fatally shot with a gun she left on her nightstand , police said .

“
She was detained for additional questioning , ” said Sgt .
Michael Woody , spokesman for the Detroit Police Department . “ She ’s not currently under arrest yet , but that may change . ”

According to police , the shooting occurred at 1:13 p.m. in the 4300 block of Tireman on the city ’s west side .

Three teens , ages 13 , 14 and 15 , invited the victim to play games at the house , said Detroit Police Officer Nicole Kirkwood .
Many school districts in the area had canceled classes Monday because of the snow .

The teens found the grandmother ’s gun sitting on a nightstand , Kirkwood said .

“
One of the boys — we’re not sure who — picked up the weapon and pointed it in the direction of the 13 year old , ” she said .

While playing the gun discharged , hitting the victim , Kirkwood said .

The victim was taken to a hospital where he was pronounced dead .

Police took the grandmother into custody .
Kirkwood said she did not have additional information about the woman .
