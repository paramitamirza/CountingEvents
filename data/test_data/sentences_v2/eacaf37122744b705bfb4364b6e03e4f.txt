Police say a 2-year - old girl has been wounded in a shooting that happened after some men were asked to leave a holiday gathering in Detroit .

Police say the shooting was investigated early Tuesday on the city 's east side and happened as people had gathered near a library and car wash to celebrate the Fourth of July .

The child was taken to a hospital for treatment .
Officer Nicole Kirkwood tells The Detroit News that the girl was in critical condition .

And on Monday afternoon , police say a 14-year - old boy who was walking with friends was wounded after encountering another group of people .
He was listed in temporary serious condition .
