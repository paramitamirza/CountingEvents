Man dies from gunshot wound in Mountain View ; one arrested
A man is dead after a shooting in Mountain View , Missouri .

Police say Chet Hockman , of Mountain View , died from a gunshot wound to the chest at a home .
Emergency workers could not revive him .

Police believe the shooting was accidental .
Police say a man ran to the police station saying his friend had been shot .
Officers arrested him .
Police also interviewed a witness at the scene .
