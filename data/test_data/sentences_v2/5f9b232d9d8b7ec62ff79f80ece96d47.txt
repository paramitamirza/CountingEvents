Woman dies after being found with gunshot wound in Norfolk
A 26-year - old woman is dead after an apparent shooting in Norfolk Saturday afternoon .

Police responded to calls about a gunshot victim on the 800 block of B Avenue in Norfolk Saturday afternoon and found a woman suffering from awhat appeared to be a gunshot wound , according to a release from Norfolk Police .

The 26-year - old woman was transported to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital where she was pronounced dead this afternoon .

Police are investigating the incident , which is currently classified as an " undetermined death , " according to the release .

The woman 's identity is set to be released once next of kin are notified .
