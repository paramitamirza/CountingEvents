Police have arrested two teenagers in connection with the fatal shooting of a 15-year - old Queens girl .

Police say they were alerted to a shooting inside 22 - 04 Collier Avenue at 12:03 a.m. Friday .

They say they found Wilta Wordsworth , 15 , shot in the head inside the apartment .
