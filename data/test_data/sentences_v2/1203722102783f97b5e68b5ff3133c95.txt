14-year - old injured in accidental shooting in Olathe
A 14-year - old teen was injured on Wednesday evening after getting accidentally shot by another teen , Olathe Police say .

The shooting happened at a home in the 600 block of East Welston Avenue in Olathe at about 5 p.m.

The victim was hit in the shoulder/arm area according to police , and is expected to be OK.

The investigation into the incident continues .
