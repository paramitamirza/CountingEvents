Shooting Leaves Two Injured
A gunman opens fire sending two people to the hospital , including a toddler .

The shooting happened at 10:45 Thursday night in the 1300 block of South Arago Street in Peoria .

Police say a small group of people were near the front of the house , when four gunshots rang out .

A toddler was shot in the leg , and a man believed to be in his 20 's was also hit .

Police say both victims are in stable condition ,
 Three shell casings have been recovered .

There is still no word on who pulled the trigger .
