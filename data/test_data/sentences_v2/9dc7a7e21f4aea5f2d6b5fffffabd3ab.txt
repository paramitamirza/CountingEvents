Police investigating shooting that left 5 men injured
Investigators are trying to figure out who is responsible for gunfire that erupted in a northwest Atlanta neighborhood Saturday , sending five men to the hospital .

Police are hoping to get more information from the victims .

Detectives went to Grady Memorial Hospital Sunday to interview three of the five shooting victims being treated there .

Family members told Channel 2 Action News that two of the victims were treated and released .

Atlanta police said they are trying to sort out if any of the victims are also suspects , and who was shooting at the Delmar Villas in the Adamsville neighborhood .

Police taped off three different crime scenes after the shootings .

Two victims ran to neighboring complexes before collapsing , according to police .

Neighbors said it sounded like a shootout in the common area of the apartments , and that they heard up to 50 gunshots .

" I do n't have any idea .
All I heard was just pop , pop , pop , pop like that , " resident Sudie Dorsey said .
" It 's an everyday thing . "

Atlanta police 's gang unit also helped in the investigation .

The two other victims were taken to Atlanta Medical Center , police said .
