2014-07-06
Teen dead after gun mishandled in SE Houston
Page 1 of 1
 A teen died Saturday night after he was accidentally shot at his southeast Houston home .

A friend of 17-year - old Corbin George brought a gun to the house in the 11400 block of Panay " for safekeeping , " said Houston Police Department spokeswoman Jodi Silva .

" George grabs the gun from the friend and gets accidentally shot , " Silva said .

The teen was transported to Ben Taub General Hospital where he was briefly treated before being pronounced dead , she said .

Silva did not provide any further information about the incident except to say the case will go before a Harris County grand jury , which will review whether the friend could face charges .
