5 Suspects Charged in Deadly Summit Street Sh - Erie News Now : News , Weather & Sports | WICU 12 & WSEE
Erie Police arrested four suspects Friday morning , who are among five now charged in connection to the deadly shooting during a house party at 29th and Summit Street .

Elijah Jackson and Shakur Franklin , both 16 , were killed during the shootout July 24th , 2015 .
The gunfire injured another four teenagers .

Investigators spent the past several months interviewing witnesses , reviewing evidence and tracking leads , all of which led to the charges .

The arrests follows months of pleas for justice from the family and friends of the victims .
