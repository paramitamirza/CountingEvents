2 dead , 3-year - old wounded in shooting on Indy 's east side
INDIANAPOLIS -- Two men died and a 3-year - old boy was wounded after gunfire rang out in Indianapolis Saturday night .

Police were called to a home in the 2300 block of Magnolia Place on the city 's east side around 6:30 p.m.
 Two men were shot .
One died at the scene .
The other died several hours later at the hospital .

The 3-year - old was taken to Riley Hospital with a gunshot wound to the hand .

Police say the gunfire broke out following a gathering after a funeral .

They are still trying to determine who fired the shots .
