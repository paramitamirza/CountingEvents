2 children killed in Kansas City shooting
Two boys were killed and a teen girl was injured in a shooting early Saturday morning in Kansas City police said .

VIDEO
 An 8-year - old boy , a 9-year - old boy and a 16-year - old girl were shot just before 2 a.m. in the 5700 block of College Avenue .
They were taken to a hospital , where the boys died .

" Very sad .
No child should lose his life , " said neighbor Vernon Trice .
" And since I have two young boys myself , no child should lose their life . "

The girl was expected to recover .

Police said the boys were cousins .

Kansas City Mayor Sly James said the gun violence in the city is an ongoing frustration .
He said he believes many shootings could be prevented through a better focus on the problems of poverty and education .

" That is our fault , that is a shame on the leadership of this country , this state wherever , that we do n't do what we know needs to be done , " James said .

Anyone with information about the shooting is asked to call the TIPS Hotline at 816 - 474 - 8477 .
