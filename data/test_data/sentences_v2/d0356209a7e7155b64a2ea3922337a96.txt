Teen Accidentally Shoots Himself In The Hand - LEX18.com | Continuous News and StormTracker Weather
LEXINGTON , Ky ( LEX 18 )
Despite an initial story of a drive - by shooting , investigators now say that the teenager shot in Lexington Monday accidentally shot himself .

Police say they were originally told that someone in a car on Augusta Drive fired multiple rounds around 6:00 p.m. , striking a 15-year - old boy in the hand .

Now police say that the boy accidentally fired a gun , shooting himself in the hand .

The teenager was taken to the UK Hospital with non - life - threatening injuries .
