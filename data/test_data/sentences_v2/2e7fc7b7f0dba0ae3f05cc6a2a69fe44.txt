Update : 13-year old accidentally shot by sibling - WDAM - TV 7-News , Weather , Sports - Hattiesburg , MS
Crime scene detectives are on the scene where a 13-year - old was shot Friday morning around 9 a.m.

According to Officer Colendula Green , a 16-year - old had a gun and it was accidentally discharged grazing his sister .

Investigators are treating this as an accidental shooting .

It happened on Hillside Drive in south Jackson .

Adults were present in the home at the time of the shooting .

Police say the child was taken to a nearby hospital with non - life threatening injuries .

We will bring you more information as it becomes available to us .
