Salina Man Dies From Gunshot Wound
Salina Police are investigating a shooting death in the 1900 block of Dover .

According to Captain Chris Trocheck , witnesses called police on Sunday morning at 6:34am after a 28-year - old white , male was shot with a handgun in a driveway outside a residence .

Police say the man was transported to Salina Regional Health Center and died a short time later .

Police are not looking for any suspects in the shooting .

The victim has not been identified until next of kin are contacted .

Captain Trockeck tells KSAL News that they are still investigating the circumstances that led up to the gunshot .

Police say the man did not reside at the home but was an invited guest .
