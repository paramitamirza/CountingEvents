Police continue to investigate accidental shooting - Hometownstations.com - WLIO- Lima , OH News Weather Sports
The Putnam County sheriff 's office continues to look into an accidental shooting that left a teen dead .

The sheriff 's office says 17 year old Kyle Markins of Findlay was killed by a gun shot wound at 13915 County Road 3 .
He and 2 other friends were visiting a friend that lived in the home .
The sheriff 's office reports Markins was shooting with friends when he was fatally wounded .
Markins was the only person injured .

Investigators are treating the incident as an accidental shooting but are continuing to investigate the case .
