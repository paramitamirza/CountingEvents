 TACOMA , Wash. - Police in Tacoma are searching for a killer after a man was found shot to death in a parking lot Monday evening .

Officers responded to the scene , the Spring Haven Village Apartments in the 9200 block of South Hosmer Street in Tacoma , at around 8:30 p.m. after receiving reports of shots fired in the vicinity .

A man was found lying in the apartment complex 's parking lot .
He was pronounced dead at the scene , said Loretta Cool of the Tacoma police .

Police say that area is known for gang activity , and witnesses said they heard multiple shots fired within a two - block area .

So far there is no description of the suspect , but police believe the victim likely knew the shooter .
The victim 's name and age were not immediately released .

Anyone with information is urged to call police .
