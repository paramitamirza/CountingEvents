Teen dies in Chattanooga laundromat shooting
Published on Jun 24 , 2013
 A teenager is dead and three other men are injured after a shooting in Chattanooga Sunday .

The men were shot at a laundromat on Dodds Avenue .

19-year - old Demetrius Davis died at the hospital , while Jimmy McCane , Ladarrel Bradley , and Dominique King all suffered nonlife threatening injuries .
