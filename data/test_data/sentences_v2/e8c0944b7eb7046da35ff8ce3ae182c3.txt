Man wanted in weekend block party shooting turns himself in - MSNewsNow.com - Jackson , MS
Police say the man wanted in a weekend block party shooting has turned himself in .

22-year - old Ryan Davis is charged with six counts of aggravated assault stemming from the October 28 shooting .
Six people were shot and several others injured .

All the injured are in stable condition with non - life threatening injuries .

Police say the block party shooting happened on Lorenz Boulevard near West Street .
On Sunday , the Jackson Police Department released video of the suspected shooter and asked for the public 's help in identifying the person .
