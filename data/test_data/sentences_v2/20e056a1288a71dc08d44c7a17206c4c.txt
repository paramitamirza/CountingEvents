1-year - old struck in arm after shots fired into Richmond home
RICHMOND , Va. ( WRIC ) —
A one - year - old was rushed to the hospital after a shooting in Richmond overnight .

The shooting happened just before midnight on the 2900 block of Woodcliff Avenue , just off of E. Brookland Park Boulevard .

When police arrived , they found someone holding a one - year - old with a gunshot wound .

Residents say they heard five to eight gunshots before the police arrived on scene .

Police say someone fired shots into the home — hitting the child in the arm .
No one else inside the home was hit .

The boy is expected to be okay .

Police are now searching for a suspect .
Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at ( 804 ) 780 - 1000 .
