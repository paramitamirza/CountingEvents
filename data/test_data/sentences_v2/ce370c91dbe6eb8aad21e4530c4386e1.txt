Man killed , 6-year - old girl wounded in Park Manor shooting
 A 22-year - old man was killed and a 6-year - old girl was wounded in a shooting Thursday afternoon in Park Manor on the South Side .

They were outside near David McCray ’s home in the 6800 block of South Calumet when someone walked up and started shooting around 2:30 p.m. , striking the man in the chest and the girl in the back of the head , according to Chicago Police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office .

McCray was taken to Stroger Hospital where he died at 2:49 p.m. , authorities said .

The girl was taken to Comer Children ’s Hospital in “ guarded but stable condition , ” police News Affairs Officer Veejay Zala said .

She was not thought to be an intended target .
No one was in custody for the shooting Sunday night .
