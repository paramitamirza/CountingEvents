A North Lawndale shooting left two small boys , ages 4 and 6 , wounded Wednesday night , police said .

At 6:50 p.m. , the 4-year - old boy was walking in the 1500 block of South Trumbull near where a group of men were arguing , said Officer Ana Pacheco , a Chicago Police spokeswoman .
Moments later , shots were fired .

Once he got home , the boy told his mom he had been shot in the right foot , Pacheco said .

A 6-year - old boy also suffered a small laceration to one of his fingers during the incident , Pacheco said .
He was treated and released at the scene .

No one was in custody in the shooting .
