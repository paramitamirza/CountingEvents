Sentencing delayed for Bourbon St. shooter Trung Le - FOX 8 WVUE New Orleans News , Weather , Sports , Social
Sentencing was delayed Thursday for the man convicted in the Bourbon St. shooting that killed a woman and injured nine others
 Trung Le , 22 , and his attorney are pushing for a new trial .

A jury convicted Le of manslaughter and attempted manslaughter for the June 2014 shootout .

Brittany Thomas , 21 , was killed by the gunfire .

Police are still looking for an unidentified shooter who they say fired 11 shots into the crowd that night .

Prosecutors argued that Le was the aggressor that night , leading to the violence that caused Thomas ' death .
