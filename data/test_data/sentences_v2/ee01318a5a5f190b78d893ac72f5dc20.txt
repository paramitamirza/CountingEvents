Man arrested after 3-year - old girl shot in Youngstown
YOUNGSTOWN –
Police are continuing to investigate after a 3-year - old girl was shot Saturday night in Youngstown .

According to WKBN , the little girl was injured while inside a home on East Boston Avenue .

Dionte Robinson , 24 , was arrested on Sunday in connection with the shooting .

He was charged with child endangerment .

She was taken to St. Elizabeth ’s Hospital and it appears that she will recover fine .

She was only shot once , but the bullet went through both legs .
Police say Robinson was holding her when he took a gun out of his waistband and accidentally fired it .

She was airlifted to Akron Children ’s Hospital later on in the night .

Robinson has a concealed carry permit and has been cooperating with officials .
