× Iowa 13-year - old accused of killing 15-year - old in accidental shooting
 CEDAR RAPIDS , Iowa ( AP ) —
Cedar Rapids police have arrested a 13-year - old boy in connection with the fatal shooting of another teen .

City officials announced Wednesday that police arrested the teen Tuesday and charged him with involuntary manslaughter in connection with the March 18th shooting of 15-year - old Senquez Jackson , who died the next day .
Officials say the shooting was unintentional .

The 13-year - old is also charged with carrying weapons , preventing apprehension and obstructing prosecution .

The 13-year - old thought the gun was not loaded , after he pulled the ammo clip from the handle of a small , 38-caliber automatic pistol ; but there was still a bullet in the gun , according to a report from KCRG.

The 13-year - old was held at the Linn County Juvenile Detention Center .
