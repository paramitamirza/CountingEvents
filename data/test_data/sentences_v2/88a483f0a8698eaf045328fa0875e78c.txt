Boy steals gun , accidentally shoots himself in hand
MADISON , Wis. -
 A Madison boy accidentally shot himself in the hand with a handgun he took from an unlocked vehicle early Thursday morning , Madison police said .

The boy took the handgun from an unlocked pickup truck around 3 a.m. and went back to his home in the 300 block of Wyalusing Drive , according to a release .

The boy reportedly concealed the gun in his waistband .

When he went to unload the gun , he pulled the trigger and shot himself in the hand , police said .

The boy faces tentative charges of possession of a dangerous weapon , carrying a concealed weapon and negligent use of weapons .
