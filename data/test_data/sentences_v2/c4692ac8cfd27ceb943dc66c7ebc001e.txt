LPD : 15-year - old shoots 16-year - old while showing pistol - KCBD NewsChannel 11 Lubbock
Lubbock police are investigating after a 16-year - old male was shot by a 15-year - old male as he was showing him a pistol .

It happened just before 10 p.m. Sunday in the 1900 block of 46th Street .

Officers say that four male teenagers were in a bedroom playing with a handgun when one pulled the trigger and the firearm discharged striking a 16-year - old in the neck .

The 16-year - old was taken to UMC with life - threatening injuries and is now in stable condition .

The 15-year - old was taken into custody at the scene and a juvenile call out was made .

It is unknown where the 15-year - old got the pistol from .

No names have been released at this time .
