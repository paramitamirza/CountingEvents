 OKLAHOMA CITY - Police are investigating a shooting involving a child in northeast Oklahoma City Wednesday night .

Oklahoma City police say a 3 year old girl was shot in the 2400 block of E. Madison St.
 “ I just heard a loud bang .
I just heard it and stayed inside with my baby , ” Alora Golden , who lives down the street , said .

Police believe the young victim , who was shot inside her home , was shot while by somebody driving by .
There are several bullet holes through the front door of the home .

“
I was like
what is that ?
I heard it and I was going to check but I didn`t , ”
Golden said .

Police say the child was shot in the back and rushed to the hospital where she is now recovering .

Area residents say officers blocked off surrounding streets for hours as they investigated .

“
They were here for a long time , ”

The suspect is still on the loose .

Another neighbor , who wanted to remain anonymous , was rattled by the apparent random violence .

“ That was a major surprise .
This is a very quiet area , ” the neighbor told Newschannel 4 .

For Golden , a mother herself , it ’s not just unusual but also scary .
 “
Just to think if me and my baby were out here , it`s crazy , ” Golden said .

At last check , there was no word on the child 's condition .
