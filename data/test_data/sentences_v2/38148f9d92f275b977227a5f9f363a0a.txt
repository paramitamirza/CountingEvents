3 dead , 2 injured in south Houston shooting
HOUSTON - Houston Police say three people are dead and two others injured in south Houston .
Investigators say the shooting happened around 6:30 p.m. Wednesday night on Mallow Street near Noel Street .

Detectives believe a group of men showed up to a duplex to rob a man when gunfire started .

The suspects drove away in a Grey 2011 Chevy Malibu .
Police say they are looking for three or four black males , some wearing red pants .

Investigators say they have seen drug activity in the area before , but no drugs or weapons were found inside the duplex .

The two people transported to Methodist Hospital appeared to have gunshot wounds , but their condition is unknown .

Police say it 's too early to know if they were involved in the same shootout .
