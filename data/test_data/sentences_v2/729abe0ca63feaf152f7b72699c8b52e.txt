Authorities are looking for suspects after as many as four people were shot Sunday night in north Minneapolis .

The Minneapolis Police Department was called at about 9:20 p.m. to the area 8th Ave .
N. and Humboldt Avenue N. on a report of a fight .
While responding to the call , the incident had elevated to a report of shots fired .

When officers arrived , one woman had been shot and was injured .
She was taken by ambulance to a hospital with non - life threatening injuries .

Authorities say three other adults had also been shot and were self - transported to hospitals , all with non - life threatening injuries .
Police say no arrests have been made in connection with the incident , which is under investigation .
