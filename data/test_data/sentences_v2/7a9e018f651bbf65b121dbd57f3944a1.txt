5 adults injured in shooting on Plymouth Ave N in Minneapolis
5 adults injured in shooting on Plymouth Ave N in Minneapolis News 5 adults injured in shooting on Plymouth Ave N in Minneapolis Five people were injured in a shooting early Sunday morning in north Minneapolis , according to police .

- Five people were injured in a shooting early Sunday morning in north Minneapolis , according to police .

The shooting happened at 1717 Plymouth Avenue North around 12:30 am .

The five adult victims , three men and two women , all received non - life threatening injuries .
One man was shot in the lower torso , another was shot in the arm and the third man was hit in the leg .
One woman was wounded in the buttock , the other woman was shot in the leg .

Police believe the victim and the shooter know each other .

The case is still under investigation .
