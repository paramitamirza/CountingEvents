2016-04-21
Update : SAPD : Shooting on Spring St. accidental
Media Release from the St. Augustine Police Department at 8:35 a.m.
 * * * * * * * * * * *
 St Augustine Police Department investigating an accidental shooting
 At 8:34 pm on April 21 , 2016 what appeared to be an accidental shooting took place at 22 Spring Street .
A juvenile was shot in the right leg by a friend while hiding a shotgun .

The juvenile was transported to Flagler Hospital and then flown to UF Health in Jacksonville .

Our investigation is on - going , more witnesses are being interviewed and more information is being gathered .
