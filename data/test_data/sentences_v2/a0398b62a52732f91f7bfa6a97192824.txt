Boy , 7 , Dies After Accidentally Shooting Himself
A 7-year - old boy died Sunday after accidentally shooting himself in the head on the city 's South Side .

Police say Devon Lofton suffered an accidental , self - inflicted gunshot wound inside a home in the 6700 block of S. Aberdeen St. in the Englewood neighborhood Sunday .

According to the Chicago Fire Department , the shooting occurred at 3:51 p.m. , and the child was taken in critical condition to Comer Children 's Hospital , where he was pronounced dead .

Police do not know where the gun came from and if it was owned legally by someone in the house as they continue to investigate .
