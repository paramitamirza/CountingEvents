Juvenile suspect held in fatal shooting at Vegas rec center - KTVN Channel 2 - Reno Tahoe Sparks News , Weather , Video
LAS VEGAS ( AP )
- Police have arrested a teenage boy in a fatal shooting that broke out at a Las Vegas youth recreation center .

Authorities say the male juvenile suspect was booked Saturday for murder with a deadly weapon for allegedly shooting and killing another boy .

He is being held in Clark County Juvenile Detention Center but is not being identified because of his age .

According to investigators , a large fight erupted at the Hollywood Recreation Center around 3:30 a.m. Saturday .

During the chaos , police say at least 50 rounds were fired and one teenage boy was stabbed in the neck .

The gunfire resulted in one boy dying at the scene and three others being hospitalized .

Two suffered non - life - threatening injuries and another remains in critical condition .

The stabbing victim is in stable condition .
