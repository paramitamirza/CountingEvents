UPDATE : 14-year - old girl dies after shooting range incident at MacLean Bottoms
The Burleigh County Sheriff 's Department has released more information about the death after a shooting incident Sunday morning at MacLean Bottoms Shooting Range .

They say the victim was a 14-year - old Bismarck girl .

The department says a preliminary report suggests it was an accidental shooting .

Burleigh County Sheriff 's Department and North Dakota Game and Fish are jointly investigating the incident .
