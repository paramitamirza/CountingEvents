Five wounded in shooting at Belle Glade night club
By Pat Beall
 Palm Beach Post Staff Writer
 Five men were wounded early today in a shooting at Club 21 in Belle Glade , leaving two in critical condition .

The shootings took place at 1533 NW Ave .
L , about 1.5 miles west of the Belle Glade Municipal Airport .

Emergency crews arriving on the scene at 2:32 a.m. found four of the shooting victims .
One was flown by Trauma Hawk helicopter to Delray Medical Center .

An investigation by the Palm Beach County Sheriff ’s Office revealed a fifth victim had also been hit by gunfire .

Of the five men , two are in critical but stable condition .
The other three have non - life threatening injuries .

Names of the victims have not been released , and sheriff ’s officials are continuing to investigate today .

Anyone with information about the shooting is urged to call Crime Stoppers at 1 - 800 - 458-TIPS ( 8477 ) .

Early reports said one person had died in the shooting , but that proved to be incorrect , Palm Beach County Fire Rescue said .
