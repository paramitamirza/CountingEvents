Teen girl shot in the head while visiting friends at Northwest Side home
SAN ANTONIO - A key piece of evidence is missing from the scene where a teen is accused of shooting and killing a 14-year - old girl Tuesday night .

Police have one person in custody in the shooting that happened on Star Creek Drive near Culebra and Westover Hills .

Melody Cuerros ' family said she was a normal teen , and they 're now planning her funeral .

" She suffered a gunshot wound to the head , " said Officer Douglas Greene .
" EMS arrived on scene , attempted life saving measures , but were unable to resusitate her . "

The teens were at a garage party , the homeowner told police he did n't know they were there until a gunshot went off .

Witnesses said Ryan Rodriguez , 19 , was holding the gun .

" It was his understanding the handgun was not loaded , " said Officer Greene .
" He pointed the handgun at the victim and pulled the trigger and the gun went off .

Police arrested Rodriguez during a traffic stop , and are still trying to put the pieces together .

" We 're still looking to recover that weapon , " said Greene .

The family set - up an account for funeral costs , click here , to donate .

Original Story :

A teenage girl was killed when she was shot in the head at a home on the Northwest Side late Tuesday night .

The victim , Melody Cerros , 14 , was at a home in the 8600 block of Star Creek Drive when someone pointed a gun at her head and fired .

She was hit in the head and killed .
Investigators say she did not live at the home , but was visiting two teen girls who live there .

The homeowner told investigators he was asleep on the couch and did not realize there were teens that were ' partying ' in his garage .
The sound of gunfire woke him up and he told police he saw the teens running from the garage .

Some of the teens were rounded up by police .
Ryan Rodriguez , 19 , was later charged with manslaughter .

The teens told officers they were playing with the gun and did not realize it was loaded .
