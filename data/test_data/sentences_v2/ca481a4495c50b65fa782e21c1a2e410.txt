Minor injured after shooting in Georgetown Indiana
generic shooting graphic ( Photo : WHAS11 )
 GEORGETOWN , Ind. ( WHAS11 ) -- A minor was injured in a shooting Sunday morning in Georgetown , Indiana , according to emergency responders .

The juvenile was transported to Kosair Children 's Hospital from an industrial area off State Road 62 by air ambulance .
The condition of the juvenile is unknown at this time .

The circumstances surrounding the incident are unknown .

WHAS11 News will update this story with more information as it is released .
