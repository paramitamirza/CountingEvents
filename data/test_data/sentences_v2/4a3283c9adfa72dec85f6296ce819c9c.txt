East Knox County gunshot victim identified
Police lights .
( Photo : File/The Tennessean )

The Knox County Sheriff ’s Office has identified the East Knox County man who died of an apparent accidental shooting late last week .

According to a news release on the Sheriff ’s Office Facebook page , Robert Hutton , 27 , shot himself once Friday .
Hutton had been target shooting , according to the release .

Deputies were called to the 6500 block of Archer Road around 5:25 p.m. Friday .
A crew from Rural/Metro pronounced him dead at the scene , and his body was taken to the Knox County Regional Forensic Center .

The Knox County Sheriff 's Office Major Crimes Unit is investigating .
