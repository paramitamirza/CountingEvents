2016-07-13
Panama City Police Department actively investigating shooting involving an 8-year - old girl
Panama City Police are actively investigating a shooting involving an eight - year - old girl .

The incident happened last Sunday around 5 p.m. at Macedonia Garden Apartments on 17th Street in Panama City .

According to officials , 24-year - old Rachel Hinson was arguing with another woman , when Hinson fired a gun at her from inside of her car .

She missed her target , causing the bullet to strike the side of one of the apartment buildings , hitting an 8-year - old girl in the process , who was playing on a sidewalk nearby .

Police arrested Hinson and charged her with aggravated assault .

She has been released from jail on a $ 30,000 bond .

Police say the little girl is okay , and only suffered minor injuries .

This case is still ongoing and police may be looking for a second person of interest .

If you have any information about this case , contact the Panama City Police Department at 872 - 3112 , or leave an anonymous tip with CrimeStoppers at 785-TIPS.
