Coroner identifies 18-year - old killed in accidental shooting - Tri - State News , Weather & Sports
The Vanderburgh County Coroner 's Office has identified an 18-year - old man who died after an accidental shooting .

According to Chief Deputy Coroner Steve Lockyear , Diamante Killebrew was pronounced dead at St. Mary 's Hospital .

Police say the shooting happened around 2 a.m. Saturday in the 500 block of S. Linwood .

Investigators say a 15-year - old was handling a handgun in a reckless manner .

Police believe the gun went off while the teen was holding it .

Authorities say the bullet went through a wall and hit Killebrew , who was in another part of the house .

The 15-year - old has been placed at the Youth Care Center .

Police have charged the teen with reckless homicide , involuntary manslaughter , criminal recklessness , and possession of a handgun without a permit .

An autopsy for Killebrew has been scheduled for 3 p.m. Saturday .
