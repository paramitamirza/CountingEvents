IMPD releases suspect sketch in double homicide
INDIANAPOLIS -- Indianapolis police released a composite sketch Monday of a suspect in a fatal triple shooting from early July .

Justin Sims , 32 , and Dangelo Brown , 26 , were killed July 2 when an unidentified suspect opened fire on a funeral gathering on the east side .

A 3-year - old child was also shot in the hand , but was not fatally wounded .

 On Monday , IMPD released a composite sketch of the suspect .

He is described as a black male with a light complexion in his 20s , standing approximately 5'9 " tall .
At the time of the shooting , the suspect had a thin goatee and sideburns trimmed to a point .
He was wearing a black and red Cincinnati Reds baseball cap , a green hooded sweatshirt and a white undershirt .

Anyone with information about the shooting should contact Lead Detective Daniel Smith at 327 - 3749 or Crime Stoppers at 262-TIPS.
