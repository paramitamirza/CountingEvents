RANCHO CUCAMONGA : Teen in critical condition after accidentally shooting himself
A 17-year - old boy was in critical condition Wednesday , Dec. 30 , after accidentally shooting himself in his head while playing with a gun , the San Bernardino County Sheriff 's Department said .

Witnesses said the unidentified teen and friends were at Victoria Arbors Park in Rancho Cucamonga when the boy shot himself with a gun that he had inherited from a family member , a news release said .

The boy was flown to a hospital .
There was no evidence of foul play or a suicide attempt , the release said .

No other details were available .
