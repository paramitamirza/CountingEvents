Four shot in west Charlotte , minor injuries - WBTV 3 News , Weather , Sports , and Traffic for Charlotte , NC
Charlotte Mecklenburg Police are investigating after they say four people were shot in West Charlotte early Saturday morning .

It happened around 5 a.m. on West Boulevard near Remount Road .
Officials said three people were taken from the scene to an area hospital by ambulance .
A fourth later showed up at the hospital with a gunshot wound .

Police tell WBTV all of the victims have non life - threatening injuries .
Initially , one person was listed as having serious injuries but that status has since been downgraded .

No names have been released .

There is no word on the circumstances surrounding the shootings or any arrests .
