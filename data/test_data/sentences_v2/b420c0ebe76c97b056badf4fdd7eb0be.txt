Parents take child to hospital after he was shot in Portsmouth
PORTSMOUTH , Va. ( WAVY )
–
Police responded to a call made about mother who drove her 7-year - old son to Maryview Medical Center because of a gunshot wound on Sunday morning .

Emergency dispatchers received the call at 10:40 a.m for a gunshot victim in 700 block of Lancer Drive .
It was revealed that a 7-year - old boy was playing with an unsecured firearm and accidentally shot himself in the hand in his home according to Detective Misty Holly .

The boy was taken to Maryview Medical Center and transported to Children Hospital of the King ’s and Daughter ’s a short time later .

His injuries are non - life - threatening and the investigation is still ongoing .
Charges are pending in this case .
