2015-04-07
Boy , 8 , shot in Lawndale neighborhood
An 8-year - old boy was accidentally shot by another 8-year - old and then brought to Mount Sinai Hospital by a relative Tuesday morning , officials said .

Police said the boy was in good condition with a wound to the abdomen .

The boy and another child , also 8 , were playing with a gun inside an apartment in the 3700 block of West 13th Street when it discharged about 10:45 a.m. , police said .

Police taped off the east half of a red - brick three - story apartment building between an alley and Independence Boulevard on 13th Street .

It 's not clear whether anyone was in custody or whether anyone will face charges in connection with the incident .
Detectives remained at the scene Tuesday afternoon .
