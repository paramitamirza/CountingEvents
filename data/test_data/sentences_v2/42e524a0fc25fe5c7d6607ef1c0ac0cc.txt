2013-07-14
Teen dead , 4 wounded in Hamilton Twp .
house party shooting
Police are investigating a shooting at a birthday party in Hamilton Township , New Jersey that left one teenager dead and four others wounded .

According to Mercer County First Assistant Prosecutor Angelo Onofri , a total of five teenagers were shot , one of them fatally at a house party in Hamilton Township .

Onofri said Hamilton police received a call about gunshots on the 1100 block of Deutz Road just after 11:00 p.m. Friday night .

Police say the teens were attending a birthday party for a 15-year - old resident of the home .
They say approximately 100 people were in attendance , including an adult who was collecting admission at the door .

Police believe the shooting took place inside the living room .

I heard people trying to vacate , heard people wailing , crying and screaming .
I waited for the cops to come , " said Robert Rodriguez .

" I 'm heartbroken for the family of the boy who got killed .
As a parent , I ca n't even imagine , " said Fortuna Jordan .

Police arrived to find 17-year - old Shakir Williams with multiple gunshot wounds .
He was pronounced dead at the scene .

Four other teens were also shot at the party .

Police say a 17-year - old male who was transported to Capital Health Regional Medical Center with gunshot wounds to his torso , arms , legs and groin .
He is listed in critical condition after a five hour surgery .

Three others , an 18-year - old and two 17-year - olds were all treated and released .

So far , no word what sparked the the shooting .

The Mercer County Prosecutors office says there have been no arrests at this time and no one is in custody , but this is still a very active police investigation .
