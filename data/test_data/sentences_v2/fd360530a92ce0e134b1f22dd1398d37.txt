4 shot in Central Business District near Superdome - WAFB 9 News Baton Rouge , Louisiana News , Weather , Sports
Four victims were shot early Monday morning in the Central Business District .

The shootings happened around 2:30 a.m. Monday near the intersection of Poydras and Bolivar , just a few blocks from the Superdome .

Police say that a 16-year - old boy was shot in the backside .
A second victim was shot in the cheek and a third was shot in the stomach .

A fourth victim was found a short time later , shot in both legs .

Little information is known at this time .
FOX 8 will update the story as details become available .

Anyone with information on the incident is asked to contact Crimestoppers at ( 504 ) 822 - 1111 .
