Search continues for suspects in Clarksville shootings Posted : Thursday , August 14 , 2014 5:46 PM EDT Updated : Thursday , August 14 , 2014 5:49 PM EDT
 A man accused of shooting two people in Clarksville and leading police on a chase through two states was still on the run Thursday .

Two people were shot on Treeland Drive late last night .
Police spotted the suspect 's car a short time later , but the driver took off .

The chase went into Guthrie , KY , before the suspect got away .

Clarksville police are also looking for the man responsible for another double shooting that happened just hours before that one .

It is unclear if the two shootings are related .
