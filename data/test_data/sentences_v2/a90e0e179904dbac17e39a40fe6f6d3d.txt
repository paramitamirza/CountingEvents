Night club shooting sends seven to hospital
 A shooting at Cole 's Place has sent seven people to the hospital .
( Photo : WHAS 11 News )
 LOUISVILLE , Ky ( WHAS11 ) -- An overnight shooting has sent seven people to University Hospital .

Six adults were injured including a pregnant woman , bringing the total to seven .

LMPD responded to a shooting at Cole 's Place around 3 a.m. Sunday .
There , they found witnesses who claim a man dressed in all white began firing inside the club and hit seven people .

An overnight shooting at Cole 's Place has sent seven people to the hospital .

All seven were sent to University Hospital with what appeared to be non - life threatening injuries .

The pregnant woman gave birth , and both mother and child are expected to be okay .

WHAS11 will be following this story closely and will bring you up - to - date information as it becomes available .

If you have any information about the shooting or the gunman you are asked to call 574-LMPD.
