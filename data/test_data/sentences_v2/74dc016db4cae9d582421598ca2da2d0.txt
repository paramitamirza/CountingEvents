A teenager was shot and killed in north St. Louis Monday night .

Seventeen - year - old Damani Aitch was found with a gunshot wound inside a car in the 5900 block of Emma around 6:50 p.m.

When police found Aitch , two other teenagers , described as being 16 and 17 , were found on the scene .
The 16-year - old had a laceration to the face and a gunshot wound to his hand .
The 17-year - old was not injured .

An investigation revealed the victims were driving north on Riverview when unknown occupants of another vehicle fired shots into the rear of their vehicle .

Aitch was pronounced dead at the scene .
The 16-year - old was hospitalized in stable condition .
