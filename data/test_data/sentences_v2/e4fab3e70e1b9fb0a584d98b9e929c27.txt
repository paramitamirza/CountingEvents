Dad charged after toddler accidentally shoots mom - WCAX.COM Local Vermont News , Weather and Sports-
Police have charged a Lake Placid man in connection with a shooting .

Investigators say a 2-year - old boy got a hold of a loaded gun in his home at the Commons Way apartment complex Tuesday , and he accidentally shot his mother .

Erin Coffey , 24 , was shot in the buttock and the bullet exited her abdomen .
She has since been released from the hospital .
The child was not harmed .

Now , the toddler 's father faces charges .
Jonathan Coffey , 23 , of Lake Placid , is charged with criminal possession of a firearm , reckless endangerment and endangering the welfare of a child .
Police say Coffey did not have a permit for the weapon , and they say they found a second loaded pistol in the home when they searched it .

Police say the couple 's 11-month - old daughter was also present during the shooting .
