STOCKTON ( CBS13 )
–
Stockton police say that a man shot and killed Tuesday night in Stockton is related to a 3-year - old girl who was killed by a stray bullet in a car .

Officers were called to Madrid Drive near Farmington Road
around 9 p.m. There they found a 32-year - old unidentified man with a gunshot wound outside of a home .
He was rushed to a hospital in Modesto where he died .

Stockton Police say the victim is a relative of Melanie Martinez , the 3-year - old girl who was shot and killed by a stray bullet in South Stockton last week .

Police are not sure how the two victims are related .

Police do not have any suspects or a possible motive in either case .

Crime Stoppers is offering a reward of up to $ 20,000 for any information that leads to an arrest in the shooting of Melanie .
