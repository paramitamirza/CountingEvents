Oakland police investigate quadruple shooting
- A quadruple shooting occurred in Oakland on Wednesday night .

Police say it happened shortly before 11 p.m. on Mandela Parkway , near 11th Street .

Police say one of the victims is a minor .

There 's no word on the condition of the four shooting victims , or what led up to the gunfire .

At last check , police are still searching for suspects
 Additional details have not been released .
