A two - year - old is dead after an overnight shooting in Little Rock .

It happened on the 2000 block of Harrison Street on Tuesday evening , and police were dispatched to UAMS around 8:34 p.m.
 Officer Richard Hilgeman with the LRPD said that several people were riding in a car when shots were fired .

The child later died at the hospital .

Officers say they found multiple bullet casings in a six - block area around the scene .
The people who were in the car are being interviewed , but police have no information on a suspect right now .

This is the second murder within 24 hours in Little Rock .
Earlier Tuesday afternoon , a man was killed at the 200 block of North Taylor Street .
