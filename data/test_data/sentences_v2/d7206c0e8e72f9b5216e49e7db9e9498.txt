Two - year - old Toledo girl shot and killed , stepfather arrested
Toledo Police have confirmed to 13abc that a baby shot on Sunday morning has died and that an arrest has been made in the case .

At around 8:00 a.m. , officers were dispatched to St. Charles Hospital , where hospital staff told them that 2-year - old Journi Johnson was suffering from at least one gunshot wound .

Johnson was pronounced dead at the hospital .

Police then arrested 27-year - old Turhan Johnson , Journi 's stepfather , and charged him with child endangering .

The investigation is ongoing .

Anyone with information is asked to call the Crimestopper hotline at ( 419 ) 255 - 1111 .
