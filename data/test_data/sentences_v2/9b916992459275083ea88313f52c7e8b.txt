Uncle arrested in shooting death of nephew in Detroit
DETROIT ( WXYZ ) - Detroit police say they have arrested a man accused of fatally shooting his nephew following an argument on Friday afternoon .

According to police , James Graham , 48 , got into an argument with his 16-year - old nephew outside of a home in the 9000 block of Ward around 4 p.m. Friday .

During the argument , Graham allegedly pulled out a gun and fired a shot , hitting his nephew in the body .
The teen was rushed to the hospital where he passed away over the weekend .

Graham was on the run for several days before being arrested late Monday night .

Lenna Pridgen said it was her son , 16-year - old Freland , who was shot .
She tells 7 Action News he was visiting his grandmother when the incident occurred .
