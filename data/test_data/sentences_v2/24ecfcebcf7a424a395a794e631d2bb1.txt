2016-08-28
NOPD : Children victims of double shooting at New Orleans East home
A teenage boy and a young girl were victims of a double shooting Saturday night at a home in New Orleans East .

The gunfire was reported after 9 p.m. at a home in the 4500 block of Shalimar Drive , New Orleans police said .

Investigators said a 16-year - old boy was sitting on a porch when an unknown man approached him and started shooting .

In addition to the boy , an 8-year - old girl was shot while she was inside the home .

The condition of the boy and girl has not been released .

Detectives have not identified a suspect .
Additional updates are expected .

Anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers at 504 - 822 - 1111 .
