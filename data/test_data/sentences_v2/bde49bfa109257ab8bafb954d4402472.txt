Seven people injured in Oakland shooting
Seven people injured in Oakland shooting News Seven people injured in Oakland shooting Oakland police are investigating a shooting that injured seven male victims .

- Oakland police are investigating a shooting that injured seven male victims .

One has been treated and was released from the hospital and the others are being treated at a local hospital for their injuries .
The victims are between the ages of 27 and 45 , according to police .

Police say one person has been detained and is being questioned .

It happened Saturday night at about 11:45p.m .
, according to police .

Officers found multiple victims at the scene and then learned some other victims had already gone to the hospital , according to police .

No officers were shot at or injured during the incident .
However , when police officers arrived to the scene , individuals began shooting and police took cover behind their vehicles .

There were two separate scenes as part of the investigation .

One scene was near 77th and Bancroft Avenues , and the other was a few blocks away at 69th Avenue and Weld Street .

Police spent several hours searching for a suspect , using SWAT team vehicles to go through the neighborhood .

Several blocks in the neighborhood were shut down to traffic because of the police presence .

Some residents in the area told KTVU they were unable to leave their homes because of the intense search for the shooting suspect .

Police will be updating more Monday morning .
