NOPD :
Juvenile shoots other child while playing with gun
The NOPD is investigating a shooting involving two children in New Orleans East .
( Photo : WWL )

NEW ORLEANS --
The New Orleans Police Department is investigating a shooting Saturday afternoon they said involves two children .

It happened around 2:30 p.m. , in the 7600 block of Downman Road .

Police authorities said a 12-year - old was playing with a gun when it fired , hitting a 14-year - old in the left thigh .
According to police , the 12-year - old has been detained .

Officials said the injured child was brought to the hospital , but there is no word on the child 's condition .

Police said the investigation is ongoing .
