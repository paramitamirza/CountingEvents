Hopkinsville teen recovering after accidental shooting
HOPKINSVILLE , Ky. ( WKRN ) —
A Hopkinsville teen is recovering after an accidental shooting Saturday .

A 14-year - old boy was cleaning a handgun at his home Saturday night on Bethel Street when it fired into the teen ’s leg .

He was treated and released from the hospital .

It will be considered an accident and no formal investigation will be conducted .
