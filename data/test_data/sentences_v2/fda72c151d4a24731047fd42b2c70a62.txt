17-year - old dies in shooting at Coastland Center mall
 NAPLES , Fla.- A 17-year - old died after being shot in the jaw while in a car at the Coastland Center mall , the Naples Police Department said Tuesday .

The shooting happened Monday evening at a parking garage by the Macy ’s department store .

Police say 17-year - old Thomas Lane , a senior at Lorenzo Walker Technical High School , was showing his friends a 9 mm , semi - automatic handgun when he may have accidentally shot himself .

It is unknown where the teenager got the weapon , police said .

Three other people , including two teenagers , were in the car at the time , police said .
Police recovered the gun and questioned witnesses for hours while the garage was roped off .

Lane was transported to Naples Community Hospital where he later died .
