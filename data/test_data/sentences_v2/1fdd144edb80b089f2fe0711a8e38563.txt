Authorities investigate child 's accidental shooting
A 4-year - old was hospitalized after an accidental shooting in Pensacola .

The Escambia County Sheriff 's office responded to the Oakwood Terrace Apartments on Truman Avenue around 8 p.m.

That 's where authorities say the child was playing with a gun when it went off .

That child was taken to a hospital and reported to be in stable condition .

Deputies are investigating the incident as an accidental shooting .
