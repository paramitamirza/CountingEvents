Iredell County deputies : 8-year - old shoots 11-year - old during ' cops and robbers '
A man was charged after an 8-year - old boy accidentally shot his 11-year - old friend with a rifle while playing cops and robbers Sunday , Iredell County deputies said .

Daniel Stewart was charged with failure to store a firearm to protect a minor following an incident reported around 2:20 p.m. on Peterborough Drive south of Mooresville .

The 11-year - old boy was treated at the hospital for minor injuries to a cheek .

The boys ' parents told deputies the boys were playing cops and robbers in the back yard when the 8-year - old found a rifle while hiding in an outbuilding .
When the 11-year - old opened the door , the 8-year - old fired the rifle , deputies said .

Stewart was charged after deputies said they determined the gun was stored " in a negligent manner . "

Stewart was released on an unsecured bond .

No other information was released .
