2-year - old injured in shooting at Madison apartment Posted : Tuesday , March 31 , 2015 5:46 PM EDT Updated : Tuesday , April 14 , 2015 5:46 PM EDT
 A 2-year - old child was critically injured in a shooting inside a Madison apartment on Tuesday .

Police said Kendal Pinkerton was critically injured in the shooting at the Charter Village Apartments in the 300 block of Rio Vista Drive .
The girl was transported to the Children 's Hospital at Vanderbilt .

Police said it appeared the shooting was accidental , but an hour later , they were taking pictures , documenting tattoos and processing tests .

In all , two women went through this drill and one man was taken straight to an unmarked car and taken away .

Metro detectives are interviewing several people in the apartment to determine what happened .
One detective told Channel 4 that witnesses were n't giving them much to work with .

Neighbors were upset when they heard the news .

" My daughter called me and told me someone had been shot , a child had been shot , " said Patricia Henderson , a neighbor .
" It made me rush to get my grandbabies to make sure everybody 's safe . "

There are currently no charges in this shooting .
Police said it remains under investigation .
