HPD investigating double suicide at Turtle Bay Resort
 A 44-year - old man and a 17-year - old girl died Friday in a double suicide in Kahuku , police said .

Paramedics arrived at Turtle Bay Resort and found both parties on the ground in the parking lot , each with an apparent gun shot wound to the head , at about 8:30 p.m. , an Emergency Medical Services report said .

Police said the man and girl were seen inflicting fatal injuries to themselves , and a handgun was involved .
The victims were taken to a hospital where they died .

Homicide detectives were investigating the deaths as a double suicide , not a murder - suicide , police said .
