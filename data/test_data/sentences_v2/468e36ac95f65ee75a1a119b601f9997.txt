4 wounded in shooting outside East End bar
HOUSTON -- Houston Police are investigating a shooting that left four people wounded outside an East End bar .

The shooting was reported around 2:30 a.m. on Channelside near San Antonio Street after an alleged fight inside the bar spilled outside .

Police say two of the victims stayed at the scene while two others were taken to the hospital by private vehicle .

All four people remain in the hospital , one in critical condition and the other three in stable condition .

Police believe the suspect was driving a light blue Chrysler 300 .
