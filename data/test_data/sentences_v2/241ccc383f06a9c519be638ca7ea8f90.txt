Fort Worth Police Investigate Shooting Death Of 17-Year - Old
Follow CBSDFW.COM : Facebook | Twitter
 FORT WORTH ( CBSDFW.COM ) – Fort Worth police are investigating the death of a 17-year - old male after responding to a shooting call early Sunday morning .

Police were called to the 3400 block of Stonewall Lane in South Fort Worth at approximately 2:13 a.m.

According to police , when officers arrived , they found the victim in the driveway of the residence with an apparent gunshot wound .

The victim was pronounced dead at the scene .

The Tarrant County Medical Examiner identified the victim as 17-year - old Demarian Jones .

Investigators said Jones and a friend were in the driveway of his house when a man walked by and shot the 17-year - old .

Investigators do not know whether an altercation occurred before the shooting .

Police are still searching for a suspect .
