LOUISVILLE , Ky. ( WDRB ) --
Louisville Metro Police say they 've arrested a juvenile for the fatal shooting of a 15-year - old boy earlier this month .

The victim , Le Truong , died at University Hospital from a gunshot wound to the stomach around 7 p.m. on Nov. 10 , less than an hour after he was found shot in the 4900 block of S. First Street near Tenny Avenue .

WDRB has confirmed that Le Truong was a student at Iroquois High School .
Grief counselors were called in to the school .

According to the Louisville Metro Police Facebook page , Louisville Metro Police stopped a stolen car last night , Thursday , Nov. 17 , around 11 p.m. in the area of Wabash Court .
Police say the car matched a description in a wanted poster .

Police say they interviewed one of the occupants of the car -- a juvenile -- and charged that person with the murder of Le Truong .

The name of the suspect is being withheld due to the fact that the person in question is a juvenile .
