 A 12-year - old Tullahoma boy , Jakob Weeks , who was accidentally shot last Friday night , is expected to be released from Vanderbilt University Medical Center in Nashville later this week .

According to a report from the Tullahoma Police Department , Jakob was accidentally shot in the chest by another juvenile .
 “
He was shot with a .243 rifle , ”
Tullahoma Police Chief Paul Blackwell said Monday .

The incident was reported to have occurred around 5:30 p.m. Friday afternoon at Weeks’ residence on Avondale Drive in Tullahoma .

According to a police report by Officer Justin Smith , following the accident Jakob was transported to Harton Regional Medical Center by his parents and then airlifted to Vanderbilt .

According to the police chief , hospital officials say the boy is recovering and is expected to be released later this week .

Because it was ruled an accidental shooting , there will be no charges filed , according to police .

Detective Johnny Gore investigated the incident .
