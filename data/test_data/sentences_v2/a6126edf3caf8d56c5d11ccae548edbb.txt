1-year - old shot in arm in North Richmond - NBC12 - WWBT - Richmond , VA News
On Your Side
Richmond police say a 1-year - old baby was shot late Sunday after shots were fired outside of a home .

The shooting happened at a home in the 2900 block of Woodcliff Avenue , near East Brookland Park Boulevard , around 11:45 p.m.

According to the Richmond Police Department , officers were on their way to the scene because of several reports coming in to the dispatch center of random gunfire heard in the area .
Soon after , a call was received for a child shot in the same block area .

Neighbors described the gunfire as so intense , it felt like their homes were shaking , adding that it sounded like a jackhammer hitting metal .

hen officers arrived , they found a person holding a young child , suffering from an apparent gunshot wound .

Officers on scene also found that several bullets had hit the home , and one of the bullets hit the one - year - old in the arm .
The baby was inside the home at the time of the shooting and is expected to be okay .

That baby was rushed to a local hospital , with non - life threatening injuries .

The child 's relatives were also in the home but were not hit .

Police are now searching for a suspect and a motive .

Anyone with any information is urged to call Major Crimes Detective James Baynes at 804 - 646 - 3617 or Crime Stoppers at 804 - 780 - 1000 .
