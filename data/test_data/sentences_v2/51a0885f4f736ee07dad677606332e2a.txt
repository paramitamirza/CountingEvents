BABY RECOVERING AFTER GUNSHOT
Late Sunday afternoon Montgomery County Sheriff ’s Office responded to a report of an accidental shooting on Eastridge Drive in the Legends Ranch Subdivision .
The call came in from a woman who stated her husband was cleaning a gun and the child was accidently shot .
MCHD also responded and found a two - year old with a bullet wound to the hand .
The child was transported to Texas Children ’s Hospital in the Texas Medical Center .
According to Montgomery County Detectives , the child ’s father had been cleaning his 9mm pistol .
He became distracted and set it down .
That is when the child started to handle the weapon and it went off .
The child is in stable condition .
