2016-06-06
20-year - old shooting victim dies from injuries
The 20-year - old man injured in a shooting last week died from his injuries Sunday morning , according to Floyd County Chief Deputy Coroner Gene Proctor .

Owen Hicks was accidentally shot by his friend , Cody Throneberry , 20 , around 5:08 p.m. Thursday , according to Floyd County police .

The two were in a bedroom of Throneberry ’s home at 1249 Bell ’s Ferry Road when the 9mm pistol went off .

Proctor said he ruled the death accidental due to injuries from a gunshot wound to the head .

Hicks’ body has been sent to the GBI crime lab for confirmation .

Police are still investigating .
