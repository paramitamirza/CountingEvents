5 people shot outside of club on Detroit 's west side
DETROIT ( WXYZ ) - Detroit police are investigating after five people were shot outside of a club late Sunday night .

It happened at the Penthouse Lounge on the city 's west side around 11 p.m.
 Police say five people were shot as they left the lounge .
We 're told the suspected shooter got into an argument inside and was kicked out .
He then waited outside in the parking lot and opened fire on the victims , according to police .

The victims suffered non - life threatening injuries and are listed in stable condition at a local hospital .

Police are now searching for the shooter .
