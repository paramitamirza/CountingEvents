Police : 4 shot at Aurora house party
× Police : 4 shot at Aurora house party
 AURORA , Colo. —
Four people were shot at a large house party in Aurora late Saturday night .

Aurora Police say the call came at about 11:40 p.m. on a shooting in the 2300 block of Paris St.
 Spokesman Sgt .
Frank Fania says three juveniles were shot by a small caliber weapon and a bullet grazed a 4th person .

All four people were expected to recover from their injuries .

No arrests had been made in the case .
