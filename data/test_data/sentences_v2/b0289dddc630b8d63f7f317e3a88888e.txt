Sumter deputies seek person of interest in deadly shooting - wistv.com - Columbia , South Carolina
Sumter County Sheriff 's deputies are looking for a man they 'd like to question in the investigation of a deadly shooting early Wednesday morning .

Deputies are looking for 30-year - old Christopher Lavell Sinkler , of Country Squire Court in Sumter .
Investigators would like to talk to him about a shooting on Apollo Street that occurred at about 12:15 a.m.
 Arriving deputies found a 17-year - old lying on the ground .
The victim 's name has not been released by the Sumter County Coroner yet .

Witnesses told investigators they knocked on a door and the occupant tried to pull them inside the house .
As they ran , shots were fired , hitting the victim .

If you see Sinkler , do not approach him .
