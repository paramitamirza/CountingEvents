4-year - old accidentally shoots uncle with gun found on floor
A 4-year - old boy accidentally shot his uncle in the stomach after finding a gun on the floor of a house , Milwaukee police said Friday .

The shooting was reported at 11:42 a.m. Monday in the 4200 block of W. Spaulding Place , Capt .
Jason Smith said .

The man told police he was going to a gun range and that the firearm was on the floor when the 4-year - old picked it up , Smith said .

The investigation into the accidental shooting is ongoing .

An earlier version of this story incorrectly stated the time of the shooting .
