LITTLE ROCK , Ark. -
A man wounded in a shooting earlier this month has died .

In a news release issued Tuesday , the Little Rock Police Department ( LRPD ) reported that victim Fred Duhart , 38 , died early Sunday morning at UAMS as a result of his injuries .

The LRPD says its Homicide Unit was notified and the investigation continues .

Duhart was one of five people injured in the incident that occurred late in the evening on Nov. 2 at 2111 Bragg Street .
