A 15-year - old boy was critically wounded in an accidental shooting in Auburn Gresham Thursday morning , according to police .

The boy was in the 9000 block of South Bishop Street at 11:10 a.m. Thursday when he was accidentally shot in his face , said Officer Jose Estrada , Chicago Police spokesman .

He was taken to Advocate Christ Medical Center in critical condition , Estrada said .

Police could not say if the boy shot himself .
The investigation is ongoing , Estrada said .
