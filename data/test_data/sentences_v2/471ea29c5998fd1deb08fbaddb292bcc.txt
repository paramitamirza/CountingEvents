3 gang members in custody for murder of 7-year - old girl - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee
Three men have been arrested and charged in the murder of 7-year - old Kirsten Williams .
Memphis police director Toney Armstrong announced the first two arrests just after 4 p.m. Wednesday.“When I say we literally turned this city upside down , we literally turned this city upside down , " Armstrong said .
Jordan Clayton , 21 , and Branden Brookins , 19 , both face first - degree murder charges .
Armstrong said both men are known gang members .
He says they 've been arrested before .
A third suspect has since been identified .
Carl Johnson , 19 , was charged with first - degree murder in Williams ' death .
Johnson is also a known gang member .
Kirsten Williams was shot and killed while playing outside with friends on April 10 .
Armstrong said the shooting stemmed from a drug deal gone wrong .
Investigators have not determined if the shooter meant to shoot and kill the child or someone else at the home , but police say someone at the location was the intended target .
Armstrong seemed frustrated during his Wednesday address .
He blamed the justice system for allowing repeat criminals to walk the streets.“For whatever reason , he 's out on our streets again , and this time has committed the ultimate act of murder .
That murder of a 7-year - old child , " Armstrong said .
District Attorney Amy Weirich says Tennessee sentencing laws determine prison time and probation."Most of these offenders are going to get out of custody , " Weirich said .
" It 's a very small percentage of the criminal defendants we prosecute and put behind bars that actually spend the rest of their life behind bars."Williams ' visitation is Friday evening .
