Two dead in double suicide near Turtle Bay Resort
The Honolulu Police Dept .
report a double suicide occurred near the Turtle Bay Resort Friday night .

Two people — a 44-year - old man and a 17-year - old female — inflicted fatal injuries to themselves with the use of a handgun .

The incident happened around 8 p.m.

Officials say they were both found on the ground of the resort ’s parking lot , each with an apparent gun shot wound to the head .

The two were taken to the hospital where they were pronounced dead .
