2015-11-22
Four shot in North Lawndale
Four people were shot Sunday afternoon in the North Lawndale neighborhood on the West Side .

The shooting happened in the 4100 block of West Roosevelt at 1:25 p.m. , according to Chicago Police .
A male walked up and fired shots before taking off in a vehicle .

A 24-year - old man was shot in the leg and foot and a 25-year - old woman was shot in both legs , police said .
Both were taken to Mount Sinai Hospital , where their conditions stabilized .

A 22-year - old man and a 21-year - old man were both shot in the hip and taken in serious condition to Stroger Hospital .

A police source said all the men shot are documented gang members .
