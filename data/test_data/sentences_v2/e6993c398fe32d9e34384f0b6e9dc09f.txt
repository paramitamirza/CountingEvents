16-year - old shot while playing with a gun - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports
A 16-year - old was shot after a gun went off while he and his friends were playing with it .

Initial New Orleans Police Department reports indicate that the 16-year - old victim and his friends were playing with a gun when it went off .

Police have not determined who fired the gun .

The victim suffered a gunshot wound to the arm and stomach .
He was taken to a nearby hospital .

His condition is not known at this time .
