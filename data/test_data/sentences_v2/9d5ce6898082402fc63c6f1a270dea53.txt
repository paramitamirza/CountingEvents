2016-12-19
Teen girl killed in accidental shooting in Wichita
A 16-year - old girl died early Saturday after accidentally shooting herself , according to the Wichita Police Dept .

Police say emergency crews were called to a home near 13th and Grove , where they found the teenager dead in the basement .
Investigators determined she had accidentally shot herself .

The Associated Press identified the girl as Nautica Whittiker .
Police noted , though , that she does not live at the home .

Police say several people in their late teens were playing with a shotgun at the time .
The gun belongs to someone who lives in the home .

At this time , police say they do not expect any charges to be filed .
