Freeport teen shot by cousin over the weekend - WREX.com –
Rockford ’s News Leader
Freeport Police say a 17-year - old boy was accidentally shot by his cousin over the weekend .

Police say three juveniles were in a bedroom at a home in the 1100 block of South State Ave . in Freeport Saturday afternoon when they found a handgun .

Police say a 15-year - old boy was playing with the gun when it accidentally went off , shooting the 17-year - old in the abdomen .
The two boys are cousins .

The shooting victim was taken to FHN Memorial Hospital by family members .
Officers were called to the hospital , where they conducted interviews to investigate the shooting .

The victim was airlifted to OSF St. Anthony Medical Center in Rockford for treatment .
His condition was listed as critical but stable as of Sunday .

The 15-year - old teen was arrested and taken to the Kane County Juvenile Detention Center .
He has been charged with two counts of aggravated unlawful use of a firearm and one county of reckless discharge of a firearm .
He is scheduled to appear in court Monday morning .
