The attempted murder charges against an Evansville teen in connection with a shooting last week have been dropped .

According to court documents , the four counts of attempted murder against Jarnell Carter have been dropped .

Carter was accused of being involved in a late night shooting where two groups of teens were fighting on South Bedford Avenue near Cherry Street .

During that fight numerous shots were fired .
Four teens were taken to the hospital for non - life - threatening injuries .
