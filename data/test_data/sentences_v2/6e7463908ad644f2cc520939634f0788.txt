Police : Teen Accidentally Shot Himself , Gun Recovered
In Knoxville
PITTSBURGH ( KDKA ) —
Police are investigating after a teenager suffered a gunshot wound this afternoon in the city ’s Knoxville section .

It happened in the 300-block of Jacunda Street around 4:30 p.m.

According to Pittsburgh Police , the 14-year - old boy accidentally shot himself in the leg .
He was taken to UPMC Mercy Hospital for treatment .

Police at the scene say the suspect may have thrown the gun into a nearby manhole after the shooting .

Police K9 and firefighters were brought in and the gun was eventually recovered .

The teen is in stable condition .
