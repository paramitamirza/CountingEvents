 NEWBURGH –
As many as four people were shot at a Newburgh bar early Sunday morning , initial police reports indict .

City police responded to a report of a shooting at the Oasis Sports Bar at 203 Broadway .

Investigators reported at least one victim was inside the bar and three others went to St. Luke ’s Cornwall Hospital for treatment .

State Police and Town of Newburgh Police assisted at the scene .
