Police : 4 people shot at west Charlotte house party
By Tina Terry
 Police spent nine hours in a west Charlotte neighborhood searching for clues in a shooting that sent four people to the hospital Saturday .

" Terrible , terrible , ” neighbor Lorene Stinson said of the shooting .

He drove by as about a dozen officers combed through the crime scene on Bost Street .

IMAGES : West Charlotte shooting
 James Stover lives across the street , and said he was asleep just before 6 a.m. when he heard commotion and about 10 gunshots .

" Just some arguing , fussing , sound like a fight and then all of a sudden some shots , ” he said .

Police said four people were shot during a birthday party that had been going on at the house since Friday night .
They said several kids were inside at the time .

Officers searched the home , and said they found numerous bottles of opened and unopened liquor .
They believe someone was illegally selling liquor out of the house .

Police did not release a motive Saturday .
They said the victims were treated at a hospital and expected to recover .
