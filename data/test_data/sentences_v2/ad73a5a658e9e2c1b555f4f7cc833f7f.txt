Student injured after shooting self in leg at Kimball HS
Shooting reported at Kimball H.S. ( Photo : WFAA )

The Dallas Independent School District says classes will resume soon at Justin F. Kimball High School after a student accidentally shot himself in the leg .

It was the end of first period when a student " inadvertently shot himself " with a revolver , the district said .

The student was taken to a local hospital and his injuries are not life - threatening .
The district says the student arrived at school with the weapon for an unplanned meeting with administrators .

" Because of the circumstance , the procedures for the metal detectors was not applied , " the district said .

Parents arrived at the scene when their children told them they heard a shot in the school .
The students were moved to the school gym where they sheltered in place while crime scene investigators arrived .
