Lexington Boy Taken To Hospital After Accidental Shooting - LEX18.com | Continuous News and StormTracker Weather
LEXINGTON , Ky. ( LEX 18 ) -
A Lexington boy was sent to the hospital after police say he accidentally shot himself .

It happened just after 11 Thursday night on Lindberg Drive .
Police say the juvenile had the gun and accidentally fired a shot .
He walked to a nearby church to ask for help and was taken to the hospital .

Thankfully , his injuries were minor .
