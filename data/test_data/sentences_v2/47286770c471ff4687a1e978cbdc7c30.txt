2-year - old dies after being shot inside Madison apartment - WSMV Channel 4
The 2-year - old who was shot inside an apartment in Madison has died .

Kendal Pinkerton was critically injured in the shooting Tuesday at 333 Rio Vista Dr.
 Detectives are continuing to investigate the shooting and say they have not ruled out that the child may have fired the gun .
They say there are currently no charges in the case .

" My heart goes out to the family , " said former neighbor Cynthia Price .
" It 's tragic .
There 's no way in the world I would put my kids in harm , nor someone else 's kids in harm . "

Price said while living at Charter Village apartments , her children often played with Pinkerton .

" Sweet kid , " said Price .
" Handsome young man .
Just seeing a child running around and
now he 's in heaven , it 's just tragic .
It 's a tragedy .
Only people who were in that apartment at the time know the truth .
He 's in a better place .
I just hate it happened in this way at a young age . "
