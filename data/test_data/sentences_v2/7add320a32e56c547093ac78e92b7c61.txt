Native Americans showcased on Route 66
 ALBUQUERQUE ( KRQE ) –
A new project showcases how Native Americans helped shape Route 66 .
The American Indian Alaska Native Tourism Association is in Albuquerque to learn the untold stories of how Native Americans were affected by the famous highway and how they were able to use it for trade .
The goal is to encourage tourists to stop at the 27 tribal communities along the way .

Firefighters clear Bosque to reduce wildfires
 VALENCIA COUNTY , N.M. ( KRQE ) –
The Bosque may look little thinner Wednesday as officials reduce fuel for wildfires .
Valencia County firefighters will act as wood cutters as they hack away non - native species , such as Russian olive and Salt Cedar , from the Bosque .
Officials say the removals will greatly reduce the threat of wildfire in the area .
The state forestry is using state severance tax dollars to fund the project which will take place on land managed by the Middle Rio Grande Conservancy District .

Albuquerque man dies in Cibola County crash
 CIBOLA COUNTY , N.M. ( KRQE ) –
State police are investigating single vehicle crash that left one person dead early Wednesday morning .
Police say four people were inside the car at the time of the crash .

According to investigators , it appears the driver was traveling east on Interstate 40 near Bluewater Village when the vehicle drifted to the left and hit the median shoulder .
The driver then overcorrected the vehicle causing it to side skid and one of the passengers was partially ejected .

Joel Harrison , 34 , died at the scene , the other passenger suffered non life - threatening injuries .
Police say alcohol does not appear to be a factor in the crash .
