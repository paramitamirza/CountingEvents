Authorities investigate 15-year - old boy 's shooting death
Authorities are investigating the shooting death tonight of a 15-year - old boy .

Tommy Thomas , of central Toledo was shot about 8:50 p.m. in the 1000 block of Woodland Avenue , also in central Toledo , said Dr. James Patrick , Lucas County coroner .

The youth was taken to ProMedica Toledo Hospital where he was pronounced dead at 9:26 p.m.
 He suffered an apparent gunshot wound in the chest , Dr. Patrick said .
An autopsy is pending .
