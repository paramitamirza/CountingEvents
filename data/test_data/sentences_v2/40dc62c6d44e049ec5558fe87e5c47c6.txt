One of five victims in Hot Springs shooting dies
According to the Hot Springs Police Department , one of the victims in Thursday 's shooting that sent five people to the hospital has died .

23-year - old Tyler Donaldson died Sunday from his injuries related to the shooting .

The charges for Stephon Harris have been upgraded to capital murder .

Officers are continuing to follow up on leads to locate Harris .

The department is expected to release more information Monday morning .
