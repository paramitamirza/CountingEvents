Poplar Bluff teen recovering after accidentally being shot in fa - | WBTV Charlotte
Deputies with the Butler County Sheriff 's Office responded to a home on Monday , December 21 after a teen was accidentally shot in the face .

According to Sheriff Mark Dobbs , it happened in the 500 section of County Road 42222 .

When deputies arrived they found a female teen in her bedroom unresponsive with what appeared to be a single gunshot wound to her face .

Witnessed told deputies that another family member in the home was trying to unload a black powder rifle when it discharged and went through the wall , hitting Kaitlyn Pullam .

According to the sheriff , Pullam did not have a pulse at the scene .

Sheriff Dobbs said deputies and EMS personnel continued CPR on her .
He said she eventually regained a pulse at a Poplar Bluff hospital emergency room .

Pullam was airlifted to St. Louis Children 's Hospital for further treatment .
She is currently in stable condition .
