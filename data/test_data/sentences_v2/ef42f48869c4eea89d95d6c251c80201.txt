Teenager shot and killed while heading home from community center
A teenager was shot and killed while heading home from his local community center .

 A supervisor at the Beechmont Community Center said Le Truong was with friends at the facility until he left to go home .
We are told that was around 6:30 p.m. on Thursday .

Police have yet to figure out what happened next .

Truong was shot near the intersection of 1st Street and Tenny Avenue .
He was found on the sidewalk with multiple gunshot wounds .
Friends said the teen was on his bike .

Truong was transported to University Hospital and pronounced dead just after 7 p.m.
 Kevin Kinney , who works at the Beechmont Community Center , described Truong as a well - behaved , friendly teen .

" The kids really liked him .
That was evident , " Kinney said .

On Friday , he said the mood at the center was somber .

" We were n't thinking , as anybody would think , we were n't thinking it was our kids .
You never think it 's going to happen to people you know , " Kinney said .

Kinney said he saw Truong on Thursday .

The 15-year - old , who recently began lifting weights , spent a lot of time at the community center .

" Le was kind of a quiet kid .
You did n't really know that he was in the building a lot of the times because he never caused any trouble , " Kinney said .

Kinney remembers seeing Truong just before the teen left to go home .
He said the 15-year - old was wearing his signature smile .

" It was the last memory I have of him .
He was outside and he brought in the basketball goals , "
Kinney said .

Kinney said he was heartbroken to learn the teen was shot a short time later .
He expressed his condolences for Truong 's loved ones .

" It 's really a loss , you know , for the center , for his friends , and for his family , " Kinney said .

News of the crime also shocked neighbors who live near the scene .

" This is a kid - friendly neighborhood , you know , so I think it hit us doubly hard , " Janine Carr said .

Carr 's grandchildren played with Truong .

She said his murder is a wake - up call for the neighbors and city leaders .

" We have had a few isolated incidents , five years ago , eight years ago , but this is right at home , " Carr said .

Carr said police need to continue their efforts to combat violence in the area .

" The only thing I could suggest is just to stick with it , " Carr said .

On Monday , there will be an emergency meeting at the Beechmont Community Center to discuss how to keep similar crimes from happening in the future .
It begins at 7:30 p.m.
 Police have yet to make an arrest in Truong 's murder .
But detectives said there were several reports of shots fired in the area .
