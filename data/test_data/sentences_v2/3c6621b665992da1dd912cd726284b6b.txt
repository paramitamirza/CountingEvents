 Two teenagers have been shot and killed in Kalamazoo .

Police report that the 17-year - old males were found in an alley Monday night in Kalamazoo , south of Grand Rapids .

Officers initially responded to a report of one person shot .

Police say both teens were pronounced dead at the scene after 9 p.m. Monday .
Their names were not released .

The deaths are under investigation and no arrests have been reported .
