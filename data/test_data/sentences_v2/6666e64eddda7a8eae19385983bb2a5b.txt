New Suspect Arrested in Attempted Murder Case - 44News | Evansville , IN
Just days after charges are dropped against one man , another is arrested for alleged involvement in the incident .

16-year - old Eric Ogburn was arrested on attempted murder charges Tuesday night .

He is accused in a late night shooting , where two groups of teens were fighting on South Bedford Avenue near Cherry Street more than a week ago .

None of the teens were seriously hurt .

Charges against Jarnell Carter were dropped Friday .
