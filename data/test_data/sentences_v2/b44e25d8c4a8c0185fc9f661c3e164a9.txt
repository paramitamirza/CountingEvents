A 4-year - old girl was shot in the leg on Thursday night in a Durham apartment , police said .

Durham police responded around 9:30 p.m. to the 800 block of Underwood Avenue to an unknown problem .
Officers on scene were told that a child was shot and was taken to the hospital in a private vehicle .

Police said the girl was inside the apartment when the shot was fired , and the bullet struck her in the leg and grazed her arm .
The bullet entered an adjoining apartment , but no one else was injured .

The unidentified girl 's injuries are not life - threatening .

A police K-9 located a gun in the bushes near the apartment .

The investigation is ongoing .

Anyone with information should call Crime Stoppers at 919 - 683 - 1200 .
