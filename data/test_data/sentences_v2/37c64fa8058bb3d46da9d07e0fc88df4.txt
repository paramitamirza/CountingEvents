Nashville 3-Year - Old Shoots Self In Hand With Father 's Gun
HERMITAGE , TN —
A 3-year - old is recovering with non - life - threatening injuries after boy shot himself in the hand with his father 's gun Tuesday night .

Police were flagged down on Briley Parkway by someone transporting the child to the hospital .
The officers learned the incident took place at the nearby Glastonbury Apartments near Nashville International Airport .

Police entered the apartment and found the gun .
The child 's father is a licensed security guard with a handgun carry permit .

Neither the child nor his father are being identified at this time .

The police investigation is ongoing and the case will be presented to the district attorney ’s to determine if charges should be filed .

The Department of Children ’s Services is also investigating .
