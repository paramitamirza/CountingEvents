Authorities : Wednesday shooting deaths not connected ; one ruled as suicide
We 're learning more about two people found dead in local neighborhoods .

Authorities said the two shooting deaths on Wednesday night were not connected .

The Mesa County Coroner ruled the death of the 20-year - old found behind Bookcliff Liquor Store as a suicide .

About 5 miles away , authorities found a 16-year - old shot to death in the Midlands Village Mobile Home Park in Clifton , on 32 Road .
That was around 4:30 p.m. Wednesday .

That death is still under investigation , according to the coroner .

The coroner will not be releasing any names , according to a press release .
