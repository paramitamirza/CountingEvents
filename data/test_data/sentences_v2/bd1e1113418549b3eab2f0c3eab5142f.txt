A teen was arrested Friday for shooting a man several times during a robbery on a Stapleton street , prosecutors said .

Destin Burks , 17 , and another unapprehended suspect approached a 29-year - old man and his friend who were sitting on a Sands Street stoop on June 17 at about 5:10 a.m. , prosecutors said .

Burks pulled out a gun and demanded money while the other suspect searched through the victims ' pockets , prosecutors said .

The victim grabbed for the gun because he did n't believe it was real and it went off during the scuffle , grazing the man on the head , the Staten Island Advance reported .

Burks fired more shots , hitting the victim in the right hip and inner thigh , before he ran off with the other suspect , the Advance reported .

The victim was taken to Richmond University Medical Center in stable condition , the Advance reported .

Burks was arrested on Friday for the shooting and charged with attempted murder , robbery and criminal possession of a weapon at his Saturday arraignment , according to the Staten Island District Attorney 's office .

His bail was set at $ 250,000 bond and he 's due back in court on July 10 , according to online court records .

His lawyer did not respond to a request for comment .
