Police say a shooting outside a party in southern Michigan left four people wounded .

Police say someone in a vehicle opened fire on the group late Thursday in Albion .
The wounded were taken from the scene for treatment of injuries that were described by authorities as non - life - threatening .

WWMT - TV reports police say the shooting may have been related to an earlier altercation , but the circumstances are under investigation .

WOOD - TV says police are searching for a suspect .
