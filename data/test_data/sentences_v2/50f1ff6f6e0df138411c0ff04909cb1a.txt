Teen shot while playing with friend in Detroit
DETROIT ( WXYZ ) - Detroit police are investigating after a 15-year - old was shot while playing with a gun on the city 's east side .

According to police , it happened around 11 a.m. in the 5900 block of Yorkshire near Chandler Park Dr. and Outer Dr.

Police say the 15-year - old was with another 15-year - old playing with the gun .
That 's when one of the teens shot the other in the leg .

According to police , the teen who accidentally shot the other was being held for questioning .

It 's not known whether that teen will be arrested or if they will face charges .
We were not told the condition of the teen who was shot .
