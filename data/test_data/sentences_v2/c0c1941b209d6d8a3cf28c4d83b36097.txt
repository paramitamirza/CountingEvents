 KNIGHTSTOWN , Ind. —
The Henry County Prosecutor ’s Office has released information regarding the man and two children found dead in a Knightstown home on Thursday .

Preliminary autopsy results show that the two children , 5-year - old Thomas Horn and 11-year - old Corinne Horn , both died from gunshot wounds .

Their father , 48-year - old Joseph Horn , died from an apparent self - inflicted gunshot wound .

Deputies with the Henry County Sheriff ’s Department did a welfare check after neighbors said the kids never showed up for school .

Investigators say this is an ongoing investigation .

Evidence collected at the scene will be sent to the Indiana State Police Lab for testing and analysis .
