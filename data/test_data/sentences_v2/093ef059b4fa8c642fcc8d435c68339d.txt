Teen injured after accidentally shooting himself in foot
MECOSTA COUNTY , Mi ( WPBN/WGTU)--
A 14-year - old was injured after accidentally shooting himself in the foot , deputies report .

The incident happened Sunday around 10:30 a.m. in Deerfield Township .

According to the Mecosta County Sheriff 's Office , deputies were dispatched to the Big Rapids Spectrum Health Hospital for an accidental shooting that caused an injury .

Deputies say a 14-year - old male from the Comstock Park area shot himself in the foot with a .22-caliber pistol while he was staying in the Deerfield area .

The teenager was treated at the hospital for non - life threatening injuries .
