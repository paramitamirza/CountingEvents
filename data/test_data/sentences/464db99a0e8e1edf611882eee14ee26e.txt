IN : 13-year - old injured by accidental gun shot
AP NEWLINE FLOYD CO. , Ind. –
The Floyd County Sheriff ’s Department responded to a report of a juvenile who was shot in the chest . NEWLINE
It happened in the 6200 block of State Road 62 . NEWLINE
Officials said that a 13-year - old boy had suffered a shotgun wound to the right armpit . NEWLINE
The child was transported to Kosair Hospital and is said to be in stable condition . NEWLINE
The investigation revealed that the child had been hunting and while loading the equipment in the truck , the firearm accidentally discharged striking the child under the arm . NEWLINE
The Sheriff ’s Department wants to remind the public about basic gun safety : NEWLINE
· Always keep the gun pointed in a safe direction . NEWLINE
·
Always keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot . NEWLINE
· Always keep the gun unloaded until ready to use .
