8 killed , 46 wounded in Labor Day weekend shootings
Eight people were killed and at least 46 others wounded in shootings across Chicago over Labor Day weekend , police said . NEWLINE
The final shooting of the weekend turned fatal in the Old Irving Park neighborhood early Tuesday on the Northwest Side . NEWLINE
A 31-year - old man got into a fight shortly after 3 a.m. in the 3300 block of North Keeler with someone who then pulled out a gun and shot him in the chest , according to Chicago Police .
He was taken to Advocate Illinois Masonic Medical Center , where he later died , police said . NEWLINE
Monday night , a 23-year - old man was shot and killed in the East Garfield Park neighborhood , police said . NEWLINE

Myles , who lived in the 2000 block of West Maypole , was taken to Stroger Hospital , where he later died , authorities said . NEWLINE
