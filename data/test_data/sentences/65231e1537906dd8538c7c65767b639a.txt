Boy who was shot in the face has died
A 5-year - old boy who was shot in the face at a northwest Harris County home has died , a medical examiner confirmed . NEWLINE
The boy was shot after he got into his uncle ’s bedroom with two other children and found a gun Tuesday night in the 9700 block of Shady Tree Lane near Fallbrook Drive and Highway 249 . NEWLINE
The boy ’s mother rushed him to Willowbrook Methodist Hospital and he was later taken by Life Flight to Memorial Hermann Hospital in the Medical Center . NEWLINE
Investigators with the Harris County Sheriff ’s Office said it appears to be an accidental shooting but their investigation is ongoing .
