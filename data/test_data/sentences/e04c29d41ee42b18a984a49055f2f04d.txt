3-year - old among shooting victims in Central Bakersfield
Three people , including a 3-year - old and an 18-year - old , are recovering after a shooting in Central Bakersfield late Tuesday night . NEWLINE
According to Bakersfield Police , three people were shot just before 11 p.m. Tuesday in the 300 block of Donna Avenue near 4th Street . NEWLINE
Police say one victim was at the scene and was transported to a hospital to be treated for minor injuries .
Two other victims , a 3-year - old and an 18-year - old , were not at the scene when police arrived , but had already been taken to a hospital for treatment of minor injuries . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made .
