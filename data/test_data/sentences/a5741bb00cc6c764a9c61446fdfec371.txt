Police : 13-year - old girl accidentally shot , killed older brother
A teenager accidentally shot and killed her brother Friday night in northeast Houston .
Police were called to a home near the corner of Force and Crown around 11 pm for a shooting .
We've learned that the victim and a friend , both in their 20s were inside the house alone with his 13-year - old sister .
Houston police tell us the two friends were allegedly drinking and started playing with a gun .
At some point the little sister picked the gun up and accidentally shot her brother .
He was taken to the hospital where he died hours later .
The victim 's parents tell us his name was Gregory Higdon .
He was 22 .
And his mom says he was her only son .
