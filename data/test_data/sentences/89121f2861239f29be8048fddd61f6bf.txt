Four injured in bar shooting
EAST JACKSON , Tenn. —
Four people were injured outside in the parking lot of a bar after a shooting in East Jackson overnight . NEWLINE
According to the Jackson Police Department , the shooting occurred at “ B and B Bar Dive ” on North Parkway Sunday morning around 2 a.m. NEWLINE
The four victims were taken to the hospital for their injuries .
The incident is currently under investigation .
Police did not say if they are looking for suspects or if anyone is in custody .
