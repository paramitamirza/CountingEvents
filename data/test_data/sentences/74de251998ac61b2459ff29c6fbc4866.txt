4 wounded , including pregnant woman , in Morgan Park shooting
Four people , including a pregnant woman , were wounded in a shooting in the Morgan Park neighborhood Saturday , according to the Chicago Fire Department .
The fire department received a call on the shooting in the 1400 block of W. 114th Place at 11:43 a.m .
Two men in their 20s were taken to Stroger Hospital with non - life threatening injuries , according to the fire department .
Two women in their 20s were taken to Advocate Christ Hospital .
One woman who is pregnant was taken in critical condition to Advocate Christ Hospital .
The other woman was also taken to Advocate Christ Hospital with non - life threatening injures .
