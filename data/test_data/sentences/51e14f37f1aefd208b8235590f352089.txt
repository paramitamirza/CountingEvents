UPDATE : Edenton shooting suspect turned himself in , says shooting was accidental
Darian Copeland , the suspect in an Edenton shooting investigation , turned himself in to police Saturday . NEWLINE
Edenton Police say Copeland met with investigators at the Edenton Police Department and admitted to accidentally shooting Malik Etheridge from the backseat of a car . NEWLINE
Copeland told investigators his .357 magnum pistol accidentally discharged . NEWLINE
Copeland has been charged with Involuntary Manslaughter and is behind bars at the Chowan County Jail under a $ 100,000 bond . NEWLINE
