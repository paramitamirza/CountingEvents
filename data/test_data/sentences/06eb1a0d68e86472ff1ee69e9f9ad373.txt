Largo Teen Injured In Apparent Accidental Shooting
LARGO , FL — The Largo Police Department is investigating a shooting that occurred Tuesday afternoon on Starkey Road . NEWLINE
According to the agency , it received a call just after 1 p.m. from a 16-year - old boy who said he had shot himself in the left hand .
Officers and emergency crews responded to the 900 block of Starkey Road and transported the teen to an area hospital for treatment . NEWLINE
The wound , police wrote in an email to media , is not life threatening . NEWLINE
While the case remains under active investigation , authorities say the incident is believed to be accidental at this time . NEWLINE
No further details , including the teen ’s name , are being released . NEWLINE
