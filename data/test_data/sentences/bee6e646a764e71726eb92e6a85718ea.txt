Back to Gallery NEWLINE Detectives in Humble are investigating a shooting that sent four children to the hospital . NEWLINE
The shooting happened shortly after 6 p.m. Monday in the 2100 block of Grand Linden Court . NEWLINE
The wounded children 's ages were 6 , 11 , 15 and 16 , officials said . NEWLINE
They were taken by ambulance to the Texas Medical Center for treatment . NEWLINE
Their injuries were not expected to be life - threatening , Harris County Sheriff 's officials said . NEWLINE
The shooting may have been the result of a fight between more than a dozen teenagers , authorities said late Monday . NEWLINE
Harris County sheriff 's deputies at the scene did n't know if the shooting was the result of gang activity . NEWLINE
Deputies said the two younger children were " innocent bystanders . " NEWLINE
Sheriff 's deputies took nine people into custody to question them about what happened at the scene . NEWLINE
The gun used in the shooting had not been found later Monday . NEWLINE
The children all live in the neighborhood .
Sheriff 's officials said there have been past shootings in the general area but not at the location where the children were wounded . NEWLINE
Deputies are trying to locate shell casings so they can determine what weapon was used in the shooting . NEWLINE
The darkness and a Monday rainstorm that pounded the neighborhood made the task of gathering evidence difficult , officials said . NEWLINE
The search is expected to resume at first light , officials said . NEWLINE
The identities of the wounded children have not been released . NEWLINE
David Taylor contributed to this report .
