Orlando police : 9-year - old girl shot in arm by celebratory gunfire
A 9-year - old girl was shot in the arm just after midnight , possibly the result of celebratory gunfire , Orlando police said . NEWLINE
Officers responded to a church in the 800 block of South Kirkman Road after receiving reports of a shooting victim . NEWLINE
The girl , who police have not identified , had just left church when she was shot in the right arm . NEWLINE
She was treated at the scene by the Orlando Fire Department .
No bullet was recovered , police said . NEWLINE
Officers have no suspect information because the round " seemed to be from the voluminous amounts of gunfire " happening at the time , they said . NEWLINE
On Wednesday , Orange County Sheriff Jerry Demings said celebratory gunfire is illegal and dangerous . NEWLINE
" There are many ways to enjoy the New Year , but firing random shots in the air should not be among them , " Demings said in a statement . NEWLINE
Charges for firing a firearm in public or on a residential property range from a first - degree misdemeanor to a third - degree felony , Demings said . NEWLINE
