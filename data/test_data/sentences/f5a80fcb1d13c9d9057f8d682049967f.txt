Hummelstown Juvenile dead from gunshot wound
× Hummelstown Juvenile dead from gunshot wound NEWLINE DAUPHIN COUNTY , Pa. – Borough police responded to a home in the 200 block of West Main Street in Hummelstown Borough shortly before 6 pm Thursday .
They discovered a juvenile male suffering from a gunshot wound .
The county coroner was called to the home a short time later . NEWLINE
Police are withholding the name of the victim pending the notification of next of kin .
They say their investigation continues .
