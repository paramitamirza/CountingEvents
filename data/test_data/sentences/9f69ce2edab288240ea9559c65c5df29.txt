Teen hunter shot in Oconto County
OCONTO , Wis. - The Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources has confirmed that a 14-year - old boy was shot by another youth hunter while preparing to go hunting in Oconto County . NEWLINE
The incident happened at 10:00 a.m. on Wednesday , November 11 . NEWLINE
According to reports , the two boys were getting ready to go hunting when the other youth hunter , a 16-year - old boy , accidentally shot the 14-year - old in the leg . NEWLINE
The DNR states that the 14-year - old boy is expected to make a full recovery . NEWLINE
This story is still developing as the DNR further investigates the incident . NEWLINE
