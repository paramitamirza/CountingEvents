Miami man killed 2 daughters in car before shooting himself , deputies say
Three bodies were found inside a car along U.S. Highway 41 in Collier County , near the Miami - Dade County line . NEWLINE
MIAMI -
The bodies of three people found shot to death last week inside a car in Collier County have been identified as a man and his two daughters , Collier County Sheriff 's Office spokeswoman Michelle Batten said . NEWLINE
They were identified Thursday as Jesus Valencia , 47 , Jael Valencia , 18 , and Melani Valencia , 7 . NEWLINE
Detectives believe that Jesus Valencia fatally shot his two daughters Oct. 20 inside their car along U.S. Highway 41 before turning the gun on himself . NEWLINE
A passerby found him dead with a pistol in his hand , authorities said . NEWLINE
Miami police said they were asked by deputies in Collier County to conduct a welfare check at an apartment complex on Northwest 51st Avenue , where they found a woman dead inside apartment 38 . NEWLINE
The woman was identified Thursday as Neina Flores , 47 , who died just days before her birthday . NEWLINE
Miami police Lt .
Freddie Burden said detectives are investigating her death as a homicide . NEWLINE
Detectives have not said how the woman was killed or what her relationship was to Valencia and his daughters . NEWLINE
Anyone with information is asked to call police or Miami - Dade Crime Stoppers at 305 - 471-TIPS. NEWLINE
