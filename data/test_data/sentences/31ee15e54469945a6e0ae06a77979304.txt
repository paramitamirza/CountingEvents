Utah teen shoots self in leg while trying to clean handgun
A Logan teenager accidentally shot himself in the leg when he was trying to clean a handgun Saturday evening , police say . NEWLINE
The 16-year - old boy was home by himself and decided to clean a .40-caliber handgun at about 5:30 p.m. , said Logan Police Capt .
Curtis Hooley . NEWLINE
The teen had not removed the ammunition from the gun , and as he was manipulating it , he shot himself in the leg just above the knee cap , Hooley said . NEWLINE
The teen called his grandmother , who then called police , Hooley said , and the boy was taken to the hospital by ambulance . NEWLINE
Police anticipate the teen will make a full recovery .
