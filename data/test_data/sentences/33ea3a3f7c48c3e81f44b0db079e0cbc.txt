1 Dead , 2 Hurt In Denver Bar Shooting
DENVER ( AP/CBS4 ) – One person is dead and at least two people are hurt after a shooting in a Denver bar . NEWLINE
The shooting happened early Sunday at a bar called The Beach located near Federal Boulevard and 16th Avenue . NEWLINE
Police tweeted that the other victims had non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
Federal had to be closed between 17th and Colfax for hours for the investigation . NEWLINE
There were no details on suspects . NEWLINE
