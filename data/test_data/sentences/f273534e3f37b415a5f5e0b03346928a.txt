Teen arrested for homicide
Jordan Malik Dumas
( Photo : Submitted to News Journal ) NEWLINE
Escambia County Sheriff 's deputies arrested Jordan Malik Dumas , 19 , and charged him for negligent manslaughter , weapons offense and destruction of evidence for the death of a juvenile .
Dumas is being held at the Escambia County Jail with no bond . NEWLINE
Deputies responded to the 800 block of Montclair Road on Sunday in reference to a possible suicide , but after further investigation , the Sheriff 's Office determined Dumas shot and killed the juvenile . NEWLINE
