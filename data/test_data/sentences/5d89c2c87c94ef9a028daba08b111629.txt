7 people shot outside of a Memphis nightclub - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee
( WMC ) - Seven people were shot outside of a Memphis nightclub early Sunday morning . NEWLINE
Police say the victims were shot at Club 108 at 2517 Shasta , near North Hollywood Street , just after 2 a.m. NEWLINE
All seven were taken to the hospital , six in non - critical and one in critical condition . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made and police are investigating the incident . NEWLINE
