Montrose County fined for pregnancy discrimination
MONTROSE , Colo. ( AP ) —
A jury has found Montrose County guilty of discriminating against a pregnant woman . NEWLINE
The former employee was fired after seeking to work part - time during her pregnancy . NEWLINE
The ( Grand Junction ) Daily Sentinel reports ( http://bit.ly/1VFOy9A ) that Stephanie Barnett won an award of $ 306,400 mostly in backpay damages . NEWLINE
Barnett was the former director of the county ’s internal services division .
She was fired in 2013 after talking with a manager about a transition to working part - time during her pregnancy . NEWLINE
Nine days after the meeting , Barnett was fired . NEWLINE
Hours after she was fired , Barnett started to experience abdominal pain and she miscarried her baby less than week later . NEWLINE
The county said that the pregnancy was not why Barnett was fired , though jurors disagreed .
