NOPD : Children victims of double shooting at New Orleans East home
A teenage boy and a young girl were victims of a double shooting Saturday night at a home in New Orleans East . NEWLINE
The gunfire was reported after 9 p.m. at a home in the 4500 block of Shalimar Drive , New Orleans police said . NEWLINE
Investigators said a 16-year - old boy was sitting on a porch when an unknown man approached him and started shooting . NEWLINE
In addition to the boy , an 8-year - old girl was shot while she was inside the home . NEWLINE
The condition of the boy and girl has not been released . NEWLINE
Detectives have not identified a suspect .
Additional updates are expected . NEWLINE
Anyone with information is asked to call Crimestoppers at 504 - 822 - 1111 . NEWLINE
