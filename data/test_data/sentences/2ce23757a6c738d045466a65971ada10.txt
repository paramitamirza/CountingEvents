Jahaun Jones Turns Himself in to Police - Erie News Now : News , Weather & Sports | WICU 12 & WSEE
The fifth and final suspect in the summer shooting that killed two Erie teens is now in police custody . NEWLINE
Erie Police tell Erie News Now Jahaun Jones turned himself in Sunday morning .
He was arraigned then taken to the Erie County Prison . NEWLINE
Jones is one of five people charged in the fatal shooting of Elijah Jackson and Shakur Franklin .
Both teens were 16-years - old when they were shot and killed at a house party near West 29th and Summit Streets in July .
