Lake Superior water level jumps in May NEWLINE
A new report says the level of Lake Superior has made a big jump . NEWLINE
The International Lake Superior Board of Control reported Friday that the level of Lake Superior jumped 9 inches in May.
That 's more than twice the normal monthly increase and the second most for any month since 1918 . NEWLINE
Lake Superior usually rises only about 4 inches each May.
But thanks to late snow and a cold spring that held back runoff , May more than made up for the difference . NEWLINE
The lake now sits just 7 inches below its long - term average for June 1 .
It is a full 3 inches above the level at this time last year . NEWLINE
The Duluth News Tribune reports Lakes Huron and Michigan also continued their rise from near - record lows in late winter . NEWLINE

Free swim lessons offered at Wilson Park NEWLINE

S. 25th St. in Milwaukee . NEWLINE
Those who sign up will also get a free swimming cap and a free pair of goggles . NEWLINE

Parents who would like to register their children can send checks to SWAT Swim America , P.O. Box 138 , Muskego , WI 53150 .
