.. .... .... .. .... NEWLINE ALBUQUERQUE , N.M. —
A house party near Yale and Gibson SE erupted in gunfire early Saturday morning , leaving four people injured –
two critically – and one of the hosts in jail , according to Albuquerque police . NEWLINE
When officers arrived at the house in the 2600 block of Mesa SE , they found one man who had been shot in the neck and several people , including three men who live at the house , still inside .
One of the residents , Triston Whitmire , 19 , was arrested Saturday and charged with four counts of aggravated battery with a deadly weapon .
He was booked into the county jail on a $ 60,000 bond . NEWLINE
According to a criminal complaint filed in Metropolitan Court , Whitmire said he and his two roommates were having a party at their home when a couple of people he knew from the neighborhood showed up uninvited and began allowing others into the house .
He told police he saw that one was armed so he tried to shut down the party . NEWLINE
But , Whitmire said , he also put his own gun in his pocket . NEWLINE
Whitmire said he locked several people outside , but the would - be partygoers started kicking the door to get back inside and broke a window . NEWLINE
He stayed in the house but began firing at them , according to the complaint . NEWLINE

As soon as the glass was shattered , Triston removed his firearm and fired through the recently broken glass , ” the officer wrote in the complaint .

Triston fired at least four rounds outside .

Officers were dispatched to the house , which police say has a history of loud parties and gunfire , around 2:20 a.m. to find a man who had been shot lying in the front yard .
The 23-year - old had been shot in the neck and was taken to the hospital in critical condition , the complaint says . NEWLINE
A short time later , three more men showed up at the University of New Mexico Hospital .
An 18-year - old had several gunshot wounds to his abdomen , leg and pelvic area , a 21-year - old had been shot in the left leg , and a 22-year - old had been shot in the right leg , according to the complaint . NEWLINE
The scene outside the home midmorning Saturday showed signs of the abandoned party and bloodshed . NEWLINE
The front door of the home was open and what appeared to be a shirt and a shoulder bag were left covered in blood in the yard .
Five pickup trucks were parked in the front yard , and several other cars lined the streets . NEWLINE
Three trails of blood could be seen leading away from the house in various directions , and the yard and nearby streets were littered with empty beer bottles , cans and liquor bottles . NEWLINE
Lisa Gray , who lives nearby , said she awoke between 1 and 2 a.m. to hear her neighbors throwing a party . NEWLINE
Gray said she heard the gunshots and looked outside to see two or three men walking down the street toward the party house .
She said she could see the muzzle flashes as one of them began firing off several rounds .
