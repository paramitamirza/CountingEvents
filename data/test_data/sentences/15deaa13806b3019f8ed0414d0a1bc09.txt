Police : No link between 2 weekend shootings in Champaign
CHAMPAIGN , Ill. ( AP ) - Champaign police now believe two weekend shootings that left one man dead and four people wounded were not related . NEWLINE
Police initially said the Sunday morning shooting at a party at an apartment on Green Street in the University of Illinois campus ' primary bar - and - restaurant district and another a short time later may have been related . NEWLINE
But on Tuesday police spokeswoman LaEisha Meaderds said interviews with witnesses about the second shooting led investigators to conclude there was no link . NEWLINE
One man , 22-year - old George Korchev of Mundelein , died in the first shooting and three other people were injured .
Police said all four were just passing by the apartment where the large party was held . NEWLINE
The later shooting west of the campus injured another person . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made . NEWLINE
