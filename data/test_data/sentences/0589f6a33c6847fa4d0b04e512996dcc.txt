Mass shooting at Houston nightclub , six injured
Houston Police investigate a mass shooting at the Empire nightclub in downtown Houston .
( Photo : KHOU 11 News ) NEWLINE HOUSTON - Six people were injured in a mass shooting at a downtown nightclub early Sunday morning , according to Houston Police . NEWLINE
Around 1 a.m. police responded to a call about a shooting at Empire nightclub on San Jacinto at Elgin and found six people shot . NEWLINE
According to HPD , two groups got into an argument inside the club earlier Saturday night . NEWLINE
One of the groups left the club and as the second group was standing outside , the first group drove by and opened fire , shooting randomly . NEWLINE
The victims ' injuries were not life - threatening and they are all in stable conditions at local hospitals , police said . NEWLINE
No other information is available at this time .
This investigation is ongoing . NEWLINE
