Five people hospitalized after shot by man in Bronx : police
Michael Ip/for New York Daily News
A 20-year - old man is fighting for his life , as four others he was with on a Bronx street were hospitalized after a man approached the group and started firing , police said Sunday . NEWLINE
Guns were blazing in the city over the weekend , sending the number of shootings for the week skyrocketing - doubling over the same period last year . NEWLINE
A 20-year - old man was fighting for his life and four others were hospitalized after a man with a gun approached them on a Bronx street and began firing , police said Sunday . NEWLINE
The group was standing on Creston Ave .
near East 184th St. in Fordham Heights when the suspect walked up to them and fired several times about 9 p.m. Saturday , cops said . NEWLINE
A 20-year - old man who was shot in the chest was rushed to St. Barnabus Hospital in critical condition , officials said . NEWLINE
Another 20-year - old man who was shot in the buttocks was also taken to St. Barnabus Hospital , while a 21-year - old man who was shot in the ankle brought himself to the hospital , sources said . NEWLINE
Two men , 26 and 21 , who were both shot in the leg , brought themselves to New York - Presbyterian The Allen Hospital , sources said . NEWLINE
Nearby video footage of the scene showed the suspect firing several shots before fleeing , authorities said . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made and the investigation is ongoing .
A motive for the shooting was not immediately available . NEWLINE
Shootings are up in the city compared to the same time last year , as there were 44 shooting incidents with 53 victims this week as of 12 a.m. Sunday compared to 21 shooting incidents for the same week in 2014 , data shows . NEWLINE
There were eight murders this week , compared to five murders in the same time period last year , according to NYPD data . NEWLINE
