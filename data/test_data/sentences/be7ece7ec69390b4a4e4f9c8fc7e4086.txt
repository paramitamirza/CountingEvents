Charges are filed in the accidental shooting of a 3-year - old girl
Jessie Ross III , 26 , allegedly owned the .38-caliber revolver that several children found in a house in the 3800 block of Chestnut Avenue before the little girl was wounded in the abdomen Jan. 7 .
Ross is charged with endangering the welfare of a child and unlawful possession of a firearm .
