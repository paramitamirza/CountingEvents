4 injured in North Memphis shooting - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee
Four men were injured in a North Memphis shooting early Thursday morning . NEWLINE
Police rushed to the scene at Ayers Street and Griffith Avenue around 12:30 a.m. where they found bullet holes in the back window of a car . NEWLINE
It 's unclear if the victims were sitting in the car or standing nearby , but all four men were shot or grazed by bullets . NEWLINE
Police said none of the victims knew who shot them . NEWLINE
According to officers , two of the victims refused to be treated at the scene .
Two others were taken to Regional Medical Center in non - critical condition .
The men , all between the ages of 18 and 23 , are all expected to be OK. NEWLINE
Police have no suspect information at this time . NEWLINE
