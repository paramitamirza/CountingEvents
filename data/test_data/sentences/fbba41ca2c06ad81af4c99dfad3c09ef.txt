Police confirm child shot in Enid shooting
An 11-year - old boy suffered gunshot wounds in Enid Monday afternoon , city police said . NEWLINE
The child was taken to a hospital with gunshot wounds to his finger and shoulder .
His injuries are non - life - threatening , police said . NEWLINE
A police spokesperson said the shooting happened just about 3:15 p.m. in the 300 block of South Seventh Street . NEWLINE
Witnesses told police a man possibly wearing a red hat , red T - shirt and blue jeans or shorts exited a dark blue or grey car and opened fire on South Seventh toward West Street . NEWLINE
Officers are looking for the gunman . NEWLINE
