No Bail for Teen Charged in Shooting That Killed 1 , Injured 6
No Bail for Teen Charged in Shooting That Killed 1 , Injured 6 NEWLINE A 16-year - old boy arrested on a murder charge in connection with a weekend shooting that killed a man and left six other people injured by gunfire in the Boston suburbs , faced a judge Tuesday . NEWLINE
The suspect , identified as Emanuel Marrero in court , was ordered to be held without bail in the Chelsea shooting that killed killed 19-year - old Pablo Villeda . NEWLINE
Marrero , who 's from Lynn , faces a slew of charges including murder and armed assault with intent to murder .
He 's being charged as an adult . NEWLINE
Police say the shooting happened early Sunday during a party in a vacant apartment .
Seven other people were taken to the hospital with injuries not considered life - threatening .
One of them had injuries unrelated to the gunfire . NEWLINE
The surviving shooting victims are three females , ages 15 , 17 and 18 , and three males , ages 15 , 18 and 22 . NEWLINE
Marrero 's next court date is April 8 .
He 's being represented by Jonathan Sharpiro . NEWLINE
