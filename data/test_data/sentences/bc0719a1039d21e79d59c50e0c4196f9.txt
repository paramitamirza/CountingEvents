Man , 17-month - old child dead in apparent murder - suicide near ...
By Alison Grande NEWLINE
A 17-month - old child was the victim of an apparent murder - suicide in Mason County Thursday night .
According to court documents that toddler killed was Tiberius Durcan .
His mother posted a picture of the toddler on Facebook on Friday . NEWLINE
Mason County sheriff 's deputies were called to the home in the 700 block of East Road of Tralee 6 miles north of Shelton at about 6 p.m. after a woman called 911 asking for a welfare check on her estranged husband and two small children . NEWLINE
The 32-year - old woman said she had recently separated from her husband , 52-year - old Sean Durcan from Shelton , and had sought refuge at a local motel in the Shelton area .
She said she was worried about her estranged husband and two children since she was unable to contact them .
She told deputies that Durcan had become despondent because of the separation .
Durcan was served with divorce papers on Wednesday . NEWLINE
Deputies who went to the home smelled a strong odor of gasoline coming from the house .
Investigators believe Durcan planned to set the house on fire .
When deputies went inside , they found a 4-year old boy sitting in the living room area .
He was taken out of the house and reunited with his mother . NEWLINE
During a search of the home , deputies found Durcan dead from an apparent self - inflicted gunshot wound .
A deceased 17-month - old child was found with him . NEWLINE
The investigation into the deaths is ongoing .
An autopsy is scheduled for Tuesday to determine how the toddler was killed .
