Three people were killed and 12 were injured during a shooting at a South Los Angeles birthday party early Saturday morning , according to the Los Angeles Police Department . NEWLINE
Dozens gathered to celebrate a birthday at a home that was used as a non - permit makeshift restaurant in the 2800 block of Rimpau Boulevard when shots rang out around 12:28 a.m. , police said .
A man and woman were taken in custody as " persons of interest , " according to the L.A. Times , but authorities said they were still searching for at least one suspect late Saturday night . NEWLINE
At some point during the night , three people , possibly two men and one woman , left the party and returned with guns , Sgt .
Frank Preciado of the LAPD said . NEWLINE
The group opened fire and shots were returned , according to police .
Three people were pronounced dead at the scene and 12 were transported to local hospitals in various conditions . NEWLINE
The death toll is expected to rise , officials said , as the victims ' injuries range from serious , to critical , to grave . NEWLINE
Details were not released on the deceased , but police said all the victims were adults . NEWLINE
" When officers responded here , my understanding was that there were at least 50 people running from the location of 2800 Rimpau Boulevard , " said Preciado .
" This a very chaotic situation at this point . " NEWLINE
Police found a semi - automatic handgun at the home , located in the West Adams District . NEWLINE
Witnesses told KTLA that people were having a good time and enjoying the party when the violence began . NEWLINE

" I went to go hide next door by the trashcan .

It is currently unknown what caused the shooting .
