Mass shooting in Oakland leaves 7 victims injured
Seven men were shot on an East Oakland street Monday evening , leaving two of them in critical condition , a police spokeswoman said . NEWLINE
Police received multiple calls of shots fired near the corner of 96th Avenue and Olive Street at 6:08 p.m. and the city 's ShotSpotter system alerted officers to a hail of gunfire in the area , police spokeswoman Officer Johnna Watson said . NEWLINE
Responding officers found multiple victims in the sidewalk area there , and more victims turned up at a hospital a short time later . NEWLINE
A 29-year - old man and a 31-year - old man were left in critical condition .
Five other men -- two 29-year - olds , a 28-year - old , a 27-year - old and a 23-year - old -- are expected to survive their injuries , Watson said . NEWLINE
Police have blocked off streets and are still processing evidence tonight to determine who shot the seven victims and why . NEWLINE
Watson said police believe that people in the area witnessed the shooting and are urging anyone with information to contact Oakland police .
