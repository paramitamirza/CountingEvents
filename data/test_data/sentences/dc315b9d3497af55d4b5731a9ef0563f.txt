Boy in stable condition after accidental shooting
JACKSONVILLE , Fla. - NEWLINE An 11-year - old is in stable condition after he was accidentally shot by his twin brother in their Westside home about 9 p.m. Wednesday . NEWLINE
According to the Jacksonville Sheriff 's Office , twin boys got their mother 's long - barrel rifle when one of the boys accidentally shot the other in the home on Redrac Street . NEWLINE
According to the police report , the bullet entered the boy 's arm and exited from his back .
He was taken to UF Health Jacksonville with non - life - threatening injuries . NEWLINE
Police were told one of the boys pointed the gun down the hall and pulled the trigger as his brothers walked into the hall .
The boy told police he did not see his brother . NEWLINE
Authorities say the mother went shopping , leaving the boys home alone .
Detectives said the family and brother were cooperating in the investigation . NEWLINE
The Florida Department of Children and Families is also investigating .
