As the Elmira Police Department continues investigating the shooting incident from early Thursday morning at Patrick 's Pub on College St. in Elmira , a ' person of interest ' has been named . NEWLINE
WETM - TV in Elmira reports that 27-year - old Zecary Banks of Elmira has been named as the ' person of interest ' in the shooting incident which resulted in five people being hospitalized . NEWLINE
Of the five shooting victims , four remain in the hospital as of 3:45 p.m. NEWLINE Banks is described as a black male , standing 6'01 " tall , weighing 220 lbs .
He was last seen wearing a black shirt , light colored shorts and a red baseball hat . NEWLINE
