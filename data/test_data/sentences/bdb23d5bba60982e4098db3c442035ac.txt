Teen charged in deadly Christmas Day shooting in Henderson County NEWLINE by WBBJ 7 Eyewitness News Staff NEWLINE HENDERSON COUNTY , Tenn. —
A juvenile is now facing a homicide charge in a deadly Christmas Day shooting . NEWLINE

According to the documents , the juvenile told deputies he picked up a gun he believed was not loaded , pointed it at the victim and pulled the trigger . NEWLINE
The juvenile ’s name has not been released . NEWLINE
Deputies responded around 10 a.m. Sunday to the 6000 block of Middleburg Road .
They say the victim and suspect were sitting on a couch at a mobile home at the time of the shooting . NEWLINE
