7 men shot at Antioch house ; shooter remains at large
Seven men who were shot at a house in Antioch in Northern California this past weekend are expected to survive their injuries , authorities said . NEWLINE
Five of the men were discovered by police officers responding to a call around 11:50 p.m. Saturday in the 5500 block of Cedar Point Way .
The men were transported to local hospitals for injuries that did not appear to be life - threatening , according to the Antioch Police Department . NEWLINE
While officers were at the scene of the shooting , two more men arrived at area hospitals seeking treatment for non - life - threatening gunshot wounds .
It was later determined that they were victims of the same incident but had left the location before police arrived . NEWLINE
Officials said not all the men lived at the house and were having a " get together " when the shooting occurred . NEWLINE
An investigation into the identity of the shooter remained ongoing Monday . NEWLINE
