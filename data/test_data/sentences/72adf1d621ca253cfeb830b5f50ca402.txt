4 people wounded in Kalamazoo shootings , police say
Four people have been wounded in early morning shootings in western Michigan 's Kalamazoo . NEWLINE
Public safety officers heard numerous shots about 1:45 a.m. Saturday and later found two women suffering gunshot wounds to their legs . NEWLINE
A man later arrived at an area hospital with wounds to his face and leg . NEWLINE
Another man was shot in the leg about 2 a.m. Saturday in another area of the city . NEWLINE
Police say none of the victims suffered life - threatening wounds .
No arrests have been made .
