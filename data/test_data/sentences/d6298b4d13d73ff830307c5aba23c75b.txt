CLEVELAND-- Two people were shot during an attempted robbery at a barbershop on Cleveland 's west side Wednesday night . NEWLINE
It happened at about 7:40 p.m. at Prodigy Cuts at West 59th Street and Storer Avenue . NEWLINE
Cleveland police said employees exchanged fire with three teens in self defense . NEWLINE
A 26-year - old man was shot in the back , while a 4-year - old boy suffered a gunshot wound to the foot .
Both victims were taken to MetroHealth Medical Center in stable condition . NEWLINE
While at the scene , police learned a 16-year - old was dropped off at the hospital in a private vehicle .
Police said he is one of the three suspects and the other two remain at large . NEWLINE
