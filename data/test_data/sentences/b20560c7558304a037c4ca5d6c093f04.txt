MIAMI GARDENS , Fla. ( WSVN ) --
Authorities are investigating after a security guard was fatally shot at a Miami Gardens night club . NEWLINE
The shooting happened outside of Mint Lounge , just after 4 a.m. , Tuesday , along Northwest 25th Avenue and Miami Gardens Drive . NEWLINE
According to police , the guard was among four people shot .
The three other victims were transported to nearby hospitals . NEWLINE
Police are now looking for the shooter . NEWLINE
If you have any information on this shooting , call Miami - Dade Crime Stoppers at 305 - 471-TIPS.
