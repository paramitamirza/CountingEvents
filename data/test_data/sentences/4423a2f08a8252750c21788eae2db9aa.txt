Infant among victims in S. Houston drive - by shooting
Police are searching for clues after five people , including an infant , were injured in a drive - by shooting in south Houston late Sunday .
( Photo : Metro ) NEWLINE HOUSTON –
Police are searching for clues after five people , including an infant , were injured in a drive - by shooting in south Houston late Sunday . NEWLINE
According to the Houston Police Department , officers responded to a call of a drive - by shooting in - progress at 9 p.m. in the 5100 block of Idaho . NEWLINE
When HPD arrived at the scene , they found several people injured with gunshot wounds .
None of the wounds were life - threatening . NEWLINE
The first call of the drive - by shooting came from a woman with an infant , who got into a private vehicle to flee the scene .
They were transported to a local hospital . NEWLINE
Police said two other male victims were transported from the scene on Idaho to a local hospital .
One other male victim walked up and told police he was injured . NEWLINE
Homicide detectives were on the scene investigating .
They said they do not know if the victims were in a house or sitting outside yet . NEWLINE
Police found multiple shell casings in the street . NEWLINE
Detectives did release a brief description of two suspect vehicles –
one dark - colored SUV and a white Ford F-150 pickup truck . NEWLINE
Police are hoping more people with information will come forward . NEWLINE
