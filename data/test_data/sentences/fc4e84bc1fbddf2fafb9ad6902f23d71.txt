Oakland :
Four hospitalized after shooting
OAKLAND -- Police in Oakland are investigating a shooting Thursday night that sent four people to the hospital . NEWLINE
Officers with the Oakland Police Department responded at 10:08 p.m. to a shooting in the 1600 block of 7th Street and learned four people had been shot . NEWLINE
The victims , identified as three men and one woman , were transported to the hospital . NEWLINE
No other information on the incident was immediately available . NEWLINE
