Union County deputies are investigating a death that appears to be the result of an accidental shooting , Sheriff David Taylor said . NEWLINE
Taylor said deputies were initially called to the hospital Saturday night after receiving reports that a man had accidentally shot himself in the foot . NEWLINE
Deputies soon learned that the victim , 31-year - old Devane Carter , had shot himself in the thigh and the bullet had exited from the lower part of his leg near the ankle .
His girlfriend rushed him to the hospital , which occurred at Carter 's resident on Buffalo Street , Taylor said . NEWLINE
The bullet ruptured a major artery causing the victim to lose too much blood .
Taylor said Carter died during surgery . NEWLINE
An autopsy has been scheduled to help determine a cause of death .
Taylor said the shooting happened in a car outside Carter 's home .
Crime scene technicians are still processing the vehicle . NEWLINE
Taylor said Monday that the shooting appears to have been accidental in nature but the case was still being investigated . NEWLINE
