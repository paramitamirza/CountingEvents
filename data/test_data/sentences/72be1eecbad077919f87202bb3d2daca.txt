Police Ask for Help ID’ing Gunman Who Shot 4 , Killing 1 , Outside Downtown L.A. ’s Standard Hotel
Surveillance video was released Monday that police said captured the man who shot four people , killing one , outside The Standard hotel in downtown Los Angeles . NEWLINE
Police have asked anyone who recognizes the man to come forward . NEWLINE
The fatal incident began with an argument outside the hotel , located in the 500 block of South Flower Street , around 2:30 a.m. on Sunday , said Mike Lopez , with the Los Angeles Police Department ’s Media Relations . NEWLINE
The argument escalated to a physical altercation before one of the men pulled out a handgun . NEWLINE
Three people were shot in front of the hotel , and the gunman chased a fourth person to the back of the hotel , Lopez said . NEWLINE
All four victims were transported to the hospital , where the man who was chased was pronounced dead . NEWLINE
He was identified by the L.A. County coroner ’s office Monday as 21-year - old Thomas Johnson . NEWLINE
The condition of the three surviving victims was not known . NEWLINE
Police initially said one victim was located about 10 blocks from the hotel , at the intersection of West Olympic Boulevard and South Hill Street .
It was unclear how he or she got to the location . NEWLINE
The gunman was described by police as a black man in his 20s , with a mustache and black hair styled in cornrows .
He was wearing a black trench coat the night of the shooting . NEWLINE
The surveillance footage released came from the hotel elevator , which he took to get to a rooftop nightclub , Lopez said .
