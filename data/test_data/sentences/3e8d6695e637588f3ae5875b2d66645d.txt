Police : Teen accidentally shoots mom in Evansville parking lot - Tri - State News , Weather & Sports
A woman was taken to the hospital after an accidental shooting . NEWLINE
It happened Thursday night around 9:40 in the parking lot of the east side Target store . NEWLINE
Police say Stacey Craven and her 15-year - old son were sitting in their vehicle when the teen removed a gun from under a seat . NEWLINE
According to police , the teen pulled the trigger thinking the gun had an external safety that would prevent the gun from firing .
However , the gun had a trigger assembly safety and went off when he pulled the trigger . NEWLINE
EPD says Craven was shot in the back . NEWLINE
She was taken to St Mary 's Medical Center for treatment .
Police say her injuries are not believed to be life threatening . NEWLINE
Another one of the Craven 's sons tell us doctors are happy with the progress his mother has made so far , but she will have surgery again Saturday morning . NEWLINE
According to her son 's Facebook page , the woman underwent surgery last night and today she is able to respond to questions . NEWLINE
Since police say the incident appears to be an accident , no charges have been filed . NEWLINE
