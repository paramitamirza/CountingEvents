Orange , NJ , shooting leaves 1 dead , 3 injured
Prosecutors say a shooting in northern New Jersey left one person dead and three others wounded .
Gunfire broke out just before 10 p.m. Thursday in Orange on Taylor Street .
According to the Essex County Prosecutor 's Office , Davon Jones , 17 , of Orange , was shot and taken to University Hospital , where he was pronounced dead .
The three other victims all suffered non - life threatening gunshot wounds .
They were treated at area hospitals , according to the prosecutor 's office .
Investigators did n't disclose what led up to the shooting .
The investigation continues and no arrests have been made .
