2008-10-02
LA firefighters tackle house fire
On Tuesday , September 30 , 8 Companies of Los Angeles Firefighters , 2 LAFD Rescue Ambulances , 1 Arson Unit , 1 Hazardous Materials Team , 1 EMS Battalion Captain , 2 Battalion Chief Officer Command Teams and 1 Division Chief Officer Command Team , a total of 56 Los Angeles Fire Department personnel under the direction of Battalion Chief Ralph Ramirez , responded to a Greater Alarm Structure Fire at 501 North Citrus Avenue near the Hancock Park area of Los Angeles . NEWLINE
Firefighters arrived quickly to discover a large 2 story single family home on a corner lot in the final stages of construction and fully involved with fire . NEWLINE
An aggressive and well - coordinated assault with handlines and heavy streams allowed firefighters to confine the flames to the 3,000+ square foot structure of origin . NEWLINE
A single family home to the north sustained door and window damage only from radiant heat .
Residents of that location , as well as the owners of the burning structure staying in a remote guest house on their property , were untouched by the flames . NEWLINE
The fire was extinguished in just 31 minutes .
There were no injuries . NEWLINE
Preliminary structural loss is estimated to be at least $ 500,000 .
The cause of this intense early morning blaze , which nearly consumed the building , remains under investigation .
