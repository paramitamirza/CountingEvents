3 charged in Denver killings linked to illegal pot
DENVER - NEWLINE Denver 's district attorney has charged three men with murder , saying violence broke out after they came from Oklahoma to buy marijuana illegally at a northeast Denver home . NEWLINE
In a statement Thursday , Denver DA Mitch Morrissey said 32-year - old Chadd Evans , 23-year - old Dejuan Jones and 24-year - old Deashaun Turrentine were each charged with two counts of first - degree murder , five counts of attempted first - degree murder and one count of aggravated robbery . NEWLINE
The DA says Evans was taken into custody in Tulsa , Oklahoma and Turrentine in Las Vegas .
Jones is at large .
Attempts to seek comment from the two in custody were unsuccessful because no attorneys were on record for them . NEWLINE
The DA says one of the three men shot and killed last week was an associate of the three suspects . NEWLINE
