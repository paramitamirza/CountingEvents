( Source : Christopher 2X ) NEWLINE LOUISVILLE , KY ( WAVE ) - Crystal Teasley was celebrating her son Kendall 's 21st birthday at Cole 's Place when he and six others were shot at the club early Sunday morning . NEWLINE
Teasley said Kendall , her nephew and a woman in their group were hit when someone opened fire in the club at about 3 a.m. NEWLINE
The music was loud so she did n't hear the shots , but she saw everyone in the club take off in a stampede . NEWLINE
When she noticed Kendall had been shot three times , she loaded him up in her car and drove him to the hospital .
He is expected to recover . NEWLINE
So are all of the others who were shot that night , including a woman eight months pregnant and her then - unborn baby . NEWLINE
The baby was born at the hospital on Sunday and is " doing ok , " Teasley said . NEWLINE
The incident brought back harsh memories for Crystal , who said her husband , Michael Teasley , was murdered at Club 502 in 2005 . NEWLINE
Crystal Teasley said did not see the shooter , who is still on the loose and has not been identified . NEWLINE
