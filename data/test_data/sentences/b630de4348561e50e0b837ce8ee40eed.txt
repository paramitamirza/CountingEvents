16-year - old murder suspect tops most wanted list
by : Nicole Carr Updated : Nov 9 , 2016 - 6:57
PM NEWLINE CLAYTON COUNTY , Ga. - A 16-year - old boy who was the number one spot on Clayton County ’s most wanted list has turned himself in . NEWLINE
Police say Stanley Dixon shot and killed Cedric Clark , a junior at Drew High School , on Nov. 2 . NEWLINE
Clark , a popular student athlete and JROTC cadet , was found dead in the backyard of a home near his school . NEWLINE
TRENDING STORIES : NEWLINE
Dixon was believed to be with his father , Stanley Brooks , who is a convicted armed robber and drug dealer . NEWLINE
On Wednesday evening , Brooks handed Dixon over the authorities in Clayton County . NEWLINE
Police warn that both men should be considered armed and dangerous . NEWLINE
Dixon is the first juvenile to ever be placed on the most wanted list . NEWLINE
Detectives have obtained a murder warrant for Dixon and are asking anyone with information on the murder or his whereabouts to call 911 . NEWLINE
