Man arrested in shooting that wounded Humble kids
Investigators say the suspect , Timothy Caston , is out of jail after posting a $ 30,000 bond .
( Photo : HCSO ) NEWLINE HUMBLE , TEXAS - The Harris County Sheriff 's Office says a 19-year - old man has been charged in the shooting that wounded two kids and two teens in Humble on Sept. 26 . NEWLINE
Investigators say the suspect , Timothy Caston , is out of jail after posting a $ 30,000 bond . NEWLINE
Investigators say Caston was at his home in the Kenswick Meadows subdivision at about 6 p.m. when a group of teens approached and an argument ensued .
Caston allegedly got a handgun and shot at the teens . NEWLINE
An 18-year - old male and his 15-year - old sister were grazed by bullets , and two children , ages 6 and 11 , were struck in the lower leg area across the street from Caston ’s home . NEWLINE
Witnesses identified Caston as the shooter , and he was later taken into custody .
He ’s charged with aggravated assault with serious bodily injury , according to the HCSO. NEWLINE All four victims were treated and released after making a full recovery . NEWLINE
Caston has a court date of Nov. 1 . NEWLINE
