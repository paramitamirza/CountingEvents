2015-06-14
1 dead , 3 wounded in Camden shooting
A shooting in Camden has left one man dead and three others hospitalized .
It happened after 12:00 a.m. Sunday in the 2800 block of Saunders Street .
Police say a man in his 40s was shot in the back and died at Cooper University Hospital .
Three others , all in the 20s , were also taken to Cooper .
One was listed in critical condition , the other two had stable vital signs .
There was no immediate word what led to the shooting .
Police have made no arrests .
