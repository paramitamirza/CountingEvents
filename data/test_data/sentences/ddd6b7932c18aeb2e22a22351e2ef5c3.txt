Officials have identified the boy as Zachary Woodruff of Smithfield , Utah . NEWLINE
The incident was reported at 7:29 p.m. in an area about one mile southeast of Red Spur , which is the far southeast corner of the forest service area of Cache County .
A Cache Valley boy was sitting on the floorboard of four wheeler trying to stay warm and out of the wind when a hunting companion pulled next him and got off his four wheeler , according to a press release from Cache County Sheriff 's Office . NEWLINE
The hunting companion was removing his rifle from the front of his four wheeler when the gun went off and struck the victim , the release states . NEWLINE
Life Flight was called as well as the sheriff 's office , Cache County Search and Rescue and Logan City Paramedics were en route . NEWLINE
The victim was pronounced dead at the scene by a Life Flight crew . NEWLINE
No charges are pending at this time as it appears to be an accident . NEWLINE
