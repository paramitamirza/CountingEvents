Lott says shooting was not " senseless " but planned and thought o - KAUZ - TV : Newschannel 6 Now | Wichita Falls , TX
The Wichita Falls man who is the suspect in the shooting of two teenage girls on Friday told police during an interview that his actions were not “
senseless , ” as he planned it out and spoke to the devil about what he was going to do . NEWLINE
According to Kody Lott ’s arrest affidavit , Lott confessed to police that he shot Lauren Landavazo and Makayla Smith .
He told police he knew both girls and the fact that one of them was already in a relationship made him angry . NEWLINE
Lott went on to recount the events of that day , telling police that he was upset that he did not have a girlfriend .
Lott said he went to his parent ’s residence at the Fountain Gate Apartment complex and took his step - father ’s .22 caliber rifle , waited at a window for the girl to pass by ; then drove his pickup truck up to the girls and opened fire . NEWLINE
Lott told police he shot Lauren first and then shot Makayla “ a couple of times , ” then he made his way back to Lauren before leaving the area and attempting to dispose of the rifle on a rural road .
He then said he returned to the field to collect the weapon and return it to his parent ’s apartment . NEWLINE
Lott was arrested on Sunday after a call to police said he fit the description of the suspect in media reports .
Officers found spent .22 caliber shell casings in his car , the affidavit states . NEWLINE
Lott is being held on over of more than $ 4 million in bonds and is charged with murder and aggravated assault . NEWLINE
