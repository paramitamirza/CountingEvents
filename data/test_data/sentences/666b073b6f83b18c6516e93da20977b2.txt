Two year old accidentally shoots 12 year old - WSMV Channel 4
Metro police say that a woman brought her two kids to her sisters home in Donelson to play . NEWLINE
Police say she had a pistol in the side compartment of her car . NEWLINE
While unloading the kids , police say a two year old got a hold of the gun and shot a 12 year old as he was helping to unload the other kids .
The bullet went through his leg and out his groin . NEWLINE
He was taken to Vanderbilt Medical Center with serious injuries , but is expected to live .
No charges have been filed as of yet . NEWLINE
