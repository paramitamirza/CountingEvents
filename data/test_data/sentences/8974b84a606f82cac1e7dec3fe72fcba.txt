× 15-year - old accidentally shoots self while taking selfie –
Police NEWLINE OVERLAND , MO ( KTVI ) –
The Overland teen who shot himself in the chest and died last week was attempting to take a selfie when the gun went off , the Overland Police Department said . NEWLINE
The shooting happened Friday , June 3 around 7:30 p.m. in the 9500 block of Page .
The teen , identified as DaMontez Jones , was home alone with his three other brothers at the time of the incident .
His mother and her fiancé were not at the residence . NEWLINE
According to our partners at the St. Louis Post - Dispatch , police believe Jones found the gun on the top shelf in a bedroom closet .
The firearm did not have a safety lock . NEWLINE
Jones was rushed to a local hospital with a gunshot wound to the chest and later died . NEWLINE
Investigators believe the shooting to be accidental and give credence to the selfie theory because Jones’ older brothers told their father about the idea and because police found a cellphone near the teenager . NEWLINE
Police have sent the cellphone to a lab to see if they can find any pictures of Jones with the gun .
