11-year - old boy wounded after shooting himself in Milwaukee
An 11-year - old boy suffered a self - inflicted gunshot wound early Thursday morning on Milwaukee 's north side , police said . NEWLINE
The shooting occurred in the 3600 block of N. 12th St. just before 7:30 a.m. , and the child was taken to a hospital .
