Iowa City police , DHS investigating shooting
IOWA CITY - The Iowa City Police Department and Department of Human Services are investigating a weekend shooting involving a juvenile victim . NEWLINE
Iowa City police were called to Mercy Hospital at 7:44 p.m. Sunday for a report of a juvenile who had suffered a gunshot wound to his leg .
Responding officers determined the 10-year - old boy had been shot in his lower leg .
His injuries were determined to be non - life threatening . NEWLINE
Police determined the shooting happened at a residence in the city .
Officers went to the residence and recovered the gun used in the shooting . NEWLINE
The shooting has been described as an “ isolated incident ” by police , who say there is no threat to the general public .
The police department and DHS are continuing to investigate .
