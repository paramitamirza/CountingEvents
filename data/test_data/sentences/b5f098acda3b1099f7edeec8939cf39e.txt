Police Investigate Quadruple Shooting In Fairhill
September 28 , 2014 9:51 AM NEWLINE PHILADELPHIA ( CBS ) — Police are investigating a quadruple shooting in North Philadelphia . NEWLINE
It happened around 5 a.m. Sunday along the 2800 block of Hope Street in the city ’s Fairhill neighborhood . NEWLINE
Investigators tell CBS 3 that a group of people got into an argument and then gunfire erupted . NEWLINE
Police say four people were shot and wounded , including a 17-year - old boy who was pistol whipped in the head , and then shot in the ankle .
A 30-year - old was shot three times in the legs .
A 41-year - old man suffered a graze wound to the back and a 37-year - old man was struck in the thigh . NEWLINE
The victims were all taken to Temple University Hospital in stable condition . NEWLINE
Witnesses tell police the shooter was a Hispanic man .
He and an accomplice drove off in a dark - colored vehicle . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made . NEWLINE
