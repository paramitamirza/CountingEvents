4 seriously wounded in West Garfield Park shooting
4 seriously wounded in West Garfield Park shooting NEWLINE Four men were seriously wounded in a shooting Sunday morning in the West Garfield Park neighborhood . NEWLINE
Three of the men were standing outside with a group of people about 3:30 a.m. in the 100 block of South Springfield when someone fired shots from a moving vehicle , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
A 23-year - old man was shot in the right thigh , and a 25-year - old man was shot in the lower back and right thigh , police said .
Both were taken to Stroger Hospital in serious condition . NEWLINE
A 37-year - old man suffered gunshot wounds to the torso and both legs , police said .
He was taken to Stroger Hospital by a friend in serious condition . NEWLINE
The fourth victim , 26 , was shot in the face while sitting in a parked car , police said .
He was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital in serious condition .
