6 teens wounded at birthday party shooting
A birthday celebration in Miami went out with the wrong kind of bang , leaving a number of teenage guests with gunshot wounds .
Three suspects were arrested on Wednesday in connection to the sweet sixteen shooting . NEWLINE
Miami - Dade Police charged 18-year - old Michael Melton , 18-year - old Dontavious Byrd , and 20-year - old Travonte Berry with Attempted Premeditated Murder with use of a deadly weapon . NEWLINE
Around 10 p.m. Saturday , teens were celebrating a sweet sixteen birthday party at a home located at 20582 southwest 119 Avenue .
All was fun and games until suspect Melton approached one of the victims asking , " Are we gonna fight or shoot it out ? " NEWLINE
Trying to avoid any confrontation , the victim walked away . NEWLINE
According to investigators , Melton , along with Byrd and Berry , then jumped into a car and began to shoot into the crowd . NEWLINE
Up to six minors were injured in the drive - by shooting .
Their ages range from the youngest being just 12-years - old to the oldest being 17-years - old . NEWLINE
All of the injured teens were immediately taken to area hospitals - with four transported to Jackson South , one to Baptist Hospital in Kendall and the last airlifted to Kendall Regional .
The six are in stable condition , and expected to be okay . NEWLINE
Police are asking for the communities help with their investigation .
