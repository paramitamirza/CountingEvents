2 children killed in Clayton County home invasion
by : Matt Johnson Updated : Oct 23 , 2016 - 12:21 AM NEWLINE JONESBORO , Ga. - Police are investigating a deadly home invasion involving children in Clayton County . NEWLINE
Officers found Tatiyana Coates , 11 , and her brother Daveon , 15 , shot to death inside the house on Libby Lane in the Marlborough subdivision in Jonesboro Saturday morning . NEWLINE
There were several other children , as young as 6 years old , inside the house at the time .
One of them called 911 after witnessing the shooting around 5 a.m. NEWLINE
Police say it appears two families live in the home and no parents were there at the time of the shooting . NEWLINE
