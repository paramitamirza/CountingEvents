2016-06-27
One dead , three injured in SW Houston shooting
One person is dead and three are injured after a shooting in SW Houston on Sunday evening .
The Houston Police Department homicide division is investigating the incident , which took place after a fight broke out at the pool at the Napoleon Square Apartments in the 6000 block of Elm Street .
When authorities arrived , an adult male was dead .
Another adult male , an adult female and a 4-year - old boy were transported to area hospitals for medical care .
All three are expected to survive .
Neither the status of the shooter nor their relationship of the victims are known at this point .
