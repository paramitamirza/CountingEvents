Teen accidentally shot by friend in east Las Vegas - FOX5 Vegas - KVVU
Police said they are looking for the person who accidentally shot his friend in east Las Vegas early Friday morning . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 3 a.m. at an apartment near Lamb Boulevard and Vegas Valley Drive . NEWLINE
According to Las Vegas Metro police , a 17-year - old boy was being shown a handgun by his 18-year - old friend , when the gun fired .
The boy was wounded in the leg . NEWLINE
The victim was taken to Sunrise Trauma to be treated , police said .
He is expected to recover . NEWLINE
