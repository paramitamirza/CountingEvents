Teenager killed , 6 wounded in Baltimore shootings
A teenager was killed and six other people were injured in separate shootings in Baltimore . NEWLINE
In the most recent shooting , officers were called to an area hospital at 2:15 p.m. Wednesday for a report of a walk - in shooting victim .
Police they found an 18-year old man with a gunshot wound to his arm and foot .
The victim told investigators he was standing at the intersection of Brunswick Street and Wilkens Avenue when he was shot by someone .
Detectives ask anyone with information to call 410 - 396 - 2488 or 866 - 7-LOCKUP. NEWLINE Police said a 16-year - old was fatally shot in the chest in a quadruple shooting at about 1:12 a.m. Wednesday in the 3500 block of West Garrison Boulevard .
Also injured in the shooting were three men , ages 19 , 21 and 22 , who were shot in the hand , the arm and grazed in the head , respectively .
Anyone with information on the case is asked to call police at 410 - 396 - 2100 . NEWLINE
A 21-year - old man was shot in the hand and leg around 1:25 a.m. in the 2100 block of Germania Avenue .
Police said they found him in the 2800 block of Kennedy Avenue .
The victim was taken to the hospital and is in stable condition .
Anyone with information on the case is asked to call police at 410 - 396 - 2433 . NEWLINE
Finally , a 52-year - old man was shot in the abdomen around 10:20 p.m. Tuesday in the 5000 block of Denmore Avenue .
He was taken to the hospital and is in stable condition .
Anyone with information on the case is asked to call police at 410 - 396 - 2466 . NEWLINE
Police said anyone with information on the shootings can also call Metro Crime Stoppers at 866 - 7LOCKUP. NEWLINE
After the latest violence , there have now been 168 homicides reported in Baltimore this year compared to 106 at this point in 2014 .
There have also been 328 shootings reported in 2015 compared to 168 at this point last year .
