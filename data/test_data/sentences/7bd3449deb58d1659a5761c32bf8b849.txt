Jonesboro police : 15-year - old killed in accidental shooting
JONESBORO , Ark. ( AP ) - Police in Jonesboro say a 15-year - old boy was killed in an accidental shooting in north Jonesboro . NEWLINE
Police say officers were called shortly before 10 p.m. Saturday to the home just north of the Arkansas State University campus and found the boy with a gunshot wound to the head and lying on a bed .
The boy was taken to a hospital where he later died . NEWLINE
The teenager 's name was not immediately released . NEWLINE
The Arkansas Democrat - Gazette reports ( http://bit.ly/1IkKfgv )
that police spokesman Paul Holmes said the shooting is believed to be accidental and no charges are expected . NEWLINE
