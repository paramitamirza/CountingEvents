Police identify 10-year - old killed in memorial service shooting
INDIANAPOLIS ( WISH ) —
A 10-year - old boy was killed and three other people were wounded in a shooting Saturday evening on the near north side of the city , according to the Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department . NEWLINE
Police have identified the juvenile killed in the shooting as 10-year - old De’Shaun Swanson . NEWLINE
It happened in the 3900 block of Graceland Avenue , near the intersection of 38th Street and Meridian Street . NEWLINE
Officers arrived to the scene around 8:30 p.m. and found two male adults and two male juveniles had been shot . NEWLINE
Family and friends were gathered for a memorial service when the shooting took place . NEWLINE
The memorial service was being held for the grandmother of the 10-year - old ’s best friend , family members told 24-Hour News 8 . NEWLINE
All four people were shot in the front yard of a home , police said . NEWLINE
Swanson was transported to Riley Hospital for Children at IU Health in critical condition and later died , police said . NEWLINE
A 16-year - old was shot and is in stable condition , police say .
One adult was taken to Riley in stable condition .
The second adult was transported to Sidney and Lois Eskenazi Hospital in stable condition . NEWLINE
According to police , the others injured in the shooting are expected to survive . NEWLINE
Police have not released any suspect information , but they say the shooting does not appear to be random . NEWLINE
