Multiple people shot outside Hamden bar - WFSB 3 Connecticut
Hamden police are still searching for the suspect they believe is responsible for an early Saturday morning shooting . NEWLINE
That shooting broke Saturday at Highwood Bar and Grill around 1:30 a.m. NEWLINE Responders found one male , who was shot twice in the legs and two female victims , who were also shot once in the legs inside the bar .
Police said the victims were transported to Yale New Haven Hospital where they were treated for non life - threatening injuries . NEWLINE
A neighbor nearby , Carletha Bynes told Eyewitness News that she heard the gunshots ring out , " I was sitting there and I had just walked in the house .
And two minutes later , I heard three gunshots . " NEWLINE
A fourth victim was grazed by a bullet but walked away from the scene , according to police . NEWLINE
" People were running to their cars and they were leaving the bar , " said Bynes .
" It was like , really crazy . " NEWLINE
Police on patrol said the suspect , who they describe is a black male who is 5'10 and around 210 lbs , took off running toward Morse St. NEWLINE They believe he has glasses and was wearing a red , white , and black plaid shirt with a fisherman hat at the time of the shooting . NEWLINE
Bynes told Eyewitness News that the neighborhood was once quiet and safe , but now she hopes that going forward there will be extra security . NEWLINE
" I hope that there 's more cops around the area to secure the area because sometimes I have my grandson on my porch with me , and it 's kind of scary . " NEWLINE
Anyone with information is urged to contact Hamden Police Department at 203 - 230 - 4000 . NEWLINE
