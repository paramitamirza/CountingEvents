2014-10-09
3 shot in van outside Historic Ebenezer Baptist Church
A van riddled with bullets pulled up to the lobby of the Marriott Marquis late Wednesday night with three wounded people inside . NEWLINE
Police say a white Mercedes Benz van pulled up to the downtown hotel , located on Peachtree Center Avenue , with six people inside .
Three of them had non - life - threatening gunshot wounds . NEWLINE
Police said several victims were lying down on the floor inside the hotel and in the driveway of the hotel . NEWLINE
The victims were identified as Akeem Lee , 24 , Gabriel Adesamya , 26 , Shareen Valentino , 21 , and Rapheal Acosta , 23 . NEWLINE
" I saw the white van pull up and a bunch of guys jumped out and started yelling and stuff , " hotel guest Jeff Johnson said . NEWLINE
Lee told police he and the group were riding in the van when , out of nowhere , someone began shooting from outside the van , according to police .
Police said Valentino described hearing what she thought were firecrackers and being pulled down by her friend in the van .
She was shot in the back and was alert and conscious when medics rushed her to Grady Memorial Hospital , according to police . NEWLINE
The shooting took place on Auburn Avenue , just outside Historic Ebenezer Baptist Church in Sweet Auburn .
Crime scene techs found at least nine shell casings and shattered glass in the street . NEWLINE
Investigators said the victims were entertainers who performed at a club on Edgewood Avenue Wednesday night and may have had a dispute with the shooter . NEWLINE
Police say the incident is under investigation and that no arrests have been made .
