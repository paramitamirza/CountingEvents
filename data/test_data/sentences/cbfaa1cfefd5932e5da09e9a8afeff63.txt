SAN ANGELO , Texas ( AP ) - Sheriff 's officials say a 2-year - old girl has been shot and killed at a mobile home in West Texas . NEWLINE
The Tom Green County Sheriff 's Office says deputies responding to a 911 call Sunday found the girl 's body at the home in San Angelo .
The office statement sent Monday does n't name the girl or detail the circumstances of her death . NEWLINE
The statement says the girl 's mother and three siblings aged 1 , 3 and 6 years were also at home when deputies arrived . NEWLINE
An autopsy has been ordered . NEWLINE
San Angelo is 200 miles northwest of Austin . NEWLINE
