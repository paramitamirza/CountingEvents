Four men injured in drive - by shooting in Englewood NEWLINE Four men were injured in a drive - by shooting Thursday afternoon in the Englewood neighborhood on the South Side . NEWLINE
They were all shot at 4:09 p.m. in the 6800 block of South Throop when someone in a passing vehicle opened fire , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
A 43-year - old man was shot in the abdomen and was taken in serious condition to Advocate Christ Medical Center in Oak Lawn , police said . NEWLINE
Two 30-year - old men suffered gunshot wounds to the foot and were taken to St. Bernard Hospital , where they were both listed in good condition , police said . NEWLINE
Another 30-year - old man was shot in the hand and leg , and took himself in fair condition to Holy Cross Hospital , police said .
