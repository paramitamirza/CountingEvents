Report : Woman , son involved in Roundup murder - suicide identified - KXLF.com | Continuous News | Butte , Montana
The woman who shot her son , then herself in an apparent murder - suicide in Roundup last week have been identified . NEWLINE
Colorado news outlets The Denver Post and television station KDVR identified the woman as Sara Atkinson , 41 , and her 5-year - old son Kenny Kreuscher . NEWLINE
Musselshell County Sheriff Mike Thomas on Thursday said both died from a gunshot wound to the forehead . NEWLINE
Thomas said autopsies were performed Tuesday and Wednesday and he is confident from the report that the woman killed her child and then herself . NEWLINE
The motive is not entirely clear , Thomas said , but the woman was involved in a heated custody battle with the boy 's father . NEWLINE
KDVR and The Denver Post spoke with the boy 's father , Ken Kreuscher , who described the situation growing more dire as time passed . NEWLINE
" In recent months , the battle had escalated , and Kreuscher told caseworkers the boy 's mother was growing increasingly paranoid , believing there were cameras and bombs in her home , " the Post reported . NEWLINE
Thomas said the mother and child were visiting relatives in Roundup when the shooting occurred . NEWLINE
Relatives were awoken in the early morning hours Monday to the sound of the gunshots and came out to find the mother and child shot . NEWLINE
Thomas said they are still awaiting a toxicology report . NEWLINE
The family of the mother and child called 911 and the victims were transported to the Roundup Memorial Hospital . NEWLINE
They were then transported to a Billings hospital for care , where they were both pronounced dead . NEWLINE
Thomas said the Roundup community and his department are reeling from the devastating event . NEWLINE
It was reported Kreuscher attempted to reach his son before he died at a Billings hospital . NEWLINE
Kenny Kreuscher would have turned 6-years - old on Saturday .
