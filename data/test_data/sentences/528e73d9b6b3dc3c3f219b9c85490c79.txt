Caretaker booked after 8-year - old shoots , injures brother Posted : Wednesday , May 27 , 2015 7:13 AM EDT Updated : Wednesday , May 27 , 2015 7:13 AM EDT NEWLINE BATON ROUGE , La. ( AP ) - Deputies say an 8-year - old Baton Rouge boy shot his 17-year - old brother as the teen was playing a video game earlier this month . NEWLINE
On Tuesday , East Baton Rouge Parish sheriff 's deputies arrested the adult who was supervising the child at the time of the shooting . NEWLINE
The Advocate reports 23-year - old Robert Payne was booked with child desertion and negligent injuring . NEWLINE
Sheriff 's deputies say the May 15 shooting shattered the teen 's spine and he 's paralyzed from the waist down . NEWLINE
Online jail records did not list an attorney for Payne . NEWLINE
