Man arrested in 5-year - old 's shooting
Patrick Glenn ( Photo : Charlotte - Mecklenburg Police ) NEWLINE
CHARLOTTE , N.C. -- Police have made an arrest in the case of a child shot last month just north of uptown Charlotte . NEWLINE
According to Charlotte - Mecklenburg Police , officers responded to the 7-Eleven at the corner of Dalton Avenue and North Tryon Street a little after 9 p.m. on Thursday , November 10 . NEWLINE
When they arrived , officers found a 5-year - old girl suffering from a gunshot wound .
The girl was taken by MEDIC to CMC , where she underwent surgery . NEWLINE
CMPD says that a vehicle with three adults and the child was traveling on 12th Street when a vehicle pulled up to them .
A person in that vehicle then fired multiple shots into the car , hitting the child . NEWLINE
Investigators identified 27-year - old Patrick Glenn as the suspect in the shooting .
Police say that Glenn has outstanding warrants for assault with a deadly weapon , discharging a firearm into occupied dwelling causing serious injury , and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon . NEWLINE
Police say it appears the suspect knew one of the adult males in the victim vehicle . NEWLINE
Glenn was arrested Tuesday and taken into custody .
He 's charged with assault with a deadly weapon inflicting serious injury , four counts of assault with a deadly weapon , discharging a firearm into occupied vehicle in operation causing serious injury and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon . NEWLINE
