13-year - old girl injured in Columbia shooting - wistv.com - Columbia , South Carolina
The Columbia Police Department is investigating a shooting that left one child in the hospital . NEWLINE
According to a post on CPD 's social media account , the shooting happened early Saturday morning on the 2400 block of McKinley Street near Farrow Road . NEWLINE
Officers say a 13-year - old girl was grazed by a bullet in the shooting .
She was taken to a local hospital with what appeared to be non - life threatening injuries , according to officers . NEWLINE
CPD continues to investigate the shooting .
