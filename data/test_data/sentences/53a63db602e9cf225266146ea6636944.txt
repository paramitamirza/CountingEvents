2014-07-12
3 Dead , 22 Wounded In Weekend Shootings
( STMW ) –Three people were killed and at least 22 others have been wounded in shootings across the city since Friday evening . NEWLINE
The most recent fatal shooting happened Saturday afternoon on the North Side . NEWLINE
William Lewis , 28 , was standing on the street in the 1300 block of West Devon Avenue about 3:20 p.m. when someone approached on foot , produced a weapon and shot him in the back , police said . NEWLINE
Lewis , of the 4700 block of North Hermitage Avenue , was taken to Presence Saint Francis Hospital in Evanston , where he was pronounced dead at 3:58 p.m. , authorities said . NEWLINE
Police said the shooting may have been gang - related . NEWLINE
Early Saturday , a 50-year - old woman was seriously injured and her son was killed in a domestic - related shooting in a Chicago Lawn neighborhood apartment on the Southwest Side . NEWLINE
About 1:15 a.m. , a 47-year - old man got into an argument with a mother and son in the 6200 block of South Rockwell Street , left the apartment , then returned with a gun and shot both people in the chest , police said . NEWLINE
Adarrius Thompson , 27 , was dead at the scene , according to the Cook County Medical Examiner ’s office .
He lived in the block where the shooting happened , authorities said . NEWLINE
His mother was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital in serious condition , police said . NEWLINE
The exact relationship between the 47-year - old — who is not in police custody — and the two others was not immediately known , police said . NEWLINE
Friday night , a man was killed and three other people were injured in a possible drive - by shooting shortly after 8 p.m. in the Austin neighborhood . NEWLINE
The shooting happened in the 5200 block of West Quincy Street , police said . NEWLINE
Damien Bryant , 32 , was shot in the head and pronounced dead at the scene at 8:13 p.m. , according to police and the medical examiner ’s office .
Bryant lived in the block where the shooting happened , authorities said . NEWLINE
A 30-year - old woman who suffered multiple gunshot wounds in the shooting showed up at Loretto Hospital and was later transferred to Stroger Hospital in “ stable ” condition , police said . NEWLINE
Another male , whose age was not known , was shot in the right arm and refused medical treatment , police said .
He did not cooperate with authorities , police said . NEWLINE
A 41-year - old man took himself to West Suburban Medical Center in Oak Park with a gunshot wound to his hand , police said .
He was listed in “
stable ” condition . NEWLINE
Additionally , a 37-year - old man was taken from the scene of the shooting to West Suburban with a broken foot , police said .
He was listed in good condition . NEWLINE
Police believe the shooting may have been a drive - by attack . NEWLINE
The most recent nonfatal shooting happened about 1:15 a.m. Sunday in the North Lawndale neighborhood on the West Side . NEWLINE
A 16-year - old boy was standing on a sidewalk in the 3600 block of West 15th Street when a male walked up , pulled out a handgun and shot him in the leg , police said .
He was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital in “ stable ” condition , police said . NEWLINE
Saturday evening , a 19-year - old man was shot in both legs near Midway Airport in the West Elsdon neighborhood . NEWLINE
A friend drove him to Mount Sinai about 7 p.m.
The man told police he had been shot in the 4500 block of West 58th Street .
He was listed in good condition early Sunday . NEWLINE
At least 16 other people have been wounded in other citywide shootings since 6 p.m. Friday . NEWLINE
