Seven people are shot in Oakland , including one person in critical condition .

Seven men were shot on an East Oakland street early Monday evening , causing the police chief of the city to decry the violence and ask for the public 's help in finding the shooters . NEWLINE
" This is really kind of outrageous , " Oakland police Chief Sean Whent told the Oakland Tribune after arriving at the scene .
" Six o'clock at night is n't that late at night .

Seven People Shot in Oakland NEWLINE Police in Oakland , California say seven people were shot and wounded on Monday evening .
There are no immediate reports of suspects or arrests .
( Published Tuesday , Nov. 26 , 2013 ) NEWLINE
Whent told the newspaper he was unsure at this point if all were the intended targets , or if some of the victims - all men between the ages of 23 and 31 - were innocent bystanders . NEWLINE
As of Tuesday morning , one of the men was still in critical condition and six were in stable condition at both Highland Hospital in Oakland and Eden Medical Center in Castro Valley .
They all were expected to live . NEWLINE

Still , the number of gunshots fired - and right about dinnertime - had many people on edge . NEWLINE
Police Officer Johnna Watson said the city 's ShotSpotter system and multiple phone calls of shots fired near the corner of 96th Avenue and Olive Street sent police scrambling to the intersection just after 6:08 p.m. NEWLINE
Officers found multiple victims in the sidewalk area there , and more victims turned up at a hospital a short time later . NEWLINE
No motive has been established and no suspects have been arrested . NEWLINE
