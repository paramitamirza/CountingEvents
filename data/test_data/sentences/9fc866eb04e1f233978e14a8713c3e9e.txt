Police find man wanted in Hot Springs shooting of 5
Police have located Stephon Harris , the 20-year - old man wanted in the shooting that killed one person and injured four others in Hot Springs last Thursday . NEWLINE
Harris surrendered to authorities at 9:55 p.m. Wednesday , according to a news release from the Hot Springs Police Department .
Investigators had offered a reward earlier that evening of up to $ 5,000 for information leading to his arrest , Arkansas Online previously reported .
Police thanked the community for the tips they received over the past week . NEWLINE
Harris originally faced a capital - murder charge after Tyler Donaldson , 23 , died Sunday from injuries he sustained during the shooting , but it was changed to first - degree murder .
Harris was also wanted on four counts of first - degree battery . NEWLINE
Police had previously arrested Anthony Camden , 23 , the other man they say shot five people , on five counts of first - degree battery , as reported by the Hot Springs Sentinel - Record .
One of Camden 's charges was also changed to first - degree murder after Donaldson died . NEWLINE
An argument about money between one of the victims , a 17-year - old boy , and Camden led to the shooting , the Arkansas Democrat - Gazette previously reported . NEWLINE
