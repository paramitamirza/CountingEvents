Police investigate deadly shooting near the University of Florida
Early Thursday morning , the Gainesville Police Department responded to a shooting at the 800 block of NW 4th Place near the University of Florida . NEWLINE
According to reports , two black male suspects came into the residence wearing masks and began shooting from the entrance late Wednesday night . NEWLINE
Police say that 35-year - old Antonio Mason was shot in the chest , while holding his 11-month - old infant daughter during the home - invasion .
The child was shot in the arm .
Another man , 21 , also suffered a gunshot wound to the arm . NEWLINE
Officers who arrived on the scene immediately began CPR and emergency aid on Mason .
He was later pronounced deceased on - scene .
Both the second male and child were transported to local hospitals .
Police say the injuries are non - life threatening . NEWLINE
According to police , 20 other people that were in the house when the shooting occurred were fleeing the area .
The two unidentified suspects fled the scene before police arrived .
Witnesses say that one occupant tried to run after the suspects , but was unsuccessful . NEWLINE
Authorities are investigating but do not have a clear motive for the attack .
GPD detectives , K9 units and the Joint Aviation Unit Helicopter are working the scene . NEWLINE
According to reports , authorities believe that this was not a random attack .
Police ask that residents remain inside for their safety . NEWLINE
Patrols will be increased , while officers attempt to identify the shooters . NEWLINE
