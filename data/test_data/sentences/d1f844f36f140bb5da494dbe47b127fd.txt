2-Year - Old Killed in Little Rock Shooting
( via KARK ) -- A 2-year - old child died after gunshots were fired at a car on the city 's south side . NEWLINE
Around 8:30 p.m. Tuesday , Little Rock Police officers received a call from UAMS saying they were treating a two - year - old for gunshot wounds . NEWLINE
The child was reportedly in the car with her 27-year - old mother , the mother 's 37-year - old cousin , and four other children when an unknown car drove by and fired shots at them . NEWLINE
The mother 's cousin , who was driving the car , said that she and the child 's mother were driving toward Van Buren St. when they turned a corner and heard several gunshots as a car drove by . NEWLINE
The child 's mother said she was in the back seat of the car , holding her daughter , at the time of the incident .
When she realized her daughter had been shot , she drove her to the hospital . NEWLINE
The child died in the emergency room from her injuries shortly after 9 p.m. NEWLINE
Police say no one involved in the incident saw a suspect . NEWLINE
Officers were able to pinpoint the area of the shooting as the 2000 block of S. Harrison St. , near the crossing of Charles Bussey and Harrison . NEWLINE
Officers said they found several empty shell casings in that area . NEWLINE
Police say the motive of the shooting is unknown . NEWLINE
