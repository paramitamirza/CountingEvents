Police identify man fatally shot while on North City porch Tuesd - KMOV.com
Man fatally shot , four others injured during shooting in the 2500 block of Semple Avenue Tuesday night NEWLINE ST. LOUIS , Mo. ( KMOV.com ) –
A late - night shooting Tuesday resulted in multiple victims , one suffering fatal injuries , in north St. Louis . NEWLINE
Around 11:15 p.m. , an unknown suspect opened fire , while sitting inside a four - door sedan , on a group of people who were on the front porch of a home in the 2500 block of Semple Avenue , hitting three women and two men . NEWLINE
Dominque Young , 21 , of the 2500 block of Semple , sustained multiple gunshot wounds and was pronounced dead at the scene .
The other four victims were taken to the hospital with various gunshot wounds and were listed in stable condition . NEWLINE
The shooting was one of five reported overnight across the St. Louis area . NEWLINE
Tuesday night ’s shooting occurred in the same block where a man in his 20 ’s sustained multiple fatal gunshot wounds Sunday .
Authorities have not said if they believe the two shootings are related . NEWLINE
The identity of the deceased victim has not been released . NEWLINE
Authorities have not released any information regarding suspects . NEWLINE
The investigation in ongoing . NEWLINE
Anyone with information regarding the shooting is asked to contact CrimeStoppers . NEWLINE
