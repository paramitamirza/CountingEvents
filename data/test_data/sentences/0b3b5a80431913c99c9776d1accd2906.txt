Pittsburgh man to stand trial for Allentown homicide
An Allentown man will stand trial on charges he shot and killed a 20-year - old man earlier this month . NEWLINE
Kenneth Murphy , 18 , had a preliminary hearing today before District Judge James Motznik on charges that he killed Christopher Rodriguez , 20 , in the 1500 block of Arlington Avenue on Aug. 1 . NEWLINE
According to police , Mr. Rodriguez was shot multiple times .
Witnesses told police that Mr. Rodriguez had said he was going to sell Mr. Murphy a gun , and that the men had spoken on the phone 20 minutes before the shooting . NEWLINE
In addition to criminal homicide , Mr. Murphy is charged with receiving stolen property , possession of a prohibited firearm , tampering with evidence and robbery .
