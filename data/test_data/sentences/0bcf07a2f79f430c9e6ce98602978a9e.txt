4 Wounded In Shooting Outside Lancaster Nightclub , 2 Suspects At Large
LANCASTER ( CBSLA.com ) —
Four men were wounded in a shooting outside of an Antelope Valley nightclub Saturday morning . NEWLINE
The shooting occurred just after 2 a.m. outside of the Industry Theater , 43145 Business Center Parkway , in Lancaster , according to the Los Angeles County Sheriff ’s Department . NEWLINE
Officials said fight began among patrons inside the nightclub and spilled out into the parking lot where someone fired a gun into the crowd . NEWLINE
The victims — ages 22 , 26 , 29 and 37 — were taken to local hospitals with non - life threatening injuries , sheriff ’s officials said . NEWLINE
Two suspects fled the scene in a unknown vehicle .
No description was given . NEWLINE
