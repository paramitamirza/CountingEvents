Norwich Police : Person killed in accidental shooting
NORWICH , N.Y. ( WBNG ) -- The City of Norwich Police Department says an accidental shooting claimed a life Thursday . NEWLINE
According to officers , the shooting was reported shortly after 6 p.m. at a home on Division Street . NEWLINE
The police department says upon arrival , responding officers and the Norwich Fire Department found a victim with an apparent gunshot wound . NEWLINE
Police say the victim was transported to Chenango Memorial Hospital where they later died from their injuries . NEWLINE
The Norwich Police Department and the New York State Police Forensic Investigation Unit ( FIU ) are currently conducting an investigation into the circumstances surrounding the shooting .
The Norwich Police Department has been in contact with the Chenango County District Attorney 's Office , according to a news release .
