Arrest warrants issued for suspects in Houma quadruple shooting Updated : Thursday , October 29 , 2015 2:43 PM EDT NEWLINE Arrest warrants were issued for two men wanted in connection to a Houma shooting that left one person dead and three others wounded . NEWLINE
The Houma Police Department issued warrants for 21-year - old Elward Williams and 24-year - old Anthony Sylvester . NEWLINE
Sylvester , who was initially identified Wednesday as a shooting victim , remain hospitalized .
Upon his release , he will be charged with 2nd degree murder , attempted 2nd degree murder and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon . NEWLINE
Williams is wanted for 2nd degree murder and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon . NEWLINE
A 19-year - old male who was shot in the shoulder was released from the hospital after receiving treatment .
A 25-year - old who was shot in the face remains hospitalized in critical condition . NEWLINE
