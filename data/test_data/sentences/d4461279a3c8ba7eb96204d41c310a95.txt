5 year old boy grazes self with bullet , Metro police investigate
SAVANNAH ( WSAV )

Savannah - Chatham Metropolitan Police Department say Friday morning at an apartment on the 12000 block of Middleground Road a 5 year old boy accidentally shot himself .
Police say the boy found the gun in the apartment and pulled the trigger .
Police say the boy was grazed by the bullet and received non - life threatening injuries .
The child was taken to Memorial Univesity Medical Center , The 5-year - old ’s mother and two juvenile siblings also were home during the incident .
