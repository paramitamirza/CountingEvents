Pittsburgh police investigate two Hill District shootings
One man was killed and three others were injured in two shootings this afternoon in the Hill District , Pittsburgh police said . NEWLINE
City public safety spokeswoman Sonya Toler said one man died in an alley near the scene of a shooting on Dinwiddie Street near Reed and Wick streets that occurred about 5:20 p.m. The Allegheny County medical examiner 's office identified him as Christopher Richardson , 20 , of West Oakland .
He was shot in the torso , Ms. Toler said . NEWLINE
Mr. Richardson was driving a silver Volvo sedan with two other men inside when it was struck by several bullets , Ms. Toler said .
The vehicle 's front passenger - side window had been shot out , she said . NEWLINE
About the same time , officers found a man outside in the 200 block of Burrows Street with injuries to his lower back that Ms. Toler said were possibly caused by fragments from a separate shooting .
Zone 2 police are investigating that incident . NEWLINE
Two of the wounded men were taken to UPMC Mercy , and the other was taken to UPMC Presbyterian .
Ms. Toler did not know their conditions .
Police have not said what led up to the shootings . NEWLINE
Large crowds gathered as police were collecting evidence and inspecting the Volvo .
At one point , Aaron Myrick , 21 , of Carrick tried to cross the crime scene tape and ignored officers ' commands to stay back , Ms. Toler said .
One officer was knocked to the ground and another used a Taser on Mr. Myrick , who was arrested and taken to the Allegheny County Jail .
