Police ID 13-year - old Iowa girl shot to death ; 3 charged
Police have identified a 13-year - old girl shot to death in Cedar Rapids and say three other teens have been charged in her death . NEWLINE
Police said Wednesday in a written release that Ireshia Parks was shot a little before 8 a.m. Tuesday .
She was taken to a hospital , where she was pronounced dead .
Police say an autopsy is pending . NEWLINE
Responding officers arrested three people within minutes of the shooting .
On Wednesday , police said a 13-year - old boy has been charged with involuntary manslaughter , carrying a weapon and interference with official acts .
A 15-year - old and 18-year - old , both male , also face charges of interfering with official acts . NEWLINE
A witness told television station KCRG that the boys were playing with a gun when it fired , hitting Parks , who was sitting in a car .
