Terre Haute officers investigating after 17-year - old falsely reports being shot
TERRE HAUTE , Ind. ( WTHI ) –
The Terre Haute Police Department is currently investigating after a juvenile falsely reported being shot on Thursday . NEWLINE
THPD officers were called to Regional Hospital for a 17-year - old who had been shot . NEWLINE
When officers spoke with the victim , he told police that he was walking across the parking lot at Davis Park Elementary when he and his cousin heard two gun shots . NEWLINE “
The victim told officers that he immediately noticed that he had been shot , ” a press release from THPD stated . NEWLINE
But , during their investigation , detectives determined the victim ’s story was not accurate and that he had suffered from a self - inflicted gunshot wound . NEWLINE
Also , the shooting did not happen on school property . NEWLINE
Officials were able to recover the gun that was used in the shooting .
The victim was treated for non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
The case continues to be under investigation pending a review by the Vigo Prosecutors Office . NEWLINE
