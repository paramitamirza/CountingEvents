Child among 4 shot at southwest Houston apartment complex
Officers put up crime scene tape around the area as homicide detectives worked the scene Sunday evening .
( Photo : KHOU 11 News ) NEWLINE HOUSTON -
A young child was among four people shot , one fatally , at an apartment complex in southwest Houston , police said Sunday . NEWLINE
The shooting happened in the 6000 block of Elm Street around 6:35 p.m. NEWLINE One victim , a 40-year - old male , died at the scene . NEWLINE
Police said three other victims , a 21-year - old male , a 19-year - old female and a 3-year - old boy were transported to area hospitals with non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
Police at the scene said a bullet grazed the boy ’s head . NEWLINE
The shooting happened near the pool area of the apartment complex .
Witnesses told KHOU 11 News they heard people arguing before the shots rang out . NEWLINE
" We were here in the apartments and everybody panicked and wanted to see what was going on because you never know if it 's going to be a little kid ... " said one resident at the complex . NEWLINE
Homicide detectives were on the scene Sunday evening .
So far , police have no leads on the suspects or a possible motive . NEWLINE
Anyone with additional information is urged to contact the HPD Homicide Division at 713 - 308 - 3600 or Crime Stoppers at 713 - 222-TIPS. NEWLINE ( © 2016 KHOU )
