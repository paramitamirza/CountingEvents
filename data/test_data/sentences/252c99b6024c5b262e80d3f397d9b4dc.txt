Four people injured in Feltonville shooting
A shooting in the Feltonville section of Philadelphia wounded at least four people early Saturday . NEWLINE
The shooting took place about 5:20 a.m. along the 4200 block of Macalester Street , according to preliminary information from Philadelphia police .
A 22-year - old woman was shot in her left foot ; another woman , also 22 , was shot in her left arm , police said . NEWLINE
Officers rushed both victims to Temple University Hospital , where they were placed in stable condition . NEWLINE
A 21-year - old man suffered a graze wound to his head and was taken by private vehicle to Temple University Episcopal Hospital , police said .
He was listed in stable condition .
