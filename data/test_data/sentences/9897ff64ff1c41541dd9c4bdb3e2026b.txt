Gary shootout leaves 2 dead , several wounded : Gary News
GARY |
A dispute over money at a Miller bar might have led to a neighborhood shooting that left two Gary men dead and four wounded , police said . NEWLINE
The early morning dispute between two groups Saturday started at Murphy 's Tavern , 5425 U.S. 12 , Police Chief Wade Ingram said . NEWLINE
One of the groups apparently went to another location to arm themselves with guns while the other group of eight went to a house in the 9300 block of Sunrise Boulevard , Ingram said . NEWLINE
The group that went back to the Sunrise Boulevard home was congregating outside at 3:45 a.m. when at least one car without its headlights on drove by and began firing into the crowd . NEWLINE
The dead have been identified by the Lake County coroner 's office as Vincent E. Wainwright , 32 , of the 9200 block of East 6th Avenue , and Da'Vaughn Pitts , 18 , of the 300 block of North Lake Street . NEWLINE
Wainwright died from a single gunshot and Pitts died from multiple gunshot wounds , the coroner 's office reported . NEWLINE
Wainwright was pronounced dead at 5 at the scene .
Pitts was pronounced dead at 5:30 p.m. at the Methodist Hospitals Northlake Campus in Gary . NEWLINE
Four others were shot in the incident , including a Portage woman and a Gary woman .
The other two gunshot victims are Gary men . NEWLINE
One of the wounded men is in critical condition , Ingram said . NEWLINE
All of the wounded were transported to area hospitals . NEWLINE
Investigating officers told witnesses to stay at the scene when one man began to run at 5:14 a.m. Officers stopped David Pitts , 20 , and took him into custody after discovering he was carrying a handgun .
David Pitts is the older brother of Da'Vaughn Pitts . NEWLINE
Pitts was arrested and is being held on charges of resisting arrest and possessing a gun without a permit . NEWLINE
Police are asking anyone with information about the shootings to contact Sgt .
Dan Callahan , Detectives Alex Jones , Lorenzo Davis , Daryl Gordon or Simon Lillie at ( 219 ) 881 - 1210 .
