Two killed in South Chicago shooting identified
Two killed in South Chicago shooting identified NEWLINE Two people who were killed in a domestic - related shooting that left two others critically wounded early Thursday in the South Chicago neighborhood have been identified . NEWLINE
The four people were shot by a known suspect in the 8000 block of South Shore Drive about 12:10 a.m. , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
The suspect got into argument with one of the victims and it turned physical , police said .
He left the home , returned with a gun and shot all four victims . NEWLINE
A 20-year - old woman , Emoni House ; and a 16-year - old boy , Elijah House , were pronounced dead at the scene , according to police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office . NEWLINE
Emoni House suffered multiple gunshot wounds , and Elijah House suffered a gunshot wound to the back , according to the medical examiner ’s office .
Both lived on the block where the shooting happened . NEWLINE
A 42-year - old woman was shot in the chest , and a 30-year - old woman was also shot , police said .
They were taken in critical condition to Christ Medical Center in Oak Lawn . NEWLINE
The shooting was domestic - related , police said . NEWLINE
No one was in custody as of Monday morning as Area South detectives investigate .
