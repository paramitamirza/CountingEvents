13-year - old accidentally shoots himself in the foot - WCAX.COM Local Vermont News , Weather and Sports-
A 13-year - old boy is recovering after he accidentally shot himself in the foot while hunting . NEWLINE
Vermont State Police say it happened Wednesday just after 4 p.m. in Waitsfield .
Members of the Vermont State Police Uniform Division , the Bureau of Criminal Investigation Unit , and the Vermont Department of Fish and Wildlife responded to investigate the incident . NEWLINE
Police say the boy accidentally discharged a small caliber gun and the bullet hit his left foot .
He was transported to the UVM Medical Center for treatment .
