One dead after shooting incident
IRON RIVER —
One man is dead after what police are calling an accidental shooting occurred in Iron River on Sunday night . NEWLINE
Iron River Police say they responded to a 911 call about the incident shortly before 10:00 p.m. An 18-year - old man was fatally shot in the incident .
The shooting remains under investigation at this time . NEWLINE
Iron River Police were assisted by the Iron County Sheriff ’s Office , the Michigan State Police , and Aspirus EMS.
