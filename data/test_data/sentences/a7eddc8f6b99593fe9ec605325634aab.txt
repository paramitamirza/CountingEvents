Five injured by gunfire in Fort Worth
Five people were shot in the 1700 block of Southcrest Drive in Fort Worth on October 27 , 2015 .
( Photo : WFAA ) NEWLINE
Fort Worth police are investigating the shooting of five people in southwest Fort Worth on Tuesday evening . NEWLINE
The victims were sprayed with shotgun pellets around 6 o'clock in the 1700 block of Southcrest Drive . NEWLINE
All of the victims were adults ; no one was seriously injured .
Two who were struck refused treatment at the scene and three others were taken to John Peter Smith Hospital for treatment of minor injuries . NEWLINE
Detectives are piecing together the circumstances that led up to the gunfire . NEWLINE
