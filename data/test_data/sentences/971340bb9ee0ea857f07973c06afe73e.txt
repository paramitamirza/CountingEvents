A man has been arrested in DeKalb County for the killing of two men in May. NEWLINE Tony Thomas , 19 , was taken into custody by members of the DeKalb County Sheriff 's Office on Thursday and charged with two counts of murder and one count of aggravated assault . NEWLINE
