Mother , 2 children found dead on Navajo Nation NEWLINE KAYENTA , Ariz. ( AP ) - Authorities are investigating the deaths of a mother and her two children on the Navajo Nation . NEWLINE
FBI spokesman Kurt Remus says the agency was notified Monday of the deaths that appear to be a murder - suicide . NEWLINE
He says the children were found dead in their beds at a home in Kayenta ( kuh - YHEN'-tuh ) of possible gunshot wounds .
He says an adult female likely killed herself . NEWLINE
Their names are being withheld as authorities work to notify all family members .
Remus says the FBI and Navajo Nation police are investigating what led to the deaths . NEWLINE
