Woman accidentally shot in hand in Chicago Lawn NEWLINE A woman was wounded Tuesday night when a gun accidentally discharged inside a Chicago Lawn neighborhood apartment on the Southwest Side . NEWLINE
About 10:20 p.m. , the 23-year - old was inside the apartment with a male who was handling a gun in the 6200 block of South Spaulding when the gun accidentally fired and she was struck in the left hand , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
She showed up at Holy Cross Hospital , where she was listed in good condition , police said . NEWLINE
No one was in custody early Wednesday as Area Central detectives investigated .
