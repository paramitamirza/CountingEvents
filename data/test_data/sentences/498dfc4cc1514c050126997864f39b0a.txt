Man , 19 , Charged In Teen 's Shooting Death
ACWORTH , GA -- A 19-year - old Acworth man has been charged with the shooting death of a 17-year - old teenager . NEWLINE
Bartow County sheriff 's investigators have arrested Shady Al - Hussien and charged him with involuntary manslaughter , pointing a pistol at another person and tampering with evidence , spokesperson Sgt .
Jonathan Rogers said . NEWLINE
Al - Hussien is accused of pulling the trigger on a firearm that killed 17-year - old Kyle Sheriff , and was arrested at a home in the 6800 block of Mohawk Drive near Acworth . NEWLINE
He was booked into the Bartow County Jail early Friday evening . NEWLINE
Deputies with the sheriff 's office and emergency personnel responded around 5 p.m. Oct. 11 to a home in the 4800 block of Ryan Road near Acworth . NEWLINE
Crews arrived on the scene to find the 17-year - old in the home " with a gunshot wound to the abdomen , " Rogers previously told Patch . NEWLINE
Sheriff was transported to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead from his injuries .
Rogers said Al - Hussien and Sheriff were friends , and " it is believed that the shooting was accidental . " NEWLINE
Al - Hussien remains held at the jail without bond . NEWLINE
