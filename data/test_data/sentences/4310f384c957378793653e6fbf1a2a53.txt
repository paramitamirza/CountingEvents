Norfolk deputy accidentally shoots himself in foot at city jail
NORFOLK , Va. –
A deputy was shot in the foot after he accidentally discharged his weapon . NEWLINE
According to dispatch , it happened Thursday at 6:12 p.m. on East City Hall Avenue at the Norfolk City Jail . NEWLINE
The deputy accidentally shot his weapon at the firearm clearing station at the loading dock area of the jail , according to a spokesperson with Norfolk Sheriff ’s Office . NEWLINE
The deputy was taken to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital with non - life threatening injuries .
