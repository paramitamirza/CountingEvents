UPDATE : Suspect in Fatal Shooting of 14-Year - Old Turns Himself
In
ST. LOUIS ( KMOX/AP ) —
A 14-year - old girl has been fatally shot in a St. Louis home — the 14-year - old boy suspected of shooting her has turned himself in . NEWLINE
Police identified the victim as Jamayha Luss .
She was found suffering from a gunshot to the back Sunday afternoon at a friend ’s home .
She was rushed to a hospital , where she died . NEWLINE
Two teenage boys had come to the friend ’s house before the shooting .
Authorities believe that one of the teens had a gun .
The teenage boys left before police arrived . NEWLINE
St. Louis police didn’t make any other information about the suspect available . NEWLINE
