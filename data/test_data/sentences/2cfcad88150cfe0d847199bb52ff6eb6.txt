5-year - old girl , woman shot , wounded on the Southwest Side
5-year - old girl , woman shot , wounded on the Southwest Side NEWLINE A 5-year - old girl and a 34-year - old woman were shot Friday evening in the Marquette Park neighborhood on the Southwest Side . NEWLINE
They were on a sidewalk about 8 p.m. in the 6700 block of South Artesian when someone walked out of an alley and opened fire , striking the girl in the foot and the woman in the left ankle , according to the police . NEWLINE
The woman drove them to St. Bernard Hospital , where the woman was treated , and the girl was transferred to Holy Cross Hospital in good condition , police said .
