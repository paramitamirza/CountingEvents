Jefferson student who shot himself has died
CEDAR RAPIDS —
Police continue to investigate the suicide of a student outside Jefferson High School and have been monitoring social media posts since the 15-year - old student shot himself Friday morning . NEWLINE
The student , who has not been identified , died Saturday afternoon , police said . NEWLINE
Police said in a news release Monday that they have not received “ any information that indicates that anyone else was involved other than the 15-year - old male who took his own life .
” NEWLINE
The social media posts purport to show links to school shootings , leading to speculation that the student may have been planning a school shooting of his own , but it was not possible to verify the authenticity of those posts Monday . NEWLINE

are aware , have been monitoring and are continuing to investigate several social media posts that have been shared since Friday ’s incident .
