1 Killed , 2 Wounded In Austin Shooting
CHICAGO ( STMW ) —
A man was killed and two others were wounded in a shooting in the West Side Austin neighborhood Wednesday evening . NEWLINE
Ronald Holliman , 18 , and two other men , ages 19 and 26 , were in the 4900 block of West West End Avenue about 5 p.m. when a group of males got out of a vehicle from an alley and fired multiple shots , according to police and the Cook County Medical Examiner ’s office . NEWLINE
Holliman was shot in the left buttocks , left thigh and right knee , while the 21-year - old was shot in the left arm , left leg and stomach , authorities said .
The 26-year - old was shot twice in the chest and once in the side , police said . NEWLINE
Holliman , of the 5200 block of West Race Avenue , was taken to Mount Sinai Hospital , where he died at 6:28 p.m. , a spokesman for the medical examiner ’s office said . NEWLINE
The 21-year - old was in “ stable ” condition at Mount Sinai , and the 26-year - old was in serious condition at John H. Stroger Jr. Hospital of Cook County , police said . NEWLINE
A police source said the 19- and 26-year - old are documented gang members . NEWLINE
