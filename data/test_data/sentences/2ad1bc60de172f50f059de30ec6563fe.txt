HIGHLAND : Five wounded in early morning shooting ( UPDATE )
CORRECTION :
In an earlier version of this story , the location of the shooting was incorrect . NEWLINE
Five people were injured in an early morning shooting in Highland , an official said . NEWLINE
The shooting was reported at 12:03 a.m. Tuesday at an apartment complex in the 25100 block of Fifth Street , according to a San Bernardino County sheriff 's news release . NEWLINE
The four female victims and one male victim , all between the ages of 18 and 24 , were hanging out in a garage when an unknown man walked up and opened fire , the release stated . NEWLINE
The five victims were taken to Loma Linda University Medical Center .
An 18-year - old woman was in critical condition , and a 21-year - old man was in moderate to critical condition , the release said . NEWLINE
