Cheyenne Police Investigating Baby Shooting
Tracie Perkins , Townsquare Media NEWLINE Cheyenne Police are investigating the shooting of a 9-month - old child on Thursday night . NEWLINE
Spokesman Officer Dan Long says police were called to 5000 Ogallala Place at about 9:30 p.m. after getting a call on a child being shot .
Long says 24-year - old Joshua Karban had been working with the slide on his pistol when the gun went off . NEWLINE
Long says the bullet went through a wall and hit Karban ’s son in the leg .
The child had been asleep at the time .
The baby was taken to Cheyenne Regional Medical Center after being shot . NEWLINE
Long says police didn’t believe the wound would be fatal .
