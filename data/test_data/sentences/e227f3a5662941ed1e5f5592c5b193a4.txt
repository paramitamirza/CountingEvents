Man arrested in fatal shooting of 16-year - old in Miami 's Allapattah neighborhood
MIAMI - A man was arrested Monday in the fatal shooting of a 16-year - old boy in Miami 's Allapattah neighborhood . NEWLINE
Richard Thompson , 21 , faces charges of first - degree murder and possession of a weapon while committing an offense . NEWLINE
Records show that at the time of the shooting , Thompson was out of jail on probation for armed robbery and displaying or using a weapon while committing a felony . NEWLINE
The shooting was reported about 8:30 p.m. Nov. 22 near Northwest 17th Avenue and Northwest 31st Street . NEWLINE
According to police , two friends were walking in the area when they were confronted by two males . NEWLINE
Police said both males , including Thompson , pulled out guns and fired at the victims , striking one of them multiple times .
A bystander , who was not related to the victims , was struck once in the arm . NEWLINE
The teenager , identified as Damien Ramirez , was taken to Jackson Memorial Hospital in critical condition .
He was pronounced dead a short time later . NEWLINE
The person who was shot in the arm was also taken to JMH in stable condition . NEWLINE
According to an arrest report , surveillance video proves that Thompson was one of the gunmen .
Witnesses also identified Thompson as one of the suspects , police said . NEWLINE
Thompson was arrested at his home , where he was found in his bedroom within arms reach of a shotgun , the report said . NEWLINE
Detectives said Thompson admitted to being in the area at the time of the shooting , but claimed that he was not one of the gunmen . NEWLINE
A motive for the shooting is unknown . NEWLINE
Thompson is being held in jail without bond . NEWLINE
Anyone with information about the second gunman is asked to call Miami - Dade Crime Stoppers at 305 - 471-TIPS. NEWLINE
