Jackson County Sheriff 's Respond To 2 Hunting Accidents
Jackson County Sheriff ’s investigated two separate hunting accidents Sunday . NEWLINE
The first involved three minors hunting when no adults were present at the time .
About 7:40 a.m. Sheriff ’s received a report of a 15-year - old being shot with turkey hunting .
Three minors were hunting just north of 118th and B Road near Delia in Jackson County .
A gun being held by a 10-year - old accidentally discharged wounding the 15-year - old in the leg .
The victim was taken to a Topeka hospital .
The victim is listed in stable condition . NEWLINE
The second accident occurred just before 11:30 a.m. Officials received a report of a man who had fallen out if his tree stand near 246th and G road near Soldier .
Jackson County EMS transported the man to a Topeka hospital .
He is in stable condition . NEWLINE
