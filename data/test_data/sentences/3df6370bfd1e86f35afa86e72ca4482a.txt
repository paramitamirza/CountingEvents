( KNZA)--Two separate Jackson County hunting accidents Sunday morning left two injured . NEWLINE
Sheriff Tim Morse says the first happened around 7:40 near Delia , a half - mile north of 118th and B. Roads , when a shotgun accidentally discharged , striking a 15-year - old boy in the leg .
He was transported to a Topeka hospital for treatment .
At last check , he was listed in stable condition . NEWLINE
Morse says the teen and two other minor boys , including the ten - year - old with the shotgun , were turkey hunting at the time of the accident .
No adults were with them . NEWLINE
The second accident happened shortly before 11:30 when a man fell from his tree stand , located near Soldier , in the area of 246th and G. Road . NEWLINE
He was transported by Jackson County EMS to a Topeka hospital for treatment . NEWLINE
At last check , the man , whose name is not made public , was listed in stable condition .
