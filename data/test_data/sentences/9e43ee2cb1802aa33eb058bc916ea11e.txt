3-year - old killed in accidental shooting in Jefferson
Posted : Sunday , March 29th 2015 at 9:17pm NEWLINE 3-year - old killed in accidental shooting in Jefferson NEWLINE JEFFERSON - A 3-year - old child has been killed in what appears to be an accidental shooting in Jefferson . NEWLINE
Jefferson Police Chief Joe Wirthman confirmed the shooting Sunday evening .
He released no details , however , saying that the Georgia Bureau of Investigation is taking the lead in the investigation . NEWLINE
