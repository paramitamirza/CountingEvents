2 men , 3 women shot in Baltimore , police say
Baltimore police are investigating a shooting Saturday afternoon that injured five people . NEWLINE
City police said officers were called about 4:36 p.m. to the 1700 block of North Broadway , where two men and three women were shot . NEWLINE
Police said a 52-year - old woman was shot in her lower extremities , a 36-year - old woman was shot in the foot , a 27-year - old woman was shot in the chest , a 30-year - old man was shot in the stomach and leg , and a 23-year - old man was shot in the ankle . NEWLINE
All of the victims were taken to area hospitals . NEWLINE
No further information was immediately released . NEWLINE
