Arrest report describes chaos during shooting at wedding
The man who shot several people at a wedding reception in east Las Vegas Saturday night faces four counts of attempted murder , according to Las Vegas police . NEWLINE
Five people were shot , including one man who was shot 10 times , by 24-year - old Peter Andrade , after an altercation in the restroom at El Palacio Del Sol at 953 E. Sahara Avenue , police said . NEWLINE
Andrade shot one man in the restroom five times before the man escaped and told other patrons what happened , police said . NEWLINE
The arrest report stated the two men had a previous altercation before the shooting occurred . NEWLINE
When Andrade left the restroom and got to the front door of the building , he was taken down by two other patrons who then kicked and punched him .
Andrade pulled out a handgun and fired several shots , striking four people , police said . NEWLINE
One woman managed to take away the gun away from Andrade while he was on the floor , according to the arrest report . NEWLINE
Andrade then got up and both he and his girlfriend ran out of the building where they were detained by security officers .
He was taken into custody when Metro arrived . NEWLINE
One man was transported to University Medical Center in critical condition .
Four others were taken to the hospital with non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
