No charges after 9-year - old shot in head
The Blount County Sheriff 's Office says there will not be any charges after a 9-year - old boy was shot in the head Tuesday night . NEWLINE
It happened at Riverside Manor Apartments in Louisville , Tenn. NEWLINE
Officials said the shooting was an accidental discharge of a weapon and that the boy 's mom was handling the gun and it went off by accident . NEWLINE
Investigators said the mom was on the second floor of the apartment and the round traveled through the floor and subfloor to the first floor .
That is when the bullet struck the boy on the forehead .
Marian O'Briant , with the Blount County Sheriff 's Office says it was a freak accident and that the boy is okay . NEWLINE
A video was shared by a neighbor showing the boy walking on his own towards a stretcher and holding his hand to his head while moving towards an ambulance . NEWLINE
The Blount County Sheriff 's Office said the 9 year old boy is recovering at UT Medical Center .
