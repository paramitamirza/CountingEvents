One Killed , Two Hurt in South LA Shooting
One person was killed and two others badly hurt Wednesday in a shooting in South Los Angeles Wednesday . NEWLINE
The incident , which is suspected to stem from a family dispute , was reported at 7:18 p.m. at 81st Street and Western Avenue , Los Angeles Police Department said .
Witnesses heard several gunshots and people screaming . NEWLINE
One victim was killed in the shooting , while one was critical and another in a serious condition at hospital .
