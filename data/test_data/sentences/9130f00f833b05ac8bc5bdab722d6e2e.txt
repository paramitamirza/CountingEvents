2015-07-03
Police investigating drive - by shooting that killed one man , injured 3 others
INDIANAPOLIS ( July 3 , 2015 ) –
One man has died after officers responded to five reports of people shot within fifteen minutes late Thursday night . NEWLINE
Just before midnight , officers with the Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department responded to a call of a person shot on the city ’s near west side , near Lynn and Michigan streets .
When they arrived , they found a man suffering from a gunshot wound .
He was transported to Eskenazi Hospital , where he was pronounced dead . NEWLINE
Police said around this time , three additional patients with gunshot wounds walked into Eskenazi Hospital .
The victims told officers that they were also shot on Lynn Street .
All three victims were shot in extremities and are being treated for non - life threatening injuries . NEWLINE
According to police , all four men were shot by people in a passing vehicle while the victims were standing in front of an abandoned house .
Officers say more than 20 shots were fired in the gun battle .
Officers are now looking for a cargo van that they believe was involved , and they do not believe this was a random act . NEWLINE
Prior to the incident on Lynn Street , officers responded to a call around 11:30 p.m. about a person in the 2300 block of Stuart Street on the city ’s east side .
When they arrived on scene , they found a man shot in the left thigh .
Police said he was transported to Methodist Hospital and was awake and talking to officers at the scene . NEWLINE
Police urge anyone with information to call Crime Stoppers at 317 - 262-TIPS.
