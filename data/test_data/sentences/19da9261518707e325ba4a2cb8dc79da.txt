6 people robbed , shot in Central Lubbock - KCBD NewsChannel 11 Lubbock
Lubbock police are searching for gunmen in robbery that left six people injured . NEWLINE
Residents say seven people were walking in the 2200 block of 17th Street around 10:20 p.m. Sunday when they were approached by two men . NEWLINE
" They were trying to rob them of their phones and their wallets , and they said ' No ' .
So , they followed them back to their house , over here , and threatened them once more , " said Luke Ford .
" They got one phone out of them , I believe .
And as they were walking , over next to that tree , about 15 to 20 yards down , they fired off a round at ' em . " NEWLINE
Police say six of the seven victims were peppered with bird shot and suffered minor injuries .
All refused treatment at the scene . NEWLINE
The suspects were described as a Hispanic male , 20 - 30 years old wearing a white shirt and red shorts , and a black male , 20 - 30 years old wearing dark or black colored clothing . NEWLINE
