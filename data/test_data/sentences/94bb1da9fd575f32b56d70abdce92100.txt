( Photo : KCEN ) NEWLINE KILLEEN , Texas - Four people are dead and another is fighting for her life this morning at a Central Texas hospital after an overnight shooting on Hill and Lake Road in Killeen . NEWLINE
Police were dispatched around 10:30 pm to the 1700 block of Godman Street after multiple shots were fired . NEWLINE
Upon arrival , police found two females and and one male dead from gunshot wounds ; another female was rushed to Baylor Scott & White Hospital in Temple with critical wounds . NEWLINE
Shortly after midnight on Monday the Killeen Police Tactical Response Unit were called out to make contact with the suspect who had barricaded himself inside the home .
That suspect later died from a self - inflicted gunshot wound . NEWLINE
The bodies have been sent to Dallas for autopsies . NEWLINE
