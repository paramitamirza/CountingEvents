UPDATE : 4 Hospitalized In Kingsessing Shooting
August 13 , 2013 10:44 PM NEWLINE PHILADELPHIA ( CBS ) –
Investigators say four men are hospitalized after a shooting in Philadelphia Tuesday night . NEWLINE
The incident was reported shortly before 10 p.m. near the 1100 block of Divinity Street in the Kingsessing section . NEWLINE
Investigators say four men in their early 20 ’s were shot .
All four victims were taken to the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania . NEWLINE
One victim is listed in critical condition .
The three other victims are listed in stable condition . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made at this time . NEWLINE
There is no word on a motive . NEWLINE
The incident remains under investigation . NEWLINE
