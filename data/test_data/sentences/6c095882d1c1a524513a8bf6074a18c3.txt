10-month - old baby killed in Marrero double shooting , JPSO says
A 10-month - old baby was killed in a double shooting Saturday in Marrero . NEWLINE
Advertisement NEWLINE The shooting was reported around 4:30 p.m. in the 2100 block of Caddy Drive , the Jefferson Parish Sheriff 's Office said . NEWLINE
A spokesperson for the Sheriff 's Office said the wounded 10-month - old boy was shot , Xyahir Davis , was taken to a hospital , where he was pronounced dead . NEWLINE
The other victim , a male in his late teens to early 20s , suffered multiple gunshot wounds .
He was taken to a hospital , where he remains in serious condition . NEWLINE
Detectives said both victims were at the home with the baby 's father and three other men when the shooting occurred .
Witnesses told the Sheriff 's Office that the gunman , who has not been identified , opened the door and began firing . NEWLINE
Officials have not released a description of the gunman .
