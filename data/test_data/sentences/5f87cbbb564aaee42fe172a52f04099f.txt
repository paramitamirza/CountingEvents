BRIDGEPORT - Bridgeport police say five teens were injured in a shooting at a Sweet 16 party Saturday night . NEWLINE
It happened at Circolo Sportivo on Park Avenue around 10:30 p.m. NEWLINE
Police say a fight moved from inside to outside , where four boys and one girl were shot .
They say about 300 teens were packed inside the building . NEWLINE
They say at least two shooters fired in the back of the party venue and then near the street . NEWLINE
Four victims were taken to St. Vincent 's Medical Center and the other to Bridgeport Hospital . NEWLINE
News 12 is told the victims were shot in their arms and legs . NEWLINE
Officials say four of the five victims were treated for their injuries and released from the hospital .
They say the other remains in serious condition but is expected to recover . NEWLINE
The host of the party says a lot of people who were not invited showed up to the party , which may have led to the fight . NEWLINE
News 12 is told a loaded firearm was recovered at the scene of the shooting . NEWLINE
Police say they are reviewing surveillance video that captured the crime . NEWLINE
Anyone with information is asked to call Bridgeport police .
