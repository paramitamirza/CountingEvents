Teen Hit in Hunting - Related Shooting in Colchester
A 14-year - old riding an ATV was shot in the foot in Colchester on Wednesday in what police are calling a hunting - related shooting . NEWLINE
Colchester officers and state troopers responded to the area of Middletown Road and Bigalow Road on Wednesday afternoon . NEWLINE
According to police , a 14-year - old boy was riding an ATV in a wooded area when he was shot in the foot .
He was taken to a clinic in Marlborough with minor injuries , according to police . NEWLINE
Officers from the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection also responded to the scene and are investigating the incident . NEWLINE
Police did not say if they have located the person who fired the weapon .
