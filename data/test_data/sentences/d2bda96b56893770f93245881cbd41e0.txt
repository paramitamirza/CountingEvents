Boy , 11 , accidentally shoots self in southwest Detroit
The shooting happened around 8:30 a.m. , according to police ( Photo : file photo ) NEWLINE
An 11-year - old boy accidentally shot himself in southwest Detroit early Thursday morning , according to Detroit Police . NEWLINE
Officer Jennifer Moreno said the shooting happened around 8:30 a.m. in the 3900 block of West Vernor Highway and officers are still at the scene investigating . NEWLINE
Moreno said more details were not immediately available and it 's unclear how the boy obtained the gun . NEWLINE
