Police : Boy , 15 , dead after accidental shooting in Norristown
A 15-year - old boy is dead after what investigators say was an accidental shooting in Norristown , Pa .
It happened Sunday in the 600 block of George Street .
According to police , several other juvenile males on the scene said the boy was waving a gun around when it went off .
The bullet hit him in the head .
He was pronounced dead at Jefferson University Hospital .
The name of the teen killed has not been released .
