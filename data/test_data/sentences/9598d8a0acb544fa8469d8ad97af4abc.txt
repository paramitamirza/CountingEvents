Little Rock shooting victim dies
A man injured in a November 2 shooting at 22nd and Bragg has died . NEWLINE
Little Rock police say Fred Duhart , 38 , died at a local hospital on Sunday as a result of his injuries . NEWLINE
On November 2 , several people were standing in front of a home on 22nd and Bragg just before 10:30 p.m. when someone began firing shots on them from an unknown location . NEWLINE
Five people , including Duhart , were injured .
The other victims involved in the shooting were identified as : NEWLINE Vashon Dukes , 19 , of Little Rock NEWLINE Whitney Lewis , 29 , of Little Rock NEWLINE Shawn Adams , 47 , of Little Rock NEWLINE Natasha Stewart , 34 , of Little Rock NEWLINE
No arrests have been made .
The investigation is still ongoing .
