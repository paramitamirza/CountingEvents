5 wounded in Uptown shooting
5 wounded in Uptown shooting NEWLINE Five people were shot in the Uptown neighborhood early Saturday on the North Side . NEWLINE
It happened about 1:30 a.m. in the 4800 block of North Winthrop , according to Chicago Police . NEWLINE
The victims — a 36-year - old man shot in the buttocks , a 27-year - old man in the leg , a 17-year - old boy in the back , a 20-year - old woman in both legs and another 36-year - old man with an arm wound — showed up at Illinois Masonic Medical Center .
All were listed in good condition , police said . NEWLINE
The circumstances of the shooting were unknown .
