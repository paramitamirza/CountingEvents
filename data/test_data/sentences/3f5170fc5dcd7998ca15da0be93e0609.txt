Woman in hospital after accidental shooting in Halifax County
HALIFAX CO. ( WSLS 10 ) —
An accidental shooting in Halifax County is under investigation .
The Halifax County Sheriff ’s Department said on Sunday around 3 p.m. , it received a call of a shooting on Hudson Road . NEWLINE
When officers arrived , they were told an adult female had been shot and was taken to Sentara Hospital .
After further investigation , authorities found the female was hit once in the abdomen while walking in her driveway .
She was airlifted to Duke University Hospital where she is in stable condition . NEWLINE
According to a preliminary investigation , officers said it appears the shooting , which involved a juvenile , was accidental , however the investigation is ongoing and charges are pending .
