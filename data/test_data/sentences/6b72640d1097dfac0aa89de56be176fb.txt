Active shooter at Cascade Mall in Burlington , Washington
FOR LIVE UPDATES : Twitter , Facebook NEWLINE
The incident began just before 7 p.m. PT on Friday when an unidentified man - believed to be between 20 and 25 years old and armed with a rifle - opened fire inside Macy 's at the Cascade Mall .
Details about the circumstances of the shooting are still unclear . NEWLINE
Customers at the mall reported being put on lockdown and said they were hiding inside the stores as a large number of officers rushed to the scene and surrounded the mall .
Roads in the area have been closed by police . NEWLINE
Sgt .
Mark Francis , a spokesman for the Washington State Patrol , confirmed that four women had been killed at Macy 's .
A fifth victim , a man , was airlifted to Harborview Medical Center in very critical condition and succumbed to his injuries on Saturday morning . NEWLINE
Sgt .
Francis said the shooter is believed to have fled the mall , though officers continued searching the scene as a precaution while they treated it as an active shooter situation .
The mall had been mostly cleared just before midnight . NEWLINE
The suspected shooter is described as a skinny Hispanic male , between 20 and 25 years old , with black hair and a black shirt .
He was last seen running towards Interstate 5 , which is directly adjacent to the Cascade Mall .
A photo of the suspect was released just after 9 p.m. ( posted below ) . NEWLINE
As of early Saturday morning , authorities urged area residents to stay inside and to call 911 immediately if they see the suspect .
People who are concerned about their loved ones are advised to go to " His Place " church , where survivors have been taken . NEWLINE
Details about a possible motive for the mall shooting were not immediately known , but FBI spokeswoman Ayn Dietrich - Williams said the agency 's Joint Terrorism Task Force is not responding to the incident , though other FBI agents are . NEWLINE
" At this time , we do not have information to suggest additional attacks [ are ] planned in Washington , " she said . NEWLINE
" The FBI has personnel on the way to assist the Skagit Multi Agency Response Team in its investigation of this evening 's incident at Cascade Mall , " the spokeswoman added .
" The FBI will help as needed , whether it be in extra manpower , tactical resources , or intelligence - gathering . " NEWLINE
A SWAT situation was also underway in downtown Seattle , possibly as a lead in the investigation into the Burlington shooting , but the situation ended at about 11:45 p.m. PT. NEWLINE Washington Governor Jay Inslee , in a written statement , said " tragedy has struck " the state . "
Many questions remain but our state patrol and local law enforcement are on the scene working swiftly to locate the shooter and clear the area , " he said . NEWLINE
Inslee added : " Trudi and I send our condolences to the families of the victims and prayers for anyone injured ..
Stay close to your friends and loved ones as we await more information and , hopefully , news of the suspect 's capture . " NEWLINE
