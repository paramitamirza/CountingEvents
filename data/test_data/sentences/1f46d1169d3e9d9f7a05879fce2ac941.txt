8 shot , 1 in serious condition after Durham drive - by
Police responded to reports of multiple people shot in the 2700 block of Hinson Drive on Friday night .
Police said eight people were shot in an apparent drive - by shooting during a cookout .
Durham investigators returned to Hinson Drive Saturday to continue interviewing neighbors .
The department is working to make an arrest .
EMS and first responders arrived in droves Friday night .
Some victims were being treated on the ground , while others were rushed to the hospital .
Police say a small grey or white car rolled up to the house and someone unloaded a gun .
One neighbor said the cookout was actually a child 's birthday party .
Another neighbor , who did n't want to share her name , was putting her children to bed when the shots were fired in rapid succession."I was scared , " said the eyewitness .
" I 'm afraid now .
This has been a nice place for me , but you see , it just feels sad now."Seven of the victims are expected to be okay .
One is at the hospital now in critical condition .
One person was in trauma care and had to have surgery , according to authorities .
No word yet on the victim 's identities or a possible suspect .
