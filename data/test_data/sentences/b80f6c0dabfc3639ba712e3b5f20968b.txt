5 Shot at Teen Concert in Kansas City
KANSAS CITY , Mo. --
A night of fun turns into a night of fear as a teen party ends with five people shot in southeast Kansas City early Sunday .
The parking lot at the Tropical Palms is littered with blood , cans and crime tape .
Police were called to the banquet hall at 84th Street and Hillcrest Road just before midnight after someone started shooting inside the building where about 150 teenagers had been attending a concert .
According to police , the shooting then continued into the parking lot."The investigation is in its early stages , " said Sgt .
Marisa Barnes , a police spokeswoman .
" There are three victims who are in serious but stable condition , and two that are in critical but stable condition."Part of the agreement for holding an event at the Tropical Palms is providing adequate security .
The owner of the banquet hall says he is not sure how many security guards were working Saturday night , but he does believe the building 's cameras caught most of the incident on video .
Police are currently reviewing the footage .
Police say despite the large crowd , they have limited suspect information .
But witnesses saw four people drive away from the banquet hall just after the shooting .
No one is in custody yet.(story by KCTV , Kansas City )
