2008-10-16
Firefighters respond to bridge fire
At Approx .
7:21 Tuesday evening Portland Firefighters responded to reports of a commercial fire at North Columbia Blvd .
and Vancouver . NEWLINE
On arrival firefighters found a bridge over the Columbia Slough with fire underneath .
The fire had burned some brush and damaged a homeless camp that was located beneath the bridge . NEWLINE
Firefighters brought the fire under control however some of the bridges wooden structural members and a power line where damaged . NEWLINE
Fire officials are working to determine who owns the bridge so that the structure can be evaluated for safety .
The bridge will remain closed to vehicle traffic until the safety of the damaged components can be evaluated . NEWLINE
Fire investigators have yet to determine a cause .
