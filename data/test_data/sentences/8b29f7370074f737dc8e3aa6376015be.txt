Blytheville police investigating two deadly shootings - KAIT Jonesboro , AR - Region 8 News , weather , sports
Police in Blytheville are investigating two shootings Monday that resulted in the deaths of two men . NEWLINE
Captain Scott Adams stated in a news release Tuesday that one shooting happened Monday morning and the second happened during the evening . NEWLINE
The first shooting took place in the 700-block of East McHaney Drive around 10 a.m. NEWLINE
Officers were reportedly flagged down in reference to shots fired at that location .
Calls to police soon followed . NEWLINE
According to Adams , when officers arrived they found no complainants or victims .
A witness told police two people were shooting at each other . NEWLINE
Officers searched the area and found Kennon Daniels , 20 , suffering from a gunshot wound . NEWLINE
Daniels was taken to a Blytheville hospital where he later died from his injuries , according to Adams . NEWLINE
A second shooting was reported to police at Williams Park around 8:30 p.m. NEWLINE As officers responded they found the victim , later identified as Vincent Stone , 22 , suffering from multiple gunshot wounds , Adams said . NEWLINE
Stone was taken to a Blytheville hospital where detectives attempted to get information about the incident from him . NEWLINE
Stone was flown to a Memphis hospital where he later died . NEWLINE
Adams said several people " are believed to be witnesses in this shooting .
" They are asked to " get involved and speak with detectives " about the incident . NEWLINE
Anyone with information about these shooting deaths are asked to call the Blytheville Police Dept .
