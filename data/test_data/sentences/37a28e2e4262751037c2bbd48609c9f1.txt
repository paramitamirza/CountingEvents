UPDATE : Houma shooting leaves 1 dead , 3 injured Updated : Wednesday , October 28 , 2015 10:01 PM EDT NEWLINE Houma Police are investigating a shooting incident that left one person dead and three others injured .
The incident occurred Wednesday at 4:15 p.m. in the 400 block of Morgan Street . NEWLINE
According to police , officers responded to a call of shots fired .
When they arrived on scene , they found a man suffering from several gunshot wounds .
He was brought to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead .
The deceased victim has been identified as 18-year - old Corey Butler . NEWLINE
Police say that three other men were shot as a result of the incident .
Investigators learned that the men met on Morgan Street where a fight took place and guns were drawn .
A 19-year - old man was shot in the shoulder , a 24-year - old was shot multiple times and a 25-year - old was shot in the face .
Another man was able to escape without injury , according to police . NEWLINE
Detectives are still investigating the incident .
If you have any information that could help police with this case , call the Houma Police Department at 985 - 873 - 6371 or the Bayou Region Crime Stoppers at 1 - 800 - 743 - 7433 . NEWLINE
