Mother and boyfriend indicted in fatal shooting of 1-year - old Braylon Robinson
CLEVELAND - Shanee Robinson and Ricard Sims have been indicted for the fatal shooting of 1-year - old Braylon Robinson . NEWLINE
According to the indictment , Shanee Robinson , 24 , and Sims , 27 , were each indicted on one count of involuntary manslaughter , three counts of endangering children , one count of reckless homicide , and one count of receiving stolen property . NEWLINE
The Cuyahoga County Prosecutor 's Office said Shanee Robinson was Braylon 's mother and Sims was Shanee 's boyfriend . NEWLINE
Shonda Robinson , Braylon 's aunt , said Braylon was playing on the floor with two other children , ages 1 and 3 , who were described as the children of a family friend .
Investigators said they believe Braylon was shot by a 3-year - old boy . NEWLINE
Shonda Robinson said her sister heard the gun go off , but was on the couch facing the other direction when the 3-year - old apparently found the weapon .
A handgun was found near the 3-year - old .
Neither of the other children sustained injuries .
She said the family was unaware that any weapons were in the home and suspect it may have belonged to the child 's father . NEWLINE
Shanee Robinson and Sims are scheduled to be arraigned on March 24 at 8:30 a.m. at the Cuyahoga County Court of Common Pleas .
