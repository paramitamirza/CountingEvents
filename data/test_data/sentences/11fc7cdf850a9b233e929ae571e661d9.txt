Waverly man killed in Christmas Eve hunting accident
A Waverly man died in a hunting accident on Christmas Eve .
The Butler County Sheriff 's Office responded to report of a man shot at a rural residence near Allison on the morning of Saturday , December 24 . NEWLINE
Kirk Hummel , 47 , was hunting rabbits with his wife on his parents ' farm when the incident occurred .
Hummel 's wife , Kathleen , reported her gun accidentally fired and hit her husband . NEWLINE
Emergency responders administered immediate medical care , but Mr. Hummel died before reacing the hospital . NEWLINE
The incident remains under investigation as routine procedure .
