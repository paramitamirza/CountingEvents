17-year - old boy killed in South Austin shooting NEWLINE A 17-year - old boy was killed in a South Austin neighborhood shooting Monday night on the West Side . NEWLINE
Jeremiah Parker had just gotten out of his vehicle about 8:30 p.m. and was entering a home in the 500 block of South Lockwood when an armed male in a black hoodie confronted him and demanded his car keys , according to Chicago Police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office . NEWLINE
A struggle ensued , during which the gun went off and the boy was struck in the chest and arm , police said .
The suspect then ran away . NEWLINE
Parker was taken to Stroger Hospital , where he was pronounced dead at 10:42 p.m. , according to police and the medical examiner ’s office .
He lived in the 700 block of North Homan .
