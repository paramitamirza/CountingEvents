4 injured in suburban Denver hotel shooting
Authorities say four people were shot during a party at a hotel in the Denver suburb of Wheat Ridge . NEWLINE
Police spokesman John Romero says officers were called to a Howard Johnson hotel at about 1:40 a.m. Sunday after multiple reports that someone had opened fire on the party .
Officers arrived to find partygoers running from the scene , and at least one of the victims was taken to the hospital in a private vehicle . NEWLINE
Romero did not know what kind of gun was used , the size of the party or the conditions of the victims .
He says " the nature of the scene being so chaotic , there are a lot of differing opinions on who the suspect is . " NEWLINE
Investigators believe only one gunman was involved . NEWLINE
