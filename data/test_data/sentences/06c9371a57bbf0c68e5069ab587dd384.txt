1 dead , 3 injured in shooting outside Sacramento nightclub
A man has died and three others were injured after a shooting Saturday outside a Sacramento nightclub , police said . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 2:17 a.m. near the La Nueva Tropicana Club on Arden Way , the Sacramento Police Department said . NEWLINE
The four people were shot while standing in the parking lot of the club , police said .
They were taken to various hospitals in the area with serious injuries , and one man died of his injuries . NEWLINE
The Sacramento County Coroner 's Office identified the man as Cesar Aleman Luna , 28 , of Sacramento . NEWLINE
The current conditions of the other victims have not been released . NEWLINE

It is unclear if all the victims were club patrons , Sacramento police spokesperson Matt McPhail said .
Officers believe the shooting happened after the club closed . NEWLINE
No motive or suspect information was available at this time . NEWLINE
This is the third fatal shooting investigation Sacramento homicide detectives are dealing with in the past 24 hours , police said .
