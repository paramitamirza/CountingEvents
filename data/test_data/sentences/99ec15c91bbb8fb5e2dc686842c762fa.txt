Father of 3 fatally shot outside northeast Houston Walgreens Man struck by gunfire intended for 3 teens NEWLINE A father of three who stopped at a Walgreen 's store in northeast Houston for a snack was fatally shot early Sunday morning , a bystander struck by gunfire intended for a group of teenagers , police said . NEWLINE
The victim , 49-year - old Edward Long , had stopped at the store at Tidwell and Homestead with his girlfriend .
He was shot at about 1 a.m. as he walked into the store . NEWLINE
Long " had nothing to do with this incident , other than being here at the wrong time . " said Robert Klementich , a Houston police detective . NEWLINE
Klementich said there were " strong indications " the drive - by shooting was gang - related .
Long was simply caught in the crossfire .
He was pronounced dead at the scene . NEWLINE
The teenagers were also struck but sustained non - life - threatening injuries .

The third , a 15-year - old , was transported by private vehicle to Lyndon B. Johnson Hospital . NEWLINE
