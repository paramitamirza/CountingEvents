But earlier this month , the rapper — whose real name is Shaquon Thomas — allegedly went back to that same McDonald 's at 6740 N. Clark St. , where he got in a fight with a man who later was shot across the street from the restaurant , police said . NEWLINE
Now , Thomas — whom authorities have told residents about at community meetings — is in jail . NEWLINE

The rapper celebrated his 19th birthday in jail during the time between the shootings .
