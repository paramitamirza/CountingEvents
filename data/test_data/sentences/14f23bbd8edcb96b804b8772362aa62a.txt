One Dead , Seven Injured in Drive - By Shooting in Vallejo
Police in Vallejo said a gunman opened fire on a group of people changing a flat tire , killing one man and injuring seven others last night .
NBC Bay Area 's Kimberly Tere reports from the newsroom .
( Published Monday , Oct. 28 , 2013 ) NEWLINE
One person was killed and as many as seven others injured in a mass shooting in Vallejo late Saturday night , police said today . NEWLINE
According to a preliminary investigation , a group of young adults from South San Francisco were in four cars heading to Vallejo for a party , Vallejo police said . NEWLINE
At about 11 p.m. , one of the vehicles hit a curb and got a flat tire near the 200 block of Princeton Avenue , police said . NEWLINE
As all four cars pulled over and several individuals were changing the tire , a white SUV with tinted windows drove by and opened fire on the group , police said . NEWLINE
There appeared to be one gunman inside the vehicle . NEWLINE
Police and paramedics responded to reports of a shooting and found five people suffering gunshot wounds . NEWLINE
Three other victims from the same incident arrived at area hospitals on their own , police said . NEWLINE
One of the neighbors in the area , who was a registered nurse , said she tried to help those injured . NEWLINE
" When we came outside , we saw these victims outside , " she said .
" One gentleman was hurt in the heart , and the other gentleman , he could move his legs and his body , so we went to the victim that was unconscious and tried to revive him . " NEWLINE
A 19-year - old man was pronounced dead at the scene , police said .
His name has not been released . NEWLINE
Vallejo Police Lt .
Herman Robinson said he had never seen an incident like this . NEWLINE
" I 've been working here at the police department for 40 years , and I 've never experienced a single incident where there have been eight people shot . , " Robinson said . NEWLINE
No motive for the shooting had been determined as of Sunday night . NEWLINE
