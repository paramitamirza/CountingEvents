Investigation into Fatal Shooting at 16411 Saybrook
Incident Update NEWLINE Houston Police Department NEWLINE FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE NEWLINE DATE : September 15 , 2016 9:11:51 AM CDT NEWLINE Investigation into Fatal Shooting at 16411 Saybrook NEWLINE Houston police are investigating the fatal shooting of a woman and the wounding of three men found at 16411 Saybrook about 12:20 a.m. today ( September 15 ) . NEWLINE
The victim , 20 , was pronounced dead at the scene .
Her identity is pending verification by the Harris County Institute of Forensic Sciences . NEWLINE
HPD Homicide Division Senior Police Officer B. Nabors and Officer W. Gilbert reported : NEWLINE HPD patrol officers responded to a shooting call at the above address and found the victim deceased from an apparent gunshot wound .
Three Hispanic males , between 20 to 30 years of age , were also wounded and transported to area hospitals .
One of the males remains in critical condition at this time . NEWLINE
At this time , there are no known witnesses nor suspects . NEWLINE
