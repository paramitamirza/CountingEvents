Police investigating accidental shooting
CORPUS CHRISTI ( KIII NEWS ) -
Corpus Christi police were called out to the La Sista Motel in the 4300 Block of Leopard Friday night to investigate a shooting . NEWLINE
Police tell us a child somehow got hold of a gun and fired off a shot hitting a 73-year - old man in the upper chest .
The man was rushed to the hospital and is expected to survive .
Right now police are not releasing details on the child .
They are investigating this as an accidental shooting . NEWLINE
Police did arrest the child 's mother on an outstanding warrant . NEWLINE
