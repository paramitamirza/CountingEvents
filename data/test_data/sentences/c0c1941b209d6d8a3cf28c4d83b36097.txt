Autopsies show Henry Co. children shot , father committed suicide
Three bodies were found in a home in Henry County Thursday , August 27 , 2015 .
( WISH Photo ) NEWLINE Related Coverage Father and two children found dead inside of home in Henry County NEWLINE KNIGHTSTOWN , Ind. —
The Henry County Prosecutor ’s Office has released information regarding the man and two children found dead in a Knightstown home on Thursday . NEWLINE
Preliminary autopsy results show that the two children , 5-year - old Thomas Horn and 11-year - old Corinne Horn , both died from gunshot wounds . NEWLINE
Their father , 48-year - old Joseph Horn , died from an apparent self - inflicted gunshot wound . NEWLINE
Deputies with the Henry County Sheriff ’s Department did a welfare check after neighbors said the kids never showed up for school . NEWLINE
Investigators say this is an ongoing investigation . NEWLINE
Evidence collected at the scene will be sent to the Indiana State Police Lab for testing and analysis . NEWLINE
