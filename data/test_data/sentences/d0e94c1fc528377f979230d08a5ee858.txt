Police Search For Killer of 17-Year - Old in Fort Worth
Fort Worth police are searching for the person who fatally shot 17-year - old Demarian Jones in his driveway Sunday .
( Published Sunday , Sept. 25 , 2016 ) NEWLINE
Fort Worth police are trying to find the person who shot and killed a 17-year - old in the driveway of his home early Sunday morning . NEWLINE
The Tarrant County Medical Examiner 's office reported the victim as Demarian Jones . NEWLINE
It happened in the 3400 block of Stonewall Lane . NEWLINE
Police said they were called to home for a shooting call .
When they arrived , officers found the victim in the driveway .
He was pronounced dead at the scene . NEWLINE
Police say he got into an argument with someone before he was shot .
Neighbors reported hearing a series of gunshots . NEWLINE
Friends say Jones was turning his life around .
He moved to Fort Worth a few years ago to go to school and play basketball . NEWLINE
" I want him to be remembered as a good man , " said friend Robert Thornton . NEWLINE
Thornton and Jones would see each other daily to play pickup basketball in their neighborhood .
The pair became best friends . NEWLINE
Thornton said he and Jones were together at a friend 's birthday party a few hours before the shooting .
Thornton walked home before midnight to go to bed . NEWLINE
He learned of Jones ' death before church on Sunday morning . NEWLINE
" I do n't see him as a friend .
I see him as a brother , " said Thornton .
" He was always there for me .
It 's my time to stand tall and be there for him , and keep going with my life . " NEWLINE
Thornton 's father , Reggie Walker , described Jones as a jokester and " the life of the party . " NEWLINE
" He had a smile that would light his whole face up .
He was a real funny kid , " said Walker .
" He wanted to work , he wanted to make money , he wanted his diploma , he wanted to be the first one in his family to get his high school diploma . " NEWLINE
The suspect is still at large .
