West Oakland shooting leaves six injured , five by gunfire
OAKLAND -- A shooting in West Oakland Friday night that left six people injured , five with gunshot wounds , was possibly gang related , police said Saturday . NEWLINE
All of those injured are expected to recover . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 9:15 p.m. Friday near a liquor store in the 3200 block of San Pablo Avenue . NEWLINE
Police said a group of people were on the sidewalk when more than one suspect walked up and began shooting at them . NEWLINE
More than three dozen shots were fired , police said . NEWLINE
Five people in the group were shot in different parts of their bodies . NEWLINE
Four of them were men , one 24 , two 27 and one 30 .
A 24-year - old woman was also shot .
They are all from Oakland . NEWLINE
A stray bullet went through the windshield of a 24-year - old Oakland woman driving northbound on San Pablo sending glass shards into her eyes .
, police said . NEWLINE
Three of the injured were taken by ambulance to a hospital .
The other three got their own rides to a hospital . NEWLINE
Two of the gunshot victims were eventually treated and released , police said . NEWLINE
Investigators said based on where the shooting happened and other information they have , they have not ruled out that the attack was gang related but declined to elaborate . NEWLINE
Police were continuing to investigate the shooting on Saturday . NEWLINE
