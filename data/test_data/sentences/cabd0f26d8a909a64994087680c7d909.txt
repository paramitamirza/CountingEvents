Bismarck man arrested after 6 month old baby is shot in neck
A Bismarck , N.D. man is facing a reckless endangerment charge after he told police his 6 month old daughter was accidentally shot in the neck . NEWLINE
Police say Christopher Simmons , 36 , of Bismarck , told officers he was trying to fix his rifle in his home when it accidentally went off and hit the child . NEWLINE
Police say the baby girl was on the living room floor about four feet away from the rifle when it went off . NEWLINE
The incident happened on Sunday around 2:45 pm in the 4000 block of Trenton Drive.. NEWLINE
The child was taken to the hospital and was listed in critical condition . NEWLINE
Simmons was taken to the Burleigh County Jail in Bismarck .
