Mother shot by 4-year - old in back of the head on Indy 's north side
INDIANAPOLIS –
A woman was accidentally shot by her 4-year - old daughter Sunday morning on the city ’s north side , police said . NEWLINE
Police arrived at 1606 E. 44th Street around 8:30 a.m. and found a 4-year - old and a 25-year - old woman with gunshot wounds .
Officers said the child picked up a gun from her parent ’s nightstand and accidently fired the weapon one time . NEWLINE
The bullet grazed the 4-year - old girl ’s cheek and went into the back of the mother ’s head . NEWLINE
The two were transported to a hospital .
The child is stable and the mother immediately underwent surgery .
