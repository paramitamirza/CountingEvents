Woman charged after ricochet bullet strikes 8-year - old
An 8-year - old had a close call Sunday when a bullet ricocheted off a brick wall and struck her in the shoulder . NEWLINE
According to a Panama City Police report Rachel Hinson was arguing with another woman in a white Cadillac in the Macedonia Garden Apartment complex Sunday when she fired a gun .
The bullet missed her intended target blasted through the driver 's side window and eventually struck the child in her shoulder . NEWLINE
Hinson left the area but was tracked down thanks to witnesses on scene and charged with discharging a firearm in public , two counts of tampering with evidence and two counts of aggravated assault . NEWLINE
The child suffered minor injuries . NEWLINE
Hinson has been released from jail on a $ 30,000 bond .
