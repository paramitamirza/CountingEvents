Father charged in accidental shooting that injured 4-year - old daughter and her mother
× Father charged in accidental shooting that injured 4-year - old daughter and her mother NEWLINE INDIANAPOLIS , Ind.–
A father is facing charges in connection with the accidental shooting of his 4-year - old daughter and her mother . NEWLINE
The incident occurred on Nov. 13 in the 1600 block of East 44th Street on the city ’s north side .
According to court documents , Kyle Spivey and his girlfriend were in their bedroom with their two children , ages two and four . NEWLINE
Investigators say the 2-year - old was able to get to Spivey ’s loaded handgun off a dresser and fired a single shot , striking the 4-year - old girl in the face and her mother in the back of the head .
The girl sustained serious injuries to her face , and her mother suffered an injury to the brain .
She suffered vision loss in one eye . NEWLINE
Four other children were in the home at the time of the incident . NEWLINE
During the investigation , investigators found a photo allegedly posted by Spivey on social media about a week before the shooting .
The photo shows the two children holding a gun . NEWLINE
Spivey was arrested Friday on charges of neglect of a dependent resulting in serious bodily injury , neglect of a dependent and criminal recklessness for leaving a loaded firearm where it could be accessed by a child .
