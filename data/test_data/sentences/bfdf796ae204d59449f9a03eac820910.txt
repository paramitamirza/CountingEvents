2015-09-08
One killed in Geo News van attack
A Geo TV   Digital Satellite News Gathering ( DSNG ) vehicle was attacked by unknown gunmen tonight   in the Bahadurabad area of Karachi , killing one employee of Geo TV while leaving another injured . NEWLINE
The news van was parked near the Naheed store when this incident took place .
Three motorcyclists attacked the vehicle and fled the scene .
One of the motorcyclists got off the motorcycle to attack the van .
A total of 7 bullets were fired , hitting the driver in the shoulder . NEWLINE
Imran Khan has condemned the attack while CM Sindh has demanded a report from IG Sindh in this matter .
