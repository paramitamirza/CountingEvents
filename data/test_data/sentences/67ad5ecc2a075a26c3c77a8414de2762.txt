Sheriff 's department : Toddler shoots self at Sacramento Co. home
A 2-year - old girl accidentally shot herself in the head Monday afternoon inside a Sacramento County house while her mother and another woman were also home , deputies said . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 2 p.m. at a home in the 7600 block of Countryfield Drive . NEWLINE
