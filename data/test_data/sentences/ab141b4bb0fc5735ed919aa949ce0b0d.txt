Teen dies after accidental shooting in Phoenix - CBS 5 - KPHO
A 16-year - old boy died in what appears to be an accidental shooting .
( Source : KPHO/KTVK ) NEWLINE
A 16-year - old boy who apparently accidentally shot himself in the abdomen Monday night has died . NEWLINE
At this time , investigators believe Brandon Ramirez was mishandling a handgun when it discharged , according to Sgt .
Jonathan Howard with the Phoenix Police Department . NEWLINE
Howard said officers responded to a shooting near Missouri and 16th avenues shortly after 8 p.m. and found Ramirez suffering from a gunshot wound . NEWLINE
He was rushed to a local hospital for treatment but died overnight . NEWLINE
Another teenager remained at the scene and cooperated with police . NEWLINE
Detectives continue to investigate . NEWLINE
