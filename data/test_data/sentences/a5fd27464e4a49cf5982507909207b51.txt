4 Wounded in South Los Angeles Shooting : LAPD
Four people were wounded in a shooting in South Los Angeles on Tuesday evening , according to police . NEWLINE
Police were called to scene in the 400 block of West 89th Street ( map ) for a report of people shots just before 6 p.m. , according to Officer Christopher No of the Los Angeles Police Department ’s Media Relations Section . NEWLINE
The shooter was last seen heading southbound on Flower Street , possibly in a burgundy Jeep , No said . NEWLINE
One person was shot in the leg , but the injuries of the other three victims were not known , No said . NEWLINE
All the victims were 45 to 50 years old , No said . NEWLINE
It was not known if the shooting was gang related , he said . NEWLINE
