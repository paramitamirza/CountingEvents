Violent Friday night , Saturday morning in Milwaukee : Six shot in three separate incidents
× Violent Friday night , Saturday morning in Milwaukee : Six shot in three separate incidents NEWLINE MILWAUKEE ( WITI ) — Milwaukee police are investigating three separate shooting incidents that occurred late Friday night , August 8th and early Saturday morning , August 9th .
Police say six people sustained injuries as a result of the shootings . NEWLINE
The first shooting incident occurred in the 2700 block of N. 23rd Street ( 23rd and Center ) around 10:52 p.m. NEWLINE
According to police , a 27-year - old Milwaukee man suffered a non life - threatening gunshot wound during an ongoing neighborhood dispute . NEWLINE
He was taken to the hospital for treatment . NEWLINE
Police say there are currently no suspects in custody — and the incident remains under investigation . NEWLINE
The second shooting incident occurred in the 3000 block of N. 28th Street ( 28th and Chambers ) around 11:43 p.m. NEWLINE Police say officers on patrol in a marked squad car heard the sound of gunshots and located two shooting victims running from the scene .
Police say two other victims were located in a parking lot of a nearby business . NEWLINE
The victims , a 19-year - old male and three 17-year - old females , were taken to the hospital for treatment of non life - threatening wounds . NEWLINE
While on scene , officers were able to identify a possible suspect who was arrested at a residence nearby . NEWLINE
The circumstances surrounding this shooting are still under investigation . NEWLINE
The third shooting incident occurred in the 4000 block of N. Fond du Lac Avenue ( 40th and Fond du Lac ) around 1:10 a.m. NEWLINE
According to police , a 39-year - old West Allis woman suffered a single , non life - threatening gunshot wound during an armed robbery . NEWLINE
She was taken to the hospital by a friend for treatment . NEWLINE
Police say there are no suspects in custody — and the investigation into this matter is ongoing .
