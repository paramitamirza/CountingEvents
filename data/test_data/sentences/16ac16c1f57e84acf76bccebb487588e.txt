The shooting occurred around 12:30 p.m. in the Maple Ridge apartments in the 2100 block of Park Hill Drive . NEWLINE
Niles Young , 22 , whose address was not immediately known , was shot multiple times and died about an hour later at UPMC Presbyterian . NEWLINE
