Mesa County prosecutors charge hunter in shooting death of 14-year - old boy
Justin Burns , of Palisade , died Sept. 13 of a gunshot wound to his chest at Big Creek Reservoir NEWLINE Mesa County prosecutors Friday filed felony charges against a man who authorities say fatally shot a 14-year - old boy this month while hunting on national forest land in western Colorado . NEWLINE
Guy Pohto , 59 , faces allegations of reckless manslaughter and carelessly hunting wildlife , said Dan Rubinstein , a county prosecutor .
Pohto will be issued a felony summons and is due in court Oct. 22 . NEWLINE
Justin Burns , of Palisade , died Sept. 13 of a gunshot wound to his chest at Big Creek Reservoir in the Uncompahgre National Forest , the county coroner 's office said .
Justin was bow hunting with his father when he was shot , the sheriff 's office said . NEWLINE
Justin 's death has been ruled a homicide . NEWLINE
Authorities say Pohto was a licensed hunter . NEWLINE
Archery and muzzleloader season for various big game was in effect in Colorado at the time of the shooting .
Bear rifle season also was active across the state . NEWLINE

Family said Martin was waiting to roast marshmallows with his grandchildren when he was struck . NEWLINE
The Forest Service has introduced a plan for the Arapaho and Roosevelt national forests that it hopes will cut down on the mounting tension between shooters and other recreationists .
Affected lands would span Boulder , Grand , Clear Creek , Gilpin , Jefferson , Larimer and Park counties .
Implementation is slated for late 2016 . NEWLINE
The proposal aims to eliminate shooting on lands that are less than a half - mile from homes or in areas of highly concentrated recreational use .
It also calls for at least one designated shooting range in each of the seven counties where the forests lie . NEWLINE
