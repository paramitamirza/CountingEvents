Child recovering after being accidentally shot in Petaluma home
PETALUMA ( KRON ) —
A 11-month - old baby girl is recovering from being accidentally shot in a Petaluma home Saturday night , police said . NEWLINE
At around 6:15 p.m. , police received a report that a baby had accidentally been shot in a home on the east side of Petaluma . NEWLINE
Police brought the child to arriving paramedics from the Petaluma Fire Department . NEWLINE
The child was then rushed to Santa Rosa Memorial Hospital and flown to Children ’s Hospital in Oakland . NEWLINE
The child is expected to survive , police said . NEWLINE
Investigators are not currently planning to make any arrests .
