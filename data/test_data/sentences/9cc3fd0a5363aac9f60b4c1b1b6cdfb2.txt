Man and boy found shot to death in Back of the Yards
Man and boy found shot to death in Back of the Yards NEWLINE A man and a teenage boy were found shot to death Monday evening inside a vehicle in the Back of the Yards neighborhood on the South Side . NEWLINE
Officers discovered 19-year - old Leny Cervantes and 17-year - old Jamie Cervantes inside a vehicle about 6:15 p.m. in the 4500 block of South Wolcott , according to Chicago Police and the Cook County medical examiner ’s office . NEWLINE
The older man had been shot in the head , and the teen had been shot in the abdomen , authorities said .
Both were pronounced dead at the scene at 6:35 p.m. NEWLINE Autopsies Tuesday found both died of multiple gunshot wounds and their deaths were ruled homicides . NEWLINE
It was unclear whether the victims were related , but both lived in the 6400 block of South Bell , according to the medical examiner ’s office .
