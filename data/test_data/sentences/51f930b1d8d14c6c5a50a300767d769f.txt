7 shot in West Garfield Park
Seven people were shot in Chicago 's West Garfield Park neighborhood Friday night , officials said .
The shooting occurred in the 3300-block of Maypole .
Officials said three of the injured drove themselves to Mount Sinai Hospital .
Fire officials said one person was taken to Stroger Hospital in serious to critical condition .
Police have not yet said what the immediate circumstances of the shooting were .
An investigation is ongoing .
