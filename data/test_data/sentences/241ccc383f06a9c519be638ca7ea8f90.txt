Fort Worth Police Investigate Shooting Death Of 17-Year - Old
Follow CBSDFW.COM : Facebook | Twitter NEWLINE FORT WORTH ( CBSDFW.COM ) – Fort Worth police are investigating the death of a 17-year - old male after responding to a shooting call early Sunday morning . NEWLINE
Police were called to the 3400 block of Stonewall Lane in South Fort Worth at approximately 2:13 a.m. NEWLINE
According to police , when officers arrived , they found the victim in the driveway of the residence with an apparent gunshot wound . NEWLINE
The victim was pronounced dead at the scene . NEWLINE
The Tarrant County Medical Examiner identified the victim as 17-year - old Demarian Jones . NEWLINE
Investigators said Jones and a friend were in the driveway of his house when a man walked by and shot the 17-year - old . NEWLINE
Investigators do not know whether an altercation occurred before the shooting . NEWLINE
Police are still searching for a suspect . NEWLINE
