NORFOLK , Va. ( WVEC )
--Bond was denied Tuesday for a Norfolk father who faces two felony charges after his young son accidentally shot himself with a gun Saturday . NEWLINE
Bradley Shines told detectives that his 4-year - old son found a gun in his bedroom and the child accidentally shot himself . NEWLINE
The incident occurred around 11 a.m. in the 400 block of West 27th Street in Newport News . NEWLINE
The boy was transported to the hospital and is expected to recover . NEWLINE
Shines is charged with one count each of felony child neglect and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon . NEWLINE
Shines is scheduled for a preliminary hearing on March 21 . NEWLINE
No further information was released . NEWLINE
