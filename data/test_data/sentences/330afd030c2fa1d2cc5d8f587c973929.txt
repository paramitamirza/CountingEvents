Brooklyn shooting : Tyrone Brown arrested in connection with crime in East Flatbush neighborhood
( CNN ) -- Police in New York have arrested a man in connection with a weekend shooting at a Brooklyn house party that left nine people hospitalized . NEWLINE
Tyrone Brown , 24 , was charged with attempted murder , assault , criminal possession of a weapon and reckless endangerment , police said Tuesday . NEWLINE
The shooting started early Sunday in the East Flatbush neighborhood , police said .
A neighbor told CNN affiliate WCBS that she heard gunshots . NEWLINE
Four men and five women , all younger than 45 , were injured and taken to four hospitals . NEWLINE
The injuries were not life - threatening , New York Police Department Officer Sophia Tassy - Mason said . NEWLINE
The neighborhood at East 52nd Street is a mix of residential and commercial buildings , she said .
According to NYPD statistics , there have been 21 shooting incidents reported in the area this year . NEWLINE
CNN 's Julie Cannold contributed to this report . NEWLINE
