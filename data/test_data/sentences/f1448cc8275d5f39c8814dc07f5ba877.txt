Three charged with murder of 4-year - old girl
Ava Castillo NEWLINE HARRIS COUNTY , Texas –
Three suspects have been charged with capital murder for the shooting death of 4-year - old Ava Castillo , according to the Harris County Sheriff ’s Office . NEWLINE
The suspects have been identified as : 17-year - old Ferrell Dardar ; 17-year - old Marco Miller ; and 18-year - old Philip Battles . NEWLINE
A Harris County judge has denied bond for Dardar and Miller .
According to probable cause read in court Friday evening , Miller and Dardar helped Battles rob Castillo 's mother . NEWLINE
Ava and her 10-year - old sister , Betzida , were helping their mom , 27-year - old Diana Gomez , unload groceries when the suspects ambushed them and tried to grab Gomez ’s purse . NEWLINE
The shooting happened at the Mission Falls apartments in the 10400 block of Greens Crossing Boulevard . NEWLINE
