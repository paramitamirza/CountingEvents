Chaos erupts after Ace of Spades concert - Celebrity Examiner
| By NEWLINE SACRAMENTO , CA – At least three people were shot outside the popular concert venue , Ace of Spades , in Sacramento Friday night . NEWLINE
At approximately 11:20 p.m. Friday shots rang out in the 1400 block of R Street , just outside Ace of Spades .
According to police , several people started shooting and at one point one person jumped onto a patrol car and started firing in the air . NEWLINE
Organizers hired extra security for Rapper Nipsy Hussle ’s performance , so a number of officers were already on the scene . NEWLINE
One woman and two men were taken to a local hospital for treatment and a fourth victim drove herself to a hospital , but it is unclear if she was a shooting victim from the same location . NEWLINE
The two men received multiple gunshot wounds .
All victims are expected to survive . NEWLINE
Several businesses and cars in the area were damaged by the gunfire . NEWLINE
According to police this was not a drive - by shooting and during their investigation several people jumped on their patrol cars , with one firing a weapon into the air . NEWLINE
