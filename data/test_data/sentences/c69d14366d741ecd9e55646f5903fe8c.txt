Teen shot and killed in Overland

Police say a 15-year - old is dead after a shooting in the 9500 block of Page .
The teen was shot at at his home at around 7:15pm .
He was home alone with his three other brothers .
Their parents were out for the evening . NEWLINE
Police believe this was an accidental shooting .
Investigators have not revealed who shot the teen , or if the teen shot himself .
It does not look like charges will be filed .
Police do not believe there was criminal intent . NEWLINE
The parents were notified about the fatal shooting .
They went to the hospital where the 15-year - old was taken .
