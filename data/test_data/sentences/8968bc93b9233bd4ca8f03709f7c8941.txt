Charges filed in fatal Wichita shooting
A 22-year - old Wichita man has been charged with involuntary manslaughter for a fatal shooting death last week . NEWLINE
Jacob Pulliam made his first appearance in Sedgwick County District Court on Tuesday .
He is scheduled for a preliminary hearing on May 10th . NEWLINE
Pulliam was arrested after 19-year - old Mekko Stumblingbear was found dead April 22nd in a home in the 1800 block of West McCormick .
Officers originally thought the death was a suicide , but they learned that several other people had been in the home .
Detectives found that someone had picked up a rifle , and a shot was fired .
Pulliam was taken into custody a few hours later .
