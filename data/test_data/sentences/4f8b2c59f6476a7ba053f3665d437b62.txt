Four Injured in NE Miami - Dade Drive - By Shooting : Police
A drive - by shooting in northeast Miami - Dade over the weekend has left four people injured . NEWLINE
Miami - Dade Police say the shooting occurred Sunday evening just before 6 p.m. at Northeast Seventh Avenue and Northeast 166th Street .
Police say four victims were shot in their lower extremities by a drive - by shooter . NEWLINE
Two of the victims were taken to Aventura Hospital in stable condition , while the other two were treated on the scene and released . NEWLINE
Police have not identified a motive in the shooting . NEWLINE
Authorities are searching for an unknown black vehicle in connection with the incident . NEWLINE
