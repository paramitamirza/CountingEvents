, EMPTY 4:21 PM ET ) -- A California federal judge on Friday approved a settlement between the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission and Dialysis Clinic Inc. for a disability suit alleging that the leading dialysis nonprofit failed to reasonably accommodate an employee with breast cancer and improperly fired her .
