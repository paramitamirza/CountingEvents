Child hospitalized after shooting self in hand
( WISH Photo ) NEWLINE INDIANAPOLIS ( WISH ) – Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department confirmed that a child shot himself in the hand on the city ’s north east side Monday afternoon . NEWLINE
IMPD responded to the 9000 block of East 39th Place just after 3 p.m. for a child who was shot in the hand .
Police say it was self - inflicted . NEWLINE
The child was taken to Riley Hospital for Children at IU Health for treatment .
The extent of the child ’s injuries was unclear . NEWLINE
