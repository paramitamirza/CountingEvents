2 shot and wounded , 3 others grazed by gunfire at party in South LA
A shooting Saturday evening in the Gramercy Park neighborhood of South L.A. left several people injured , authorities said .
The incident was reported at 9:40 p.m. in the 1600 block of Century Boulevard , where a " large " party was being held , according to a spokesperson for the Los Angeles County Sheriff 's Department .
Two victims were hospitalized with major injuries after being shot but were expected to survive , the spokesperson said .
Three others were treated at the scene and released after suffering grazing wounds .
The shooter or shooters were at large .
No description was available .
The motive for the shooting was unknown .
