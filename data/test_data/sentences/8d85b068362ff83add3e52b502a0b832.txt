Several shot at Las Vegas wedding reception Saturday Posted : Sunday , February 16 , 2014 1:50 PM EST Updated : Sunday , April 27 , 2014 2:31 PM EDT NEWLINE Crime scene tape surrounds the parking lot of a strip mall Feb. 16 , 2014 , after a shooting .
( Arron Healy/FOX5 ) NEWLINE
Several people were shot at a wedding reception in Las Vegas on Saturday night , according to police . NEWLINE
The shooting happened about 11:30 p.m. at the El Palacio Del Sol banquet hall near the intersection of Sahara Avenue and Maryland Parkway . NEWLINE
A spokesman for Las Vegas Metro police at the scene told FOX5 that as many as five people were wounded in the shooting .
He said the injuries appeared not to be life - threatening . NEWLINE
The spokesman also said the gunman had been arrested . NEWLINE
The shooting is under investigation , police said . NEWLINE
