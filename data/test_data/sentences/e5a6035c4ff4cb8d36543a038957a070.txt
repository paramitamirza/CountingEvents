Boy shot while hunting west of Phoenix dies
PHOENIX -- A 9-year - old boy shot early Wednesday morning during a hunting accident west of Phoenix has died , the Maricopa County Sheriff 's Office said . NEWLINE
The boy was with two people in their early 20s and a pair of 15-year - olds near 363rd and Durango avenues in Tonopah .
He was airlifted to Phoenix Children 's Hospital with a life - threatening gunshot wound to his neck and later succumbed to his injuries .
The shot was fired about 5 a.m. NEWLINE
According to an initial investigation by the Maricopa County Sheriff 's Office , the boy may have been hit by a bullet from a handgun that ricocheted off a rock . NEWLINE
No charges have been filed .
