TAMPA , FL ( WFLA ) –

Manuel Otiz , 28 , died , three more people are at a hospital with gunshot wounds after a man opened fire at a crowd of people at the Tampa bar , according to the Hillsborough County Sheriff ’s Office . NEWLINE
It happened at about 4 a.m. near the Smokin Bull Hooka Bar on Bearss Avenue in Tampa .
According to HCSO , it started with a verbal fight , after which a man went to his car , grabbed a gun and opened fire at a crowd of about 50 people near the bar . NEWLINE
He fired about 10 rounds , deputies said .
