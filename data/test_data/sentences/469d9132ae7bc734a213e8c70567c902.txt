Five people shot in Wilmington , police say
Buy Photo Police and emergency personnel tend to a young person after multiple people were shot in the Hedgeville neighborhood of Wilmington about 9:10 p.m. Tuesday night .
( Photo : WILLIAM BRETZGER/THE NEWS JOURNAL)Buy Photo NEWLINE Wilmington police are on the scene in the area of South Van Buren and Elm Street in reference to a multiple - shooting investigation . NEWLINE
Officers were called to the Hedgeville neighborhood about 10:15 p.m. following reports of shots fired , said Sgt .
Andrea Janvier , a spokeswoman with the Wilmington Police Department . NEWLINE
A total of five individuals were transported to area hospitals and are considered to be in stable condition . NEWLINE
Police closed several blocks of the neighborhood as they investigated the shootings .
A police helicopter flew above the area . NEWLINE
At lest nine evidence cones could be seen in front of a corner house at the intersection of South Van Buren and Elm streets . NEWLINE
The investigation is in the preliminary stages and ongoing .
Anyone with information relative to this investigation is asked to call Detective Lindsay Hageman . NEWLINE
