A 2-year - old child died from a self - inflicted gunshot wound on Indianapolis ' northwest side Wednesday night , police said . NEWLINE
Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department officers were called to the 5200 block of Alameda Road , near 52nd Street and West Kessler Boulevard , on a report of a person shot shortly after 9 p.m. NEWLINE When officers arrived , they found a 2-year - old child with a gunshot wound to the shoulder .
The child was taken to Riley Hospital for Children at IU Health in critical condition , and he later died . NEWLINE
Police confirmed that the gunshot wound was self - inflicted . NEWLINE
