Goodlettsville , Tenn. police say 5-year - old boy was shot by older sibling
Courtesy of WKRN NEWLINE GOODLETTSVILLE , Tenn. ( WKRN ) –
Goodlettsville police have determined that a 5-year - old boy who died over the weekend was shot by an older sibling . NEWLINE
It happened at an apartment complex in the 400 block of Moss Trail around 5:30 p.m. Saturday . NEWLINE
Police said their investigation revealed the children ’s parents were at home at the time of the shooting . NEWLINE
Prior to the shooting , the dad had been watching television with the two children and was playing with the family ’s cat with a laser mounted onto a gun . NEWLINE
Police said the man later went into the kitchen and forgot to put the firearm out of the children ’s reach . NEWLINE
The older child picked up the gun in an effort to get the cat to chase the laser .
Police said the child was unfamiliar with how the laser worked and instead pulled the gun ’s trigger , firing one shot . NEWLINE
The 5-year - old victim was struck and killed . NEWLINE
The father stated he kept a weapon nearby for protection . NEWLINE
The Davidson County District Attorney ’s Office is reviewing the case and will determine if the father will face any charges . NEWLINE
Additional information was not released . NEWLINE
