Five wounded in shooting at Tampa nightclub
Tampa police are investigating a shooting that injured five people early Saturday at the Supa D nightclub . NEWLINE
According to officers , unidentified people fired several shots after an argument broke out near the dance floor about 2 a.m. NEWLINE Two of those wounded drove themselves to area hospitals , and three were transported to hospitals by Tampa Fire Rescue , police said .
All were expected to be treated and released . NEWLINE
No suspects have been identified .
