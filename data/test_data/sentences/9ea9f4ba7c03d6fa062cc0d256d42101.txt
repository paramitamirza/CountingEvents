Man dressed as Freddy Krueger shoots five at Halloween party
SAN ANTONIO -- A Halloween party ended with violence after police said a man dressed as Freddy Krueger opened fire on a large crowd . NEWLINE
The shooting happened on the 2900 block of Aspen Meadow around 5 a.m. on Sunday .
One of the residents who did not want to go on camera said he was throwing the party with his housemates and was taking fees at the door . NEWLINE
He said several men he did not know showed up to the home and started a fight with his friends .
He said the argument escalated into a shooting . NEWLINE
He said several people who attended the party captured part of the fight on Snapchat .
This later reportedly helped police identify that there were two shooters involved .
He said one man had a shotgun and the second man had a hand - gun . NEWLINE
There were five people who were injured in the shooting .
Police said that one injured woman tried to drive to the hospital , but wrecked her car along the way .
Jasmine De Hoyos , who attended the party , said there were more than a hundred people at the home .
She said she helped one of the victims who got shot in the arm . NEWLINE
" I kind of applied pressure to the wound to make sure he did n't bleed out .
A couple of his friends were there with us .
So , we were trying to keep him calm , " said De Hoyos . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made and police are still searching for the shooters . NEWLINE
" I 'm kind of sad that this happened in this neighborhood because I like living here .
It 's a really good neighborhood .
It is what it is , and we 're just going to keep an eye out and try to keep each other ’s back , " said Jeremy Collins , another neighbor . NEWLINE
