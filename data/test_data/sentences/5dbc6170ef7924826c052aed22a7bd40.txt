Four teens taken to hospital after Evansville shooting - Tri - State News , Weather & Sports
Four teens are taken to the hospital after a shooting in Evansville . NEWLINE
Police say two groups of teens met on South Bedford Avenue near East Cherry Street around 10:30 Monday night and got into a verbal fight , which ended when four people were shot . NEWLINE
Police confirm one of the victims was shot in the hand and another victim was shot in the leg . NEWLINE
All four teens went to the hospital , but not by ambulance .
Police say they got there on their own . NEWLINE
