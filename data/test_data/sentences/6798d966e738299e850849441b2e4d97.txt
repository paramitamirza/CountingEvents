Five people shot in Hot Springs , no shooter identified
( Photo : Jake Pendergist , THV11 ) NEWLINE HOT SPRINGS , Ark. ( KTHV ) - Five people were shot Thursday night around 6:53 p.m. in Hot Springs near the 300 block of Lacey Street . NEWLINE
Hot Springs police at this time have no suspects in custody .
One of the victims was airlifted to a hospital while the other four were treated at the local St. Vincent hospital .
Crowd control was called to the hospital after the four victims had arrived . NEWLINE
Detectives are still on the scene interviewing witnesses to determine a cause .
We will have more information as it becomes available . NEWLINE
