1 dead , 4 injured in shooting on Sobota Circle - WMC Action News 5 - Memphis , Tennessee
One person is dead and four others are injured after an early morning shooting in Southwest Memphis Sunday . NEWLINE
The shooting happened in the 3300 block of Sobota Circle in the Richmond Place Apartments . NEWLINE
First responders pronounced one person dead on the scene .
Another person was taken to the hospital in extremely critical condition .
Three others were taken to the hospital in non - critical condition . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made .
This is an ongoing investigation . NEWLINE
