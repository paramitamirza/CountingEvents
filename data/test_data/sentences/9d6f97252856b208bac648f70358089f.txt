Deadly shooting in Auburn puts schools in lockdown
Police investigate a shooting in Auburn , Wash. , Oct. 7 , 2016 .
( Credit : KING ) NEWLINE AUBURN , Wash. -- Police are searching for a shooter who killed one person and caused several schools to go on precautionary lockdown . NEWLINE
The shooting happened in the 2400 block of F Street Friday morning .
Police found the victim , a male , in a car .
The victim later died . NEWLINE
A K9 could not track the suspect , described only as a black male in his late teens or early 20s . NEWLINE
It appears the victim was inside the car and the suspect shot them from outside the car . NEWLINE
Olympic Middle School , Pioneer Elementary , and Terminal Park Elementary , were placed on lockdown as a precaution .
Gildo Rey Elementary and Mount Baker Middle School were also placed on modified lockdown .
Those lockdowns have been lifted . NEWLINE
