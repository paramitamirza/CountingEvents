7 shot in single incident in W. Baltimore early Sunday
Seven people were shot — all of them in the leg — in a single incident overnight on Baltimore 's west side , police said early Sunday . NEWLINE
Police responded to an area hospital at 2:26 a.m. Sunday , where five people walked in with gunshot wounds .
Shortly thereafter , officers encountered two additional victims from the same incident — which police said transpired in the 2700 block of Garrison Avenue in the Central Park Heights neighborhood . NEWLINE
All seven victims suffered gunshot wounds to their legs , and were listed in stable condition , police said . NEWLINE
The victims included 20- and 23-year - old women , and men ranging in ages from 22 to 25 . NEWLINE
Northwest District detectives are investigating and anyone with information may call 410 - 396 - 2466 . NEWLINE
The shootings transpired amid a violent two - hour span in Baltimore in which 10 people were shot and one man was killed . NEWLINE
The homicide was the second of the first weekend in August after 45 people were killed in July — equaling the most in a given month in at least four decades . NEWLINE
