Early Morning Shooting Was Accidental , Self - Inflicted - LEX18.com | Continuous News and StormTracker Weather
Lexington police have concluded their investigation into an early morning shooting after they learned that the shooting was accidentally self - inflicted . NEWLINE
Officers responded to the area of South Broadway and Cedar Street , about a block from the Tolly - Ho Restaurant , around 3:30 a.m. Police say the victim was about 17-years - old and suffered a gunshot wound to the leg . NEWLINE
EMS took the victim to UK Hospital . NEWLINE
Officers say they had a difficult time piecing together exactly what happened because the victim was not particularly cooperative and told different officers conflicting stories .
Eventually they determined that the victim accidentally shot himself .
