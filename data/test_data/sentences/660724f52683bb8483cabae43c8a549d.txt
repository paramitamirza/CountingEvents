Police : 5 shot in downtown Tacoma
.
( Photo : KING 5 News ) NEWLINE TACOMA
, Wash. - Tacoma Police are investigating after five people were shot in downtown Tacoma early Saturday morning . NEWLINE
Police reported hearing gunshots just before 2 a.m. on Pacific Avenue between 7th Street and 9th Street . NEWLINE
Officers found two victims at the scene with gunshot wounds and had them taken to a hospital .
Three other victims with gunshot wounds also showed up at St. Joseph Medical Center around the same time . NEWLINE
The condition of the victims is not known .
Police have not confirmed if all victims are connected to the same shooting . NEWLINE
