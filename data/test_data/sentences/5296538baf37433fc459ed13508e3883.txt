Police Say Woman Shot Her Husband & Son
DUNDALK , Md. ( WJZ ) —
Baltimore County police say a 30-year - old woman shot her estranged husband and their son Saturday evening . NEWLINE
Police say the woman had gone to her husband ’s home to pick up their son when the two started fighting .
The husband started to take the boy inside when she pulled out a gun and fired .
The husband was struck in the upper body and the son was struck in his lower body . NEWLINE
Both are expected to survive . NEWLINE
The woman was taken into custody at the scene .
