Campbellsville police investigate after parent accidentally shoots 4-year - old
Campbellsville police have launched an investigation after a 4-year - old child was accidentally shot in the leg on Sunday . NEWLINE
The Campbellsville Police Department , in a news release issued Tuesday , said officers received a call at 10:50 a.m. Sunday about someone who was injured during a shooting in the 400 block of Lowell Avenue .
When officers arrived , they found a four - year - old child who had been shot in the lower right leg . NEWLINE
