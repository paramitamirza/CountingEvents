Jury finds Trung Le guilty in Bourbon Street shooting
5 . Trung Le is being held on charges of manslaughter and attempted second - degree murder after a July shooting on Bourbon Street that killed one person and injured nine more .
( Photo : NOPD ) NEWLINE
NEW ORLEANS --
A jury found 22-year - old Trung Le guilty of manslaughter in connection with a 2014 shooting on Bourbon Street that left one person dead and nine others injured . NEWLINE
The jury deliberated for fewer than two hours Friday night before returning the verdict .
Le was also found guilty of attempted second - degree murder . NEWLINE
The July 29 , 2014 , shooting took the life of Brittany Thomas and left several others with wounds they still cope with today . NEWLINE
Brittany Thomas ' mother was present at the courtroom and was teary - eyed when she spoke to WWL - TV reporter Tania Dall .
She said she 's glad to " see justice for Brittany . " NEWLINE
