5-Year - Old Accidentally Shot
After Finding Gun
Follow CBSDFW.COM : Facebook | Twitter NEWLINE KAUFMAN COUNTY ( CBS 11 ) — Investigators are still trying to piece together what led to the accidental shooting of a 5-year - old boy near Forney .
Much of the activity CBS 11 saw from detectives focused on a trailer next to Official Paintball Games of Texas . NEWLINE
In a positive sign , a family member said when the doctors went to intubate the boy , he woke up and he was alert .
That ’s certainly good news , considering that deputies said he was shot in the back of the head . NEWLINE
Investigators say two little boys , a 5-year - old and a 6-year - old , were watching TV in a travel trailer in the remote area , in the 1700 block of Adams Trail .
Investigators said the boys were apparently the only ones inside the trailer .
The family says the boys are brothers , and their mother was inside helping sign in people at the paintball center . NEWLINE
Sheriff ’s deputies said at some point the 5-year - old found a 9mm Glock handgun .
The 6-year - old reportedly told the 5-year - old to put away the gun , but the next thing he knew , he heard a gunshot and found his brother on the floor . NEWLINE
First responders flew the 5-year - old to Cook Children ’s Medical Center , where at last check he was , according to the family , fighting the doctors as they tried to intubate him . NEWLINE
The family also said the 6-year - old was not hurt and is back home . NEWLINE
