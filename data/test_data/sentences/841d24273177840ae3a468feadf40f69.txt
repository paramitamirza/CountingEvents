The News Tribune of Tacoma reports the teen pleaded not guilty Friday in the death of 17-year - old Jatarius Tolbert .
He is being held on $ 1 million bail . NEWLINE
Court records show Tolbert was shot as he was hanging out in a Tacoma parking lot May 11 with other young men .
Someone in Tolbert 's group fired a gun at a car as it passed , and his acquaintances told police someone inside the car returned fire . NEWLINE
Prosecutors say the state crime lab later determined the bullet that killed Tolbert came from a revolver police found on the suspect when they stopped a car he was in a few days later . NEWLINE
Detectives who interviewed the suspect reportedly said he was despondent over accidentally shooting his friend . NEWLINE
