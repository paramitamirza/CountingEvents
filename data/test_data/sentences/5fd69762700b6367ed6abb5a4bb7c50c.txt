Two dead in shooting at Monroe Civic Center
Monroe Police are investigating a shooting which killed two people early Sunday morning at the Monroe Civic Center . NEWLINE
Police say the shooting happened in the north parking lot of the civic center after a closed event .
They say multiple victims were hit by bullets , two of which have died as a result of their injuries .
Police say this was a result of an ongoing feud , and the two people dead were acquaintances . NEWLINE
This is a developing story .
