Police : Man mishandles handgun , accidentally shoots himself in leg at Colorado Springs 7-Eleven
Caption + Michael Ciaglo , The Gazette NEWLINE A man mishandled a handgun and accidentally shot himself in the leg in the parking lot of a Colorado Springs 7-Eleven on Sunday afternoon , police said . NEWLINE
Reports came in just after 3 p.m. that a man suffered a self - inflicted gunshot wound at the convenient store on the 2900 block of East Fountain Boulevard , police said .
The man left the scene before the arrival of officers and went to a local hospital . NEWLINE
The unidentified man was not arrested after police said the shooting was accidental . NEWLINE
No other details were released .
