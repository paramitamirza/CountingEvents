Buckeye police investigate accidental shooting of teen girl - CBS 5 - KPHO
Buckeye police are investigating the death of 17-year - old Dannielle Rangel after her family says a rifle fell over and accidentally discharged a bullet , striking Rangel in the head . NEWLINE
On Monday around 10:30 p.m. , Rangel 's family says her brother was moving his rifle , a 22 caliber AR-15 .
He allegedly set the rifle down and it fell over and went off . NEWLINE
Rangel was rushed to a nearby hospital , but died the next day .
Her uncle , Arthur Velasquez , says the family is devastated to lose a girl who was so full of life . NEWLINE
" She was very outspoken ; she was always cheerful .
She was always polite , " Velasquez said . NEWLINE
The family also says she had a bright future ahead , looking forward to college and a career in law . NEWLINE
" She was getting prepared to take college courses to go into college and she wanted to be a district attorney , " Velasquez said . NEWLINE
The family is facing a new crisis : how to pay for her funeral .
They have set up a gofundme site to help . NEWLINE
Police are investigating , but no charges have been brought against her brother . NEWLINE
