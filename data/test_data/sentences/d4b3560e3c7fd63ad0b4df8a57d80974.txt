2016-05-29
Police seeking two suspects in shooting that injured four people near downtown hotel
Las Vegas police said four people were injured Sunday morning after an argument led to a shooting on the 200 block of Third Street . NEWLINE
Just before 6:20 a.m. , responding officers investigating the shooting found four adults — two men and two women — suffering from gunshot wounds , police said .
They were taken to University Medical Center , where one man is in critical condition . NEWLINE
The investigation shows that two unknown suspects were having an argument on the sidewalk .
As the four adults walked by them , the two groups exchanged some words , police said .
At some point , one of the suspects entered a white Lexus sedan with dark tint and a sunroof parked nearby and got a handgun . NEWLINE
The suspect then fired at the victims , striking them , police said .
The two suspects then got into the sedan and fired at the victims again as they sped away . NEWLINE
Police described the first suspect as a black man in his 20s , 6-foot-1 and about 180 pounds , wearing a maroon , long - sleeve shirt and blue jeans .
The second suspect is described as a light - skinned black man in his 20s , about 5-foot-10 , last seen wearing an orange shirt and camouflage shorts . NEWLINE
Anyone with any information is urged to contact the Metropolitan Police Department downtown area command at 702 - 828 - 4135 , or to remain anonymous , call Crime Stoppers at 702 - 385 - 5555 . NEWLINE
Ogden Street was closed between Casino Center Boulevard and Fourth Street for several hours while Metro officers investigated the shooting . NEWLINE
