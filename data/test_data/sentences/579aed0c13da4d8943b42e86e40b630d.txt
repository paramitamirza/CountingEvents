Man arrested after cousin shot while 2 were playing with gun - KSLA News 12 Shreveport , Louisiana News Weather & Sports
A 19-year - old Shreveport man spent Christmas in jail for allegedly shooting his cousin . NEWLINE
Jarrett Thigpen , of the 1900 block of Hickory Street , was arrested at 10:15 p.m. Dec. 24 then booked into Shreveport City Jail at 10:39 p.m. Dec. 24 . NEWLINE
He is charged with 1 count of illegal use of a weapon . NEWLINE
Thigpen and his cousin were playing with a gun when the firearm went off , striking the cousin in the abdomen , according to City Jail booking records . NEWLINE
The cousin 's name and the extent of the cousin 's wound were not immediately available . NEWLINE
Thigpen remained in the City Jail the evening of Dec. 25 . NEWLINE
