by Larry Lee NEWLINE FRIENDSHIP , Wis. ( WSAU ) -- A woman is dead after what Adams County authorities called an accidental shooting .
Sheriff Sam Wollin says deputies were called to a Town of Preston address just thirteen minutes after midnight Sunday morning .
The victim was fuond bleeding and unresponsive by emergency responders , and lifesaving measures were unsuccessful .
Wollin says 26-year - old Constance Heintz of Friendship was pronounced dead upon arrival at Moundview Memorial Hospital . NEWLINE
