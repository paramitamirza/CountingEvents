The Weld County Coroner ’s office today released the name of the man who died in shooting early Saturday in LaSalle . NEWLINE
Raymond Figueroa , 23 , died after being shot by a 17-year - old boy just after 1 a.m. in a residence about six miles southeast of LaSalle .
The person who called 911 told dispatchers he accidentally shot his friend while handling a gun . NEWLINE
Weld County Sheriff ’s deputies arrested the boy on suspicion of negligent homicide .
