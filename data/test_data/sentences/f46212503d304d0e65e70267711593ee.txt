Carjacking , shooting outside Atlanta Wendy 's linked to another shooting
Updated : Oct 26 , 2016 - 7:20 PM NEWLINE ATLANTA - Police are investigating a deadly shooting that covers two crime scenes in southwest Atlanta . NEWLINE
Investigators said the incident started outside a Wendy ’s restaurant on Hamilton E. Holmes Drive near Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard . NEWLINE
Officer told Channel 2 ’s Tom Jones around 3:30 p.m. Wednesday two groups in two cars , a red Lexus and a black Toyota , met at the Wendy ’s and there was an exchange of gunfire between the two groups . NEWLINE
A 15-year - old was shot and was found at the Wendy ’s .
He later died at Grady Memorial Hospital . NEWLINE
Another 15-year - old in the red Lexus was also shot .
That victim was later found at an apartment complex on Fairburn Road .
At last check , the teen was in critical condition . NEWLINE

Police are talking to possible witnesses or suspects . NEWLINE

Charles Hampton of the Atlanta Police Department said . NEWLINE
