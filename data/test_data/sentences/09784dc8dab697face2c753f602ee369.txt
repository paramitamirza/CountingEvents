Dallas police arrest murder suspect who ' started shooting at everyone ' during Motel 6 robbery
Updated at 11:13 a.m. Aug. 17 : NEWLINE Dallas police have arrested a second murder suspect in a robbery and shooting at a Motel 6 that left one dead and three injured last week . NEWLINE
Christopher Vince , 27 , and Reginald Williams , 22 , are charged with one count of capital murder .
They remain in the Dallas County Jail on $ 1 million bail . NEWLINE
Vince and Williams are suspected of killing Kelvin Jackson , 38 , and wounding three other people , Kendra Covey , 28 ; Elizabeth Taylor - Laaker , 20 ; and Jimmy Sangster , 30 . NEWLINE
According to Williams ' arrest warrant , Vince and Williams went to a Motel 6 on the 10900 block of Estate Lane around 1:30 p.m. on Friday . NEWLINE
Surveillance footage shows the two men standing outside the victims ' motel room holding guns , before they entered . NEWLINE
One of the victims told police Vince " started shooting at everyone and demanding money " once they got inside the room , while Williams pointed a gun at her and demanded money , the affidavit states . NEWLINE
The robbers ran out of the room a short time later . NEWLINE
Police arrested Williams in the parking lot of the motel .
Upon his arrest , Williams gave police Vince 's name and description .
Vince remained at large until Tuesday , when he was arrested . NEWLINE
Covey , Taylor - Laaker and Sangster are expected to recover from their wounds , police said . NEWLINE
At the time of the shooting , Williams was on probation for an aggravated robbery he committed in December .
He pleaded guilty on July 28 and received seven years of deferred probation and a $ 1,500 fine . NEWLINE
