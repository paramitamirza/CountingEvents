Police ID Suspect in Killing of Boy , 7 , in SW Miami - Dade Drive - By Shooting
Police ID Suspect in Killing of Boy , 7 , in ... NEWLINE
Police are searching for four suspects in connection with a drive - by shooting that killed a 7-year - old child in southwest Miami - Dade .
( Published Monday , Dec 28 , 2015 ) NEWLINE
A 19-year - old is wanted for second - degree murder in connection with a drive - by shooting that left a 7-year - old boy dead in southwest Miami - Dade on Sunday . NEWLINE
The Miami - Dade Police Department has identified Dravein Duke , also known as " Pop , " as a suspect in the fatal shooting , which occurred around 3:25 p.m. Sunday in the area of Southwest 152nd Street and Jackson Street . NEWLINE
According to police , a white Dodge Ram drove past the home and someone in the vehicle opened fire , hitting 7-year - old Amiere Castro as he sat inside the house . NEWLINE
The boy was pronounced dead at the scene .
Police said Castro did not live at the home and was visiting with relatives . NEWLINE
Miami - Dade Police Deputy Director Juan Perez called the shooting a " shocking event " and a " tragedy . " NEWLINE
Investigators said they are looking for a white , newer model four - door Dodge Ram with dark tinted windows and a paper tag .
They say the vehicle was occupied by four men . NEWLINE
According to police , Duke is considered armed and dangerous . NEWLINE
