Two teenagers have been charged with the fatal shooting of Congressman Danny Davis’ 15-year - old grandson in Englewood Friday evening . NEWLINE
A 16-year - old boy and 17-year - old girl are each charged with first - degree murder , according to Chicago Police .
Their names were not released Sunday morning because they are juveniles . NEWLINE
They are scheduled to appear in bond court Sunday , police said . NEWLINE
Police say a dispute over gym shoes led to the fatal shooting of 15-year - old Javon Wilson . NEWLINE
