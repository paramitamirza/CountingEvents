5 injured in Saturday night shooting - KMOV.com
Police are currently investigating a shooting that injured five late Saturday night . NEWLINE
The shooting occurred around 10:41 p.m. in the 5000 block of Kensington Avenue , just north of Delmar Boulevard near Fountain Park . NEWLINE
Police responded to the scene , where they found a man with a gunshot wound to the chest .
He was falling in and out of consciousness .
He was transported to the hospital , where his condition has since been updated from critical , stable condition . NEWLINE
In the span of of less than three hours , four more victims were conveyed to the hospital .
All four were victims of the shooting on Kensington . NEWLINE
Unlike the first victim , the other four are in stable condition for gunshot wounds ranging from the leg to grazes on the arm . NEWLINE
The investigation into the shooting is ongoing .
At this time , police do not have any information on a suspect . NEWLINE
