Two - year - old boy shot himself with father ’s gun , sheriff ’s office says
Authorities are investigating how a 2-year - old boy shot himself with his father ’s gun inside a Waldorf area apartment , a Charles County sheriff ’s office spokeswoman said Wednesday . NEWLINE
Diane Richardson , a department spokeswoman , said investigators believe the toddler shot himself in the leg while his father was sleeping . NEWLINE
Authorities got a call from the father around 3:45 p.m. He said he was asleep when he heard a gun go off inside his home in the 3300 block of Patapsco Place , according to Richardson . NEWLINE
The father , who told authorities he owns the handgun , said he saw his 2-year - old with a gunshot wound in the upper leg , she said . NEWLINE
The toddler was flown to an area hospital alert and conscious with non - life - threatening injuries , Richardson said .
Authorities , who say they believe the boy ’s father is a security guard , are investigating how the 2-year - old got hold of the weapon , she said .
