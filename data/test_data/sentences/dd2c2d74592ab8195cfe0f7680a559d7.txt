Suspect Sought
After 1 Killed , 5 More Wounded In Strip District Shooting
PITTSBURGH ( KDKA ) –
Police are looking for a suspect after an argument , reportedly over loud motorcycles , led to an overnight shooting in the Strip District that left one person dead and five others wounded . NEWLINE
It happened around 1 a.m. at Penn Avenue and 29th Street , outside of Art ’s Tavern . NEWLINE
One victim died at the scene , 40-year - old Tyrone Tomman of Penn Hills .
Two others were taken to the hospital . NEWLINE
Three more victims showed up at local hospitals , including one at Children ’s Hospital . NEWLINE
Police collected more than 20 shell casings from the hail of bullets . NEWLINE
Police say it started when four men from the North Side got into an argument with members of the Pittsburgh Gentlemen ’s motorcycle club . NEWLINE “
A group of four other people , who claim they were from the North Side , were complaining about their motorcycles being too loud and they were taking parking spaces , ” said Lt .
Daniel Herrmann , of Pittsburgh Police . NEWLINE
An argument inside eventually led to gunshots in the street . NEWLINE
Tomman died next to his motorcycle .
Police say another victim is in critical condition .
The other victims are all believed in stable condition with various gunshot wounds .
All of the victims are between the ages of 38 and 44 . NEWLINE
Police say they believe the gunmen left the area firing shots from a black SUV. NEWLINE
The same black SUV was found burning in the 500-block of Francis Street in the Hill District . NEWLINE
“
It was engulfed in flames , ” said Lt .
Herrmann .
“ We went to that scene believing it was part of our shooting incident , and we believe it was part of our shooting incident .
” NEWLINE
Police aren’t sure if anyone in the motorcycle club fired shots .
The club is not known for any criminal activity . NEWLINE
Police say they are looking for at least one suspect , a black male , 6-feet-7-inches tall and weighing approximately 240 to 250 pounds . NEWLINE
“
He was seen yelling at the victims about their motorcycles , ” Lt .
Herrmann said .
“ It ’s a very descript , physical description of a 6-foot-7 gentleman .
” NEWLINE Police don’t think the bar itself had anything to do with the shooting , but there was a large crowd of people outside and police are hoping someone in the crowd can help lead them to the suspects . NEWLINE
No arrests have been made . NEWLINE
