Man accidentally shot himself with stolen gun
SAN ANTONIO - Joe Hernandez , 17 , is accused of stealing guns from someone else 's car , then shooting himself while playing with one of those weapons . NEWLINE
An arrest warrant affidavit stated that San Antonio police responded to a call from a home on the Northwest side and found Hernandez suffering from a gunshot wound to the stomach .
Officers later determined that the wound was self - inflicted . NEWLINE
While they were handling that incident , officers received another call about a car burglary at a home in the 3300 block of Rock Creek Run , about a block from the shooting scene . NEWLINE
The affidavit stated that the car owner reported that , among other things , the thief had taken two handguns from his vehicle . NEWLINE
Investigators spoke with a friend of Hernandez who told them that Hernandez had broken into that car and stolen the weapons , the affidavit stated .
The friend said that Hernandez shot himself while " messing around " with one of the guns in his home , the affidavit stated . NEWLINE
Police obtained a warrant for Hernandez 's arrest in January and took him into custody Tuesday .
He is charged with two counts of theft of a firearm . NEWLINE
