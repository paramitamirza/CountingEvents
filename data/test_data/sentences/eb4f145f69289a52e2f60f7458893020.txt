Man gunned down in car in Hub ’s first slay of year
Boston ’s first murder of 2015 happened last night in Roxbury when a carload of people was targeted by gunfire , police said . NEWLINE
Police Commissioner William Evans , who was at the scene , said the car was on Harrishof Street about 10:30 p.m. when the people were shot as they sat in the car . NEWLINE
The white sedan full of gunshot victims sped away from the scene and was spotted by police who stopped it at Dale and Walnut streets several blocks away . NEWLINE
