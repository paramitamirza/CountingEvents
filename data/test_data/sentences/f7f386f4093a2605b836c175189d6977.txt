Lauderdale Co. deputy 's son killed in accidental shooting
Lauderdale County Sheriff Billy Sollie says the teenage son of a Lauderdale County deputy has died after an accidental shooting . NEWLINE
It happened around 8:30 Saturday evening in a home on Mt. Horeb Rd .
just off Highway 145 .
Chief Deputy Ward Calhoun says the victim was in the home with a friend at the time of the accidental shooting .
The victim was transported by Metro Ambulance to a Meridian hospital where he was pronounced dead .
